Appearance
----------


Nanomech is about a centimeter tall at full size. He has grey skin with a green circuit-like design on it. Nanomech possesses green wings as well as one large, green cyclopean-eye. His body is a carbon or silicon-based alloy, and he has triangular feet. He wore the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In *[Video Games](/wiki/Video_Games "Video Games")*, Nanomech wore a blue motion capture suit.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Nanomech's circuit design is gone, being replaced with one pushing more to his ***[Alien Swarm](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Swarm "Ben 10: Alien Swarm")*** design, and the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol is where his eye was. He has different wings and four small eyes around the Omnitrix, similar to [Stinkfly](/wiki/Stinkfly_(Classic) "Stinkfly (Classic)"). His legs have also changed shape, and his body now emits a bright green glow that completely surrounds him.

Nanomech is capable of flying, thanks to possessing a pair of wings. He is very agile and his wings glow when he is airborne.[[1]](#cite_note-UA-1)[[2]](#cite_note-2)


Nanomech can reduce his size at will, even to microscopic scales.[[3]](#cite_note-AS-3) He can return to his original size, but cannot grow any larger as his maximum size is very small.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-4)


Nanomech can launch green bioelectrical energy from his hands and eye, ranging in forms from beams to orbs.[[4]](#cite_note-I13-5)


Nanomech has the ability to adapt to his opponents very fast, such as sprouting energy tentacles like the [Decoy Queen](/wiki/Decoy_Queen "Decoy Queen") seconds after she attacked him with her own.[[3]](#cite_note-AS-3)


Nanomech's adaptability allows him to understand the functions of machinery and tech, and use that information to make them malfunction and break in a matter of seconds.[[5]](#cite_note-VG-6)


Nanomech cannot be controlled by Nanochip Queens; according to himself, it is because he is part [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)"), and "humans never give up".[[3]](#cite_note-AS-3)


Because he is a technological being, it is possible for a [Galvanic Mechamorph](/wiki/Galvanic_Mechamorph_(Classic) "Galvanic Mechamorph (Classic)") to merge with Nanomech.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-7)

History
-------


### Alien Force


* Nanomech first appeared in ***[Ben 10: Alien Swarm](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Swarm "Ben 10: Alien Swarm")***, where he was created by the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") scanning the [Nanochips](/wiki/Nanochip "Nanochip") and entered [Victor](/wiki/Victor_Validus "Victor Validus")'s brain to fight the [Decoy Queen](/wiki/Decoy_Queen "Decoy Queen"). After a grueling battle, Nanomech destroyed the Queen and thus the entirety of the chips, freeing everybody from their control.


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Video Games](/wiki/Video_Games "Video Games")*, Nanomech was used for the video game scan but was regarded as too small, so he switched into [Jetray](/wiki/Jetray_(Classic) "Jetray (Classic)"). Later, Nanomech disabled and defeated the [Stalker](/wiki/Stalker "Stalker").
* In *[Ultimate Aggregor](/wiki/Ultimate_Aggregor_(Episode) "Ultimate Aggregor (Episode)")*, Nanomech destroyed the headbands that were controlling [Ra'ad](/wiki/Ra%27ad "Ra'ad") and [P'andor](/wiki/P%27andor "P'andor"). As [the team](/wiki/Alien_Force "Alien Force") confronted [Aggregor](/wiki/Aggregor "Aggregor"), Nanomech switched into [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)").
* In *[Absolute Power: Part 1](/wiki/Absolute_Power:_Part_1 "Absolute Power: Part 1")*, Nanomech was squashed in [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)")'s [spellbook](/wiki/Gwen%27s_Spellbook "Gwen's Spellbook") by one of her [spells](/wiki/Spells "Spells"), forcing him to switch into [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)").
* In *[Inspector #13](/wiki/Inspector_13_(Episode) "Inspector 13 (Episode)")*, [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") as Nanomech snuck into the [Techadon Factory](/wiki/Techadon_Factory "Techadon Factory") and was defeated by [Inspector 13](/wiki/Inspector_13 "Inspector 13").


### Omniverse


* In *[Have I Got a Deal for You](/wiki/Have_I_Got_a_Deal_for_You "Have I Got a Deal for You")*, Nanomech briefly appeared in an accidental transformation chain before it finally ended when he switched into [Bloxx](/wiki/Bloxx "Bloxx").
* In *[The Frogs of War: Part 1](/wiki/The_Frogs_of_War:_Part_1 "The Frogs of War: Part 1")*, Nanomech defeated some [Incurseans](/wiki/Incursean "Incursean") by blowing up their guns, knocking them out.
* In *[The Ultimate Heist](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Heist "The Ultimate Heist")*, Nanomech escaped his [Plumber HQ](/wiki/Plumber_Headquarters "Plumber Headquarters") cell and avoided [Blukic](/wiki/Blukic "Blukic") and [Driba](/wiki/Driba "Driba") as much as possible.
* In *[It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 2](/wiki/It%27s_a_Mad,_Mad,_Mad_Ben_World:_Part_2 "It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 2")*, Nanomech defeated [Mad](/wiki/Mad_Ben "Mad Ben") Way Big.
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Nanomech appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

Trivia
------


* Nanomech's **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")** appearance was designed by Glenn Wong, with color styling by Chris Hooten.[[GW 1]](#cite_note-9)
* Nanomech is Ben's smallest alien, smaller than even Grey Matter.[[7]](#cite_note-TUH-10)
* Benchip was Nanomech's working name.[[DM 1]](#cite_note-11)
	+ Consequently, this makes Nanomech the fourth alien whose working name had Ben's name in it; the first three being [Blitzwolfer](/wiki/Blitzwolfer "Blitzwolfer"), [Snare-oh](/wiki/Snare-oh "Snare-oh"), and [Frankenstrike](/wiki/Frankenstrike "Frankenstrike").
* Due to him not really being a typical alien from any sort of planet, Nanomech is identified as an unknown bio-mechanical organism/creature by both [Inspector 13](/wiki/Inspector_13 "Inspector 13")'s technology[[4]](#cite_note-I13-5) and the [Plumbers](/wiki/Plumbers_(Classic) "Plumbers (Classic)")' scanners.[[7]](#cite_note-TUH-10)
* Nanomech's voice has changed in his appearances, mainly due to the perspective he is seen from:
	+ In ***Alien Swarm***, his voice was very deep due to being seen from anything of his size's perspective.
	+ In **Ultimate Alien**, his voice became squeaky and high-pitched due to being mostly seen from a human-sized perspective.
	+ In **Omniverse**, his voice is similar to his **Ultimate Alien** voice but with a more robotic tone.
* [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") finds it hard to take [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") as Nanomech seriously due to his squeaky voice.[[1]](#cite_note-UA-1) Because of his minuscule size, Kevin also thinks Nanomech is really lame.[[5]](#cite_note-VG-6)
* Throughout **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien"),** Nanomech was always transformed into via the quick change feature. He was not directly transformed into by Ben in his human form.
* Nanomech's exact nature is complicated, as it is implied in ***[Alien Swarm](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Swarm "Ben 10: Alien Swarm")*** and eventually confirmed by [Duncan Rouleau](/wiki/Duncan_Rouleau "Duncan Rouleau") that Nanomech is a Nanochip/Human-hybrid.[[DR 1]](#cite_note-duncan-12)
	+ However, [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") considered Nanomech to be more of an evolved form of Nanochip rather than a hybrid.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-13)[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-14)
	+ The bio card included with Bandai's Nanomech action figure refers to him as both a **Nanomechion** from "hive" planets and a unique creation of the Omnitrix, fusing the bio-mechanical DNA of the Nanochips with Ben.[[merch 1]](#cite_note-15)
	+ If Duncan's opinion were to be proven true, that would mean that Nanomech is the only known Omnitrix alien that is part-human and the only hybrid/fusion form the Omnitrix has access to without being broken or being upgraded into the [Biomnitrix](/wiki/Biomnitrix "Biomnitrix").
* During the Birthday Bash in *[FusionFall](/wiki/FusionFall "FusionFall")*, players could get a Nanomech helmet modeled after his live-action movie design. However, after **Ultimate Alien** premiered, it was changed to match the animated version. This item was re-released as a code item.
* Nanomech is one of four aliens to debut in a movie or special; the others being [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)"), [Eon](/wiki/Eon_(Transformation) "Eon (Transformation)"), and [Shocksquatch](/wiki/Shocksquatch "Shocksquatch").
* Nanomech is one of five aliens featured in **Omniverse** who generate green electricity; the others being [Upgrade](/wiki/Upgrade_(Classic) "Upgrade (Classic)"), [Buzzshock](/wiki/Buzzshock_(Classic) "Buzzshock (Classic)"), Frankenstrike, and [Brainstorm](/wiki/Brainstorm "Brainstorm").
