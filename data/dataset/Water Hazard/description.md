Appearance
----------


Water Hazard appears as a red, [mollusk](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mollusca "wikipedia:Mollusca")-like alien with green eyes, and a grey mouth that does not move when he speaks. Behind his legs and on the top of his arms are small spikes that are grey, there are black spots on his shoulders and legs, and on his hands, there are portholes, which he uses to shoot water. Water Hazard also has four fingers with claws on them and on each of his feet, there is a grey spike. His lower torso is grey, with two spikes on the side of it.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Water Hazard now has four slitted eyes, and also has eye-like patterns on his chest, with crab-like features on his stomach which appear to be crab legs forming a ribcage. He has much less black spots than normal. His mouth has a line running through it, his hood looks a lot rougher than before, is bigger, and more hexagonal than before, and he now has what appear to be barnacle-like portholes on his shoulders, arms, legs, and hands, from which he shoots the water. Rather than circular shoulders, he now has shoulders that end in shoulder pad-like armor above his arm, with much less black markings. He also has three spikes on the back of his legs and seems to have cracks on his arms and shoulders. The red sections near his face are now shorter and seemingly connect to his head with silver wire-like sections. His fingers are sharper and resemble dactyls, the "moveable finger" of a crab pincer. He is taller and thinner, as well as his legs becoming much thinner. His overall armor is a darker shade of red, seemingly maroon, and his grey parts are now more violet.


Water Hazard wears the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)")/[Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his chest.

Water Hazard can launch pressurized blasts of water[[note 1]](#cite_note-4) through the portholes in his hands. These blasts normally consist of hot water,[[3]](#cite_note-5) though he can change the temperature of his water powers from hot to cold. Using these same portholes, he can absorb moisture, including from the air.[[1]](#cite_note-HT-1)


Water Hazard is extremely durable, being able to withstand crashing into a wall seemingly painless.[[4]](#cite_note-BIM-6) He has an armored exoskeleton that protects his body from damage, including physical attacks, thereby making him completely invulnerable.[[5]](#cite_note-7)[[6]](#cite_note-8)


Water Hazard has enhanced strength, enough to throw [Buglizard](/wiki/Buglizard "Buglizard") into a nearby wall[[7]](#cite_note-TMTCP2-9) and punch craters into a concrete floor.[[8]](#cite_note-RT-10)


Similar to [XLR8](/wiki/XLR8_(Classic) "XLR8 (Classic)"), Water Hazard has a retractable visor hidden under his hood.[[9]](#cite_note-11) His hood is also retractable.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-12)


Water Hazard can produce sharp streams of water and use them as whips.[[10]](#cite_note-13)


Water Hazard can jump exceptionally high.[[8]](#cite_note-RT-10)


Water Hazard can withstand radiation, such as that generated by a [Prypiatosian-B](/wiki/Prypiatosian-B "Prypiatosian-B").[[11]](#cite_note-THtH-14)


Water Hazard can breathe underwater.[[DM 1]](#cite_note-15)


Water Hazard can manipulate different sources of water, including normal Earth water,[[ES 1]](#cite_note-16)[[DM 2]](#cite_note-17) into a variety of shapes.[[8]](#cite_note-RT-10)

History
-------


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Fame](/wiki/Fame "Fame")*, Water Hazard's DNA was sampled and put in the [Codon Stream](/wiki/Codon_Stream "Codon Stream") when the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") scanned [Bivalvan](/wiki/Bivalvan "Bivalvan")'s DNA.
* Water Hazard first appeared in *[Too Hot to Handle](/wiki/Too_Hot_to_Handle "Too Hot to Handle")*, where he pretended to be Bivalvan in a failed attempt to convince [P'andor](/wiki/P%27andor "P'andor") to go home. He was then defeated by P'andor, prompting Water Hazard to switch into [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill "Big Chill").
* In *[Hero Time](/wiki/Hero_Time "Hero Time")*, Water Hazard defeated [Overlord](/wiki/Carl_Nesmith "Carl Nesmith").
* In *[Revenge of the Swarm](/wiki/Revenge_of_the_Swarm "Revenge of the Swarm")*, Water Hazard briefly battled a [Victor Validus](/wiki/Victor_Validus "Victor Validus") clone until it escaped.
* In *[Double or Nothing](/wiki/Double_or_Nothing "Double or Nothing")*, Water Hazard defeated [Swamps](/wiki/Swamps "Swamps"), but was defeated and frozen by [Fridge](/wiki/Fridge "Fridge"), so he switched into [NRG](/wiki/NRG "NRG").
* In *[Night of the Living Nightmare](/wiki/Night_of_the_Living_Nightmare "Night of the Living Nightmare")*, Water Hazard was seen confronting [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") in a dream.


### Omniverse


* In *[The More Things Change: Part 2](/wiki/The_More_Things_Change:_Part_2 "The More Things Change: Part 2")*, Water Hazard battled [Buglizard](/wiki/Buglizard "Buglizard") with [Rook](/wiki/Rook_Blonko "Rook Blonko")'s help.
* In *[Hot Stretch](/wiki/Hot_Stretch "Hot Stretch")*, Water Hazard cooled off [Ester](/wiki/Ester "Ester") and [her friends](/wiki/Kineceleran_Kids "Kineceleran Kids").
* In *[Tummy Trouble](/wiki/Tummy_Trouble "Tummy Trouble")*, Water Hazard sprayed [Attea](/wiki/Attea "Attea") off the [Proto-TRUK](/wiki/Proto-TRUK "Proto-TRUK").
* In *[The Frogs of War: Part 1](/wiki/The_Frogs_of_War:_Part_1 "The Frogs of War: Part 1")*, Water Hazard "accidentally" incapacitated [Will Harangue](/wiki/Will_Harangue "Will Harangue"), put out a fire accidentally caused by [NRG](/wiki/NRG "NRG"), and fought some [Incurseans](/wiki/Incursean_(Classic) "Incursean (Classic)") before the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)")'s randomizer function switched him into [Astrodactyl](/wiki/Astrodactyl "Astrodactyl").
* In *[Return to Forever](/wiki/Return_to_Forever "Return to Forever")*, Water Hazard tried to break out of a force field, but was reverted by the [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights")' DNA scanner.
* In *[The Ultimate Heist](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Heist "The Ultimate Heist")*, Water Hazard failed to stop [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") [Goop](/wiki/Goop_(Classic) "Goop (Classic)") from getting the [Polymorphic Crystal](/wiki/Polymorphic_Crystal "Polymorphic Crystal").
* In *[For a Few Brains More](/wiki/For_a_Few_Brains_More "For a Few Brains More")*, Water Hazard defeated [Ultimate Panuncian](/wiki/Ultimate_Panuncian "Ultimate Panuncian").
* In *[Bengeance is Mine](/wiki/Bengeance_is_Mine "Bengeance is Mine")*, Water Hazard fought [Sir Morton](/wiki/Sir_Morton "Sir Morton") and the Forever Knights until [Pakmar](/wiki/Pakmar "Pakmar")'s shop got flooded.
* In *[Rook Tales](/wiki/Rook_Tales "Rook Tales")*, Water Hazard was defeated by [Kundo](/wiki/Kundo "Kundo").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Water Hazard appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

### [Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction](/wiki/Ben_10_Ultimate_Alien:_Cosmic_Destruction "Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction")


Water Hazard is one of the [Andromeda Galaxy](/wiki/Andromeda_Galaxy_(Classic) "Andromeda Galaxy (Classic)") aliens playable in the game.


Water Hazard can generate a water tornado and briefly levitate while launching water blasts. Also, he wields a water-whip that can destroy enemies.


Water Hazard is vital for progression on the Eiffel Tower and The Final Battle levels of the game.

Etymology
---------


A water hazard is an area of water on a golf course designed to make it more difficult for players to get their ball onto the green.



Trivia
------


* Each of [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s Andromeda alien forms control a basic element. As his name suggests, Water Hazard commands water.
* Water Hazard is the first Andromeda alien Ben has scanned and used.
	+ [His species](/wiki/Orishan "Orishan") is also the first to be scanned by the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)").
* Water Hazard's voice has changed a few times over the course of his appearances:
	+ When Water Hazard first appeared in *[Too Hot to Handle](/wiki/Too_Hot_to_Handle "Too Hot to Handle")*, his voice sounded exactly like [Bivalvan](/wiki/Bivalvan "Bivalvan")'s did in *[Escape from Aggregor](/wiki/Escape_from_Aggregor "Escape from Aggregor")* and *[Ultimate Aggregor](/wiki/Ultimate_Aggregor_(Episode) "Ultimate Aggregor (Episode)")* because Water Hazard was supposed to be a clone of Bivalvan.[[DM 3]](#cite_note-voice-22)
	+ Starting in *[Hero Time](/wiki/Hero_Time "Hero Time")*, his voice became deeper to help differentiate him from Bivalvan.[[DM 3]](#cite_note-voice-22)
	+ In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, his voice is slightly modified to sound like that of an underwater [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)").
* Water Hazard was designed by Steven Choi.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-23)
* Before Water Hazard shoots water in **Omniverse**, the sound of a gun cocking can be heard.
* Water Hazard was the [Alien of the Month](/wiki/Alien_of_the_Month "Alien of the Month") in January 2011.
* In the **[Family Guy](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Family_Guy "wikipedia:Family Guy")** episode *Quagmire and Meg*, during the scene where the old man is reading to his child, you can see two toys that resemble Water Hazard and [Ultimate Swampfire](/wiki/Ultimate_Swampfire "Ultimate Swampfire").
* In the online games, *[Game Creator](/wiki/Game_Creator "Game Creator")* and *[Omniverse Collection](/wiki/Omniverse_Collection "Omniverse Collection")*, Water Hazard shoots blobs of water in an upward tilted angle instead of just spraying it. He is also able to create a bubble shield to protect himself from lasers.
* Orishan have been confirmed to be separate entities from the [Cascans](/wiki/Cascan "Cascan"), another red hydrokinetic species from the Andromeda Galaxy that was introduced in the [reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)"). [Duncan Rouleau](/wiki/Duncan_Rouleau "Duncan Rouleau") likens the differences between these species to those between [panthers](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Panther "wikipedia:Panther") and [jaguars](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jaguar "wikipedia:Jaguar").[[DR 1]](#cite_note-24)
	+ Duncan is a fan of both Water Hazard and [Overflow](/wiki/Overflow "Overflow") for different reasons, yet prefers the latter for his simplicity.[[DR 2]](#cite_note-25)
	+ Unlike Cascans,[[DR 3]](#cite_note-26) Water Hazard cannot turn water into either ice or steam.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-27)
