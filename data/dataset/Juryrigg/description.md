Appearance
----------


Juryrigg is a small [Gremlin](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gremlin "wikipedia:Gremlin")-like alien with four-fingered hands, three-tooed feet, and a devil-like tail. He is small in stature, only about as big as a [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)") head.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Juryrigg had reddish-orange skin and wore the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Juryrigg's skin is much smoother and recolored crimson-red. He now wears an aviator's outfit which comes with an ascot. His eyes now have black pupils, his ears are now spiked, his sharp teeth jut out of his mouth, and his nose is bigger. He also has a spike on his chin and one near each of his eyes. The [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol is now on his waist.



Personality
-----------


Juryrigg has a strong desire to break and remake machines, making him difficult to control.


Juryrigg has a mischievous (and twisted) sense of humor, as seen when he turned [Will Harangue](/wiki/Will_Harangue "Will Harangue") into an alien as punishment for all the smear campaigns and crimes he committed against [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") and other aliens.[[1]](#cite_note-RtF-2)


Juryrigg has several catchphrases, such as "Juryrigg disassemble!", "FIX FIX FIX FIX FIX!", and "Reassemble, disassemble!"

1/8
Juryrigg has the ability to completely disassemble any kind of machinery, including [Rex Salazar](/wiki/Rex_Salazar "Rex Salazar")'s builds,[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-3) in mere seconds, fitting to his Gremlin appearance.


Despite it being harder for him to do due to his craving to destroy things, Juryrigg can construct complex machinery from scratch at a similar rate.


Juryrigg has enhanced strength for his size, being able to break both a metal car brake in half[[2]](#cite_note-TEC-4) and some metal bars that even an [Appoplexian](/wiki/Appoplexian_(Classic) "Appoplexian (Classic)") could not break.[[3]](#cite_note-5)


Juryrigg has a degree of enhanced durability, as he was able to withstand being thrown against a destroyed food truck by a [stone creature](/wiki/Stone_Creatures "Stone Creatures").[[4]](#cite_note-MI-6)


As in accordance with his break it/fix it attributes, Juryrigg boasts an impressive intellect,[[MW 1]](#cite_note-7) describing himself as "smart and stubborn".[[1]](#cite_note-RtF-2)


Juryrigg can dismantle and rebuild living mechanical species such as [Chronosapiens](/wiki/Chronosapien "Chronosapien").[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-robots-8)


Juryrigg's tail is prehensile.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-9)



Weaknesses
----------


* ![]()Small Size
* ![]()Uncontrollable Habit
1/2
Juryrigg's small size makes him unsuitable for hand-to-hand combat against larger opponents,[[4]](#cite_note-MI-6) unless they happen to be machines, in which case, he can dismantle them in seconds.[[5]](#cite_note-GF-10)


As mentioned above, Juryrigg has a near uncontrollable habit of breaking and assembling machinery.[[2]](#cite_note-TEC-4)


Like his name, most of the devices Juryrigg creates are basically on-the-fly or 'jury-rigged'. They are not normally meant to last and will eventually fail. Juryrigg's creations can also be unstable and hard to control, much like Juryrigg himself. The only exception to this is the [Tenn-Speed](/wiki/Tenn-Speed "Tenn-Speed").[[6]](#cite_note-11)



History
-------


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, Juryrigg was unlocked by [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Ultimate_Alien) "Ben 10,000 (Ultimate Alien)").
* Juryrigg first appeared in *[The Eggman Cometh](/wiki/The_Eggman_Cometh "The Eggman Cometh")*, where he began taking apart the brakes in [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)")'s [car](/wiki/Kevin%27s_Car_(Original) "Kevin's Car (Original)"). Later, Juryrigg dismantled [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)")'s mutant ray, but soon had to repair and modify it in order to stop the [mutant chickens](/wiki/Mutant_Chickens "Mutant Chickens") from wreaking havoc on [Bellwood](/wiki/Bellwood "Bellwood").
* In *[The Ultimate Enemy: Part 1](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Enemy:_Part_1 "The Ultimate Enemy: Part 1")*, Juryrigg prepared to battle [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") and [Psyphon](/wiki/Psyphon "Psyphon") before [Dagon](/wiki/Dagon "Dagon") freed himself.
* In *[The Ultimate Enemy: Part 2](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Enemy:_Part_2 "The Ultimate Enemy: Part 2")*, Juryrigg briefly appeared before reverting.


### Omniverse


* In *[Have I Got a Deal for You](/wiki/Have_I_Got_a_Deal_for_You "Have I Got a Deal for You")*, Juryrigg briefly appeared before switching into [Grey Matter](/wiki/Grey_Matter_(Classic) "Grey Matter (Classic)") as part of an accidental transformation chain.
* In *[Gone Fishin'](/wiki/Gone_Fishin%27 "Gone Fishin'")*, Juryrigg defeated the [Mechaneers](/wiki/Mechaneer "Mechaneer").
* In *[Rules of Engagement](/wiki/Rules_of_Engagement "Rules of Engagement")*, Juryrigg fixed up parts of the [Proto-TRUK](/wiki/Proto-TRUK "Proto-TRUK") and created the [Tenn-Speed](/wiki/Tenn-Speed "Tenn-Speed").
* In *[Food Around the Corner](/wiki/Food_Around_the_Corner "Food Around the Corner")*, Juryrigg tore through a door and fixed the security system.
* In *[Return to Forever](/wiki/Return_to_Forever "Return to Forever")*, Juryrigg disabled the [Highbreed Pulse Generator](/wiki/Highbreed_Pulse_Generator "Highbreed Pulse Generator") before it could destroy every alien on [Earth](/wiki/Earth_(Classic) "Earth (Classic)"), and reprogrammed the machine to instead turn [Will Harangue](/wiki/Will_Harangue "Will Harangue") into an alien.
* In *[Mystery, Incorporeal](/wiki/Mystery,_Incorporeal "Mystery, Incorporeal")*, Juryrigg was defeated by a [stone creature](/wiki/Stone_Creatures "Stone Creatures").
* In *[It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 1](/wiki/It%27s_a_Mad,_Mad,_Mad_Ben_World:_Part_1 "It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 1")*, Juryrigg was defeated by [Mad](/wiki/Mad_Ben "Mad Ben") [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Juryrigg appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

Etymology
---------


Juryrigg's name comes from the term "jury rig", which means to makeshift repairs or temporary contrivances, made with only the tools and materials that happen to be on hand.



Trivia
------


* From Arc 4 of **Omniverse** onwards, Juryrigg replaces [Echo Echo](/wiki/Echo_Echo "Echo Echo") in the opening intro of the show. He also replaces 11-year-old [Perk Upchuck](/wiki/Upchuck "Upchuck") in the opening of **Omniverse**'s Arc 5.
* Juryrigg, [Grey Matter](/wiki/Grey_Matter_(Classic) "Grey Matter (Classic)"), and [Feedback](/wiki/Feedback "Feedback") are the only aliens with their own theme song.
* [Friedkin University](/wiki/Friedkin_University "Friedkin University")'s mascot bears a resemblance to Juryrigg.[[4]](#cite_note-MI-6)
* Much like the rest of his species, Juryrigg is a great mechanic and builder.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-12)
* Besides the species listed above, Juryrigg can dismantle and rebuild [Cybertronians](https://transformers.fandom.com/wiki/Cybertronian "w:c:transformers:Cybertronian").[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-robots-8)
