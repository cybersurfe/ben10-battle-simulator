Appearance
----------


### Ben as Spitter


Spitter is a bulbous alien resembling a pufferfish with legs that is colored green on its outer side and pale green on its inner side. He has three spikes sticking out the top and bottom of his head, arms with four-fingered hands, short and stubby legs, and a long tail. His mouth covers mostly the front of him, which is filled with pointed teeth, and his eyes are connected to the corners of his lips. He wears a pair of black pants that partly cover his stomach.


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Spitter had yellowish-green eyes, dull-colored teeth, and his gums were the same hue of green as the outer side of his skin.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Spitter looks exactly the same as before, except his eyes are recolored light green, his teeth are recolored white, and his gums are recolored dark green.


Spitter wears the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his stomach.



### Ken as Spitter


[Ken](/wiki/Ken_Tennyson "Ken Tennyson")'s Spitter looked the same as [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)")'s Spitter in the original series.

--------------------

1/5
Spitter can inflate into a ball-like shape and spit out a concentrated blast of very slippery slime.


Spitter has enhanced strength, as seen when he held and pushed [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Future) "Aloysius Animo (Future)") away with no difficulty.[[1]](#cite_note-B10K-1)


Spitter's slime can be highly conductive, enough for a [Nosedeenian](/wiki/Nosedeenian "Nosedeenian") to electrify puddles of the slime to attack enemies.[[2]](#cite_note-3)



Weaknesses
----------


Spitter can accidentally slip on his own slime.


Spitter can be unintentionally shocked due to his slime being highly conductive.



History
-------


### Future (Original)


* Spitter first appeared in *[Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Episode) "Ben 10,000 (Episode)")*, where he battled [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Future) "Aloysius Animo (Future)"). Later, he fought [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Future) "Vilgax (Future)") before switching into [Arctiguana](/wiki/Arctiguana "Arctiguana").
* In *[Ken 10](/wiki/Ken_10 "Ken 10")*, Ben 10,000 as Spitter battled [Mot Snikrep](/wiki/Mot_Snikrep "Mot Snikrep") before switching into [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)"). Later, Ken as Spitter blasted [Kevin 11,000](/wiki/Kevin_11,000 "Kevin 11,000") with slime before switching into [Buzzshock](/wiki/Buzzshock_(Classic) "Buzzshock (Classic)").


### Comics


* In *[The Once and Future Ben](/wiki/The_Once_and_Future_Ben "The Once and Future Ben")*, Spitter tried to stop [Vilgax 3000](/wiki/Vilgax_3000 "Vilgax 3000"), but failed, due to him traveling back in time.

Trivia
------


* According to [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt"), Spitter was unlocked off-screen after ***[Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens](/wiki/Ben_10:_Destroy_All_Aliens "Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens")*** and is available to [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") in present day.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-4)
	+ However, according to [Matt Wayne](/wiki/Matt_Wayne "Matt Wayne"), Ben had not unlocked Spitter as of *[The Frogs of War: Part 2](/wiki/The_Frogs_of_War:_Part_2 "The Frogs of War: Part 2")*.[[MW 1]](#cite_note-5) It is unknown if he was unlocked afterwards.
* Spitter is the only known alien seen used by [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)") that present-day Ben has never used on-screen. He is also the only regular alien featured in the original series that did not appear in **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**.
	+ His absence from **Omniverse** was because the crew ran out of time. They tried to include as many aliens as they could during production. However, Spitter never came up during any episode discussions.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-6)[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-7)
* Had Spitter appeared in **Omniverse**, he probably would have looked pretty similar to his **Ultimate Alien** design but with a new costume with more green on it.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-8)[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-9) He would have also been voiced by [Fred Tatasciore](/wiki/Fred_Tatasciore "Fred Tatasciore").[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-10)
	+ In 2020, **Omniverse** art director Derrick J. Wyatt designed a version of Spitter as a third-party commission. It can be found in the [gallery](/wiki/Spitter/Gallery "Spitter/Gallery").
* Spitter is one of the only two regular aliens in **Ultimate Alien** to not have the Ultimatrix symbol on their chests and have it on their stomachs instead; the only other one being [Murk Upchuck](/wiki/Upchuck "Upchuck").
* Spitter came to be from re-purposing one of Perk Upchuck's concept designs, similar to [Arctiguana](/wiki/Arctiguana "Arctiguana"), albeit the latter's coloring was changed during re-purposing.
* Not counting [Wildmutt](/wiki/Wildmutt "Wildmutt"), Spitter is the only regular alien to have never spoken onscreen.
* There were no plans to feature Spitter in the [reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)").[[DR 1]](#cite_note-11)
