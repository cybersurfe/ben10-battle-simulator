Appearance
----------


### Ben as Grey Matter


Grey Matter is a grey-skinned, frog-like, bipedal alien that is only four inches tall with large green eyes, which have rectangular pupils and horizontal eyelids.


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Grey Matter wore a white jumpsuit with a thin vertical black stripe down the front, his eyes were yellowish-green, his forehead had a larger version of the jumpsuit's stripe, and the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol was on his back.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Grey Matter looked exactly the same as before, except his skin was slightly lighter, his eyes were green and he wore the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Grey Matter now has a green jumpsuit with a black belt. His suit collar is now black, and the stripe on his head and jumpsuit are no longer present. He wears black, fingerless gloves with a green rectangle on the back. His neck is slightly longer, and his [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol has returned to his back.


11-year-old Grey Matter in **Omniverse** looks exactly the same as he did in the original series except his collar is black, his eyes are green, and his skin tone is slightly different.



### Gwen as Grey Matter


[Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Gwen_10) "Gwen Tennyson (Gwen 10)") as Grey Matter had Gwen's hair and more oval-shaped eyes, with much smaller pupils. She wore a light blue jumpsuit with dark blue sleeves, and the Omnitrix symbol was on her back. She lacked the stripes [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s Grey Matter has.

Grey Matter's small size belies his great calculative and technical intellect. He can create complicated machines from spare parts, understand the function of any device at a glance, and make sophisticated repairs to complex machines (such as a warp-drive engine).[[2]](#cite_note-Rad-2)


Grey Matter's intellect serves as a problem solver and analytical help in difficult situations. This allows Grey Matter to think more strategically when brute force is inefficient. In fact, Grey Matter is smart enough to know how to unlock the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")'s [Master Control](/wiki/Master_Control "Master Control"), something only Ken has demonstrated.[[3]](#cite_note-3)


Grey Matter's brain capacity makes Ben smarter about things he has never even seen before, like [Kraab](/wiki/Kraab_(Classic) "Kraab (Classic)")'s alien circuitry.[[pop-up 1]](#cite_note-4)[[4]](#cite_note-5)


Grey Matter can sometimes manipulate living things by stimulating certain parts of their nervous system if such beings can be controlled by external stimuli.[[5]](#cite_note-6)


Grey Matter's size allows him to squeeze into small spaces and can climb walls due to microscopic pads on his skin.[[pop-up 2]](#cite_note-7)[[6]](#cite_note-8) He also has sharp teeth that can cause pain to those he bites, as well as slimy skin that makes him difficult to grab.


Grey Matter was the weakest alien in the original series due to his minuscule size, but he compensates with his intellect and efficient technological and calculative skills.


Grey Matter's intelligence is not limited to technology and biology, as he is smart in general. Even if he does not know something, he can learn it very quickly.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-9)


Grey Matter appears to be stronger than he looks, as he was strong enough to move a cleaner's bucket with mops in it[[7]](#cite_note-Ultimate_Weapon-10) and break a coolant pipe to freeze the [Vreedle Brothers](/wiki/Vreedle_Brothers "Vreedle Brothers").[[8]](#cite_note-11)


Grey Matter can breathe underwater[[9]](#cite_note-12) due to having gills, making him amphibious.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-13)


Grey Matter has a long prehensile tongue, owing to his insectivore nature, as demonstrated with [Blukic](/wiki/Blukic "Blukic").[[10]](#cite_note-14) As a [Galvan](/wiki/Galvan_(Classic) "Galvan (Classic)"), he also possesses a 'pre-brain' which deals with all basic bodily functions.[[11]](#cite_note-15)[[12]](#cite_note-16)


Grey Matter was able to understand [Pyxi](/wiki/Pyxi "Pyxi")'s speech.[[2]](#cite_note-Rad-2)

1/4
Grey Matter's small size makes him unsuitable for physical battle, so he must outsmart his enemies in a fight. Animals like dogs and cats can be a problem, as they may see Grey Matter as a chew toy or food to them.[[7]](#cite_note-Ultimate_Weapon-10)


Despite possessing immense intelligence, Grey Matter can still make miscalculations, as his attempt to upgrade the [Rust Bucket](/wiki/Rust_Bucket "Rust Bucket")'s air conditioner caused it to overheat instead, incapacitating the vehicle.[[13]](#cite_note-17)


According to himself, Grey Matter sometimes does not know what he is talking about.[[14]](#cite_note-18)


Grey Matter does not know everything, as seen when he (initially) did not know which galaxy his species is from.[[15]](#cite_note-ASP-19)


Being a Galvan, Grey Matter would be vulnerable to being trapped by a member of [Omnivoracious](/wiki/Omnivoracious "Omnivoracious")'s [species](/wiki/Omnivoracious_(Species) "Omnivoracious (Species)") if they were not extinct.[[16]](#cite_note-20)



History
-------


### Ben 10


* Grey Matter first appeared in *[Washington B.C.](/wiki/Washington_B.C. "Washington B.C.")*, where he searched through a whole aisle of cereal boxes for a Golden [Sumo Slammers](/wiki/Sumo_Slammers_(Classic) "Sumo Slammers (Classic)") card.
* In *[Hunted](/wiki/Hunted "Hunted")*, Grey Matter defeated [Kraab](/wiki/Kraab_(Classic) "Kraab (Classic)") and [SixSix](/wiki/SixSix_(Classic) "SixSix (Classic)").
* In *[A Small Problem](/wiki/A_Small_Problem "A Small Problem")*, the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") malfunctioned, trapping Ben as Grey Matter for most of the episode.
* In *[Secrets](/wiki/Secrets "Secrets")*, Grey Matter defeated one of [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)")'s [drones](/wiki/Drones "Drones") before the malfunctioning Omnitrix turned him into [Upgrade](/wiki/Upgrade_(Classic) "Upgrade (Classic)").
	+ Also, Grey Matter appeared as one of the many alien templates used for Vilgax's army in an imaginary sequence.
* In *[Truth](/wiki/Truth "Truth")*, Grey Matter defeated a [Null Guardian](/wiki/Null_Guardians "Null Guardians") and sent [Phil](/wiki/Phil_Billings_(Classic) "Phil Billings (Classic)") to the [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)").
* In *[Grudge Match](/wiki/Grudge_Match "Grudge Match")*, Grey Matter disabled the alien prisoners' shock implants, looked for the escape pod controls and defeated some of [Slix Vigma](/wiki/Slix_Vigma "Slix Vigma")'s [drones](/wiki/Slix_Vigma%27s_Bodyguards "Slix Vigma's Bodyguards") with [Kevin 11](/wiki/Kevin_11 "Kevin 11").
* In *[Ghostfreaked Out](/wiki/Ghostfreaked_Out "Ghostfreaked Out")*, Grey Matter fought the [Circus Freak Trio](/wiki/Circus_Freak_Trio_(Classic) "Circus Freak Trio (Classic)") and hid from [Zs'Skayr](/wiki/Zs%27Skayr "Zs'Skayr").
* In *[Ultimate Weapon](/wiki/Ultimate_Weapon "Ultimate Weapon")*, Grey Matter snuck into a building and helped [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") and [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") to get in. He then opened a safe to retrieve the [Mask of Ah Puch](/wiki/Mask_of_Ah_Puch "Mask of Ah Puch") and escaped from [Enoch](/wiki/Enoch "Enoch") and the [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights").
* In *[They Lurk Below](/wiki/They_Lurk_Below "They Lurk Below")*, Grey Matter saved a drowning man from some sharks.
* In *[Back with a Vengeance](/wiki/Back_with_a_Vengeance "Back with a Vengeance")*, Grey Matter appeared about eight times. Firstly, he appeared when Ben unlocked the [Master Control](/wiki/Master_Control "Master Control"). Then, he appeared in a photo. Later, Grey Matter escaped from Vilgax's grip while he and [Kevin 11](/wiki/Kevin_11 "Kevin 11") where arguing. In the [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)"), Grey Matter was used several times to evade Vilgax and Kevin. At the end of the episode, Ben accidentally transformed into Grey Matter, having just remembered that the Master Control was once again locked.
* In *[Midnight Madness](/wiki/Midnight_Madness "Midnight Madness")*, Grey Matter was hypnotized into being Gwen's slave.
* In *[Merry Christmas](/wiki/Merry_Christmas "Merry Christmas")*, Grey Matter accidentally destroyed the Rust Bucket's air conditioner and later (intentionally) destroyed the [Jinglelator](/wiki/Jinglelator "Jinglelator").
* In *[The Unnaturals](/wiki/The_Unnaturals "The Unnaturals")*, Grey Matter stopped some thieves from stealing the [Liberty Bell](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Liberty_Bell "wikipedia:Liberty Bell") and won an MP3 player in a contest.
* In *[Be Afraid of the Dark](/wiki/Be_Afraid_of_the_Dark "Be Afraid of the Dark")*, Grey Matter evaded Zs'Skayr and [Viktor](/wiki/Viktor "Viktor"), explained the former's plan to Max and Gwen, and built a [Sun Gun](/wiki/Sun_Gun "Sun Gun").
* In ***[Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix](/wiki/Ben_10:_Secret_of_the_Omnitrix "Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix")***, Grey Matter defeated [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)").
* In *[Perfect Day](/wiki/Perfect_Day "Perfect Day")*, Grey Matter defeated a mutated [Cash](/wiki/Cash_Murray_(Classic) "Cash Murray (Classic)") and [J.T.](/wiki/J.T._(Classic) "J.T. (Classic)") in a dream.
* In ***[Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens](/wiki/Ben_10:_Destroy_All_Aliens "Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens")***, Grey Matter escaped [Retaliator](/wiki/Azmuth%27s_Father "Azmuth's Father") and ran back to [his house](/wiki/Ben%27s_House "Ben's House"), where he was attacked by [Carl](/wiki/Carl_Tennyson "Carl Tennyson") and [Sandra](/wiki/Sandra_Tennyson "Sandra Tennyson") before the Omnitrix switched him into [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)").
* In *[Snack Break](/wiki/Snack_Break "Snack Break")*, Grey Matter attempted to retrieve his snack in a vending machine, but reverted before he could grab it and, as a result, got stuck in the machine.

* In *[Of Predators and Prey: Part 2](/wiki/Of_Predators_and_Prey:_Part_2 "Of Predators and Prey: Part 2")*, Grey Matter defeated [Tyrannopede](/wiki/Tyrannopede "Tyrannopede").

Etymology
---------


Grey Matter is named after [grey matter](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grey_matter "wikipedia:Grey matter"), one of the components of the central nervous system.



Trivia
------


* Grey Matter is Ben's smartest alien.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-22)[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-derrickfav-23)
* Grey Matter was [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt")'s favorite Omnitrix alien.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-derrickfav-23)
* Grey Matter is the second smallest alien in Ben's arsenal; the overall smallest being [Nanomech](/wiki/Nanomech "Nanomech").[[17]](#cite_note-24)
	+ Grey Matter is stated to be about 6 inches (15.24 cm) tall.[[15]](#cite_note-ASP-19)
* Grey Matter's DNA was the first DNA sample added to the Omnitrix.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-25) However, it came from neither [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)") nor [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo").[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-26)
* Grey Matter's voice has changed over the course of his appearances in the Classic Timeline:
	+ In the original series, he spoke with a high-pitched voice.
	+ In *[Gwen 10](/wiki/Gwen_10 "Gwen 10")*, [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Gwen_10) "Gwen Tennyson (Gwen 10)")'s Grey Matter had a feminine touch to her voice.
	+ In **Omniverse**, his voice is a lot deeper.
* 11-year-old Grey Matter is replaced with 11-year-old [Ripjaws](/wiki/Ripjaws_(Classic) "Ripjaws (Classic)") in the opening intro of **Omniverse**'s Arc 5, specifically in the part where the former originally appeared alongside the 11-year-old versions of [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)"), [Eye Guy](/wiki/Eye_Guy "Eye Guy"), and [Snare-oh](/wiki/Snare-oh "Snare-oh").
	+ 11-year-old Grey Matter was not necessarily planned to appear in **Omniverse** flashbacks.[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-27)
* A running gag in the original series was that when Ben sometimes selects a big and powerful alien, he gets Grey Matter instead.
* When Grey Matter gets surprised, his rectangular pupils turn into very large squares.
* Grey Matter, [Juryrigg](/wiki/Juryrigg "Juryrigg"), and [Feedback](/wiki/Feedback "Feedback") are the only aliens with their own theme song.
* Grey Matter is the first alien to stay in a whole episode due to an [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") malfunction; the second being [Rath](/wiki/Rath_(Classic) "Rath (Classic)").
* Grey Matter was first envisioned by [Steve E. Gordon](/wiki/Ben_10_(Pre-Production)#Greymatter "Ben 10 (Pre-Production)") as a stereotypical [Area 51](/wiki/Area_51 "Area 51") [grey alien](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grey_alien "wikipedia:Grey alien"), with a huge head and giant pupil-less eyes, wearing a black full-body latex suit that covers everything up to his chest. [Dave Johnson](/wiki/Dave_Johnson "Dave Johnson") initially designed him similar to that design, but since the concept was deemed to be scary for kids, Dave decided to go with a frog-like design instead.
	+ Grey Matter's **Omniverse** design was made by Derrick.[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-28)
