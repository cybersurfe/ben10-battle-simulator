Appearance
----------


Snare-oh is a [mummy](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mummy "wikipedia:Mummy")-like alien whose bandages are made from an alien material that feels like old cloth, but do have elasticity.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-2)



### Ben as Snare-oh


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Snare-oh's eyes and the gaps between his bandages glowed green. He wore a black and gold pharaoh's headdress, black bracers and greaves, and the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol on his left shoulder.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Snare-oh was slightly taller and the green lines on his body were recolored gold, additionally his chest, arms, shoulders and legs are thicker, giving him a more muscular appearance. He wore the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Snare-oh has greyish-brown and more ragged bandages, with more layers being visible and the tendrils on his back being much shorter. His headdress is black with two glowing green gaps on either side of it and a gold rimming at the bottom. His green eyes are surrounded by crown-like golden spikes and his lower face is wrapped in bandages. His chin is black with more green gaps and a golden beard-like spine jutting from it. His upper chest and shoulders are covered by a large collar/wrap-like garment with green and black stripes. He also has black-and-gold pads on each shoulder, with each one having a green "eye" on them. The gaps on his chest are gone and his heels each have an extra "toe". The ties on his [Shendyt](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shendyt "wikipedia:Shendyt") are replaced by a single bandage. His braces and greaves now have gold trimmings and two larger green gaps on them. He wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") on a green and white belt around his waist. Both the 11 and 16-year old versions share this design.



### Ben 10,000 as Snare-oh


[Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)")'s Snare-oh is recolored to look like the [Mummy](/wiki/Mummy "Mummy"), though his eyes and the gaps between his bandages glow green and, unlike the Mummy, he has no chin beard.



### Benzarro as Snare-oh


[Benzarro](/wiki/Benzarro "Benzarro")'s Snare-oh looks identical to [Ben Prime](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s Snare-oh in **Omniverse**, except his eyes, bandage gaps, and Omnitrix symbol are recolored lavender, the bandages are recolored white, the golden parts are recolored silver, and the stripe on his belt is recolored black.

Being composed of bandages, Snare-oh can reshape his body at will. This allows him to split himself apart to dodge attacks and reform to counter-attack. He can also generate more bandages for various needs. Additionally, his body is completely hollow, making him extremely flexible.


Snare-oh can regenerate himself and recover from many types of injuries, including being torn into confetti by a large fan, almost instantly.[[2]](#cite_note-UW-3)


Snare-oh can extend his bandages, from his fingers or from his body, creating a sturdy wall.[[3]](#cite_note-RtF-4) He can also sever the bandages he extends.


Although Snare-oh is frail, he has some degree of enhanced strength, allowing him to lift and throw objects and species like [Segmentasapiens](/wiki/Segmentasapien "Segmentasapien")[[4]](#cite_note-ATTWN-5) with his stretchable limbs, and can punch away a [Squid Monster](/wiki/Squid_Monsters "Squid Monsters") with ease.[[5]](#cite_note-BIM-6)


Snare-oh's bandages are strong enough to restrain even [Fourmungousaur](/wiki/Fourmungousaur "Fourmungousaur"), who has the combined strength of both [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)") and [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)"), with his bandages.[[4]](#cite_note-ATTWN-5)


Snare-oh is rather durable, as he was able to withstand being thrown around by the [Werewolf](/wiki/Werewolf "Werewolf").[[1]](#cite_note-TR-1) His bandages can even withstand lasers, as demonstrated by [Kuphulu](/wiki/Kuphulu "Kuphulu").[[6]](#cite_note-RMP-7)[[7]](#cite_note-TVSB-8)


Despite being made of bandages, Snare-oh is immune to the effects of water. As such, he can breathe underwater.[[8]](#cite_note-SoDS-9)


Snare-oh has a surprising amount of speed and agility for his size and bandages, even enough to scale walls and dodge attacks with relative ease.


By reducing himself down to single bandages, Snare-oh can slip through extremely tight spaces or small openings. This allows him to escape various traps or maneuver through cramped areas.[[5]](#cite_note-BIM-6)


Snare-oh can break his body apart to create a pile of bandages to cushion a landing. His face is separated from the rest of the bandages in the process.[[8]](#cite_note-SoDS-9)


Like all Anur System aliens, Snare-oh is immune to the effects of [Corrodium](/wiki/Corrodium "Corrodium") and can see in the dark,[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-10) the former of which has been demonstrated by the [Mummy](/wiki/Mummy "Mummy").[[2]](#cite_note-UW-3)[[9]](#cite_note-BAotD-11)


Snare-oh can block out Corrodium radiation with his bandages, allowing him to carry the dangerous crystal inside his body without irradiating anything nearby, as demonstrated by the Mummy.[[2]](#cite_note-UW-3)[[9]](#cite_note-BAotD-11)


Snare-oh can survive in the vacuum of space thanks to his composition, as demonstrated by the Mummy.[[9]](#cite_note-BAotD-11)


Snare-oh can briefly levitate through the air, as demonstrated by the Mummy.[[2]](#cite_note-UW-3)


Although he has never trained himself to do so, Snare-oh can perform [magic](/wiki/Magic_(Classic) "Magic (Classic)") similar to Egyptian curses.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-12) However, Ben cannot stay transformed as Snare-oh long enough to learn it without master control.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-13)

Etymology
---------


His placeholder name, Benmummy, is a combination of the words "Ben" and "mummy".


Snare-oh's name comes from "snare", meaning to tie up, which alludes to his ability to do so, and the "-oh" is derived from "[pharaoh](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pharaoh "wikipedia:Pharaoh")", a word for an [Egyptian](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Egypt "wikipedia:Egypt") king, referencing his appearance.



Trivia
------


* While [Tom Perkins](/wiki/Tom_Perkins "Tom Perkins") designed Snare-oh for the original series, Chap Yaep designed Snare-oh's **Omniverse** appearance based on [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt")'s roughs.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-18)
* Snare-oh's placeholder name "Benmummy" was assigned to him by the credits for *[The Return](/wiki/The_Return "The Return")* and toys during the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"). Therefore, Snare-oh is one of four aliens whose working name had Ben's name in it, the other three being [Blitzwolfer](/wiki/Blitzwolfer "Blitzwolfer"), [Frankenstrike](/wiki/Frankenstrike "Frankenstrike"), and [Nanomech](/wiki/Nanomech "Nanomech").
	+ In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, the name "Benmummy" was replaced with Snare-oh.
* When asked if Snare-oh can fling himself up in the air by grabbing two surfaces, [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") said that he probably could.[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-19)
	+ When asked if Snare-oh can fold his hands like origami and shape them into weapons, similar to [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)"), Derrick answered that it is possible, though it would be like hitting someone with a [papier-mâché](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Papier-m%C3%A2ch%C3%A9 "wikipedia:Papier-mâché") boulder.[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-20)
* Snare-oh replaces [Feedback](/wiki/Feedback "Feedback") in the opening intro of **Omniverse**'s Arc 5.
* At some point late in production for Season 3 of the original series, Snare-oh's Omnitrix symbol was intended to be on his left wrist rather than his left shoulder.[[merch 1]](#cite_note-21)
* 11-year-old Snare-oh replaces 11-year-old Wildmutt in the opening intro as of **Omniverse**'s Arc 4, specifically in the part where the latter originally appeared alongside the 11-year-old versions of [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)"), [Feedback](/wiki/Feedback "Feedback"), and [Grey Matter](/wiki/Grey_Matter_(Classic) "Grey Matter (Classic)").
* Had he appeared in **Omniverse** flashbacks, 11-year-old Snare-oh would have resembled his appearance in the original series.[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-22)
	+ In the **Omniverse** intro used from Arc 4 onwards, 11-year-old Snare-oh is incorrectly designed to look the same as his 16-year-old self.
* Like Frankenstrike, [Arctiguana](/wiki/Arctiguana "Arctiguana"), [Buzzshock](/wiki/Buzzshock_(Classic) "Buzzshock (Classic)"), and [Murk Upchuck](/wiki/Upchuck "Upchuck"), Snare-oh appeared in **Omniverse**, but he did not appear in *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*.
