Appearance

### Ben as Ghostfreak


Ghostfreak is a phantom/[ghost](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ghost "wikipedia:Ghost")-like alien with a gaseous appearance and claw-like hands, and grey skin with black lines running all over him. His skin is removable with black and white striped tentacles underneath it, however his true form is unknown.


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Ghostfreak's [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol peeked out of his skin on his lower left chest and he had a pink eye with a black pupil.


In **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")**, Ghostfreak looked exactly like [Zs'Skayr](/wiki/Zs%27Skayr "Zs'Skayr") did in the same series, except his eye was green and the original Omnitrix symbol was on his chest.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Ghostfreak looked like the [enslaved](/wiki/Zs%27Skayr%27s_Minions#Enslaved_Chimera_Sui_Generis_.28formerly.29 "Zs'Skayr's Minions") [Chimera Sui Generi](/wiki/Chimera_Sui_Generis_(Classic) "Chimera Sui Generis (Classic)") from *[Ghost Town](/wiki/Ghost_Town "Ghost Town")*, but was more muscular. His eye was bigger and green with a black pupil, and the spikes on his elbows were gone. Ghostfreak wore the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Ghostfreak now has green chains all over his front side which lead to his neck, waist, and center/chest, giving him the appearance of being contained in a [straitjacket](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Straitjacket "wikipedia:Straitjacket"), but with his arms out freely. The spikes on his elbows have returned, his skin is now a greenish-white shading, and he has two cuffs on each of his arms. The [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol is located at the center of his chains. When possessing someone, he has a green glow. His tentacles are now black and purple-striped and his eye is colored green.

Personality
-----------


During the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Ghostfreak appeared to share the same personality as [Zs'Skayr](/wiki/Zs%27Skayr "Zs'Skayr"), making himself a dangerous alien to use.[[2]](#cite_note-Ghostfreaked_Out-3) This was proven in **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")**, when Zs'Skayr completely took over Ben when he turned into Ghostfreak.[[3]](#cite_note-Ghost_Town-4)


By **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, however, Ghostfreak has begun to exist as a separate individual whose character was independent from Zs'Skayr's.[[4]](#cite_note-The_Ultimate_Sacrifice-5) As of **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Ghostfreak's connection to Zs'Skayr is no longer an issue, making him safer to use.[[1]](#cite_note-Mystery.2C_Incorporeal-2)

Ghostfreak's powers are composed of several aspects of traditional ghosts from myths and lore such as flight, levitation, phasing through solid matter, intangibility, invisibility, and body possession.


Ghostfreak is able to possess the bodies of his enemies and control their movements to the point where he can cause them to fly like himself, allowing him to freely slam them against walls or hit themselves. He can also access the abilities of other beings that possess superpowers. In the case of [Darkstar](/wiki/Michael_Morningstar_(Classic) "Michael Morningstar (Classic)"), he also had access to his powers once he possessed him.[[1]](#cite_note-Mystery.2C_Incorporeal-2)


Ghostfreak can possess [Florauna](/wiki/Florauna_(Classic) "Florauna (Classic)"),[[pop-up 1]](#cite_note-Florauna-1)[[5]](#cite_note-Be_Afraid_of_the_Dark-6) [Vladats](/wiki/Vladat "Vladat"),[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-7) [Necrofriggians](/wiki/Necrofriggian "Necrofriggian"),[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-8) [Atrocians](/wiki/Atrocian "Atrocian"), [Polymorphs](/wiki/Polymorph_(Classic) "Polymorph (Classic)"),[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-9) [Galvanic Mechamorphs](/wiki/Galvanic_Mechamorph_(Classic) "Galvanic Mechamorph (Classic)"), [Nanochips](/wiki/Nanochip "Nanochip"), [Chronosapiens](/wiki/Chronosapien "Chronosapien"),[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-10) and even [Celestialsapiens](/wiki/Celestialsapien_(Classic) "Celestialsapien (Classic)")[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-11) and other Ectonurites.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-possession-12)


Ghostfreak can make himself intangible and invisible as both a powerful defense and infiltrating high-security locations.[[6]](#cite_note-13) Ghostfreak can choose to selectively make parts of himself corporeal or slightly corporeal. This extended technique can be used to interact with the world or fight while still intangible. It can also be used in a more deadly way by interacting with someone's internal body parts, such as causing a stroke by attacking Thumbskull's brain.[[2]](#cite_note-Ghostfreaked_Out-3)


Ghostfreak is capable of making other objects or people intangible and transparent through physical contact;[[1]](#cite_note-Mystery.2C_Incorporeal-2) this feat was first demonstrated by Zs'Skayr.[[5]](#cite_note-Be_Afraid_of_the_Dark-6)


Ghostfreak can use the tentacles under his protective skin as a means of scaring his enemies into submission.[[7]](#cite_note-14) These tentacles can grow sharp thorns that can be used as lethal weapons.[[8]](#cite_note-15)


Ghostfreak has a movable eye, which is especially seen when he wears his outer skin.[[9]](#cite_note-Hunted-16) Although his outer skin protects him from burning in the sunlight, his tentacles appear to be resistant towards sunlight.[[10]](#cite_note-Bengeance_is_Mine-17)


Ghostfreak possesses enhanced strength, as shown when he used his tentacles to grab a bus full of people, and can throw one of Vilgax's drones with his hands.[[10]](#cite_note-Bengeance_is_Mine-17)


Ghostfreak is immune to both an [Ormerowon](/wiki/Ormerowon "Ormerowon")'s zombification powers[[TP 1]](#cite_note-18) and Darkstar's energy absorption ability. This is because Ectonurites do not have [mana](/wiki/Mana "Mana"), as they are not alive in the same conventional way that most other life forms are.[[1]](#cite_note-Mystery.2C_Incorporeal-2)


Ghostfreak has sharp claws, which can easily tear through [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)")'s [Drones](/wiki/Drones "Drones").[[11]](#cite_note-19) Also, when he sticks his claws into a person's head, it can either cause them pain or knock them unconscious.[[2]](#cite_note-Ghostfreaked_Out-3)


Ghostfreak can make people feel deathly cold by simply moving right through them.[[9]](#cite_note-Hunted-16)


Ghostfreak can alter his shape and deform like gas by making himself into a grey, somewhat transparent smoke-like wispy form.[[9]](#cite_note-Hunted-16) While in this form, he is immune to being absorbed by [Gutrot](/wiki/Gutrot "Gutrot")'s [species](/wiki/Gutrot%27s_Species "Gutrot's Species").[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-20)


Ghostfreak has enhanced durability, as he was able to withstand a punch from [Kraab](/wiki/Kraab_(Classic) "Kraab (Classic)"), which threw him against a wall.[[9]](#cite_note-Hunted-16)


Ghostfreak is able to disrupt electronics by phasing through them, as demonstrated by [Zs'Skayr](/wiki/Zs%27Skayr "Zs'Skayr").[[12]](#cite_note-Rad_Monster_Party-21)


Like all Anur System aliens, Ghostfreak is immune to the effects of [Corrodium](/wiki/Corrodium "Corrodium") and can see in the dark,[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-22) the former of which has been demonstrated by Zs'Skayr.[[5]](#cite_note-Be_Afraid_of_the_Dark-6)


Due to being alive in a rather unconventional sense, Ghostfreak can survive in the vacuum of space; this feat was also exclusively demonstrated by Zs'Skayr.[[5]](#cite_note-Be_Afraid_of_the_Dark-6)



### True Form


Although he has never trained himself to do so, he can rotate his skull[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-23) much like Zs'Skayr.[[12]](#cite_note-Rad_Monster_Party-21)


Ghostfreak can project powerful energy beams from either the tentacles in his chest and or his hands whenever his skin is peeled back, as demonstrated by Zs'Skayr.[[5]](#cite_note-Be_Afraid_of_the_Dark-6)[[3]](#cite_note-Ghost_Town-4)


Ghostfreak is capable of telekinesis; though he has never formally done so on his own accord, this feat was first demonstrated when Zs'Skayr took over Ben's body.[[3]](#cite_note-Ghost_Town-4)


If exposed to total darkness, like that of space, Ghostfreak's powers and abilities would be greatly enhanced to their full potential; this feat was also demonstrated with Zs'Skayr.[[5]](#cite_note-Be_Afraid_of_the_Dark-6)

Ghostfreak's intangibility is useless when faced by another being that can go intangible, as it will cancel out their ability to do so.[[13]](#cite_note-24)


Certain substances can neutralize Ghostfreak's intangibility and invisibility, such as a special orange liquid used by Kraab.[[9]](#cite_note-Hunted-16) It is possible that Gutrot's species can replicate this liquid in a gaseous form.[[DJW 10]](#cite_note-25)


Ghostfreak's second skin inhibits many of his powers, but removing it will make him vulnerable to ultraviolet radiation.[[2]](#cite_note-Ghostfreaked_Out-3) Furthermore, Ghostfreak would also be vulnerable to the radiation of a [Prypiatosian-B](/wiki/Prypiatosian-B "Prypiatosian-B"), as demonstrated with Zs'Skayr.[[12]](#cite_note-Rad_Monster_Party-21)


Ghostfreak's intangibility seems to require conscious thought to activate, so opponents will be able to hit him if he is caught off guard, as seen when he got hit by a punching bag that [Thumbskull](/wiki/Thumbskull_(Classic) "Thumbskull (Classic)") threw at him while he was distracted.[[2]](#cite_note-Ghostfreaked_Out-3)


There are certain beings Ghostfreak cannot possess. For example, he cannot possess zombies due to them lacking minds of their own.[[14]](#cite_note-26) He is also unable to possess the [Sentient Ultimate forms](/wiki/Ultimate_Forms#Sentient_Forms "Ultimate Forms")[[4]](#cite_note-The_Ultimate_Sacrifice-5) or [Dagon](/wiki/Dagon "Dagon").[[DJW 11]](#cite_note-27)


In correlation with his weakness against sunlight, Ghostfreak cannot possess [Pyronites](/wiki/Pyronite_(Classic) "Pyronite (Classic)") because of their solar-like radiation. Similarly, he cannot possess [Crystalsapiens](/wiki/Crystalsapien "Crystalsapien") because of the light they generate.[[DJW 12]](#cite_note-28)


[Magic](/wiki/Magic_(Classic) "Magic (Classic)") can harm Ghostfreak, as shown when he was incapacitated by one of [Charmcaster](/wiki/Charmcaster_(Classic) "Charmcaster (Classic)")'s [spells](/wiki/Spells "Spells").[[15]](#cite_note-29)


Like the rest of his kind, Ghostfreak cannot survive on [Anur Vladias](/wiki/Anur_Vladias "Anur Vladias").[[DJW 13]](#cite_note-30)


Ghostfreak can easily be scared by [Toepick](/wiki/Toepick "Toepick")'s [species](/wiki/Toepick%27s_Species "Toepick's Species").[[DJW 14]](#cite_note-31)


Ghostfreak can be affected by a [Nemuina](/wiki/Nemuina "Nemuina")'s sleeping dust.[[DJW 15]](#cite_note-32)


There are a number of weaknesses that Ghostfreak is vulnerable to if he remains tangible and caught off-guard. For example:



* He can be trapped by an [Evolved Arachnichimp](/wiki/Evolved_Arachnichimp "Evolved Arachnichimp")'s webbing.[[4]](#cite_note-The_Ultimate_Sacrifice-5)
* He can be immobilized by an [Evolved Necrofriggian](/wiki/Evolved_Necrofriggian "Evolved Necrofriggian")'s ice flames.[[4]](#cite_note-The_Ultimate_Sacrifice-5)
* He can be harmed by electricity, such as that generated by [Transylians](/wiki/Transylian "Transylian")[[5]](#cite_note-Be_Afraid_of_the_Dark-6) and [Conductoids](/wiki/Conductoid "Conductoid"),[[12]](#cite_note-Rad_Monster_Party-21) as demonstrated with Zs'Skayr.
* He can be controlled and hypnotized by [Vladats](/wiki/Vladat "Vladat") via their [Corrupturas](/wiki/Corruptura "Corruptura"), as demonstrated by many Ectonurite residents of [Anur Transyl](/wiki/Anur_Transyl "Anur Transyl").[[16]](#cite_note-33)

### Ben 10


* Ghostfreak first appeared in *[Permanent Retirement](/wiki/Permanent_Retirement "Permanent Retirement")*, where he frightened [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)"), escaped [Vera](/wiki/Vera_Tennyson "Vera Tennyson")'s house, drove a golf cart, was about to steal an apple pie before being disgusted by a [Limax](/wiki/Limax "Limax") disguised as a [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)") woman, and then followed another one disguised as [Marty](/wiki/Marty "Marty").

* In *[Kevin 11](/wiki/Kevin_11_(Episode) "Kevin 11 (Episode)")*, Ghostfreak snuck into a game room and played a [Sumo Slammers](/wiki/Sumo_Slammers_(Classic) "Sumo Slammers (Classic)") video game.
* In *[Secrets](/wiki/Secrets "Secrets")*, Ghostfreak briefly appeared in the middle of a fight before the malfunctioning [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") made him switch into [Grey Matter](/wiki/Grey_Matter_(Classic) "Grey Matter (Classic)"). Later, Ghostfreak escaped from [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") and searched for Gwen and [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") before switching into [Wildmutt](/wiki/Wildmutt "Wildmutt").
	+ Also, Ghostfreak appeared as one of the many alien templates used for Vilgax's army in an imaginary sequence.
* In *[The Big Tick](/wiki/The_Big_Tick "The Big Tick")*, Ghostfreak failed to defeat the [Great One](/wiki/Great_One "Great One").
* In *[Framed](/wiki/Framed "Framed")*, Gwen mentioned Ghostfreak was used by [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") to terrorize Barstow, California.
* At some point prior to *[Ghostfreaked Out](/wiki/Ghostfreaked_Out "Ghostfreaked Out")*, Ghostfreak snuck into a horror movie, during which Zs'Skayr secretly took control and contacted [Viktor](/wiki/Viktor "Viktor") with his plans.[[pop-up 2]](#cite_note-34)[[5]](#cite_note-Be_Afraid_of_the_Dark-6)
* In *Ghostfreaked Out*, Ghostfreak tricked and tortured [Wildmutt](/wiki/Wildmutt "Wildmutt") in a dream. Later, Zs'Skayr took over Ghostfreak and violently battled the [Circus Freaks](/wiki/Circus_Freak_Trio_(Classic) "Circus Freak Trio (Classic)") until he reverted.
	+ This would remove Ghostfreak from the active playlist, thereby allowing Zs'Skayr to escape the Omnitrix.
* In *[Be Afraid of the Dark](/wiki/Be_Afraid_of_the_Dark "Be Afraid of the Dark")*, Ben regained Ghostfreak (in his true form) by unknowingly scanning Zs'Skayr, though Ben never transforms into Ghostfreak for the remainder of the series out of fear the evil Ectonurite would take over again.

### Ultimate Alien

### Omniverse

Trivia
------


* Ghostfreak is a living being; the [Ectonurite](/wiki/Ectonurite_(Classic) "Ectonurite (Classic)") concept of "life" is just very different from most sentient life forms.[[DJW 16]](#cite_note-35)
* [Dave Johnson](/wiki/Dave_Johnson "Dave Johnson") designed Ghostfreak for the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"). Looking back on the design, he noted he found the eye quite funny.[[DJ 1]](#cite_note-36)
* "[Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s not here", a line said by Ghostfreak in *[Ghostfreaked Out](/wiki/Ghostfreaked_Out "Ghostfreaked Out")*, was used in auditions for the role.[[AC 1]](#cite_note-37)
* According to himself in the original series, Ben was always creeped out when he was Ghostfreak. Even six years later (especially after the Zs'Skayr incident), he was still afraid to transform into him. When he transforms into Ghostfreak in *[Mystery, Incorporeal](/wiki/Mystery,_Incorporeal "Mystery, Incorporeal")*, he states that he feels like himself, suggesting that Ghostfreak now exists independently from Zs'Skayr.
	+ As such, in the original series, Ghostfreak was the least used out of all the original ten aliens Ben had access to, only appearing in seven episodes and being used a total of eight times.
* Ghostfreak's voice has changed over the course of his appearances:
	+ In the original series, he spoke with a faint and whispery voice.
	+ In **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")** and **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, his voice was considerably deeper and raspy like Zs'Skayr.
	+ In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, his voice has returned to the faint and whispery way it was in the original series, albeit it was higher pitched.
	+ In *[And Then There Were None](/wiki/And_Then_There_Were_None "And Then There Were None")*, [Bad Ben](/wiki/Bad_Ben "Bad Ben")'s Ghostfreak sounded more like Zs'Skayr compared to his main counterpart.
* When Ghostfreak was taunting [Kraab](/wiki/Kraab_(Classic) "Kraab (Classic)") in *[Hunted](/wiki/Hunted "Hunted")*, Ben may not have been the one in control.[[pop-up 3]](#cite_note-38)[[9]](#cite_note-Hunted-16) After he reverted, Ben even stated that it felt "even weirder" than when he is normally Ghostfreak.
* In *[The Alliance](/wiki/The_Alliance "The Alliance")*, Ben wanted to use Ghostfreak to try and help an injured [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") at the hospital by "melding" with him. This indicates that Ben already knew about Ghostfreak's ability to possess people, even though he never does this onscreen until *Ghostfreaked Out*.
* Ghostfreak replaces [Shocksquatch](/wiki/Shocksquatch "Shocksquatch") in the intro for **Omniverse**'s Arc 5, specifically in the part where the latter normally appears alongside [Toepick](/wiki/Toepick "Toepick"), [Astrodactyl](/wiki/Astrodactyl "Astrodactyl"), and [Bullfrag](/wiki/Bullfrag "Bullfrag") as of Arc 4.
* Ghostfreak's powers of invisibility, intangibility, and body possession are similar to the [DC Comics](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DC_Comics "wikipedia:DC Comics") character, [Deadman](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deadman_(comics) "wikipedia:Deadman (comics)").
* Ghostfreak is one of five aliens to appear in each series of the Classic Continuity; the other four being [Cannonbolt](/wiki/Cannonbolt_(Classic) "Cannonbolt (Classic)"), [Upchuck](/wiki/Upchuck "Upchuck"), [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)"), and [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)").
	+ However, Ghostfreak is the only one of these aliens who has changed his appearance throughout all four series of the Classic Timeline, albeit it was only due to using his true form in **Alien Force** and second-skinned form in **Ultimate Alien**.
* Ghostfreak is also one of the only five aliens who escaped from the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)"); the others being [Chromastone](/wiki/Chromastone "Chromastone"), [Spidermonkey](/wiki/Spidermonkey_(Classic) "Spidermonkey (Classic)"), [Goop](/wiki/Goop_(Classic) "Goop (Classic)"), and Way Big.
* Ghostfreak is one of two exceptions to the principle that transformations are not full-blown aliens due to retaining Ben's personality, memories, and consciousness; the other being [Feedback](/wiki/Feedback "Feedback").[[DR 1]](#cite_note-39)[[DR 2]](#cite_note-40)
* Ghostfreak is the first alien form that has managed to repress Ben's personality. [Eon](/wiki/Eon_(Transformation) "Eon (Transformation)") followed this trend prior to being retconned.[[pop-up 4]](#cite_note-41)[[17]](#cite_note-42)
* Despite using him at least once to terrorize a city when he was 11,[[18]](#cite_note-43)[[19]](#cite_note-44) [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") states he never liked Ghostfreak.[[20]](#cite_note-45) This was likely due to feeling Zs'Skayr's presence while as [Kevin 11](/wiki/Kevin_11 "Kevin 11").[[DR 3]](#cite_note-46)
* In the intro for the first two seasons of the original series, Ghostfreak's eye is red instead of purple.
	+ It is also worth noting that after Season 2, when Zs'Skayr successfully escapes the Omnitrix and thus removing Ghostfreak from it, he is replaced with Cannonbolt in the intro for the third and fourth seasons.
* Had he appeared in **Omniverse** flashbacks, 11-year-old Ghostfreak would have resembled his 16-year-old self but without the chains.[[DJW 17]](#cite_note-47)
	+ Derrick eventually drew 11-year-old Ghostfreak as part of a GoFundMe in 2020.[[DJW 18]](#cite_note-48)
* If the Omnitrix times out while Ghostfreak is possessing something, Ben would pop out before becoming solid.[[DJW 19]](#cite_note-49)
* Had Ghostfreak appeared in the [reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)"), his design would have been similar to his appearance in the original series with the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Reboot) "Omnitrix (Reboot)") peaking out from under his secondary skin.[[DR 4]](#cite_note-50)
