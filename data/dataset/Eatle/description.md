Appearance
----------


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Eatle was a ten-feet-tall[[merch 1]](#cite_note-Eatle_lore-2) robotic humanoid alien with some shark-like characteristics as well. He was dark blue, almost purple, and light blue in color. He had a long horn on the top of his head connected to a shorter protrusion on the back of his head and a large mouth that spread over part of his chest. He also had a silver belt, matching wristbands, oval-shaped eyes, and the inside of his mouth was green.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Eatle's upper lip is completely covered by his lower lip, which now has different teeth. He has a blue tongue and his horn now has forks at the top, making it resemble that of a [Japanese rhinoceros beetle](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japanese_rhinoceros_beetle "wikipedia:Japanese rhinoceros beetle"). He now has two toes on his feet, and spikes on his arms and the backs of his legs, as well as two longer ones on either side of his mouth. His blue has switched to gray, and he has green clothing all over his torso, as well as a dark green, almost black shell on his back and armor of the same color.


Eatle wears the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)")/[Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol is on his chest.

1/9
Similar to [Upchuck](/wiki/Upchuck "Upchuck"), Eatle has the ability to eat just about anything[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-3) and then convert the materials' matter into energy to shoot a green laser beam out of the slot on his horn. The beam is powerful enough to cut a floating mountain of [Legerdomain](/wiki/Legerdomain "Legerdomain") in half[[2]](#cite_note-EoMF-4) and even hold down a [Lucubra](/wiki/Lucubra "Lucubra")-enhanced [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)").[[1]](#cite_note-TUEP1-1)


Eatle can run very fast, and in addition to shooting lasers, his horn can also be used as a very effective battering ram; both of these feats were shown during his fight with Vilgax.[[1]](#cite_note-TUEP1-1)


Eatle's jaws are very strong and can be used to chew up various resilient materials, like metal.[[3]](#cite_note-5)


Eatle has enhanced durability, as seen when he was able to withstand [Carl Nesmith](/wiki/Carl_Nesmith "Carl Nesmith")'s glove's laser, which was able to injure a [Chronosapien](/wiki/Chronosapien "Chronosapien") and kill a [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)") with a single shot,[[4]](#cite_note-CaFS-6) as well as an entire building from [Dagon](/wiki/Dagon "Dagon")'s [dimension](/wiki/Dagon%27s_World "Dagon's World") falling on top of him.[[1]](#cite_note-TUEP1-1)


Eatle has enhanced strength, as he was able to crush Carl's glove,[[4]](#cite_note-CaFS-6) grab a [Ship](/wiki/Ship "Ship")-enhanced [Julie](/wiki/Julie_Yamamoto "Julie Yamamoto") before pushing her through several walls of her house without stopping,[[5]](#cite_note-7) and rip off the bumper of [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)")'s [car](/wiki/Kevin%27s_Car_(Original) "Kevin's Car (Original)").[[6]](#cite_note-8)


Eatle has sharp claws that can help him climb up walls, as shown when he clambered up [Charmcaster](/wiki/Charmcaster_(Classic) "Charmcaster (Classic)")'s machine. He is also quite agile, as he could quickly jump over a [stone creature](/wiki/Stone_Creatures "Stone Creatures") and up to the machine.[[2]](#cite_note-EoMF-4)



Weaknesses

1/3
Eatle originally had a blind spot due to his eye placement, making him very easy to sneak up on.[[1]](#cite_note-TUEP1-1) He now lacks this weakness as of **Omniverse**, however, as his eyes are much farther apart.


Unlike Upchuck, there is a limit to how much solid matter Eatle can consume, as he found it impossible to completely devour Charmcaster's machine.[[2]](#cite_note-EoMF-4)


Eatle may unaccountably try to eat something against his interests, such as the remains of the [Tenn-Speed](/wiki/Tenn-Speed "Tenn-Speed").[[7]](#cite_note-9)


Eatle is rather heavy, so his weight may serve as a problem when he is fighting on short or slim objects.[[8]](#cite_note-10)


Being an [Oryctini](/wiki/Oryctini "Oryctini"), Eatle is weak against his natural predator.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-11)



History
-------


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, Eatle was unlocked by [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Ultimate_Alien) "Ben 10,000 (Ultimate Alien)").
* Eatle first appeared in *[A Knight to Remember](/wiki/A_Knight_to_Remember "A Knight to Remember")*, where he tried to stop [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") from breaking the [Seal](/wiki/Seal "Seal") but was instantly defeated by him.
* In *[Enemy of My Frenemy](/wiki/Enemy_of_My_Frenemy "Enemy of My Frenemy")*, Eatle defeated a water creature that [Addwaitya](/wiki/Addwaitya "Addwaitya") had created. Later, Eatle tried to devour [Charmcaster](/wiki/Charmcaster_(Classic) "Charmcaster (Classic)")'s machine before switching into [Chromastone](/wiki/Chromastone "Chromastone") once he realized he could not eat the entire machine.
* In *[Catch a Falling Star](/wiki/Catch_a_Falling_Star "Catch a Falling Star")*, Eatle fought [Carl Nesmith](/wiki/Carl_Nesmith "Carl Nesmith"), but was defeated by a mentally unstable [Jennifer Nocturne](/wiki/Jennifer_Nocturne "Jennifer Nocturne").
* In *[Night of the Living Nightmare](/wiki/Night_of_the_Living_Nightmare "Night of the Living Nightmare")*, Eatle defeated a [Ship](/wiki/Ship "Ship")-enhanced [Julie](/wiki/Julie_Yamamoto "Julie Yamamoto") in a dream.
* In *[The Ultimate Enemy: Part 1](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Enemy:_Part_1 "The Ultimate Enemy: Part 1")*, Eatle battled a [Lucubra](/wiki/Lucubra "Lucubra")-enhanced Vilgax with the help of [Sir George](/wiki/Sir_George "Sir George") before eventually switching into [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)") when Vilgax started gaining the upper hand.


### Omniverse


* In *[Hot Stretch](/wiki/Hot_Stretch "Hot Stretch")*, Eatle chased after [Ester](/wiki/Ester "Ester") in [Undertown](/wiki/Undertown "Undertown") and retrieved a [Nuclear Fusion Device](/wiki/Nuclear_Fusion_Device "Nuclear Fusion Device") from her only to lose it to the [Kraaho](/wiki/Kraaho "Kraaho").
* In *[Store 23](/wiki/Store_23 "Store 23")*, Eatle briefly flew through the air due to being launched by [Liam](/wiki/Liam "Liam")'s cart of [Vaxasaurian](/wiki/Vaxasaurian_(Classic) "Vaxasaurian (Classic)") eggs before reverting.
* In *[Return to Forever](/wiki/Return_to_Forever "Return to Forever")*, Eatle tried to break out of a force field, but was reverted by the [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights")' [DNA Scanner](/wiki/DNA_Scanner "DNA Scanner").
* In *[OTTO Motives](/wiki/OTTO_Motives "OTTO Motives")*, Eatle briefly fought a giant [Otto](/wiki/Otto "Otto").
* In *[Let's Do the Time War Again](/wiki/Let%27s_Do_the_Time_War_Again "Let's Do the Time War Again")*, Eatle ate part of the wrecked [Tenn-Speed](/wiki/Tenn-Speed "Tenn-Speed") offscreen before being picked up by [Rook](/wiki/Rook_Blonko "Rook Blonko").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Eatle appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

Etymology
---------


Eatle's name is a portmanteau of "eat", the primary ability he has, and "[beetle](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beetle "wikipedia:Beetle")", the insect he is based on. His name also roughly sounds like "eat all".



Trivia
------


 [![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/8/89/GfT_%2845%29.png/revision/latest?cb=20150809093949) [![](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/8/89/GfT_%2845%29.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/218?cb=20150809093949)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/8/89/GfT_%2845%29.png/revision/latest?cb=20150809093949)  The golf course statue

  
* The red robot-like statue of the golf course from *[Greetings from Techadon](/wiki/Greetings_from_Techadon "Greetings from Techadon")* bears a lot of resemblance to Eatle's design in **Ultimate Alien**.
* Eatle's existence was first brought to light in a listing for the **Ultimate Alien** four-inch line by [Bandai](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bandai "wikipedia:Bandai").[[Bandai 1]](#cite_note-12)
* Initially, in **Ultimate Alien**, Eatle's mouth only ever opened when he was about to devour something and never actually opened when he talked. Much like [Lodestar](/wiki/Lodestar "Lodestar"), however, Eatle's mouth now moves when he speaks in **Omniverse**.
* Eatle is one of the four regular aliens to get a complete redesign in **Omniverse**; the others being [Shocksquatch](/wiki/Shocksquatch "Shocksquatch"), [Swampfire](/wiki/Swampfire "Swampfire"), and [Chromastone](/wiki/Chromastone "Chromastone").
* On iTunes, Eatle's name is misspelled as "Eetle".
* Concept art for Eatle in **Ultimate Alien** shows him with four arms and a horn similar in appearance to the one featured in **Omniverse**.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-13)
	+ Also, concept art for **Omniverse** shows a more animalistic version of Eatle standing on all fours with the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on the top of his horn.
* Despite the confirmed existence of Eatle's predator, it was a concept that ultimately never made it into the show.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-14)[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-15)
