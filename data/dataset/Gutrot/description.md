Appearance
----------


### Ben as Gutrot


Gutrot has the appearance of a mechanical humanoid in a green and white outfit, with the exception of his head and shoulders being a bluish-purple color with black spots. His eyes are green and he has four fingers on each white gloved hand, which are equipped with green and white vambraces and arm length black sleeve covers. Like [Water Hazard](/wiki/Water_Hazard "Water Hazard"), he has nozzles or openings, from which he can eject gas. He has two on each of his forearms, one on each shoulder, three on the white and black rim between his shoulders and neck, three on his head, one on his back and one in the center of his posterior. He has a green belt with white stripes, black accents, and a window on his stomach so that the gas he uses can be seen.


Gutrot wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on what appears to be some kind of gas mask in front of his mouth.



### Nega Ben as Nega Gutrot


[Nega Ben](/wiki/Nega_Ben "Nega Ben")'s Gutrot looks the same as [Ben Prime](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s, except the green parts of his outfit are recolored pale blue and his eyes, skin,[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-1) and Omnitrix symbol are recolored grey.

Personality
-----------


Gutrot speaks with a [Brooklyn](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brooklyn "wikipedia:Brooklyn") accent and a gruff tone that gives him a rather tough yet easygoing "wise guy" sort of vibe. He also has a cynical sense of humor, and is usually pretty calm and level-headed.

 | *Your innards are a walking chemical laboratory... You have the ability to combine various chemicals and expel them as gas!* | ”

Gutrot's innards contain various gaseous elements, making him a "walking chemical laboratory".[[2]](#cite_note-3)[[1]](#cite_note-Animo_Crackers-2)


Gutrot has the ability to make compounds of almost any type and expel them as gas from the various nozzles on his body for both offense and defense. The strength of these gases depends on the amount expelled and their chemical makeup.


Technically speaking, Gutrot can generate just about any kind of chemical gas imaginable,[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-gas-4) including:



* **Sulphur Dioxide**, a highly combustible and toxic gas that can be used as a form of tear gas.[[1]](#cite_note-Animo_Crackers-2)
* **Nitrous Oxide**, more commonly known as "laughing gas", which can be used to incapacitate an opponent by making them laugh uncontrollably.[[1]](#cite_note-Animo_Crackers-2)
* **Fluoromethyl-hexafluoroisopropyl-ether**, also known as "Sevoflurane". While it is normally used as an anesthetic, it is also useful as a knockout gas with short-term memory loss as a side-effect.[[1]](#cite_note-Animo_Crackers-2)
* **Hydrogen Chloride**, an acidic and corrosive gas that can burn or melt through solid materials such as a [Segmentasapien](/wiki/Segmentasapien "Segmentasapien")'s constructs.[[3]](#cite_note-ATTWN-5)
* **Sleeping Gas**, although not used, this gas was mentioned by Gutrot and was about to be used against the [Krakken](/wiki/Krakken "Krakken").[[4]](#cite_note-Vreedlemania-6)
* **Protoplasm Freezing Spray**, a gaseous form of a special orange liquid used by [Kraab](/wiki/Kraab_(Classic) "Kraab (Classic)"),[[5]](#cite_note-7) it is capable of neutralizing an [Ectonurite](/wiki/Ectonurite_(Classic) "Ectonurite (Classic)")'s intangibility and invisibility.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-8)


Gutrot has a very advanced knowledge of chemistry, as well as some degree of knowledge on a species' biology. This allows him to create a gas that can specifically target the species in question. For example, he can create a pheromone designed to attract [To'kustars](/wiki/To%27kustar_(Classic) "To'kustar (Classic)"), causing them to follow Gutrot wherever he wishes to direct them.[[6]](#cite_note-WXIP2-9)


Gutrot can jump exceptionally high.


Gutrot can absorb gas by pulling it through his nozzles.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-10) Likewise, he can intake and get rid of harmful gases.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-11)


If Gutrot creates a gas inside his body, then discovers he no longer needs it, he can simply "neutralize" it instead of releasing it.[[4]](#cite_note-Vreedlemania-6)


Gutrot's intelligence seems to be innate since, similar to some of [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s other intelligent aliens, he may not fully understand what he is talking about from his point of view. Furthermore, Gutrot seems to be very intelligent in general, as he discussed the ramifications of time travel and that hitting someone on the head would simply result in a concussion rather than amnesia.[[1]](#cite_note-Animo_Crackers-2)


Gutrot is immune to poisonous[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-12) and acidic[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-13) gases, as he can inhale them and break them down into something harmless. He is also immune to a [Nemuina](/wiki/Nemuina "Nemuina")'s sleep dust.[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-14)


Gutrot can survive in the vacuum of space.[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-15)



Weaknesses
----------


* ![]()Below Average Reflexes
* ![]()Vulnerability to Time Bombs
1/2
When Gutrot uses his gases, they make a large cloud, which is susceptible to prevailing winds. This can endanger allies, as well as innocent bystanders.


Some of Gutrot's gases are flammable, so he must be careful in situations that might ignite them.


Creatures who are shielded from gases, such as those with a gas mask or insulation, are immune to Gutrot's gases.


Gutrot has poor reflexes and cannot react quickly, as shown when he accepted a hit from [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") and made no effort to dodge or block it.[[6]](#cite_note-WXIP2-9)


Like any other organic being, Gutrot is vulnerable to being erased from existence by a [Chronosapien Time Bomb](/wiki/Chronosapien_Time_Bomb "Chronosapien Time Bomb").[[3]](#cite_note-ATTWN-5)


Gutrot cannot generate spores because he only works with chemical gases.[[DJW 10]](#cite_note-16)


Gutrot cannot replicate the [Fountain of Youth](/wiki/Fountain_of_Youth "Fountain of Youth")'s water as a form of steam.[[DJW 11]](#cite_note-17)


Despite being able to absorb gases, Gutrot cannot absorb an Ectonurite in wisp form.[[DJW 12]](#cite_note-18)



History
-------


### Omniverse


* Gutrot first appeared in *[Animo Crackers](/wiki/Animo_Crackers "Animo Crackers")*, where he defeated [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)") and [Future Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic_Future) "Aloysius Animo (Classic Future)").[[note 1]](#cite_note-20)
* In *[Weapon XI: Part 2](/wiki/Weapon_XI:_Part_2 "Weapon XI: Part 2")*, Gutrot lured two [Way Bads](/wiki/Way_Bads "Way Bads") to the [Rooter Headquarters](/wiki/Rooter_Headquarters "Rooter Headquarters") and attempted to fight [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)"), but was quickly defeated and reverted back to [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)") when Kevin [absorbed some power](/wiki/Kevin%27s_Omniverse_Mutation "Kevin's Omniverse Mutation") from the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)").
* In *[Vreedlemania](/wiki/Vreedlemania "Vreedlemania")*, Gutrot prepared to stop the [Krakken](/wiki/Krakken "Krakken"), but [Sceles](/wiki/Isosceles_Right_Triangle_Vreedle "Isosceles Right Triangle Vreedle") stopped it first. He then witnessed the reunion of [Ma](/wiki/Ma_Vreedle "Ma Vreedle") and [Pa Vreedle](/wiki/Pa_Vreedle "Pa Vreedle") before reverting.
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Gutrot appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.


### Nega Ben's Timeline


* In *[And Then There Were None](/wiki/And_Then_There_Were_None "And Then There Were None")*, [Nega](/wiki/Nega_Ben "Nega Ben") Gutrot tried to capture [No Watch Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(No_Watch_Timeline) "Ben Tennyson (No Watch Timeline)") only to be blocked by [Build-A-Guy](/wiki/Build-A-Guy "Build-A-Guy") and then wiped out by the [Chronosapien Time Bomb](/wiki/Chronosapien_Time_Bomb "Chronosapien Time Bomb").
* In *[And Then There Was Ben](/wiki/And_Then_There_Was_Ben "And Then There Was Ben")*, Nega Gutrot was brought back into existence by No Watch Ben as [Clockwork](/wiki/Clockwork "Clockwork").

Etymology
---------


Gutrot's name is a reference to "Rotgut", a name shared by several fictional characters. Rotgut is also a Prohibition-era slang term for homemade alcoholic beverages, alluding to Gutrot's ability to mix chemicals inside himself. "Gut rot" is also a slang for "stomachache".



Trivia
------


* [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") came up with Gutrot,[[DJW 14]](#cite_note-21) having modeled his appearance and powers after [Koffing](http://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Koffing_(Pok%C3%A9mon) "bulbapedia:Koffing (Pokémon)") from the **Pokémon** franchise.[[DJW 15]](#cite_note-22)
* Technically speaking, Gutrot's name was revealed by the [future Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Future) "Aloysius Animo (Future)").
	+ Gutrot went under the working name Rotgut.[[DJW 16]](#cite_note-23)
* Ironically, chemistry is one of Ben's weaker subjects, though Gutrot seems to be an expert on it.
* Gutrot's voice was inspired by Peter Falk's [Columbo](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Columbo_(character) "wikipedia:Columbo (character)") character.[[DJW 17]](#cite_note-24) He even squints his right eye like Peter Falk, who had a glass eye since the age of three, and was known for his trademark squint.
* Gutrot replaces 11-year-old [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)") in the opening intro for **Omniverse**'s Arc 5.
* Gutrot bears resemblance to the Sontarans from **[Doctor Who](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Doctor_Who "wikipedia:Doctor Who")**.
	+ Interestingly, the first story to feature the Sontarans since the show's revival in 2005 (The Sontaran Stratagem/The Poison Sky, both 2008) revolved around the Sontarans trying to convert the [Earth](/wiki/Earth "Earth")'s atmosphere into clone feed - a poisonous gas to humans.
* Gutrot can generate any gas seen in cartoons and comics, including a gaseous form of truth serum. However, there are some gases he may have to see to know how to make.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-gas-4)
	+ Gutrot is probably immune to freezing gases. However, he would still be vulnerable to gases as strong as liquid nitrogen.[[DJW 18]](#cite_note-25)
* When asked if he could create a cure for COVID-19, Derrick answered that Gutrot could make a sterilizing gas but not a vaccine.[[DJW 19]](#cite_note-26)
* Had Gutrot appeared in the [reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)"), he would be very useful because of his immunity to both the [Forgeti](/wiki/Forgeti "Forgeti")'s memory gas and [Nanny Nightmare](/wiki/Nanny_Nightmare "Nanny Nightmare")'s [baby powder](/wiki/Regressive_Baby_Powder "Regressive Baby Powder").[[DR 1]](#cite_note-27)
