Appearance
----------


### Ben as Ultimate Echo Echo


Because he is made of living sound waves, Ultimate Echo Echo wears a tall blue containment suit[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-2) with black stripes, an overall rounded design, long arms and legs, and stumped feet. Unlike his devolved counterpart, Ultimate Echo Echo has [fifteen blue, removable speaker-like components](/wiki/Sonic_Disk "Sonic Disk") attached to his body and a marking on his back resembling a cassette tape. Ultimate Echo Echo wears the evolved [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.



### Albedo as Negative Ultimate Echo Echo


[Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Ultimate Echo Echo has red eyes and red cables that attach from his back to his hands and smaller mouth. His head is wide and angular, with a silvery forehead and black "ears" on the sides. His Sonic Disks are red instead of blue and they look like his head, not having a sunken look to them. Two of the spikes on the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") symbol, which look more tubular, connect to the discs on his chest, and a third one seems to crawl up the side of his neck. He also has black stripes on his stomach and legs.

1/12
Like Echo Echo, Ultimate Echo Echo can release sonic screams and ultrasonic waves from the [Sonic Disks](/wiki/Sonic_Disk "Sonic Disk") on his body. These sound waves are powerful enough to throw [Ultimate Kevin](/wiki/Ultimate_Kevin "Ultimate Kevin") through several buildings at once,[[1]](#cite_note-AP2-1) damage a [Petrosapien](/wiki/Petrosapien_(Classic) "Petrosapien (Classic)"),[[2]](#cite_note-TUS-3) and paralyze an [Opticoid](/wiki/Opticoid_(Classic) "Opticoid (Classic)").[[3]](#cite_note-TUH-4)


Ultimate Echo Echo can create force fields out of sonic waves that are strong enough to contain a blast from a [nuclear fusion grenade](/wiki/Nuclear_Fusion_Grenade "Nuclear Fusion Grenade").[[4]](#cite_note-TEoME-5)


Ultimate Echo Echo can also use his sonic waves to push objects, seen when he pushed [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)")'s [mana](/wiki/Mana "Mana") force field across a pool of acid.[[5]](#cite_note-MoI-6)


Ultimate Echo Echo can fly by using sound waves.[[6]](#cite_note-7)


Because his appearance is a containment suit, Ultimate Echo Echo can survive in the vacuum of space. He can also produce airwaves, as he is capable of using his sonokinetic powers while in space as well.[[4]](#cite_note-TEoME-5)


Ultimate Echo Echo possesses enhanced strength, enough to briefly help [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") hold open a door in the temple on [Mikd'lty](/wiki/Mikd%27lty_(Classic) "Mikd'lty (Classic)").[[5]](#cite_note-MoI-6)


Ultimate Echo Echo is extremely resistant to low temperatures like those on Mykdl'dy.[[5]](#cite_note-MoI-6)


Ultimate Echo Echo is durable enough to withstand being thrown through the walls of [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s [house](/wiki/Ben%27s_House "Ben's House").[[7]](#cite_note-TPG-8)



### Equipment


Ultimate Echo Echo manipulates the power of sound, but he can also send out the Sonic Disks on his body to project powerful sonic blasts, which can knock someone out, destroy projectiles or even a military tank, with relative ease.


Ultimate Echo Echo can multiply the Sonic Disks once they have separated from his body, creating as many as needed.


Ultimate Echo Echo can use his Sonic Disks to use an exceedingly deadly move called Sonic Doom, where his discs surround the enemy and fire full sonic blasts. This move is powerful enough to subdue Ultimate Kevin,[[1]](#cite_note-AP2-1) who had managed to win almost every previous battle he had got into.


Ultimate Echo Echo's Sonic Disks can continue flying and attacking even after he transforms back to normal.[[8]](#cite_note-GfT-9)


Although he has never trained himself to do so, Ultimate Echo Echo can use his Sonic Disks to duplicate.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-10)[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-11)[[note 1]](#cite_note-14)

Because Ultimate Echo Echo's containment suit is made of metal, he can be easily magnetized by a [Biot-savartian](/wiki/Biot-savartian "Biot-savartian").[[1]](#cite_note-AP2-1)


Like Echo Echo, Ultimate Echo Echo and his Sonic Disks are vulnerable to electricity, such those generated by a [Gimlinopithecus](/wiki/Gimlinopithecus "Gimlinopithecus").[[3]](#cite_note-TUH-4)


Ultimate Echo Echo can be suffocated if a large amount of matter enters his lungs.[[7]](#cite_note-TPG-8)


Ultimate Echo Echo's Sonic Disks are not very durable, as they can be destroyed by a [Techadon robot](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots")'s laser blasts[[8]](#cite_note-GfT-9) or an [Anodite](/wiki/Anodite "Anodite")'s mana attacks, the latter of which was demonstrated by his [sentient counterpart](/wiki/Sentient_Ultimate_Echo_Echo "Sentient Ultimate Echo Echo").[[2]](#cite_note-TUS-3)


Ultimate Echo Echo can be knocked out of the sky by species with as much strength as a [To'kustar](/wiki/To%27kustar_(Classic) "To'kustar (Classic)"), as also demonstrated by his sentient counterpart.[[2]](#cite_note-TUS-3)



History
-------


### Ultimate Alien


* Ultimate Echo Echo first appeared in *[Map of Infinity](/wiki/Map_of_Infinity_(Episode) "Map of Infinity (Episode)")*, where he defeated some [Necrofriggians](/wiki/Necrofriggian "Necrofriggian") and explored the temple on [Mikd'lty](/wiki/Mikd%27lty_(Classic) "Mikd'lty (Classic)"), during which he defeated [Cerberus](/wiki/Cerberus "Cerberus"). He eventually switched into [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)") to help [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") hold a door open.
* In *[The Enemy of My Enemy](/wiki/The_Enemy_of_My_Enemy "The Enemy of My Enemy")*, Ultimate Echo Echo saved the [Plumber's Academy](/wiki/Plumbers%27_Academy "Plumbers' Academy") from a [nuclear bomb](/wiki/Nuclear_Fusion_Grenade "Nuclear Fusion Grenade").
* In *[Absolute Power: Part 2](/wiki/Absolute_Power:_Part_2 "Absolute Power: Part 2")*, Ultimate Echo Echo defeated [Ultimate Kevin](/wiki/Ultimate_Kevin "Ultimate Kevin") and prepared to finish him off before [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") convinced him otherwise.
* In *[Viktor: The Spoils](/wiki/Viktor:_The_Spoils "Viktor: The Spoils")*, Ultimate Echo Echo saved the [Rust Bucket 3](/wiki/Rust_Bucket_3 "Rust Bucket 3") from some missiles.
* In *[Basic Training](/wiki/Basic_Training "Basic Training")*, Ultimate Echo Echo briefly appeared at the beginning after battling [Trombipulor](/wiki/Trombipulor "Trombipulor").
* In *[Greetings from Techadon](/wiki/Greetings_from_Techadon "Greetings from Techadon")*, Ultimate Echo Echo battled [a silver Techadon](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots") and figured out how to stop it.
* In *[Double or Nothing](/wiki/Double_or_Nothing "Double or Nothing")*, Ultimate Echo Echo defeated [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") [Rath](/wiki/Rath_(Classic) "Rath (Classic)").
* In *[The Perfect Girlfriend](/wiki/The_Perfect_Girlfriend "The Perfect Girlfriend")*, Ultimate Echo Echo fought [Elena](/wiki/Elena_Validus "Elena Validus") who nearly suffocated him to death with her [Nanochips](/wiki/Nanochip "Nanochip") until [Julie](/wiki/Julie_Yamamoto "Julie Yamamoto") convinced her to spare him.
* In *[Night of the Living Nightmare](/wiki/Night_of_the_Living_Nightmare "Night of the Living Nightmare")*, Ultimate Echo Echo was seen confronting [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") in a dream.


### Omniverse


* In *[The Ultimate Heist](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Heist "The Ultimate Heist")*, Negative Ultimate Echo Echo defeated [Eye Guy](/wiki/Eye_Guy "Eye Guy"), but was defeated by [Shocksquatch](/wiki/Shocksquatch "Shocksquatch") and switched into Negative [Ultimate Spidermonkey](/wiki/Ultimate_Spidermonkey "Ultimate Spidermonkey").
* In *[And Then There Was Ben](/wiki/And_Then_There_Was_Ben "And Then There Was Ben")*, Negative Ultimate Echo Echo chased an Omnitrixless [Ben Prime](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") only to be sent back to the Prime Timeline by [No Watch Ben](/wiki/No_Watch_Ben "No Watch Ben") as [Clockwork](/wiki/Clockwork "Clockwork").

### [Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction](/wiki/Ben_10_Ultimate_Alien:_Cosmic_Destruction "Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction")


Ultimate Echo Echo is a playable alien in all versions of the video game but only on a small section in the [Amazon base](/wiki/Amazon "Amazon") to defeat an army of [Robotic Extermination Devices](/wiki/Robotic_Extermination_Devices "Robotic Extermination Devices"). Strangely, he has five fingers instead of four.


Ultimate Echo Echo can launch his Sonic Disks at enemies, send 2 disks out to make a shockwave on far away enemies, and spin sonic disks around him, damaging any nearby enemies in a small radius, then making a shockwave, damaging all nearby enemies.


He can dash forward, punching through enemies. He moves around by floating above the ground.



### [Ben 10: Galactic Racing](/wiki/Ben_10:_Galactic_Racing "Ben 10: Galactic Racing")


Ultimate Echo Echo is a playable racer in the game. He fires sonic discs at his opponents.



### [Project Exonaut](/wiki/Project_Exonaut "Project Exonaut")


Ultimate Echo Echo is a playable Exosuit of the Banzai Squadron on Project Exonaut. His Weapon Mod, similar to Phantom, increases the firing rate of the Marksman. Ultimate Echo Echo is available for purchase at level 20 for 1000 coins.



### [TKO](/wiki/TKO "TKO")


His special moves are Sonic Disc and Sound Barrier and his super move is Echo Chamber.

Trivia
------


* Ultimate Echo Echo's mouth does not move when he speaks, but only when he uses his sonic screams or is expressing his emotions.
* Ultimate Echo Echo's voice is more robotic-sounding than Echo Echo's, making him sound similar to a speaker.
* [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") thinks that Ultimate Echo Echo's name should be "Ultimate Echo Ultimate Echo" instead.[[5]](#cite_note-MoI-6)
* Ultimate Echo Echo was replaced by his devolved form for the **Omniverse** version of *[Game Creator](/wiki/Game_Creator "Game Creator")*.
* Ultimate Echo Echo was the [Alien of the Month](/wiki/Alien_of_the_Month "Alien of the Month") in November 2011.
	+ However, in Australia, [Ultimate Kevin](/wiki/Ultimate_Kevin "Ultimate Kevin") took Ultimate Echo Echo's place during that time.
* In *[FusionFall](/wiki/FusionFall "FusionFall")*, there is a helmet based on Ultimate Echo Echo that can be bought inside [Ship](/wiki/Ship "Ship").
* Ultimate Echo Echo first appeared in [Fashion Victim](/wiki/Fashion_Victim "Fashion Victim"), a non-canon [comic](/wiki/Comics "Comics") released concurrently to **Ultimate Alien**, before his official on-screen debut in *Map of Infinity*.
