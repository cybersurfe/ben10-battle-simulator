Appearance
----------


### Ben as Gravattack


Being a silicon-based lifeform,[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-2) Gravattack is literally a living planet. He is a large, bulky, reddish-brown alien with rocky skin, a giant mouth with teeth that are also made of rocks,[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-3) and a molten lava planet core at the center of his body. His feet are tan with three toes, and he has three fingers and one thumb on each hand. He wears black shorts with green stripes, a green belt with white stripes and the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his forehead.



### Albedo as Negative Gravattack


[Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Gravattack is magenta and red-colored and his core, eyes, and [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") symbol are all red.



### Bad Ben as Bad Gravattack


[Bad Ben](/wiki/Bad_Ben "Bad Ben")'s Gravattack is violet colored, his core is purple, and his eyes and Omnitrix symbol are mint green.

Powers and Abilities
--------------------


* ![]()Gravikinesis
* ![]()Planetoid Form/Flight
* ![]()Orbitakinesis
* ![]()Force Field Generation
* ![]()Black Hole Generation
* ![]()Enhanced Strength
* ![]()Enhanced Durability
* ![]()Space Survivability
1/8
Gravattack can control gravity to manipulate the weight and motion of objects, allowing him to suspend and levitate targets in the air, send them flying back or forth, or slam them down to the ground with hand gestures. His control over gravity allows him to change its direction, allowing him to telekinetically send targets flying upwards or across the air as if they were falling.


Gravattack can place objects into an orbital track, causing them to fly in circles at various speeds. When using his powers, he emits an aura of hazy energy[[note 1]](#cite_note-7) which he can also form into walls, barriers and domes.


Gravattack can use the laws of general relativity to his advantage and can reduce the speed of objects (or in some cases, creatures) to nothing even if they were moving at the speed of light before.


Gravattack can make matter or energy orbit around him whenever he manipulates his own gravity in order to levitate and retracts his limbs, curling his body to a planetoid sphere.


Gravattack possesses enhanced strength, enough to punch a hole in the ground.[[4]](#cite_note-LDtTWA-8)


Gravattack has enhanced durability, as seen when he endured physical attacks from [Fistrick](/wiki/Fistrick "Fistrick")'s [armor](/wiki/Class_12_Armored_Mecha_Suit "Class 12 Armored Mecha Suit").[[5]](#cite_note-AJftP-9)


Because he is a living planetoid, Gravattack can survive in the vacuum of space.[[6]](#cite_note-MA-10)


With enough practice, Gravattack can create a Quasar.[[DR 1]](#cite_note-11)



Weaknesses
----------


* ![]()Constrained by Large Size
* ![]()Thrown Off Orbit
* ![]()Unintentional Weight Damage
* ![]()Uncontrollable Black Hole Creation
* ![]()Core Approaching Meltdown
1/5
If he takes large amounts of damage to his core, it can cause Gravattack immense pain. This will also make him to enter meltdown and explode, which can be dangerous enough to destroy the whole area he is in.[[7]](#cite_note-FAtC-12)


Gravattack's large body makes it hard for him to move in small spaces.[[8]](#cite_note-13)


Sudden changes of mass can throw off Gravattack's orbit.[[2]](#cite_note-FaFBM-5)


Gravattack cannot stop someone from using energy-based attacks.[[9]](#cite_note-14)


If Gravattack manipulates the gravity of an opponent or object too much, he can accidentally create a black hole and will be unable to undo this despite his impressive ability to create it in the first place.[[4]](#cite_note-LDtTWA-8) There is also a structural limit that Gravattack's target reaches long before the fusion starts.[[MW 1]](#cite_note-15)



History
-------


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, Gravattack was unlocked by [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Ultimate_Alien) "Ben 10,000 (Ultimate Alien)").[[note 2]](#cite_note-18)


### Omniverse


* Gravattack first appeared in *[A Jolt from the Past](/wiki/A_Jolt_from_the_Past "A Jolt from the Past")*, where he defeated [Fistrick](/wiki/Fistrick "Fistrick") and saved the [Megawhatts](/wiki/Nosedeenian "Nosedeenian") with [Rook](/wiki/Rook_Blonko "Rook Blonko")'s help.
* In *[Hot Stretch](/wiki/Hot_Stretch "Hot Stretch")*, Gravattack attempted to battle the [Kraaho](/wiki/Kraaho "Kraaho"), but he reverted instantly.
* In *[Vilgax Must Croak](/wiki/Vilgax_Must_Croak "Vilgax Must Croak")*, Gravattack defeated [Attea](/wiki/Attea "Attea"), [SixSix](/wiki/SixSix_(Classic) "SixSix (Classic)"), [SevenSeven](/wiki/SevenSeven "SevenSeven"), and [EightEight](/wiki/EightEight "EightEight").
* In *[While You Were Away](/wiki/While_You_Were_Away "While You Were Away")*, Gravattack defeated [Sheriff Wat-Senn](/wiki/Sheriff_Wat-Senn "Sheriff Wat-Senn") and [Rook Shar](/wiki/Rook_Shar "Rook Shar").
* In *[Food Around the Corner](/wiki/Food_Around_the_Corner "Food Around the Corner")*, Gravattack helped negotiate a peace treaty between the [Appoplexians](/wiki/Appoplexian_(Classic) "Appoplexian (Classic)") and the [Lewodans](/wiki/Lewodan "Lewodan"). However, the [Hulex](/wiki/Hulex "Hulex") landed on him and they tried to destroy his core to make him explode. Fortunately, he was able to revert before this could happen.
* In *[For a Few Brains More](/wiki/For_a_Few_Brains_More "For a Few Brains More")*, Gravattack was seen twice. Firstly, he broke out of the [Proto-TRUK](/wiki/Proto-TRUK "Proto-TRUK") wreckage and fought [Ultimate Albedo](/wiki/Ultimate_Albedo "Ultimate Albedo") before being defeated by [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") [Armodrillo](/wiki/Armodrillo "Armodrillo"). Afterwards, Negative Gravattack briefly appeared before going [Ultimate](/wiki/Ultimate_Gravattack "Ultimate Gravattack").
* In *[The Rooters of All Evil](/wiki/The_Rooters_of_All_Evil "The Rooters of All Evil")*, Gravattack defeated the [Rooters](/wiki/Rooters "Rooters").
* In *[Fight at the Museum](/wiki/Fight_at_the_Museum "Fight at the Museum")*, Gravattack used his powers to keep [Exo-Skull](/wiki/Exo-Skull "Exo-Skull") stuck in the floor.
* In *[It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 2](/wiki/It%27s_a_Mad,_Mad,_Mad_Ben_World:_Part_2 "It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 2")*, Gravattack argued with [Dino-Mighty](/wiki/Dino-Mighty "Dino-Mighty") for not transforming him into [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)") before reverting the latter into [Ben 23](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Dimension_23) "Ben Tennyson (Dimension 23)").
* In *[Let's Do the Time War Again](/wiki/Let%27s_Do_the_Time_War_Again "Let's Do the Time War Again")*, Gravattack chased and trapped the [Time Beast](/wiki/Time_Beast "Time Beast"), accidentally created a black hole in the process.
* In *[Malgax Attacks](/wiki/Malgax_Attacks "Malgax Attacks")*, Gravattack helped [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") and [Luhley](/wiki/Luhley "Luhley") escape from the [Chimeran Hammer](/wiki/Chimeran_Hammer_(Classic) "Chimeran Hammer (Classic)") and traveled back to [Galvan Prime](/wiki/Galvan_Prime_(Classic) "Galvan Prime (Classic)") with them.
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Gravattack appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.


### Bad Ben's Timeline


* In *[And Then There Were None](/wiki/And_Then_There_Were_None "And Then There Were None")*, [Bad](/wiki/Bad_Ben "Bad Ben") Gravattack tried to capture [No Watch Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(No_Watch_Timeline) "Ben Tennyson (No Watch Timeline)") before battling [Astrodactyl](/wiki/Astrodactyl "Astrodactyl") and [Build-A-Guy](/wiki/Build-A-Guy "Build-A-Guy") until he was wiped out by the [Chronosapien Time Bomb](/wiki/Chronosapien_Time_Bomb "Chronosapien Time Bomb").
* In *[And Then There Was Ben](/wiki/And_Then_There_Was_Ben "And Then There Was Ben")*, Bad Gravattack was brought back to existence by No Watch Ben as [Clockwork](/wiki/Clockwork "Clockwork").

Etymology
---------


Gravattack's name comes from the combination of "[gravity](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gravity "wikipedia:Gravity")", his main power, and "attack", the way he mainly uses said power.



Trivia
------


* Gravattack's concept name was Graviton early on in production of **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-19)[[AC 1]](#cite_note-20)
* [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") did not come up with Gravattack because the idea for the transformation had already started by the time he joined the crew.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-21) His powers were created before his design and name were conceived.[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-22)
	+ It was Derrick's idea for Gravattack to convert into a planetoid form.[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-23)
* Like [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)"), Gravattack's size is portrayed inconsistently in many of his appearances. This is due to a problem with the size comparisons.[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-24)
* Like the rest of [his species](/wiki/Galilean "Galilean"), Gravattack is considered to be wise and patient.[[10]](#cite_note-25)[[7]](#cite_note-FAtC-12)
* Gravattack was considered to appear in the [reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)") but did not make the cut because he is too powerful for the Earthbound villains on the show to fight.[[DR 2]](#cite_note-26)
* Gravattack's voice started out on a deep note, but it eventually raised in pitch until it almost matched [David Kaye](/wiki/David_Kaye "David Kaye")'s portrayal of [Optimus Prime](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Optimus_Prime "wikipedia:Optimus Prime") from **[Transformers: Animated](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Transformers:_Animated "wikipedia:Transformers: Animated")**.
