Appearance
----------


Ultimate Way Big is almost identical to [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)"). The white parts of his body except for his chest and hands are changed to blue, and he has two curved horns on the left and right sides of his head, similar to [Alien X](/wiki/Alien_X_(Classic) "Alien X (Classic)") and [Ditto](/wiki/Ditto "Ditto"). The red parts of his body except for his shoulders and cheeks are now white, and the black parts of his body are now red. He is also bigger in size than Way Big.


Ultimate Way Big wears the evolved [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.



Powers and Abilities
--------------------


* ![]()Cosmic Rays
* ![]()Cosmic Discs
* ![]()Flight
* ![]()Speed Rotation/Attack Repulsion
* ![]()Wall Scaling
1/5
Ultimate Way Big is stronger, more durable and bigger than Way Big, as he was able to pull [Dagon](/wiki/Dagon "Dagon") to the ground, who was gargantuan when compared to Ultimate Way Big's size at the time.


Ultimate Way Big could spin around fast enough to repel Dagon's laser beams.


Ultimate Way Big has the ability to fly.


Ultimate Way Big's cosmic rays are stronger than Way Big's. He can produce the rays as either projectiles or as beams.


Ultimate Way Big is able to create cosmic energy discs that were able to cut off one of Dagon's tentacles.


Like Way Big, Ultimate Way Big can survive in space and extreme temperatures.



Weaknesses
----------


* ![]()Durability Limit
* ![]()Vulnerability to Acid
* ![]()Vulnerability to Energy Attacks
1/3
As with his base form, Ultimate Way Big's size restricts him from fighting in confined spaces. Being even larger than his base form, Ultimate Way Big is even more limited by this fact than base Way Big is.


Despite his enhanced durability, Ultimate Way Big was shown to be vulnerable to Dagon's lasers and acid rain.

Trivia
------


* Appearance-wise, Ultimate Way Big bears a striking resemblance to [Ultraman Taro](https://ultra.fandom.com/wiki/Ultraman_Taro_(character) "w:c:ultra:Ultraman Taro (character)") from the *[Ultraman](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ultraman "wikipedia:Ultraman")* franchise, and also has very similar powers. However, color-wise, he bears more of a resemblance to [Ultraman Dyna](https://ultra.fandom.com/wiki/Ultraman_Dyna_(character)) instead.
	+ Ultimate Way Big leaping into the air with his right fist extended upwards is a reference to the "Rise" transformation used in the *Ultraman* franchise.
	+ Ultimate Way Big's body spinning and cosmic disk powers are references to Ultraman's High Spin and Ultra-Slash techniques respectively.
* Ultimate Way Big was the first [Ultimate Form](/wiki/Ultimate_Forms "Ultimate Forms") to be hinted on the DVD cover of **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")** named *The Ultimate Ending*, where his back is shown, with Ultimate Way Big facing Dagon.
* While Way Big shoots his cosmic ray by putting his right hand vertical and left hand horizontal, Ultimate Way Big does it by putting his left hand vertical and his right hand horizontal.
* When asked about any of Ben's aliens that could survive [Element X](/wiki/Element_X "Element X"), Duncan Rouleau said that Ultimate Way Big can tank it for sure.[[DR 1]](#cite_note-solar-1)
