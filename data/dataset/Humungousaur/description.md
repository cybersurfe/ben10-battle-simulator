Appearance
----------


### Ben as Humungousaur


Humungousaur is a somewhat humanoid [dinosaur](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dinosaur "wikipedia:Dinosaur") standing about twelve feet tall with hard skin that is colored brown on its outer side and beige on its inner side. Both Gwen and Azmuth share these traits as Humungousaur.


In **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")** and **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Humungousaur wore the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In *[Video Games](/wiki/Video_Games "Video Games")*, Humungousaur wore a blue motion capture suit.


In *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, the plates on his chest ended in spikes and he had spikes above his eyes.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Humungousaur wears black briefs with a white waistband, and a green and a black sash across his chest (going from his right shoulder to the left side of his lower back) containing the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol. Humungousaur now has grey fingernails instead of brown, a slightly bigger chin, green eyes in the same hue of his Omnitrix symbol, a shorter build, and his nose is positioned on his forehead.



### Albedo as Negative Humungousaur


In *[Good Copy, Bad Copy](/wiki/Good_Copy,_Bad_Copy "Good Copy, Bad Copy")*, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Humungousaur looked exactly like [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s Humungousaur. In *[The Final Battle: Part 1](/wiki/The_Final_Battle:_Part_1 "The Final Battle: Part 1")*, Negative Humungousaur had red eyes and his Ultimatrix symbol was red as well.


In **Omniverse**, Negative Humungousaur has a darker skin color, creamy-white inner side and a red [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") sash across his chest in the opposite direction as Ben's Humungousaur. His eyes are the same red as his sash.



### Alpha as Humungousaur


[Alpha](/wiki/Alpha "Alpha") as Humungousaur had a more monstrous look, with green skin, red eyes, and red lines across his chest. He wore a red Ultimatrix symbol on his chest.

1/16
Humungousaur possesses incredible strength, being able to lift and throw objects (heavy or light) up to a high altitude, even into near-earth orbit. Technically speaking, he is physically stronger than [Methanosians](/wiki/Methanosian "Methanosian")[[DM 1]](#cite_note-link-4) and [Crystalsapiens](/wiki/Crystalsapien "Crystalsapien").[[DM 2]](#cite_note-5)


Humungousaur possesses a thick, armored hide, which has proven to be durable enough to withstand blaster fire and heavy impacts. His skin is also resistant to heat.


Humungousaur has the power to increase or decrease his own body size and mass. He can grow bigger up to about 60 feet at will, increasing his natural strength as he grows. He can also shrink down to about 10 feet.[[DM 3]](#cite_note-6)


When Humungousaur grows, his dinosaur features become more pronounced, growing [Stegosauride](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stegosauridae "wikipedia:Stegosauridae") features such as plates on his back, a crest on his forehead, and spikes on his tail. The plates on his shoulders and arms also separate and grow, giving him a more ferocious look. These features give him an extra edge in battle both defensively and offensively. Humungousaur can also generate his Stegosauride features without changing size.[[2]](#cite_note-7)[[DM 4]](#cite_note-8)


Humungousaur is durable enough to survive in the vacuum of space for a few minutes[[3]](#cite_note-9)[[4]](#cite_note-AaB-10) and pass through dangerous force fields.[[5]](#cite_note-11)


Humungousaur is capable of jumping extremely high despite his size and weight. His tail is also powerful enough to easily knock away [Malware](/wiki/Malware "Malware")'s third form.[[6]](#cite_note-SP2-12)


Humungousaur can run on all fours in a similar fashion to a [bull](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bull "wikipedia:Bull") or a [rhinoceros](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rhinoceros "wikipedia:Rhinoceros").[[7]](#cite_note-13)


Humungousaur is quite agile for his size, as he could somersault onto one of the [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights")' battle horses,[[8]](#cite_note-14) perform a slide tackle,[[9]](#cite_note-15) and evade attacks while jumping.[[6]](#cite_note-SP2-12)


Humungousaur has powerful jaws that are strong enough to cause pain to a [Highbreed](/wiki/Highbreed "Highbreed").[[10]](#cite_note-B10RP2-16)


Humungousaur's immense size and weight make him very useful for moving against strong suction forces[[11]](#cite_note-17) and powerful winds.[[12]](#cite_note-18)


Humungousaur has a powerful roar, which he once used to scare away a large group of reporters.[[13]](#cite_note-19)


Similar to [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)"), Humungousaur is strong enough to create shock waves by clapping his hands together.[[MW 1]](#cite_note-20)


Also like Four Arms, when infected with a cold, Humungousaur gains the ability to spit slime.[[14]](#cite_note-TSoC-21)

1/14
Humungousaur's strength has a clear limit, as with such cases with moving and lifting beings or objects as heavy as [Tyrannopede](/wiki/Tyrannopede "Tyrannopede"), whose sheer weight proved a challenge for Humungousaur to manage.[[15]](#cite_note-:0-22) Technically speaking, Humungousaur is physically weaker than [Tetramands](/wiki/Tetramand_(Classic) "Tetramand (Classic)"),[[DM 1]](#cite_note-link-4) Highbreeds,[[10]](#cite_note-B10RP2-16)[[16]](#cite_note-23) and [Evolved Vaxasaurians](/wiki/Evolved_Vaxasaurian "Evolved Vaxasaurian"), the latter of whom is able to lift a To'kustar in record timing.[[17]](#cite_note-24) He was also incapable of harming [Ultimate Aggregor](/wiki/Ultimate_Aggregor "Ultimate Aggregor") with physical blows, despite having given him a vicious beating.[[18]](#cite_note-MoI-25)


Humungousaur is very slow and combined with his naturally large physique, it makes him a very easy target for enemy attacks. Even his base size makes it impossible for him to fit into small spaces.


Humungousaur can be discomforted by an [Aerophibian](/wiki/Aerophibian_(Classic) "Aerophibian (Classic)")'s neuroshock blasts.[[19]](#cite_note-GCBC-26)


Humungousaur is weak against electricity, such as that generated by [Amperi](/wiki/Amperi "Amperi")[[18]](#cite_note-MoI-25) and [Nosedeenians](/wiki/Nosedeenian_(Classic) "Nosedeenian (Classic)"), the latter of which was demonstrated by [Suemungousaur](/wiki/Suemungousaur "Suemungousaur").[[20]](#cite_note-27)


Being a [Vaxasaurian](/wiki/Vaxasaurian_(Classic) "Vaxasaurian (Classic)"), Humungousaur is vulnerable to being trapped by a [Kaosseffexx Ultimasauria](/wiki/Kaosseffexx_Ultimasauria "Kaosseffexx Ultimasauria"), as shown when he was encased in Tyrannopede's cocoon and was unable to escape on his own.[[15]](#cite_note-:0-22)[[21]](#cite_note-28)


Humungousaur is more of a land-based alien, meaning he cannot breathe or swim underwater.[[22]](#cite_note-29)Furthermore, due to his immense size and weight he can sink in water.


Despite being resistant to heat, Humungousaur can be harmed by a [Methanosian](/wiki/Methanosian "Methanosian")'s fire attacks.[[19]](#cite_note-GCBC-26) Similarly, he can be harmed by a [Pyronite](/wiki/Pyronite_(Classic) "Pyronite (Classic)")'s fire attacks even while he has his Stegosauride armor.[[4]](#cite_note-AaB-10)


Despite his enhanced durability, Humungousaur's feet can be badly hurt by a strong enough blow, similar to those of a [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)").[[23]](#cite_note-30) He can also feel pain if he bangs his head on something solid.[[24]](#cite_note-31)[[25]](#cite_note-32)


Despite his wind resistance, Humungousaur can still be blown off his feet if the wind is strong enough, as shown when he battled [Sir George](/wiki/Sir_George "Sir George").[[26]](#cite_note-33)


Like Four Arms, if Humungousaur is under the effects of a cold virus, his strength and stamina will be severely reduced.[[14]](#cite_note-TSoC-21)



History
-------


### Alien Force


* Humungousaur first appeared in *[Ben 10 Returns: Part 2](/wiki/Ben_10_Returns:_Part_2 "Ben 10 Returns: Part 2")*, where he battled some [DNAliens](/wiki/DNAliens "DNAliens"), but was defeated by a [Highbreed](/wiki/Highbreed "Highbreed"). He then jumped onto a [Highbreed Ship](/wiki/Highbreed_Ship "Highbreed Ship") and destroyed its laser cannon before falling off, saving [Bellwood](/wiki/Bellwood_(Classic) "Bellwood (Classic)").
* In *[All That Glitters](/wiki/All_That_Glitters "All That Glitters")*, Humungousaur stopped a highway overpass from collapsing long enough for [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") to save everyone from falling off it.
	+ The same scene reoccurred on TV during [Will Harangue](/wiki/Will_Harangue "Will Harangue")'s news report in *[Fame](/wiki/Fame "Fame")*.
* In *[Pier Pressure](/wiki/Pier_Pressure "Pier Pressure")*, Humungousaur saved [Baz-l](/wiki/Baz-l "Baz-l") from an exploding ship.
* In *[Max Out](/wiki/Max_Out "Max Out")*, Humungousaur briefly fought a couple of DNAliens.
* In *[Be-Knighted](/wiki/Be-Knighted "Be-Knighted")*, Humungousaur scared [Squire](/wiki/Squire "Squire") into telling him the truth about the [Dragon](/wiki/Dragon "Dragon").
	+ The same scene reoccurred on the slideshow presentation in *[Pet Project](/wiki/Pet_Project "Pet Project")*.
* In *[Paradox](/wiki/Paradox "Paradox")*, Humungousaur saved [Hugo](/wiki/Hugo "Hugo") from getting [sucked](/wiki/Trans-Dimensional_Monster "Trans-Dimensional Monster") into [Professor Paradox](/wiki/Professor_Paradox "Professor Paradox")'s [time machine](/wiki/Entropy_Pump "Entropy Pump") and changed the present.
* In *[Darkstar Rising](/wiki/Darkstar_Rising "Darkstar Rising")*, Humungousaur defeated a Highbreed before having his powers drained by [Darkstar](/wiki/Michael_Morningstar_(Classic) "Michael Morningstar (Classic)"). Later, Humungousaur defeated Darkstar.
* In *[Good Copy, Bad Copy](/wiki/Good_Copy,_Bad_Copy "Good Copy, Bad Copy")*, Humungousaur appeared thrice. Firstly, [Negative Humungousaur](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") battled the [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights") to find about [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s location. Later, Ben as Humungousaur fought Negative [Jetray](/wiki/Jetray_(Classic) "Jetray (Classic)"). Finally, Negative Humungousaur defeated [Goop](/wiki/Goop_(Classic) "Goop (Classic)"), but was defeated by [Swampfire](/wiki/Swampfire "Swampfire"), forcing the former to switch into Negative [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill "Big Chill").
* In *[Grounded](/wiki/Grounded "Grounded")*, Humungousaur saved [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") from a Highbreed and was nearly defeated by the latter before [Carl](/wiki/Carl_Tennyson "Carl Tennyson") and [Sandra](/wiki/Sandra_Tennyson "Sandra Tennyson") arrived to save him.
	+ The same scene reoccurred in a flashback in *[The Ultimate Enemy: Part 2](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Enemy:_Part_2 "The Ultimate Enemy: Part 2")*.
* In *[Unearthed](/wiki/Unearthed "Unearthed")*, Humungousaur fought [Tiny](/wiki/Tiny "Tiny").
* In *[Voided](/wiki/Voided "Voided")*, Humungousaur was defeated by [D'Void](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)"), so he switched into [Brainstorm](/wiki/Brainstorm "Brainstorm").
* In *[Inside Man](/wiki/Inside_Man "Inside Man")*, Humungousaur helped Gwen pull the Oscillator Key, but then had to protect it from a DNAlien ship.
* In *[War of the Worlds: Part 2](/wiki/War_of_the_Worlds:_Part_2 "War of the Worlds: Part 2")*, Humungousaur broke himself, [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)"), Gwen, and Kevin out of a prison on [Augstaka](/wiki/Augstaka "Augstaka"). He then confronted the [Highbreed Councilors](/wiki/Highbreed_Councilors "Highbreed Councilors") and figured out a way to help them.
* In ***[Ben 10: Alien Swarm](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Swarm "Ben 10: Alien Swarm")***, Humungousaur defeated a large swarm of [Nanochips](/wiki/Nanochip "Nanochip").
* In *[Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 2](/wiki/Vengeance_of_Vilgax:_Part_2 "Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 2")*, Humungousaur was defeated by [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)").
* In *[Don't Fear the Repo](/wiki/Don%27t_Fear_the_Repo "Don't Fear the Repo")*, Humungousaur showed [Judge Domstol](/wiki/Judge_Domstol "Judge Domstol") the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")'s ability to transform Ben into aliens.

Humungousaur is able to use his incredible strength to smash open gates, pick up a tractor and throw it at a wall, remove a massive metallic lid and activate pressure-sensitive platforms by standing on them. Humungousaur is also immune to freezing weaponry.


Humungousaur grows to full size at segments of the A Change in the Weather level to destroy three [Highbreed Weather Arrays](/wiki/Highbreed_Weather_Array "Highbreed Weather Array").


Humungousaur is vital for progression on the Rural Rumble, Running on Autopilot, and A Change in the Weather levels of the game.



#### Nintendo DS Version


Humungousaur and Swampfire are the only aliens available on Ben's arsenal at the beginning of the game.


Humungousaur is able to break down weak walls.


Humungousaur is vital for progression on the Warehouse 2-3 level of the game.



### [Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks](/wiki/Ben_10_Alien_Force:_Vilgax_Attacks "Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks")


Humungousaur is a playable alien character in the game.



#### Xbox 360, Nintendo Wii, PlayStation 2, and PlayStation Portable Versions


Due to the Omnitrix rebooting after Ben's abuse of the quick-change feature during his battle with an animated [Mr. Smoothy](/wiki/Mr._Smoothy "Mr. Smoothy") mascot, Humungousaur, along with [Swampfire](/wiki/Swampfire "Swampfire"), [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill "Big Chill"), [Spidermonkey](/wiki/Spidermonkey_(Classic) "Spidermonkey (Classic)"), and [Cannonbolt](/wiki/Cannonbolt_(Classic) "Cannonbolt (Classic)") were the only aliens available on Ben's arsenal upon his arrival on [Vulpin](/wiki/Vulpin "Vulpin").


Humungousaur can use his incredible strength to destroy obstacles, such as vehicles and weak walls, open partially sealed gates, drag large crates to activate pressure-sensitive platforms, and pull levers for various purposes. He can also do a shoulder charge and make shockwaves by slamming the ground.


Earthquake is an unlockable combo move for Humungousaur.


Humungousaur is vital for progression on the [Bellwood](/wiki/Bellwood_(Classic) "Bellwood (Classic)"), Terradino, [Encephalonus IV](/wiki/Encephalonus_IV "Encephalonus IV"), [Anur Phaetos](/wiki/Anur_Phaetos_(Classic) "Anur Phaetos (Classic)"), [MorOtesi](/wiki/MorOtesi "MorOtesi"), and [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)") levels of the game.

Humungousaur is able to destroy temple columns, pillars, and other rock formations.


Humungousaur is vital for progression on the Terradino, Encephalonus IV, and Null Void levels of the game.

Etymology
---------


Humungousaur's name is a portmanteau of "humongous," meaning "big," referencing his size, and "dinosaur," referencing his appearance.



Trivia
------


* Humungousaur's voice has changed over the course of his appearances in the Classic Timeline:
	+ In *[Ben 10 Returns: Part 2](/wiki/Ben_10_Returns:_Part_2 "Ben 10 Returns: Part 2")*, *[All That Glitters](/wiki/All_That_Glitters "All That Glitters")*, and *[Inside Man](/wiki/Inside_Man "Inside Man")*, he had a hoarse voice with a graveling effect that was electronically edited to sound deeper.
	+ In the rest of his **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")** appearances and throughout **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, his voice did not have any electronic edits.
	+ In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, his voice is deeper and clearer.
* Humungousaur is the last alien used by Ben with the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)").
* Humungousaur is the first alien used on-screen in **Ultimate Alien**, as well as the first alien to go ultimate on-screen.[[27]](#cite_note-34)
* As a running gag in **Omniverse**'s Arc 1, Ben had many unsuccessful attempts in transforming into Humungousaur until *[Of Predators and Prey: Part 2](/wiki/Of_Predators_and_Prey:_Part_2 "Of Predators and Prey: Part 2")*.
* Out of all transformations, Humungousaur is the alien used by most characters other than Ben, having been used by [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo"), [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)"), Vilgax's [Bioids](/wiki/Bioids "Bioids"), [Plant Ben](/wiki/Plant_Clones "Plant Clones"), [Alpha](/wiki/Alpha "Alpha"), and [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)").
	+ Humungousaur is also the most used transformation in the entire Classic Timeline, appearing a total of 89 times.
* Humungousaur never used his size alteration powers in **Omniverse** because no one from the crew ever brought it up.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-35)
* From Arc 4 of **Omniverse** onwards, Humungousaur is replaced with 16-year-old [Blitzwolfer](/wiki/Blitzwolfer "Blitzwolfer") in the opening intro of the show.
* Humungousaur is [Ryan Kelley](/wiki/Ryan_Kelley "Ryan Kelley")'s favorite alien.[[AC 1]](#cite_note-36)
* While [Dee Bradley Baker](/wiki/Dee_Bradley_Baker "Dee Bradley Baker") likes Humungousaur,[[AC 2]](#cite_note-37) he does not like doing the alien's voice because it is hard on his throat.[[DM 5]](#cite_note-38)[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-39)[[AC 3]](#cite_note-40)
* During the pre-production stage of **Alien Force**, Humungousaur had concept art drawn by Eric Canete.[[EC 1]](#cite_note-41)
* Humungousaur was originally supposed to appear a second time in *[It's Not Easy Being Gwen](/wiki/It%27s_Not_Easy_Being_Gwen "It's Not Easy Being Gwen")* to defeat [Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)").[[JHK 1]](#cite_note-42) His role was taken by [Ultimate Cannonbolt](/wiki/Ultimate_Cannonbolt "Ultimate Cannonbolt") in the final version of the episode.
* The sash Humungousaur wears in **Omniverse** is similar to that of [Monsieur Mallah](https://teentitans.fandom.com/wiki/Monsieur_Mallah "w:c:teentitans:Monsieur Mallah")'s from **Teen Titans**.
