Appearance
----------


Jetray is a red, humanoid [manta ray](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manta_ray "wikipedia:Manta ray")-like alien with green eyes, black outlines on his face, yellow horns extending from his nose, gills underneath his arms, yellow patagia which he uses to fly and glide through water, and two black lightning bolt-like stripes coming down from his shoulders to the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In *[Video Games](/wiki/Video_Games "Video Games")*, Jetray wore a blue motion capture suit.


In *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, the black stripes on Jetray's body are straight rather than lightning-bolted.

1/13
Jetray is [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s fastest flier as he can fly at supersonic speeds. He can maneuver through the air with little to no flapping and accelerates to great speeds with ease. A side effect of this high speed flight creates a sonic boom. 


Jetray is capable of surviving and maneuvering through space,[[3]](#cite_note-3) a task which no amount of flapping would allow. It would appear that he has an additional key to flight beyond his wings. In proportion to his size, he has a very short wingspan, although his weight is unknown.


Jetray can fire green neuroshock blasts from his eyes and tail. These blasts are strong enough to knock down a [Pyronite](/wiki/Pyronite_(Classic) "Pyronite (Classic)")[[4]](#cite_note-ETAtW-4) and an [Amperi](/wiki/Amperi "Amperi"),[[5]](#cite_note-TRoAE-5) and even cause great discomfort to a [Vaxasaurian](/wiki/Vaxasaurian_(Classic) "Vaxasaurian (Classic)")[[6]](#cite_note-GCBC-6) and destroy a [Sonorosian](/wiki/Sonorosian "Sonorosian") with just one shot.[[7]](#cite_note-7)


Jetray is durable enough to withstand multiple blows from Vilgax and gunfire from a fighter jet.[[8]](#cite_note-Fame-8) He is also heat resistant and immune to radiation, as he could withstand a [Prypiatosian-B](/wiki/Prypiatosian-B "Prypiatosian-B")'s radioactive blasts[[9]](#cite_note-THtH-9) and heat generated by a Pyronite.[[4]](#cite_note-ETAtW-4)


Jetray can safely fly through the [Earth](/wiki/Earth "Earth")'s atmosphere and into space, and travel on an interstellar level. When he is done flying, he can land on his two feet perfectly.[[10]](#cite_note-EotB-10)


Jetray has enhanced strength, which he demonstrated in his battles with [Alan](/wiki/Alan_Albright "Alan Albright")[[4]](#cite_note-ETAtW-4) and [Ultimate Kevin](/wiki/Ultimate_Kevin "Ultimate Kevin").[[11]](#cite_note-TEoME-11) His enhanced strength is enough to stop a spaceship mid-air.[[12]](#cite_note-12)


Jetray's strength extends to his feet, which are prehensile, as he is able to carry a Vaxasaurian in the air with his feet without being tired or weighed down.[[6]](#cite_note-GCBC-6)


In addition to his feet, his tail is also prehensile, as shown when Jetray carried [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") while flying up in the air.[[13]](#cite_note-13)


Jetray can enter hyperspace.[[10]](#cite_note-EotB-10)[[note 1]](#cite_note-17)[[note 2]](#cite_note-19)


Jetray is highly adaptable underwater.[[14]](#cite_note-20)[[15]](#cite_note-21)[[16]](#cite_note-Fused-22)


Jetray's neuroshock blasts are capable of starting fires, as demonstrated by [Swift](/wiki/Swift "Swift").[[17]](#cite_note-WXIP1-23)


Jetray is immune to electricity, such as that generated by an [Amperi](/wiki/Amperi "Amperi").[[16]](#cite_note-Fused-22)[[5]](#cite_note-TRoAE-5)

1/10
Jetray's neuroshock blasts can hurt himself, as seen when they struck him after being deflected by the [Stalker](/wiki/Stalker "Stalker").[[18]](#cite_note-24)


Jetray's neuroshock blasts can be absorbed by either [Prypiatosian-Bs](/wiki/Prypiatosian-B "Prypiatosian-B")[[9]](#cite_note-THtH-9) or the plasma generated by members of [Ball Weevil](/wiki/Ball_Weevil "Ball Weevil")'s [species](/wiki/Ball_Weevil%27s_Species "Ball Weevil's Species"), the latter of which has been demonstrated with Swift.[[5]](#cite_note-TRoAE-5)


Despite his enhanced durability, Jetray can still feel discomfort when he is being fired at.[[8]](#cite_note-Fame-8)


Despite his electricity immunity, Jetray can still be thrown into the air by a strong enough electric blast.[[16]](#cite_note-Fused-22)


Jetray is vulnerable to strong winds.[[11]](#cite_note-TEoME-11)


Jetray's neuroshock blasts are useless against certain non-living targets, such as mechanical-like species like [Talpaedans](/wiki/Talpaedan "Talpaedan")[[19]](#cite_note-25) and robots like [Computron](/wiki/Computron "Computron")'s [minions](/wiki/Computron%27s_Minions "Computron's Minions").[[20]](#cite_note-26)


Jetray cannot walk very well due to his proportionally thin hind limbs.


Jetray can be ensnared by an [Arachnichimp](/wiki/Arachnichimp "Arachnichimp")'s webs.[[6]](#cite_note-GCBC-6)


On the ground, Jetray is slower than [Kinecelerans](/wiki/Kineceleran_(Classic) "Kineceleran (Classic)").[[DM 1]](#cite_note-27)


Jetray cannot fly in [Legerdomain](/wiki/Legerdomain "Legerdomain") due to its sky being unparallel to [Earth](/wiki/Earth "Earth")'s.[[21]](#cite_note-28)[[22]](#cite_note-WtMH-29)


Jetray is vulnerable to magic, as shown when he was incapacitated by one of [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)")'s spells.[[22]](#cite_note-WtMH-29)



History
-------


### Alien Force


* Jetray first appeared in *[Everybody Talks About the Weather](/wiki/Everybody_Talks_About_the_Weather "Everybody Talks About the Weather")*, where he defeated [Alan](/wiki/Alan_Albright "Alan Albright") and discovered some [suspicious-looking crop circles](/wiki/Highbreed_Weather_Array "Highbreed Weather Array") while fighting with the latter in the air.
* In *[All That Glitters](/wiki/All_That_Glitters "All That Glitters")*, Jetray was defeated by [Michael Morningstar](/wiki/Michael_Morningstar_(Classic) "Michael Morningstar (Classic)").
* In *[Pier Pressure](/wiki/Pier_Pressure "Pier Pressure")*, Jetray pursued [Julie](/wiki/Julie_Yamamoto "Julie Yamamoto") who had been taken captive by [Ship](/wiki/Ship "Ship"). After finding them, Jetray revealed his secret to Julie.
* In *[Max Out](/wiki/Max_Out "Max Out")*, Jetray battled some [DNAliens](/wiki/DNAliens "DNAliens") and destroyed some of their trucks.
* In *[What Are Little Girls Made Of?](/wiki/What_Are_Little_Girls_Made_Of%3F "What Are Little Girls Made Of?")*, Jetray was defeated by [Verdona](/wiki/Verdona "Verdona").
* In *[Paradox](/wiki/Paradox "Paradox")*, Jetray fought the [Trans-Dimensional Monster](/wiki/Trans-Dimensional_Monster "Trans-Dimensional Monster").
* In *[Darkstar Rising](/wiki/Darkstar_Rising "Darkstar Rising")*, Jetray briefly battled Darkstar before turning into [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)").
* In *[Good Copy, Bad Copy](/wiki/Good_Copy,_Bad_Copy "Good Copy, Bad Copy")*, Jetray appeared thrice. Firstly, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Jetray flew away from a Forever Knight castle. Later, Negative Jetray battled Humungousaur. Lastly, Ben as Jetray fought [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") [Echo Echo](/wiki/Echo_Echo "Echo Echo") but was trapped by Negative [Spidermonkey](/wiki/Spidermonkey_(Classic) "Spidermonkey (Classic)"), forcing Jetray to switch into [Chromastone](/wiki/Chromastone_(Classic) "Chromastone (Classic)").
* In *[Save the Last Dance](/wiki/Save_the_Last_Dance "Save the Last Dance")*, Jetray briefly appeared before the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") forcibly changed him straight into [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill "Big Chill").
* In *[Birds of a Feather](/wiki/Birds_of_a_Feather "Birds of a Feather")*, Jetray saved [Lu](/wiki/Lu "Lu") from falling to his death.
* In *[Pet Project](/wiki/Pet_Project "Pet Project")*, Jetray flew to where Gwen, [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)"), and Julie were.
* In *[Inside Man](/wiki/Inside_Man "Inside Man")*, Jetray battled some DNAliens to get the Oscillator Key, but was unable to destroy it when he decided to rescue [Tyler](/wiki/Tyler "Tyler") instead.
* In *[War of the Worlds: Part 1](/wiki/War_of_the_Worlds:_Part_1 "War of the Worlds: Part 1")*, Jetray battled an army of DNAliens before switching into [Swampfire](/wiki/Swampfire "Swampfire").
* In *[Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 1](/wiki/Vengeance_of_Vilgax:_Part_1 "Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 1")*, Jetray flew to help Gwen and Kevin fight [Ssserpent](/wiki/Ssserpent "Ssserpent") before switching into [Cannonbolt](/wiki/Cannonbolt_(Classic) "Cannonbolt (Classic)").
* In *[Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 2](/wiki/Vengeance_of_Vilgax:_Part_2 "Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 2")*, Jetray was defeated by [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)"), so he switched into Big Chill.
* In *[Inferno](/wiki/Inferno "Inferno")*, Jetray destroyed a bomb and escaped the explosion.
* In *[Don't Fear the Repo](/wiki/Don%27t_Fear_the_Repo "Don't Fear the Repo")*, Jetray battled the [Vreedle Brothers](/wiki/Vreedle_Brothers "Vreedle Brothers") before transforming into Echo Echo.
* In *[Simple](/wiki/Simple "Simple")*, Jetray flew down to confront the red troops
* In *[Singlehanded](/wiki/Singlehanded "Singlehanded")*, Jetray escaped some [Vulpimancers](/wiki/Vulpimancer "Vulpimancer") in the [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)") and went to look for [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)")'s old lair.
* In *[If All Else Fails](/wiki/If_All_Else_Fails "If All Else Fails")*, Jetray hyperspace-traveled to [Augstaka](/wiki/Augstaka "Augstaka") (offscreen) to bring [Reinrassig III](/wiki/Reinrassig_III "Reinrassig III") to [Earth](/wiki/Earth "Earth"). Later, Jetray flew towards the [Highbreed Tree Monster](/wiki/Highbreed_Tree_Monster "Highbreed Tree Monster") to get inside of it.
* In *[Trade Off](/wiki/Trade_Off "Trade Off")*, Jetray flew with Gwen on a mission to stop the [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights").
* In *[The Secret of Chromastone](/wiki/The_Secret_of_Chromastone "The Secret of Chromastone")*, Jetray fought Vilgax until he reverted.
* In *[The Final Battle: Part 1](/wiki/The_Final_Battle:_Part_1 "The Final Battle: Part 1")*, Jetray destroyed some asteroids, but failed to destroy a giant one, forcing him to retreat to Ship. Later, Jetray flew to [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo")'s lair.
* In *[The Final Battle: Part 2](/wiki/The_Final_Battle:_Part_2 "The Final Battle: Part 2")*, Jetray escaped from Vilgax and the explosion caused by the [Chimeran Hammer](/wiki/Chimeran_Hammer_(Classic) "Chimeran Hammer (Classic)").


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Fame](/wiki/Fame "Fame")*, Jetray was seen on TV. Later, Jetray battled some Air Force planes before saving one of the pilots.
* In *[Video Games](/wiki/Video_Games "Video Games")*, Jetray was scanned by [Oliver Thompson](/wiki/Oliver_Thompson "Oliver Thompson") for the video game. Later, Jetray saved a family in a minivan from the [Stalker](/wiki/Stalker "Stalker") before being defeated by it.
* In *[Too Hot to Handle](/wiki/Too_Hot_to_Handle "Too Hot to Handle")*, Jetray saved [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") and fought [P'andor](/wiki/P%27andor "P'andor") before switching into [Water Hazard](/wiki/Water_Hazard "Water Hazard").
* In *[Andreas' Fault](/wiki/Andreas%27_Fault "Andreas' Fault")*, Jetray entered a [Forever Knight](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights") castle, where he encountered [Argit](/wiki/Argit "Argit") and [Andreas](/wiki/Andreas "Andreas") and scanned the latter's DNA.
* In *[Fused](/wiki/Fused "Fused")*, Jetray defeated [Ra'ad](/wiki/Ra%27ad "Ra'ad").
* In *[Hero Time](/wiki/Hero_Time "Hero Time")*, Jetray failed to defeat [Computron](/wiki/Computron "Computron")'s [minions](/wiki/Computron%27s_Minions "Computron's Minions"), so he switched into [Armodrillo](/wiki/Armodrillo "Armodrillo").
* In *[Where the Magic Happens](/wiki/Where_the_Magic_Happens "Where the Magic Happens")*, Jetray tried to fight some [stone creatures](/wiki/Stone_Creatures "Stone Creatures") in [Legerdomain](/wiki/Legerdomain "Legerdomain"), but was unable to fly, so he switched into [Cannonbolt](/wiki/Cannonbolt_(Classic) "Cannonbolt (Classic)") instead.
* In *[The Enemy of My Enemy](/wiki/The_Enemy_of_My_Enemy "The Enemy of My Enemy")*, Jetray battled [Ultimate Kevin](/wiki/Ultimate_Kevin "Ultimate Kevin") on the [Rust Bucket 3](/wiki/Rust_Bucket_3 "Rust Bucket 3").
* In *[Eye of the Beholder](/wiki/Eye_of_the_Beholder "Eye of the Beholder")*, Jetray went into hyperspace to meet up with Gwen, [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)"), [Julie](/wiki/Julie_Yamamoto "Julie Yamamoto"), and [Ship](/wiki/Ship "Ship").
* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, Jetray destroyed the [Hands of Armageddon](/wiki/Hands_of_Armageddon "Hands of Armageddon"), which resulted in [Eon](/wiki/Eon "Eon")'s defeat.
* In *[It's Not Easy Being Gwen](/wiki/It%27s_Not_Easy_Being_Gwen "It's Not Easy Being Gwen")*, Jetray went to Gwen's house to get Kevin so the latter could help him fight the [Mutant Frog](/wiki/Mutant_Frog "Mutant Frog").
* In *[The Perfect Girlfriend](/wiki/The_Perfect_Girlfriend "The Perfect Girlfriend")*, Jetray went to fight [Ssserpent](/wiki/Ssserpent "Ssserpent") only to find his shedded skin instead.
* In *[The Mother of All Vreedles](/wiki/The_Mother_of_All_Vreedles "The Mother of All Vreedles")*, Jetray flew to the Rust Bucket 3 to meet with Gwen and Kevin.
* In *[Ben 10/Generator Rex: Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, Jetray appeared as one of the many aliens when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was explaining his story to [Rex](/wiki/Rex_Salazar "Rex Salazar").
* In *[Inspector #13](/wiki/Inspector_13_(Episode) "Inspector 13 (Episode)")*, the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") turned Kevin into Jetray.
* In *[The Eggman Cometh](/wiki/The_Eggman_Cometh "The Eggman Cometh")*, Jetray flew around with the evolution ray on his chest, evolving all of the [mutant chickens](/wiki/Mutant_Chickens "Mutant Chickens") into normal chickens and unknowingly evolving the [Bellwood Sheriff](/wiki/Sheriff_of_Bellwood "Sheriff of Bellwood")'s intelligence to superhuman levels.
* In *[Couples Retreat](/wiki/Couples_Retreat "Couples Retreat")*, Jetray was defeated by [Darkstar](/wiki/Michael_Morningstar_(Classic) "Michael Morningstar (Classic)") twice.


### Future (Ultimate Alien)


* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, [Ultimate Ben](/wiki/Ultimate_Ben "Ultimate Ben") used a combination of Jetray's flight and strength to stop the Rust Bucket 3 from crash-landing.

### [Ben 10: Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force_(Video_Game) "Ben 10: Alien Force (Video Game)")


Jetray is a playable alien in the Nintendo Wii, PlayStation 2, and PlayStation Portable versions of the game.


Jetray is initially unavailable, but is unlocked at the beginning of the Bombs Away! level.


Jetray is able to use his sonic dash to quickly cross long gaps. He can also dive underwater and move freely, something that the rest of Ben's aliens and Ben himself are unable to do.


Jetray is used at the end of the A Few Bad Eggs level to escape the collapsing facility underneath Parkville.


Jetray is vital for progression on the A Few Bad Eggs, Rural Rumble, Running on Autopilot, and A Change in the Weather levels of the game.



### [Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks](/wiki/Ben_10_Alien_Force:_Vilgax_Attacks "Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks")


Jetray is a playable alien in the game.



#### Xbox 360, Nintendo Wii, PlayStation 2, and PlayStation Portable Versions


Due to the Omnitrix rebooting after Ben's abuse of the quick-change feature during his battle with an animated [Mr. Smoothy](/wiki/Mr._Smoothy "Mr. Smoothy") mascot, Jetray was temporarily locked until Ben's arrival in the [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)").


Jetray is able to glide and ride on wind currents to temporarily increase his altitude like [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill "Big Chill"). However, he can do so for considerably longer distances and with greater speed than Big Chill.


Tri Shot is an unlockable combo move for Jetray.


Jetray is vital for progression on the Null Void level of the game.



#### Nintendo DS Version


Jetray and [Murk Upchuck](/wiki/Upchuck "Upchuck") are reunlocked upon Ben's arrival in the Null Void.


Jetray is able to use his sonic dash to quickly cross long gaps in the Null Void.



### [Ben 10 Alien Force: The Rise of Hex](/wiki/Ben_10_Alien_Force:_The_Rise_of_Hex "Ben 10 Alien Force: The Rise of Hex")


Jetray is a playable alien in the game.


Due to the Omnitrix sorting out after Ben fiddled with it, Jetray was temporarily locked until the beginning of Level 9.


Jetray is able to double jump and use his sonic dash to quickly cross long gaps.


Jetray is the last alien used in the game, in order to escape the collapsing catacombs after the battle with [Hex](/wiki/Hex_(Classic) "Hex (Classic)").

Etymology
---------


Jetray's name is a portmanteau of a "[jet](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jet_aircraft "wikipedia:Jet aircraft")", a fast aircraft vehicle, referencing his enhanced speed, and "manta ray", the creature his appearance is based on. Additionally, he can fire neuroshock rays, adding to the "ray" part of his name.



Trivia
------


* Jetray had concept art drawn by Eric Canete,[[EC 1]](#cite_note-30) whereas his final design was created by Glenn Wong, with color styling by Chris Hooten.[[GW 1]](#cite_note-31)
* Jetray's voice was somewhat high-pitched until *[Paradox](/wiki/Paradox "Paradox")*, where it became deeper.
* [Dwayne McDuffie](/wiki/Dwayne_McDuffie "Dwayne McDuffie") wrote an underwater battle scene for Jetray, but it was eventually scrapped.[[DM 2]](#cite_note-32)
* In *[Fame](/wiki/Fame "Fame")*, [Jimmy](/wiki/Jimmy_Jones "Jimmy Jones") thought Jetray's name was "Jeffrey" until Ben corrected him, similar to how a kid at Ben's school called [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)") "Quad Hands" until he was corrected by Ben in *[Goodbye and Good Riddance](/wiki/Goodbye_and_Good_Riddance "Goodbye and Good Riddance")*.
* Even though Jetray is more adaptive in water, Ben mostly uses him for aerial fights or traveling through space.
* Jetray is the only regular alien featured in both **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")** and **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")** to not be used in **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**. However, he was mentioned in *[Tummy Trouble](/wiki/Tummy_Trouble "Tummy Trouble")*, *[Weapon XI: Part 1](/wiki/Weapon_XI:_Part_1 "Weapon XI: Part 1")*, and *[The Color of Monkey](/wiki/The_Color_of_Monkey "The Color of Monkey")*.
	+ Had Jetray physically appeared in **Omniverse**, [Dee Bradley Baker](/wiki/Dee_Bradley_Baker "Dee Bradley Baker") would have reprised his role.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-33)
* It was initially rumored that, because [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") disliked Jetray's design and thought he was "overused" during previous series, he decided to create [Astrodactyl](/wiki/Astrodactyl "Astrodactyl") as Jetray's "replacement" in **Omniverse**. Derrick, however, denied these rumors, saying that the crew never intended or even wanted to replace any aliens.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-34)[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-35)[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-36)
	+ Elaborating on the subject of Jetray's absence, Derrick stated that the real reason he was excluded from **Omniverse** was because he could not figure out how he wanted Jetray to look or how to put clothes on him.[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-37)
	+ Derrick eventually drew 15 and 16-year-old versions of Jetray in the **Omniverse** art style as part of a GoFundMe in 2020.[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-38)
* [Ben 23](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Dimension_23) "Ben Tennyson (Dimension 23)") does not have his own version of Jetray in his [Omnitrix](/wiki/Hero_Watch "Hero Watch")[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-39) but if he did, then the transformation's name would be Fish Kite.[[DJW 10]](#cite_note-40)
* Jetray is the final alien used onscreen in **Alien Force**.
