### Ben as Goop


Being a 200-pound,[[merch 1]](#cite_note-AFDS-2) single celled-organism[[DM 1]](#cite_note-3) that is neither liquid nor solid but a colloidal suspension,[[DM 2]](#cite_note-4) Goop is a humanoid pile of goo that can take any shape at will. Goop has bright green eyes and an [Anti-Gravity Projector](/wiki/Anti-Gravity_Projector_(Classic) "Anti-Gravity Projector (Classic)") that allows him to be mobile in [Earth](/wiki/Earth_(Classic) "Earth (Classic)")'s gravity and also projects his voice. His brain is spread throughout his body.[[DM 3]](#cite_note-link-5)


In **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")** and **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Goop wore the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, the black outlines around Goop's eyes were thicker.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Goop has a nose-like beak and an amoeba-like shape. His Anti-Gravity Projector has been redesigned and the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol is on the top of it.



### Albedo as Negative Goop


In **Alien Force**, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Goop looked exactly like [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s Goop.


In **Omniverse**, Negative Goop looks mostly the same as Ben's Goop except his body, eyes, and the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") on his Anti-Gravity Projector are recolored red.

1/23
Goop is indestructible, making it difficult to defeat him by attacking his body.


Goop is able to generate and shoot slime from any part of his body. The slime he generates can either be acidic[[2]](#cite_note-GCBC-6) or adhesive.[[3]](#cite_note-AaB-7) He can shoot it in continuous streams similar to a water hose[[2]](#cite_note-GCBC-6) or in blob-like projectiles.[[3]](#cite_note-AaB-7) He is even capable of spitting out either slime or acid despite having no visible mouth.[[3]](#cite_note-AaB-7)[[4]](#cite_note-8)


Goop is capable of making himself[[5]](#cite_note-9)[[6]](#cite_note-Primus-10) and his touch acidic at will.[[7]](#cite_note-11)[[8]](#cite_note-12) His acid is also able to dissolve a large variety of materials.


Goop can stretch, bend and deform himself into any shape or form.


Due to being made of slime, Goop can hold objects inside his body. He can also do this with individuals to protect them.[[9]](#cite_note-13)


Goop can produce multiple slime tentacles from his body to grab multiple opponents.[[10]](#cite_note-TBotE-14)


Goop can regenerate and rebuild himself with ease.


Goop possesses enhanced strength and can increase the impact power of his attacks by stretching himself backwards and using the recoil to launch enemies with a single punch.[[11]](#cite_note-IaMBW1-15)


Goop can breathe underwater, as seen when he was in the sewer waiting to ambush Ben.[[12]](#cite_note-VoVP2-16)


Goop can change his density depending on his desire.[[DM 4]](#cite_note-17)


Goop can pass under an enemy's foot in order to make them slip.[[13]](#cite_note-18)[[10]](#cite_note-TBotE-14)


Goop can stick and scale on walls without difficultly.


Goop can survive in intense heat, such as the one on his home planet [Viscosia](/wiki/Viscosia_(Classic) "Viscosia (Classic)").[[merch 1]](#cite_note-AFDS-2)


Goop can mimic the shape of simple objects to fool an opponent.[[merch 2]](#cite_note-19)


Similar to [Ghostfreak](/wiki/Ghostfreak "Ghostfreak"), Goop has movable eyes.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-20)



### Equipment


Goop's Anti-Gravity Projector allows him to change his shape, form, length, width, and height at will, as well as granting Goop the ability to fly and levitate. If Goop loses his Anti-Gravity Projector, the Omnitrix/Ultimatrix can immediately generate a new one.[[DM 5]](#cite_note-21)


With his Anti-Gravity Projector, Goop has enough strength to dismantle a [Techadon robot](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots") from the inside[[14]](#cite_note-22) and effortlessly subdue [Magister Prior Gilhil](/wiki/Magister_Prior_Gilhil "Magister Prior Gilhil"), someone who could easily overpower an armored [Osmosian](/wiki/Osmosian "Osmosian").[[15]](#cite_note-23) Goop has even defeated both [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") and [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") in combat with both his strength and slime.


Goop can control and launch his Anti-Gravity Projector at enemies to attack them due to the edges of it being razor-sharp. In fact, if Goop is fast enough with his Anti-Gravity Projector, he can temporarily prevent a [Methanosian](/wiki/Methanosian "Methanosian") from regenerating.[[12]](#cite_note-VoVP2-16)


Thanks to his Anti-Gravity Projector, Goop is capable of high-speed flight, being able to easily evade [Trombipulor](/wiki/Trombipulor "Trombipulor")'s shots.[[16]](#cite_note-BaDGtMS-24)

1/10
Goop is limited to his Anti-Gravity Projector controlling his body. If it is separated from him by more than two feet or turned off, Goop's body collapses into an immobilized puddle until it recollects it. He can only struggle to move since he cannot change shape or make appendages to move without his Anti-Gravity Projector.


Goop's Anti-Gravity Projector can be disabled by a shot from a Positron Blaster, which makes him spin around extremely quickly and then separate into multiple parts.[[11]](#cite_note-IaMBW1-15)


Goop's Anti-Gravity Projector can be switched off.[[6]](#cite_note-Primus-10)


Goop can be hurt if he is spun really fast in a centrifuge, which will cause him to begin separating into his constituent compounds.[[17]](#cite_note-25)


While running, Goop is not very fast and cannot outrun [humans](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)").[[18]](#cite_note-Deep-26)


Goop cannot regenerate if he is being continuously attacked.[[2]](#cite_note-GCBC-6)[[10]](#cite_note-TBotE-14)


Goop is vulnerable to being blown away by a [Geochelone Aerio](/wiki/Geochelone_Aerio "Geochelone Aerio")'s aerokinesis.[[19]](#cite_note-27)


Goop is vulnerable to suction, as shown when he got sucked into Trombipulor's nose.[[16]](#cite_note-BaDGtMS-24)


Goop is vulnerable to an [Evolved Galvan](/wiki/Evolved_Galvan "Evolved Galvan")'s energy beams.[[20]](#cite_note-28)


Goop can be affected by a [Nemuina](/wiki/Nemuina "Nemuina")'s sleeping dust.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-29)


Goop is vulnerable to being hypnotized.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-30)

### Alien Force


* Goop first appeared in *[The Gauntlet](/wiki/The_Gauntlet "The Gauntlet")*, where he defeated a [Techadon robot](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots").
* In *[Darkstar Rising](/wiki/Darkstar_Rising "Darkstar Rising")*, Goop defeated [Magister Prior Gilhil](/wiki/Magister_Prior_Gilhil "Magister Prior Gilhil").
* In *[Good Copy, Bad Copy](/wiki/Good_Copy,_Bad_Copy "Good Copy, Bad Copy")*, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Goop hid from [the team](/wiki/Alien_Force "Alien Force") in a computer store and scared many customers away in the process. Later, [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") as Goop battled Negative [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)") before switching into [Swampfire](/wiki/Swampfire "Swampfire").
* In *[Undercover](/wiki/Undercover "Undercover")*, Goop saved [Cooper](/wiki/Cooper "Cooper") from some [DNAliens](/wiki/DNAliens "DNAliens").
* In *[Birds of a Feather](/wiki/Birds_of_a_Feather "Birds of a Feather")*, Goop helped [Simian](/wiki/Simian "Simian") escape from some guards.
* In *[Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 2](/wiki/Vengeance_of_Vilgax:_Part_2 "Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 2")*, Goop escaped from the Omnitrix and hid in the sewers. He fought Swampfire before getting reabsorbed by him.
	+ The same scene reoccurred on TV in *[Fame](/wiki/Fame "Fame")*.
* In *[Don't Fear the Repo](/wiki/Don%27t_Fear_the_Repo "Don't Fear the Repo")*, Goop fought the [Vreedle Brothers](/wiki/Vreedle_Brothers "Vreedle Brothers").
* In *[Simple](/wiki/Simple "Simple")*, Goop was used to avoid getting hurt after being pushed off a cliff.
* In *[Fool's Gold](/wiki/Fool%27s_Gold "Fool's Gold")*, Goop defeated [Mayor Coleman](/wiki/Mayor_Coleman "Mayor Coleman").
* In *[Singlehanded](/wiki/Singlehanded "Singlehanded")*, [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)") turned into Goop to save himself from a bridge collapse while [Julie](/wiki/Julie_Yamamoto "Julie Yamamoto") was using his hand as a weapon, disgusting her.
* In *[In Charm's Way](/wiki/In_Charm%27s_Way "In Charm's Way")*, Goop battled a [stone creature](/wiki/Stone_Creatures "Stone Creatures") until it was sucked into a [magic](/wiki/Magic_(Classic) "Magic (Classic)") vortex.
* In *[Primus](/wiki/Primus_(Episode) "Primus (Episode)")*, Ben turned [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") into Goop and pulled the Omnitrix off his chest, removing the Omnitrix from Vilgax.[[note 1]](#cite_note-32)
* In *[Above and Beyond](/wiki/Above_and_Beyond "Above and Beyond")*, Goop defeated [Pierce](/wiki/Pierce_Wheels "Pierce Wheels").

### [Ben 10: Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force_(Video_Game) "Ben 10: Alien Force (Video Game)")


Goop is a playable alien in the Nintendo DS version of the game.


Goop is initially unavailable, but is unlocked upon completion of the Warehouse 2-1 level.


Goop is able to stretch his limbs, deform his body in various shapes to attack and turn into a pile of slime to evade enemy attacks.


Goop is capable of throwing blobs of goo and spraying acid from his hands. He can also rotate his feet around like helicopter blades to damage enemies.


Goop is not required to be used at all in order to complete the game.



### [Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks](/wiki/Ben_10_Alien_Force:_Vilgax_Attacks "Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks")


Goop is a playable alien in the game.



#### Xbox 360, Nintendo Wii, PlayStation 2, and PlayStation Portable Versions


Due to the Omnitrix rebooting after Ben's abuse of the quick-change feature during his battle with an animated [Mr. Smoothy](/wiki/Mr._Smoothy "Mr. Smoothy") mascot, Goop was temporarily locked until Ben's arrival on [Terradino](/wiki/Terradino_(Classic) "Terradino (Classic)").


Goop has the ability to stick to slime-covered walls and wall jump, thus reaching otherwise inaccessible areas.


Acid Hose is an unlockable combo move for Goop.


Goop is vital for progression on the Terradino, [MorOtesi](/wiki/MorOtesi "MorOtesi"), and [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)") levels of the game.



#### Nintendo DS Version


Only Goop and [Swampfire](/wiki/Swampfire "Swampfire") are available on Ben's arsenal upon his arrival on [Vulpin](/wiki/Vulpin "Vulpin").


Goop is able to safely walk on the acid rain puddles littering Vulpin's surface and is the only alien form capable of defeating [Darkstar](/wiki/Michael_Morningstar_(Classic) "Michael Morningstar (Classic)").



### [Ben 10 Alien Force: The Rise of Hex](/wiki/Ben_10_Alien_Force:_The_Rise_of_Hex "Ben 10 Alien Force: The Rise of Hex")


Goop is a playable alien in the game.


Due to the Omnitrix sorting out after Ben fiddled with it, Goop was temporarily locked until the beginning of Level 10.


Goop is able to turn into a pile of slime to fall through specially designated holes and to slide under incredibly small openings, thereby navigating areas Ben and all of his other alien forms are unable to access.

Trivia
------


* Goop's [Anti-Gravity Projector](/wiki/Anti-Gravity_Projector_(Classic) "Anti-Gravity Projector (Classic)") isn't part of his [Polymorph](/wiki/Polymorph_(Classic) "Polymorph (Classic)") biology; he just has one due to the difference of gravity between [Viscosia](/wiki/Viscosia_(Classic) "Viscosia (Classic)") and [Earth](/wiki/Earth_(Classic) "Earth (Classic)").[[DM 3]](#cite_note-link-5) The projector is created using the same mechanism in which nanomachines break down [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s clothing during his transformations.[[DM 7]](#cite_note-33)
	+ Goop's Anti-Gravity Projector became a substitute for the missing Anti-Gravity Multiplier on the planet of [Piscciss](/wiki/Piscciss_(Classic) "Piscciss (Classic)").[[18]](#cite_note-Deep-26)
	+ It was [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt")'s idea to put Goop's [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his Anti-Gravity Projector in **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**. In his first drawing, the Omnitrix symbol was supposed to be floating inside Goop.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-34)
* Goop is one of the five aliens who escaped from the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)"); the others are [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)"), [Ghostfreak](/wiki/Ghostfreak "Ghostfreak"), [Chromastone](/wiki/Chromastone_(Classic) "Chromastone (Classic)"), and [Spidermonkey](/wiki/Spidermonkey_(Classic) "Spidermonkey (Classic)").
* When Goop speaks in **Omniverse**, a crackle of static is heard at the end of each line as if he is speaking through a walkie-talkie.
	+ Ben believes Goop's voice prevents others from taking him seriously.[[10]](#cite_note-TBotE-14)
* Unlike other aliens, during night time scenes, Goop's eyes are shaded.
* During the pre-production stage of **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")**, Goop had concept art drawn by Eric Canete.[[EC 1]](#cite_note-35)
* Derrick believed that each alternate counterpart of Goop has a different color and flavor: Ben Prime's Goop is green/lime-flavored, Negative Goop is red/raspberry-flavored, [Muck-A-Muck](/wiki/Muck-A-Muck "Muck-A-Muck") is blue/blue-raspberry-flavored,[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-36) [Bad](/wiki/Bad_Ben "Bad Ben") Goop is mint green/licorice-flavored, [Mad](/wiki/Mad_Ben "Mad Ben") Goop is orange/sour orange-flavored, [Nega](/wiki/Nega_Ben "Nega Ben") Goop is grey/black pepper-flavored, [Benzarro](/wiki/Benzarro "Benzarro")'s Goop is purple/grape-flavored,[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-37) [Argitrix](/wiki/Argitrix "Argitrix") Timeline [Argit](/wiki/Argit_(Argitrix_Timeline) "Argit (Argitrix Timeline)")'s Goop is peach/peach-flavored,[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-38) and [Gwen 10](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Gweniverse) "Gwen Tennyson (Gweniverse)")'s is pink/strawberry-flavored.[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-39)[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-40)
