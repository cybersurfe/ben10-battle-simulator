Appearance
----------


Lodestar has a magnetically floating metal head surrounded by a glowing white aura and levitates in an arch between his shoulders. He has crab-like claws for hands (similar to [Brainstorm](/wiki/Brainstorm "Brainstorm")'s) and his body is mainly brownish-black with yellow feet, hands, shoulders, and chest. His body is also made of living alien metal.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-5)


In *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, the yellow parts of Lodestar's body are slightly darker, to be more fitting in **Generator Rex's** artstyle.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Lodestar has a yellow eyebrow-like crest on his forehead, which looks similar to that of [Jetray](/wiki/Jetray_(Classic) "Jetray (Classic)"). His head is more skull-shaped with has lines beneath his mouth. The glowing aura around his head is recolored green, the yellow from his feet go up to his thighs, his shoulders are now larger, and the yellow stripes by his chest are shorter.


Lodestar wears the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)")/[Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his chest.

1/11
Lodestar is able to project magnetic fields that are capable of overloading electrical circuits and attracting magnetic materials to himself. He can also emit magnetic pulses that allow him to magnetize any ferromagnetic metals (iron, nickel and cobalt). This will cause objects magnetized by him to attract everything magnetic before flying towards and colliding with the target, even mechanical beings like [Evolved Sonorosians](/wiki/Evolved_Sonorosian "Evolved Sonorosian")[[4]](#cite_note-6) and [Chronosapiens](/wiki/Chronosapien "Chronosapien").[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-7)


Lodestar can use his magnetic fields to block solar energy attacks.[[3]](#cite_note-Vendetta-3)


Lodestar can use his magnetism to damage and destroy any magnetic substance at will, as seen when he opened the [Naljian Destructor](/wiki/Naljian_Destructor "Naljian Destructor"),[[5]](#cite_note-BB-8) dismantled a [Techadon robot](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots"),[[6]](#cite_note-9) and tore apart the [Toltech Battle Armor](/wiki/Toltech_Battle_Armor "Toltech Battle Armor").[[1]](#cite_note-Duped-1)


Lodestar can generate a green magnetic force field that protects him from most ballistic attacks.[[7]](#cite_note-UA-10)


If Lodestar gets destroyed by something as dangerous as an explosion, he can regenerate by pulling his shattered pieces back together with his magnetism.[[7]](#cite_note-UA-10)


Lodestar has telekinetic control over metal, allowing him to levitate and move it around without physical contact.[[5]](#cite_note-BB-8) He can also use this "magno-telekinesis" to make himself levitate and fly.[[8]](#cite_note-11)


Lodestar possesses enhanced durability, as he was able to withstand being hurt by the red troops' laser blasts,[[9]](#cite_note-Simple-12) being thrown through the walls of a house,[[10]](#cite_note-VG-13) and the [Forever Ninja](/wiki/Forever_Ninja "Forever Ninja")'s throwing stars.[[11]](#cite_note-RtF-14)


Lodestar can use his magnetic abilities to perform electrokinesis.[[5]](#cite_note-BB-8)


Lodestar possesses enhanced strength, as he was able to briefly grapple with the [Stalker](/wiki/Stalker "Stalker") but with some difficulty.[[10]](#cite_note-VG-13) Furthermore, if an [Osmosian](/wiki/Osmosian "Osmosian") ally like [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") absorbs metal, Lodestar can use his magnetism to throw said ally toward their enemies.[[12]](#cite_note-15)[[13]](#cite_note-16)


Lodestar can act as a compass.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-17)


Lodestar can survive in the vacuum of space due to his head not being connected to his body.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-18)



Weaknesses
----------


* ![]()Ineffective Magnetism
* ![]()Vulnerable Head
* ![]()Magnetic Head
* ![]()Seperated Head
1/4
Despite being disconnected from his body, Lodestar's head is magnetically bonded to it. Wherever his head goes, his body follows.[[10]](#cite_note-VG-13) As such, Lodestar's head can be knocked away from his body, leaving the latter blind, helpless, and unable to reconnect with the head.[[14]](#cite_note-19)


Lodestar's head can also be flipped over and spun if something hits it, though he can always touch his head to stop it from moving so rapidly.[[11]](#cite_note-RtF-14)


Lodestar's powers are useless against non-magnetic objects, such as the Forever Ninja's throwing stars.[[11]](#cite_note-RtF-14)


Despite his enhanced durability, Lodestar can still feel discomfort while being fired at.[[9]](#cite_note-Simple-12)



History
-------


### Alien Force


* In *[War of the Worlds: Part 2](/wiki/War_of_the_Worlds:_Part_2 "War of the Worlds: Part 2")*, Lodestar was unlocked onscreen after the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") was reset and [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") immediately transformed into him offscreen.
* Lodestar first appeared in *[Simple](/wiki/Simple "Simple")*, where he defeated the red troops and tried to reason with them.
* In *[Busy Box](/wiki/Busy_Box "Busy Box")*, Lodestar battled the [Naljian Destructor](/wiki/Naljian_Destructor "Naljian Destructor") and ripped it open to release its cube form, but was unable to destroy it when the [Naljian](/wiki/Naljian "Naljian") arrived.
* In *[Primus](/wiki/Primus_(Episode) "Primus (Episode)")*, Lodestar fought and dismantled a red [Techadon robot](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots"), but reverted before he could destroy it.
* In *[Vendetta](/wiki/Vendetta "Vendetta")*, Lodestar battled [Ragnarok](/wiki/Ragnarok "Ragnarok") until the latter escaped.
* In *[The Final Battle: Part 2](/wiki/The_Final_Battle:_Part_2 "The Final Battle: Part 2")*, Lodestar was briefly used by [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)")'s [Bioids](/wiki/Bioids "Bioids").


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Duped](/wiki/Duped "Duped")*, Lodestar defeated [Urien](/wiki/Urien "Urien").
* In *[Video Games](/wiki/Video_Games "Video Games")*, Lodestar was defeated by the [Stalker](/wiki/Stalker "Stalker").
* In *[Ultimate Aggregor](/wiki/Ultimate_Aggregor_(Episode) "Ultimate Aggregor (Episode)")*, Lodestar and [the team](/wiki/Alien_Force "Alien Force") defeated a group of [Aggrebots](/wiki/Aggrebots "Aggrebots"). Afterwards, Lodestar boarded [Aggregor](/wiki/Aggregor "Aggregor")'s [ship](/wiki/Aggregor%27s_Ship "Aggregor's Ship") and was destroyed when it exploded, but survived and regenerated.
* In *[Absolute Power: Part 1](/wiki/Absolute_Power:_Part_1 "Absolute Power: Part 1")*, Lodestar violently attacked some [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights") to find out about [Ultimate Kevin](/wiki/Ultimate_Kevin "Ultimate Kevin")'s location.
* In *[Girl Trouble](/wiki/Girl_Trouble "Girl Trouble")*, Lodestar battled some of [Computron](/wiki/Computron "Computron")'s [minions](/wiki/Computron%27s_Minions "Computron's Minions").
* In *[The Purge](/wiki/The_Purge "The Purge")*, Lodestar fought a [Dragon Robot](/wiki/Dragon_Robot "Dragon Robot") and destroyed it with [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)")'s help.
* In *[Ben 10/Generator Rex: Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, Lodestar battled [Rex](/wiki/Rex_Salazar "Rex Salazar") before switching into [Rath](/wiki/Rath_(Classic) "Rath (Classic)").
* In *[Night of the Living Nightmare](/wiki/Night_of_the_Living_Nightmare "Night of the Living Nightmare")*, Lodestar appeared twice in a dream. Firstly, Ben as Lodestar was defeated by Aggregor. Later, Lodestar was seen confronting [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo").


### Omniverse


* In *[The More Things Change: Part 1](/wiki/The_More_Things_Change:_Part_1 "The More Things Change: Part 1")*, Lodestar stopped [Zombozo](/wiki/Zombozo_(Classic) "Zombozo (Classic)") from stealing a brain from the bra​in bank.
* In *[Return to Forever](/wiki/Return_to_Forever "Return to Forever")*, Lodestar battled the [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights") and saved [Jimmy](/wiki/Jimmy_Jones "Jimmy Jones") from them.
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Lodestar appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.


### Comics


* In *[One Man's Trash](/wiki/One_Man%27s_Trash "One Man's Trash")*, Lodestar magnetised the alien ship.
* In *[Time Saver](/wiki/Time_Saver "Time Saver")*, Lodestar was used for a short period of time to attack [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)"), before switching to [Brainstorm](/wiki/Brainstorm "Brainstorm").

### [Ben 10 Alien Force: The Rise of Hex](/wiki/Ben_10_Alien_Force:_The_Rise_of_Hex "Ben 10 Alien Force: The Rise of Hex")


Being the main alien and the replacement for [Alien X](/wiki/Alien_X_(Classic) "Alien X (Classic)"), Lodestar is a playable alien in the game.


Due to the Omnitrix sorting out after Ben fiddled with it, Lodestar was temporarily locked until the beginning of Level 7.


Lodestar is able to magnetically attract himself to metallic emblems embedded on the walls of the temple and catacombs underneath [Bellwood](/wiki/Bellwood_(Classic) "Bellwood (Classic)"). He can increase his altitude, move across considerable distances and slow his descent to avoid potential hazards.


Throughout the game, Lodestar strangely interacts with 15 mystic talismans. Because of this, he is the only alien form capable of defeating [Hex](/wiki/Hex_(Classic) "Hex (Classic)"), destroying all but one talisman to weaken the otherwise invincible foe. Lodestar uses the remaining talisman to trap Hex inside it.

Etymology
---------


Lodestar's name comes from [lodestone](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lodestone "wikipedia:Lodestone"), a naturally magnetized piece of the mineral called [magnetite](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magnetite "wikipedia:Magnetite").[[DM 1]](#cite_note-20)



Trivia
------


* Lodestar is the first alien used by 16-year-old [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") in **Omniverse**.
* In **Alien Force** and **Ultimate Alien**, Lodestar appears to have a mouth, but with the exception of several errors, it never opens. However, in **Omniverse**, his mouth moves when he talks, much like [Eatle](/wiki/Eatle "Eatle").
* Lodestar's magnetic pulses have changed color throughout his appearances:
	+ When he is introduced in *Simple*, the pulses are white. In his subsequent **Alien Force** appearances, they are green.
	+ In **Ultimate Alien**, they are white again.
	+ In *Heroes United* and **Omniverse**, they are green again.
* Lodestar's power are similar to those of the [Marvel Comics](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marvel_Comics "wikipedia:Marvel Comics") characters [Magneto](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magneto "wikipedia:Magneto") and [Polaris](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polaris_(Marvel_Comics) "wikipedia:Polaris (Marvel Comics)").
* In the code Nano section on the *[FusionFall](/wiki/FusionFall "FusionFall")* website, a picture of Lodestar's head can be seen.
* The **[Ben 10](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)")** reboot game *Brains vs. Bugs* has a magnet upgrade that resembles Lodestar's upper body minus the shoulders, arms, and head.
* Lodestar is the longest known alien in Ben's possession whose home planet isn't named or known.
