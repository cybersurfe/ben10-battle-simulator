Appearance
----------


### Ben as Upgrade


Upgrade has a black exterior with green stripes that resemble circuitry all over him. The green circle on his head is his eye, which glows whenever he talks. His body is made up of billions of small cell-like creatures called nanites.[[pop-up 1]](#cite_note-2)[[2]](#cite_note-Game_Over-3)


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Upgrade's front torso was white and the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol was on his chest.


In *[Back with a Vengeance](/wiki/Back_with_a_Vengeance "Back with a Vengeance")*, Upgrade wore a yellow raincoat.


In ***[Destroy All Aliens](/wiki/Ben_10:_Destroy_All_Aliens "Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens")***, Upgrade looked the same as he did in the original series except he had more of a metallic texture, giving the appearance of a computer's screen with circuitry patterns within.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Upgrade's front torso was recolored green with black circuitry patterns and the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest was recolored green.


In *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, Upgrade looked the same as he did in **Ultimate Alien** except his green circuitry stripes glowed yellow. His Ultimatrix symbol was also not visible until he merged with [Rex](/wiki/Rex_Salazar "Rex Salazar").


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, 16-year-old Upgrade is more muscular, his circuit pattern has changed slightly, and the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol is inside the ring of his eye.


11-year-old Upgrade looks exactly the same as he did in the original series, except his neck is slightly longer and the Omnitrix symbol on his chest is recolored green.



### Kevin as Upgrade


[Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)")'s Upgrade looked exactly like Ben's did in the original series, but without an Omnitrix symbol.

Upgrade is completely amorphous as he can switch between liquid and bipedal form. The nanites that makes up his body allows Upgrade to reshape and move like liquid even though he's a solid being.[[pop-up 2]](#cite_note-4)[[2]](#cite_note-Game_Over-3) Due to this, he can reshape himself to let projectiles or blows pass through him by creating a hole in his body at will. His physiology also makes him extremely flexible. Upgrade's shapeshifting abilities allow him to grow technological spikes and tentacles, even when merging with technology.


Upgrade can alter his body shape to match or mimic the body structure of another species, such when he morphed into a [Tetramand](/wiki/Tetramand_(Classic) "Tetramand (Classic)") and grew an extra pair of arms.[[3]](#cite_note-DAA-5) He can also alter his own size, as demonstrated by a [Mechamorph Guard](/wiki/Mechamorph_Guard "Mechamorph Guard").[[4]](#cite_note-TUS-6)


Upgrade's DNA is "programmed" with blueprints of every alien technology,[[pop-up 3]](#cite_note-7)[[5]](#cite_note-DWS-8) allowing him to merge with and possess any technology by encasing himself over it. The size of the device is not significant and he controls it as naturally as he would his own body. When he merges, he enhances the technology into "ultra-tech",[[merch 1]](#cite_note-Bandai-9) making it far more advanced and futuristic beyond human capability, causing new features to form. After he separates from a machine, it returns to normal.


Upgrade's technological possession can work on non-electrical machinery, such as a row machine in a gym.[[6]](#cite_note-The_Alliance-10) This ability extends to even sentient beings that are mechanical in nature, like [Chronosapiens](/wiki/Chronosapien "Chronosapien")[[7]](#cite_note-11)[[8]](#cite_note-12) and probably [Nanochips](/wiki/Nanochip "Nanochip").[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-13)


Upgrade can reconfigure technology to suit any situation, like transform the [Rust Bucket](/wiki/Rust_Bucket_(Classic) "Rust Bucket (Classic)") into a battle armor, with an arsenal of heavy energy weapons, or even retractile robotic manipulator arms.[[9]](#cite_note-14)


Possessing technology allows Upgrade to transform it with seemingly no limit, such as merging with a baseball pitching machine and turning it into a tripod-like robot that fires a seemingly infinite amount of baseballs.[[10]](#cite_note-15)


Upgrade can make his head or face appear anywhere on the machines he possesses.[[11]](#cite_note-Heroes_United-16)


Upgrade can merge with organic beings, such as [Rojo](/wiki/Rojo "Rojo")[[6]](#cite_note-The_Alliance-10) and [Rex](/wiki/Rex_Salazar "Rex Salazar"),[[11]](#cite_note-Heroes_United-16) as long as there is machinery integrated within them.


Upgrade can enhance certain devices, such as [Rook](/wiki/Rook_Blonko "Rook Blonko")'s [Proto-Tool](/wiki/Proto-Tool "Proto-Tool"), just by touching them.[[12]](#cite_note-SZTWC-17)


By energizing his biomechanical blood,[[pop-up 4]](#cite_note-18)[[2]](#cite_note-Game_Over-3) Upgrade can launch a powerful green plasma blast from his eye.[[13]](#cite_note-TT-19)[[14]](#cite_note-BAotD-20)[[15]](#cite_note-FHtE-21)


Upgrade can form energy constructs such as an energy spike and can generate a beam of light that can harm an [Ectonurite](/wiki/Ectonurite_(Classic) "Ectonurite (Classic)"),[[15]](#cite_note-FHtE-21) though mostly while merged with machinery and upgrading them into weapons.[[16]](#cite_note-SE-22)


Upgrade can travel through electrical currents[[17]](#cite_note-23) and phase through most technology.


Upgrade can reshape his body to resemble a parachute in order to gently descend and glide through the air before landing safely.[[18]](#cite_note-Secrets-24)


Being inorganic, Upgrade is able to survive in the vacuum of space.[[14]](#cite_note-BAotD-20)


Due to being made of living metal, Upgrade is able to regenerate himself.[[3]](#cite_note-DAA-5)


Upgrade possesses enhanced strength, being able to throw a sewer lid at a [Megawhatt](/wiki/Nosedeenian_(Classic) "Nosedeenian (Classic)"),[[13]](#cite_note-TT-19) push [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") off of a [ship](/wiki/Chimerian_Hammer_(Classic) "Chimerian Hammer (Classic)"),[[18]](#cite_note-Secrets-24) and overpower [Viktor](/wiki/Viktor "Viktor").[[14]](#cite_note-BAotD-20)


Upgrade is durable enough to withstand a blast from the Proto-Tool without physical damage.[[12]](#cite_note-SZTWC-17)


Upgrade can generate electrical charges capable of shocking his opponents into submission.[[5]](#cite_note-DWS-8)


Upgrade can separate and release a part of him into technologies and control them without fully merging to them.[[12]](#cite_note-SZTWC-17)


Due to being technological, Upgrade possesses digitalized vision.[[16]](#cite_note-SE-22)


Upgrade can repair damaged machinery with ease and come out unscathed, as demonstrated by [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)") when he used the [Retaliator Armor](/wiki/Retaliator_Armor "Retaliator Armor") to repair the Rust Bucket by absorbing its remains into itself and reassembling them.[[3]](#cite_note-DAA-5)


Upgrade can turn his hands into weapons or tools, such as maces[[2]](#cite_note-Game_Over-3) and a flashlight, the latter of which has been demonstrated by several Mechamorph Guards.[[19]](#cite_note-25)


Upgrade can extend parts of his body into cable or hand-like structures, as demonstrated by [Malware](/wiki/Malware "Malware").[[20]](#cite_note-TH-26)[[21]](#cite_note-27)


Having a vast library to draw upon, Upgrade may be able to mimic and shapeshift into technology[[DR 1]](#cite_note-28) and non-electrical items, as demonstrated by [Ship](/wiki/Ship "Ship")[[22]](#cite_note-29) and a Mechamorph Guard.[[4]](#cite_note-TUS-6)[[note 1]](#cite_note-33)

Upgrade's control over machines is not absolute, as sapient, intelligent machines like [Slix Vigma](/wiki/Slix_Vigma "Slix Vigma") can easily fight off Upgrade if he merges with them against their will.[[23]](#cite_note-GM-34) He may also be unable to override existing security systems, such as those of the [Megacruiser](/wiki/Megacruiser "Megacruiser")[[23]](#cite_note-GM-34) or [Plumber Headquarters](/wiki/Plumber_Headquarters "Plumber Headquarters").[[24]](#cite_note-35)


Upgrade is sensitive to electromagnetic pulses/fields and magnetic fields.[[25]](#cite_note-36)


Upgrade can be hurt if he is pulled and stretched hard enough, as shown when he was being pulled and stretched by [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)")'s [Mutant Squid](/wiki/Mutant_Squid "Mutant Squid").[[5]](#cite_note-DWS-8)


If he is being continuously attacked, Upgrade cannot regenerate himself.[[15]](#cite_note-FHtE-21)


Upgrade can be affected by technorganic viruses[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-37) and a [Nemuina](/wiki/Nemuina "Nemuina")'s sleeping dust.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-38)


Despite his enhanced durability, Upgrade is weak against acid[[AC 1]](#cite_note-39) and intense heat such as lava.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-40) This is because any sort of corrosive can upset his delicate chemistry.[[merch 1]](#cite_note-Bandai-9)


Being a swarm of nanomachines, Upgrade is weak against electricity, such as that generated by [Nosedeenians](/wiki/Nosedeenian_(Classic) "Nosedeenian (Classic)")[[13]](#cite_note-TT-19) and [Conductoids](/wiki/Conductoid "Conductoid"), the latter of which has been demonstrated with [Malware](/wiki/Malware "Malware").[[26]](#cite_note-41)


Upgrade can be encased by [Petrosapiens](/wiki/Petrosapien_(Classic) "Petrosapien (Classic)"), as also demonstrated with Malware.[[20]](#cite_note-TH-26)


Upgrade can be controlled by [Vladats](/wiki/Vladat "Vladat") via their [Corrupturas](/wiki/Corruptura "Corruptura").[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-42)



History

### Ben 10


* Upgrade first appeared in *[Permanent Retirement](/wiki/Permanent_Retirement "Permanent Retirement")*, where he stopped some thieves from robbing an ATM machine.
* In *[Last Laugh](/wiki/Last_Laugh "Last Laugh")*, Upgrade defeated the [Circus Freaks](/wiki/Circus_Freak_Trio_(Classic) "Circus Freak Trio (Classic)").
* In *[Tourist Trap](/wiki/Tourist_Trap "Tourist Trap")*, Upgrade fought a [Megawhatt](/wiki/Nosedeenian_(Classic) "Nosedeenian (Classic)") to no avail and accidentally duplicated it.
* In *[The Alliance](/wiki/The_Alliance "The Alliance")*, Upgrade defeated [Rojo](/wiki/Rojo "Rojo") by merging with her, while also coming face to face with [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") for the first time.
* In *[Side Effects](/wiki/Side_Effects "Side Effects")*, Upgrade defeated a thief before finding an ice cream truck.
* In *[Secrets](/wiki/Secrets "Secrets")*, Upgrade entered and played with [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)")'s laptop. Later, Upgrade defeated some [Drones](/wiki/Drones "Drones") and saved [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") from Vilgax; after doing so, Upgrade glided down to [Mt. Rushmore](/wiki/Mt._Rushmore "Mt. Rushmore"), but before he could land safely, the malfunctioning [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") made him switch into [Ripjaws](/wiki/Ripjaws_(Classic) "Ripjaws (Classic)").
	+ Also, Upgrade appeared as one of the many alien templates used for Vilgax's army in an imaginary sequence.
* In *[The Big Tick](/wiki/The_Big_Tick "The Big Tick")*, Upgrade failed to defeat the [Great One](/wiki/Great_One "Great One").
* In *[Framed](/wiki/Framed "Framed")*, [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") as Upgrade took control of a cable car and defeated [Wildmutt](/wiki/Wildmutt "Wildmutt").
* In *[Grudge Match](/wiki/Grudge_Match "Grudge Match")*, Upgrade merged with [Slix Vigma](/wiki/Slix_Vigma "Slix Vigma") and freed some prisoners from the [Megacruiser](/wiki/Megacruiser "Megacruiser").
* In *[They Lurk Below](/wiki/They_Lurk_Below "They Lurk Below")*, Upgrade escaped the [Cyber Squids](/wiki/Cyber_Squids "Cyber Squids") with Gwen and [Eddie](/wiki/Eddie_GrandSmith "Eddie GrandSmith").
* In *[Back with a Vengeance](/wiki/Back_with_a_Vengeance "Back with a Vengeance")*, Upgrade appeared when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") accidentally unlocked the [Master Control](/wiki/Master_Control "Master Control").
* In *[Midnight Madness](/wiki/Midnight_Madness "Midnight Madness")*, Upgrade was hypnotized by [Sublimino](/wiki/Sublimino "Sublimino") to steal a giant clock.
* In *[Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Episode) "Ben 10,000 (Episode)")*, Upgrade battled [Future Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Future) "Vilgax (Future)") before [Future](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)") [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)") switched him into [Cannonbolt](/wiki/Cannonbolt_(Classic) "Cannonbolt (Classic)").
* In *[Game Over](/wiki/Game_Over "Game Over")*, Upgrade cheated on a [Sumo Slammers](/wiki/Sumo_Slammers_(Classic) "Sumo Slammers (Classic)") video game and accidentally trapped himself and Gwen in the game, where they defeated some Robo-Sumos. Later, Upgrade defeated [Kenko](/wiki/Kenko "Kenko"), revived [Ishiyama](/wiki/Ishiyama "Ishiyama") and freed himself and Gwen from the game.
* In *[Under Wraps](/wiki/Under_Wraps "Under Wraps")*, Upgrade defeated the [Mummy](/wiki/Mummy "Mummy").
* In *[Be Afraid of the Dark](/wiki/Be_Afraid_of_the_Dark "Be Afraid of the Dark")*, Upgrade travelled into space and fought [Viktor](/wiki/Viktor "Viktor") before ditching him. He then defeated the Mummy with the help of Max and Gwen.
* In *[Divided We Stand](/wiki/Divided_We_Stand "Divided We Stand")*, Upgrade battled [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)")'s [Mutant Squid](/wiki/Mutant_Squid "Mutant Squid").
* In *[Ben 4 Good Buddy](/wiki/Ben_4_Good_Buddy "Ben 4 Good Buddy")*, Upgrade fought the [Road Crew](/wiki/Road_Crew "Road Crew").
* In ***[Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens](/wiki/Ben_10:_Destroy_All_Aliens "Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens")***, Upgrade appeared thrice. Firstly, Upgrade destroyed an alien tank with Gwen's help. Then, Upgrade attacked Ben in a dream. Lastly, Upgrade was interrogated by [Retaliator](/wiki/Azmuth%27s_Father "Azmuth's Father") before the Omnitrix switched him into [Stinkfly](/wiki/Stinkfly_(Classic) "Stinkfly (Classic)").
* In *[Radio Dazed](/wiki/Radio_Dazed "Radio Dazed")*, Upgrade tried to switch the music but accidentally broke the [Rust Bucket](/wiki/Rust_Bucket "Rust Bucket")'s radio.
* In *[Road Trip Rumble](/wiki/Road_Trip_Rumble "Road Trip Rumble")*, Upgrade fought Gwen in order to decide whether the Omnitrix or [magic](/wiki/Magic_(Classic) "Magic (Classic)") is better.

Trivia
------


* Throughout his appearances, Upgrade's voice sounds essentially the same as Ben's albeit through a speaker. The reason for this is because Galvanic Mechamorphs cannot "upgrade" living tissue, thus making the merge with Ben's DNA incomplete.[[pop-up 5]](#cite_note-incomplete-32)[[2]](#cite_note-Game_Over-3)
	+ In *[Framed](/wiki/Framed "Framed")*, Upgrade's voice is Kevin's recurring voice from the original series through a speaker.
	+ In *[Gwen 10](/wiki/Gwen_10 "Gwen 10")*, Upgrade's voice is [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Gwen_10) "Max Tennyson (Gwen 10)")'s voice through a speaker.
	+ In ***Destroy All Aliens*** and *[From Hedorium to Eternity](/wiki/From_Hedorium_to_Eternity "From Hedorium to Eternity")*, 10/11-year old Upgrade's voice is mostly the same as in the original series, except they have a robotic tone similar to [Azmuth's father](/wiki/Azmuth%27s_Father "Azmuth's Father") whenever he wears the [Retaliator Armor](/wiki/Retaliator_Armor "Retaliator Armor"), as well as Malware.
	+ In **Omniverse**, 16-year-old Upgrade's voice is mostly the same as in *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")* but with the robotic tones his younger self uses.
* In ***Destroy All Aliens***, Upgrade's texture was based on a high-tech computer tablet of an unspecified brand.[[AC 2]](#cite_note-43)
* Upgrade is one of five aliens featured in **Omniverse** who generate green electricity; the others being [Buzzshock](/wiki/Buzzshock_(Classic) "Buzzshock (Classic)"), [Frankenstrike](/wiki/Frankenstrike "Frankenstrike"), [Brainstorm](/wiki/Brainstorm "Brainstorm"), and [Nanomech](/wiki/Nanomech "Nanomech").
* Upgrade is the final alien used onscreen by 11-year-old Ben in **Omniverse**.
