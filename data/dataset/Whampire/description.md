Appearance
----------


Whampire is a [vampire](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vampire "wikipedia:Vampire")-like alien with pale skin, green eyes, and sharp yellow teeth. He wears a black jumpsuit with a green chest and markings, black gloves with green fingers that possess sharp claws, and a green mask with spikes on it. His head is rather large and features green eyes, and his head spikes are part of his suit and they do not cover any part of his body. Whampire also has green flaps under his arms, which resemble the wings of a bat.


Whampire wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his stomach.


Whampire's [Corrupturas](/wiki/Corruptura "Corruptura") are crystalline entities,[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-2) they are small, dark green, diamond-shaped objects with light green eyes, little horns, and black bat-like wings.


In his bat form, Whampire becomes a small black ball with wings of the same color and size. A miniature version of his face is on the front of his body.


While burning in sunlight, Whampire's clothes seem to burn off first, leaving his skin, which would be completely grey due to the sun, exposed before he himself burns.

Just like [Lord Transyl](/wiki/Lord_Transyl "Lord Transyl"), Whampire possesses hypnosis from his eyes and can control other beings by shooting Corrupturas at their foreheads.


Whampire's Corrupturas are effective on other races of the Anur System, such as [Transylians](/wiki/Transylian "Transylian"), [Thep Khufans](/wiki/Thep_Khufan "Thep Khufan"), [Loboans](/wiki/Loboan_(Classic) "Loboan (Classic)"), and tangible [Ectonurites](/wiki/Ectonurite_(Classic) "Ectonurite (Classic)").[[1]](#cite_note-TVSB-1)


Other races shown to be effective on Corrupturas include [Revonnahganders](/wiki/Revonnahgander "Revonnahgander"),[[1]](#cite_note-TVSB-1) [Merlinisapiens](/wiki/Merlinisapien "Merlinisapien"),[[2]](#cite_note-TEoaE-3) [Splixsons](/wiki/Splixson "Splixson"),[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-Splixson-4) [Crystalsapiens](/wiki/Crystalsapien_(Classic) "Crystalsapien (Classic)"),[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-5) [To'kustars](/wiki/To%27kustar_(Classic) "To'kustar (Classic)"),[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-6) [Sonorosians](/wiki/Sonorosian "Sonorosian"),[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-7) [Galvanic Mechamorphs](/wiki/Galvanic_Mechamorph_(Classic) "Galvanic Mechamorph (Classic)"),[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-8) [Piscciss Premanns](/wiki/Piscciss_Premann_(Classic) "Piscciss Premann (Classic)"),[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-9) and [Arburian Pelarotas](/wiki/Arburian_Pelarota_(Classic) "Arburian Pelarota (Classic)").[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-10)


Whampire can absorb and feed off the energy of living beings by sucking on them. He can even drain the life force of a [Celestialsapien](/wiki/Celestialsapien_(Classic) "Celestialsapien (Classic)").[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-11) If Whampire were to completely drain his victims of their energy, they would look similar to [Darkstar](/wiki/Michael_Morningstar_(Classic) "Michael Morningstar (Classic)")'s victims.[[DJW 10]](#cite_note-12)


Whampire has the ability to fly and is highly agile while in flight.


Whampire has a certain level of enhanced strength, being able to hold his own against a mob of [Anur Transyl](/wiki/Anur_Transyl "Anur Transyl") citizens before eventually being overrun,[[1]](#cite_note-TVSB-1) get [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") out of [Mal](/wiki/Malware_Armor "Malware Armor")[gax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)")'s clutches, and flip the latter over afterwards.[[3]](#cite_note-MA-13)


Whampire possesses enhanced durability, as shown when he was able to withstand being thrown against a wall by Malgax.[[3]](#cite_note-MA-13)


Whampire can hang upside down like bats and real vampires using his prehensile feet. According to himself, he does his best thinking while he is upside down.[[1]](#cite_note-TVSB-1)


Whampire can turn into a bat-like creature.[[4]](#cite_note-BaDGtA51-14)[[DJW 11]](#cite_note-chibi-15)


Whampire can see the internal structure, like the nerves and energy of his victims using his infrared vision.[[1]](#cite_note-TVSB-1)


Whampire can withstand other [Vladats](/wiki/Vladat "Vladat") hypnotizing him depending on his willpower,[[DJW 12]](#cite_note-Vladat_Willpower-16) as demonstrated in Lord Transyl's failed attempt to control him.[[1]](#cite_note-TVSB-1)


Whampire can create a huge sonic explosion as a last-resort means of self-defense that was powerful enough to send [Rook Blonko](/wiki/Rook_Blonko "Rook Blonko"), [Scout](/wiki/Scout "Scout"), [Rad Dudesman](/wiki/Rad_Dudesman "Rad Dudesman"), and a mob of Anur Transyl citizens flying in every direction.[[1]](#cite_note-TVSB-1)


Whampire can survive in the vacuum of space.[[DJW 13]](#cite_note-17)


Like all Anur System aliens, Whampire can see in the dark.[[DJW 14]](#cite_note-18)


Whampire possesses smoke mimicry.[[DJW 15]](#cite_note-19)


Whampire can control undead aliens, such as [Benzarro](/wiki/Benzarro "Benzarro")'s transformations, [[DJW 16]](#cite_note-20) and possessed inanimate objects using his Corrupturas. In the case of the latter, the possessor's consciousness would have to be contained within the object they are inhabiting.[[DJW 17]](#cite_note-Chucky-21)


Whampire can use Corrupturas as projectile weapons.[[DJW 18]](#cite_note-22)

----------

1/4
Whampire, like all Vladats, can be harmed by sunlight and cannot stay in such an environment for more than a short period of time.[[4]](#cite_note-BaDGtA51-14) He can also be harmed by intense heat such as that generated by [Pyronites](/wiki/Pyronite_(Classic) "Pyronite (Classic)"),[[DJW 19]](#cite_note-23) [Prypiatosian-Bs](/wiki/Prypiatosian-B "Prypiatosian-B"),[[DJW 20]](#cite_note-24) and members of [Atomix](/wiki/Atomix "Atomix")'s [species](/wiki/Atomix%27s_Species "Atomix's Species").[[1]](#cite_note-TVSB-1)


Whampire's explosion ability will not only blow away his opponents, it will also blow away his allies, causing a chance of accidental collateral damage.


When the Omnitrix times out, Whampire's Corrupturas will break apart, freeing his victims from their control and returning control of the victims' bodies.[[1]](#cite_note-TVSB-1)


Whampire's Corrupturas are not very durable, as they can easily be destroyed by species with as much or more strength than a Transylian, as demonstrated by Lord Transyl.[[1]](#cite_note-TVSB-1)


Whampire must resist the urge to drain the energy of other lifeforms, especially intelligent beings, making him potentially dangerous to his teammates and other civilians.


Whampire's Corrupturas cannot attach to beings with wet or slippery skin, such as [Ickthyperambuloids](/wiki/Ickthyperambuloid "Ickthyperambuloid")[[DJW 21]](#cite_note-25) and members of [Hobble](/wiki/Hobble "Hobble")'s [species](/wiki/Hobble%27s_Species "Hobble's Species").[[1]](#cite_note-TVSB-1)


Whampire's Corrupturas also cannot attach to beings that are intangible, though they can attach to said species if they are caught off guard and are tangible when the Corruptura is shot at them.[[1]](#cite_note-TVSB-1) Additionally, the Corrupturas are ineffective on [Dagon](/wiki/Dagon "Dagon").[[DJW 22]](#cite_note-26)


Whampire is unable to control an entire set of [Splixson](/wiki/Splixson "Splixson") clones using his Corrupturas because each clone is an independent being.[[DJW 23]](#cite_note-27) Furthermore, the Corrupturas will not be copied across said clones.[[DJW 24]](#cite_note-28)


Whampire will not receive a cosmic power boost from feeding off a Celetialsapien because he would feel like he just slammed an energy drink.[[DJW 25]](#cite_note-29)


There are certain beings that both Whampire's hypnosis and Corrupturas are ineffective against, such as other Vladats if their willpower is strong,[[1]](#cite_note-TVSB-1)[[DJW 12]](#cite_note-Vladat_Willpower-16) and cyborgs like [Exo-Skull](/wiki/Exo-Skull "Exo-Skull").[[2]](#cite_note-TEoaE-3)


Whampire's hypnosis is ineffective on [Florauna](/wiki/Florauna_(Classic) "Florauna (Classic)"), as their nature as plant-like creatures makes them immune to being hypnotized.[[5]](#cite_note-30) His Corrupturas are also possibly ineffective on Florauna, though this depends on the willpower of an individual.[[DJW 26]](#cite_note-31)


Whampire cannot absorb the life energy of certain beings, such as Ectonurites because they have none in the conventional sense.[[6]](#cite_note-32)[[7]](#cite_note-33) Similarly, he cannot absorb the life energy of [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)") zombies because they have no life force left.[[DJW 27]](#cite_note-34)


Unlike [Ghostfreak](/wiki/Ghostfreak "Ghostfreak"), Whampire is not immune to [Darkstar](/wiki/Michael_Morningstar_(Classic) "Michael Morningstar (Classic)")'s energy absorption ability.[[DJW 28]](#cite_note-35)


Whampire's Corrupturas are weak against acid.[[DJW 29]](#cite_note-36)


Whampire can easily be scared by [Toepick](/wiki/Toepick "Toepick")'s [species](/wiki/Toepick%27s_Species "Toepick's Species").[[DJW 30]](#cite_note-37)


Whampire is possibly allergic to garlic.[[DJW 31]](#cite_note-38)



History

### Omniverse


* Whampire first appeared in *[The Vampire Strikes Back](/wiki/The_Vampire_Strikes_Back "The Vampire Strikes Back")*, where his DNA was obtained by scanning [Lord Transyl](/wiki/Lord_Transyl "Lord Transyl") and he took control of [Viktor](/wiki/Viktor "Viktor"), [Kuphulu](/wiki/Kuphulu "Kuphulu"), and [Crüjo](/wiki/Cr%C3%BCjo "Crüjo"). Afterward, Whampire used the [Lovely Duck](/wiki/Lovely_Duck "Lovely Duck") to break into [Z'Castle Zs'Skayr](/wiki/Z%27Castle_Zs%27Skayr "Z'Castle Zs'Skayr") and battle Lord Transyl and [Zs'Skayr](/wiki/Zs%27Skayr "Zs'Skayr").
* In *[Blukic and Driba Go to Area 51](/wiki/Blukic_and_Driba_Go_to_Area_51 "Blukic and Driba Go to Area 51")*, Whampire was selected by mistake and almost burned to death until [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") hit his [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol and switched him into [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)").
* In *[Malgax Attacks](/wiki/Malgax_Attacks "Malgax Attacks")*, Whampire battled [Mal](/wiki/Malware_Armor "Malware Armor")[gax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") and, with the help of [Skurd](/wiki/Skurd "Skurd"), defeated him.
* In *[The End of an Era](/wiki/The_End_of_an_Era "The End of an Era")*, Whampire defeated [Exo-Skull](/wiki/Exo-Skull "Exo-Skull") and [Subdora](/wiki/Subdora "Subdora").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Whampire appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

Etymology
---------


Whampire's name is a portmanteau of "wham", which is onomatopoeic, coming from hitting something very hard as well as the name of the band *[Wham!](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wham! "wikipedia:Wham!")*,[[DJW 32]](#cite_note-39) and "vampire", referencing his appearance.



Trivia
------


* Whampire speaks with a [Romanian](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Romania "wikipedia:Romania") accent.
* [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") came up with Whampire[[DJW 33]](#cite_note-40) and designed his appearance, which were based on his roughs, alongside Steven Choi.[[DJW 34]](#cite_note-41)
	+ Whampire is modeled after the [Marvel Comics](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marvel_Comics "wikipedia:Marvel Comics") character [Baron Blood](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Baron_Blood "wikipedia:Baron Blood").[[DJW 35]](#cite_note-42)
* As the Vladats are a functionally extinct race, Whampire and Lord Transyl are the only remaining Vladats in the universe.
* Whampire's ability to turn into a chibi-fied bat is an actual power and not just for comedic effect.[[DJW 11]](#cite_note-chibi-15)
* Whampire is the only alien introduced in **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")** who was unlocked through obtaining a DNA sample. However, unlike most other aliens that were unlocked through this procedure, Whampire's DNA sample was acquired in extraordinary circumstances: Lord Transyl was brought back to life by [Zs'Skayr](/wiki/Zs%27Skayr "Zs'Skayr") using the immense [magical](/wiki/Magic_(Classic) "Magic (Classic)") power he stole from the [Alpha Rune](/wiki/Alpha_Rune "Alpha Rune").[[1]](#cite_note-TVSB-1)
* While Vladats are one of the few known to prey on sentient species bigger than them, Whampire is not the only known predatory alien in the Omnitrix.[[DJW 36]](#cite_note-43)
* Whampire is the final alien introduced in **Omniverse**, as well as in the Classic Timeline as a whole.
	+ Coincidentally, Whampire replaces [Heatblast](/wiki/Heatblast_(Classic) "Heatblast (Classic)"), the first alien ever introduced in the Classic Timeline, in the opening intro of **Omniverse'**s Arc 5.
* [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo")'s [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") does not contain Whampire's DNA.[[DJW 37]](#cite_note-44)
