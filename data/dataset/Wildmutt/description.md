Appearance
----------


### Ben as Wildmutt


Wildmutt appears to be a large orange dog-like creature with no eyes, ears, nose, or tail. He stands on all fours and his movements are somewhat apelike. His teeth are very defined and his bottom jaw sticks out of his mouth. The various [spines](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spine_(zoology) "wikipedia:Spine (zoology)") found all across his body function similarly to whiskers with tactile sensing, and are not fur.[[DJ 1]](#cite_note-2) Since Wildmutt has no eyes, he uses his sense of smell and hearing to navigate, which are aided by three gill-like nostrils located on each side of his neck.


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Wildmutt wore a black-and-white brace with the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol on his left shoulder.


While when under the effects of a cold in *[Side Effects](/wiki/Side_Effects "Side Effects")*, Wildmutt's fur discolors from its vivid orange to a pale yellowish-orange tone, and his lips and claws turn from black to a sickly dark green hue.


In *[Back with a Vengeance](/wiki/Back_with_a_Vengeance "Back with a Vengeance")*, Wildmutt wore a yellow raincoat.


In *[Merry Christmas](/wiki/Merry_Christmas "Merry Christmas")*, Wildmutt briefly wore fake antlers while disguised as a reindeer.


In *[Don't Drink the Water](/wiki/Don%27t_Drink_the_Water "Don't Drink the Water")*, 4-year-old Wildmutt, nicknamed Wildpup, looked very similar to his 10-year-old self, but a lot smaller and thinner and having holes in place of claws.


In [Destroy All Aliens](/wiki/Ben_10:_Destroy_All_Aliens "Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens"), Wildmutt's skin was lighter and furry, resembling that of several quadrupedal furry creatures. 


In ***[Race Against Time](/wiki/Ben_10:_Race_Against_Time "Ben 10: Race Against Time")***, Wildmutt looks the same, except his skin is darker.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, 16-year-old Wildmutt looks mostly the same except his skin is lighter in color. While his lips were still black in the **Ultimate Alien** intro, they were in the same color as his fur in the actual series. He wore the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest. Kevin as Wildmutt shares these traits.


In *[The Forge of Creation](/wiki/The_Forge_of_Creation "The Forge of Creation")*, 10-year-old Wildmutt looked mostly the same as in the original series, except his lips were in the same color has his skin, his shoulder brace did not have any white on it, and the original Omnitrix symbol was recolored green.


In *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, 16-year-old Wildmutt looked exactly the same as in the original series but his skin was a darkish orange.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Wildmutt's lips have returned to being black and his forearms are a bit bigger. 16-year-old Wildmutt wears a green and white collar with the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol.


11-year-old Wildmutt looks closer his appearance in the original series than 10-year-old Wildmutt in **Ultimate Alien**, what with the white returning to the shoulder brace, though the Omnitrix symbol is still colored green.

1/18
Wildmutt's main ability is his sense of smell. As he has no eyes, his "vision" is made up of a dull 3D mapping of where his senses originate, similar to a thermograph. Combined with his enhanced hearing, his senses take the form of a radar or sonar, allowing him to still perceive his environment more clearly.


Wildmutt can sense invisible entities, therefore making invisibility useless against him.[[2]](#cite_note-FaTM-3)


Wildmutt's enhanced speed and reaction time allows him to dodge, evade, and counterattack effortlessly with the help of his senses.


Wildmutt can track almost anything and anyone by their scent.[[3]](#cite_note-PN775IM-4)


Wildmutt has enhanced strength, as he could tear apart one of [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)")'s [Drones](/wiki/Drones "Drones") with ease,[[4]](#cite_note-5) throw a dumpster at a group of [Limax](/wiki/Limax "Limax"),[[5]](#cite_note-6) and tear off the roof of a car[[6]](#cite_note-Last_Laugh-7) at age 10. By age 16, he is strong enough to carry a [Florauna](/wiki/Florauna_(Classic) "Florauna (Classic)") on his back without difficulty (even while running),[[7]](#cite_note-8) hold his own against a [Buglizard](/wiki/Buglizard_(Species) "Buglizard (Species)"),[[8]](#cite_note-OPaPP1-9) and pin down a [Merlinisapien](/wiki/Merlinisapien "Merlinisapien").[[3]](#cite_note-PN775IM-4)[[2]](#cite_note-FaTM-3)


Wildmutt can dig deep craters at a fast speed, even while digging through solid stone ground.[[9]](#cite_note-10)


Wildmutt has enhanced agility that allows him to run, jump, and climb at an inhuman rate. He is highly acrobatic and gymnastic, with such movements being similar to that of an ape.[[10]](#cite_note-11)


Wildmutt is durable enough to survive a steel tower falling on top of him and jump through a glass window.[[6]](#cite_note-Last_Laugh-7)[[11]](#cite_note-12)[[2]](#cite_note-FaTM-3)


Wildmutt is a quadruped. However, he is capable of standing on his hind legs, and he can punch, somewhat awkwardly, by doing this. In addition, his feet are prehensile, as he caught [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") with his foot as she was about to be sucked into space.[[12]](#cite_note-13)


Wildmutt possesses razor-sharp teeth and claws, the latter of which he can use to climb vertical surfaces.


Because of how he does not see in the conventional sense, Wildmutt is not afraid of [Toepick](/wiki/Toepick "Toepick")'s [species](/wiki/Toepick%27s_Species "Toepick's Species").[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-14)


Although he has never trained himself to do so, the porcupine-like quills on Wildmutt's back supplement his sonar and can also serve as weapons, either protecting him while he's rolling or as projectiles fired off at will. However, he can only do this when he reaches adulthood.[[pop-up 1]](#cite_note-15)[[13]](#cite_note-TV-16)

1/4
Wildmutt cannot talk in [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)") languages. Instead, he communicates through a combination of barking and snarling. Due to this, it is extremely difficult for anyone to understand him.


Wildmutt's lack of eyes makes him completely dependent on his other senses (mainly olfactory).


Wildmutt's senses are very sensitive, making him vulnerable to anything that can overwhelm his senses, such as a high-pitched sound.[[14]](#cite_note-17)[[8]](#cite_note-OPaPP1-9)


When infected with a cold, Wildmutt's nostrils (on the sides of his neck) get blocked by mucus, making him unable to smell or hear and rendering him essentially "blind."[[15]](#cite_note-18)



History
-------


### Ben 10


* Wildmutt first appeared in *[And Then There Were 10](/wiki/And_Then_There_Were_10 "And Then There Were 10")*, where he defeated one of [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)")'s [drones](/wiki/Drones "Drones").
* In *[Permanent Retirement](/wiki/Permanent_Retirement "Permanent Retirement")*, Wildmutt and [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") escaped from a group of [Limax](/wiki/Limax "Limax") before they defeated one that was impersonating [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)"), and followed the scent of the Limax ship to find it.
* In *[Last Laugh](/wiki/Last_Laugh "Last Laugh")*, Wildmutt battled the [Circus Freaks](/wiki/Circus_Freak_Trio_(Classic) "Circus Freak Trio (Classic)"), but was scared into defeat by [Zombozo](/wiki/Zombozo_(Classic) "Zombozo (Classic)").
* In *[Lucky Girl](/wiki/Lucky_Girl "Lucky Girl")*, Wildmutt tried to save some construction workers from falling, but Lucky Girl ended up doing so instead.
* In *[Side Effects](/wiki/Side_Effects "Side Effects")*, Wildmutt, due to a cold, was unable to battle [Clancy](/wiki/Clancy "Clancy") and ended up accidentally destroying things, including a market, and almost got Gwen blown up by fireworks.
* In *[Secrets](/wiki/Secrets "Secrets")*, Wildmutt briefly appeared while he was captured. Later, Wildmutt surrendered to Vilgax so the latter would spare Max and Gwen.
	+ Also, Wildmutt appeared as one of the many alien templates used for Vilgax's army in an imaginary sequence.
* In *[Truth](/wiki/Truth "Truth")*, Wildmutt tried to negotiate by talking with two [Vulpimancers](/wiki/Vulpimancer_(Classic) "Vulpimancer (Classic)"), but failed and was forced to fight them.
* In *[The Big Tick](/wiki/The_Big_Tick "The Big Tick")*, Wildmutt failed to defeat the [Great One](/wiki/Great_One "Great One").
* In *[Framed](/wiki/Framed "Framed")*, Gwen mentioned Wildmutt was used by [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") to terrorize Tallahassee, Florida. Later, Ben as Wildmutt was defeated by Kevin as [Upgrade](/wiki/Upgrade_(Classic) "Upgrade (Classic)") and saved a cable car from falling into the sea.
* In *[Ghostfreaked Out](/wiki/Ghostfreaked_Out "Ghostfreaked Out")*, Wildmutt tried to help a kid down from a tree only to be tricked and tortured by [Ghostfreak](/wiki/Ghostfreak "Ghostfreak") in a dream.
* In *[Camp Fear](/wiki/Camp_Fear "Camp Fear")*, Wildmutt searched for Max, during which he discovered the lower parts of [Camp Opinicon](/wiki/Camp_Opinicon "Camp Opinicon") and the sentient plants.
* In *[Tough Luck](/wiki/Tough_Luck "Tough Luck")*, Wildmutt was used in a magic act and battled [Hex](/wiki/Hex_(Classic) "Hex (Classic)") and [Charmcaster](/wiki/Charmcaster_(Classic) "Charmcaster (Classic)").
* In *[Back with a Vengeance](/wiki/Back_with_a_Vengeance "Back with a Vengeance")*, Wildmutt appeared three times. Firstly, he was used by the [Master Control](/wiki/Master_Control "Master Control") and accidentally scared a man in the process, causing him to fall off the boat and prompting Wildmutt to switch into [Ripjaws](/wiki/Ripjaws_(Classic) "Ripjaws (Classic)") to save him. Then, Wildmutt was seen in a photo. Later, in the [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)"), Wildmutt tried to avoid Vilgax and [Kevin 11](/wiki/Kevin_11 "Kevin 11"), but was caught and had to switch into [Stinkfly](/wiki/Stinkfly_(Classic) "Stinkfly (Classic)").
* In *[Midnight Madness](/wiki/Midnight_Madness "Midnight Madness")*, Wildmutt tried to stop some thieves before being chased by the police.
* In *[Merry Christmas](/wiki/Merry_Christmas "Merry Christmas")*, Wildmutt pretended to be a reindeer in order to get Gwen and [Elsgood](/wiki/Elsgood "Elsgood") into [Mr. Jingles](/wiki/Thomas_Jingles "Thomas Jingles")' factory.
* In *[Under Wraps](/wiki/Under_Wraps "Under Wraps")*, Wildmutt hoed a field.
* In *[The Visitor](/wiki/The_Visitor "The Visitor")*, Wildmutt stole Gwen's kite, prompting her to put him under a [spell](/wiki/Spells "Spells") that made several dogs fall in love with him. Wildmutt escaped them by hiding in a tree.
* In ***[Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix](/wiki/Ben_10:_Secret_of_the_Omnitrix "Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix")***, Wildmutt appeared when the Omnitrix malfunctioned. Later, Wildmutt helped [Tetrax](/wiki/Tetrax_Shard_(Classic) "Tetrax Shard (Classic)") search for an intruder, who turned out to be Gwen. He then accidentally opened the air lock and saved Gwen from being sucked into space.
* In *[Don't Drink the Water](/wiki/Don%27t_Drink_the_Water "Don't Drink the Water")*, four-year-old Wildmutt (Wildpup) defeated Hex's digger monster.
* In *[Ben 10 vs. the Negative 10: Part 2](/wiki/Ben_10_vs._the_Negative_10:_Part_2 "Ben 10 vs. the Negative 10: Part 2")*, Wildmutt battled the [Mutant Prairie Dog](/wiki/Mutant_Prairie_Dog "Mutant Prairie Dog") and [Cooper](/wiki/Cooper "Cooper")'s security drones.
* In *[Dogged Pursuit](/wiki/Dogged_Pursuit "Dogged Pursuit")*, Wildmutt saved an old lady's purse and got complimented by her.
* In ***[Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens](/wiki/Ben_10:_Destroy_All_Aliens "Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens")***, Wildmutt attacked Ben in a dream.
* In *[Fame](/wiki/Fame "Fame")*, Wildmutt was seen on TV.

Trivia
------


* Wildmutt served as a replacement for [Digger](/wiki/Ben_10_(Pre-Production)#Digger "Ben 10 (Pre-Production)"), an orange humanoid hero with digging abilities, when the crew decided to switch from superheroes to aliens. [Dave Johnson](/wiki/Dave_Johnson "Dave Johnson") designed him but noted that, like [Ripjaws](/wiki/Ripjaws_(Classic) "Ripjaws (Classic)") and [XLR8](/wiki/XLR8_(Classic) "XLR8 (Classic)"), it was hard for animators to draw him.[[DJ 2]](#cite_note-20)
* Wildmutt is Ben's only alien that is incapable of verbal speech, due to lacking the vocal anatomy to do so.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-21) However, the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") appears to fix this problem with [Ultimate Wildmutt](/wiki/Ultimate_Wildmutt "Ultimate Wildmutt").
	+ In ***Race Against Time***, Wildmutt had a higher-pitched snarl that sounded almost like screaming.
	+ In *[Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Episode) "Ben 10,000 (Episode)")*, [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)")'s Wildmutt has a snarl that sounds similar to a lion.
* Wildmutt is the only alien featured in ***Race Against Time*** to have his voice actor reprise his role from the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10").
* Wildmutt was originally planned to be one of the playable aliens in the *[Game Creator](/wiki/Game_Creator "Game Creator")*, but was soon removed from the game when **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")** aired.
* 11-year-old Wildmutt is replaced with 11-year-old [Snare-oh](/wiki/Snare-oh "Snare-oh") in the opening intro of **Omniverse**'s Arc 4, specifically in the part where the former originally appeared alongside the 11-year-old versions of [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)"), [Feedback](/wiki/Feedback "Feedback"), and [Grey Matter](/wiki/Grey_Matter_(Classic) "Grey Matter (Classic)").
* Had he appeared on-screen, [Mad Ben](/wiki/Mad_Ben "Mad Ben")'s Wildmutt would have some kind of harness and collar with spikes.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-22)
* Wildmutt's name in the Croatian version of the cartoon, "Psina," means "Great Dog," but the same word is sometimes used to name several sharks, especially the [Great white shark](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_white_shark "wikipedia:Great white shark"), which sometimes caused naming confusions between him and Ripjaws.
* Wildmutt has a very similar concept to the *[Doom 3](https://doom.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_3 "w:c:doom:Doom 3")* version of the ["Pinky" Demon](https://doom.fandom.com/wiki/Demon_(Doom_3) "w:c:doom:Demon (Doom 3)") enemy: both are canine-esque beings whose faces display no visible eyes, ears or noses, walk on all fours, roar loudly and have massive powerful jaws with huge pointy teeth sticking out.
* Had Wildmutt been featured in the [reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)"), he would have been more humanoid. The crew did not want him looking anything but a dog,[[DR 1]](#cite_note-23) so he was not included in the show, even though [Duncan Rouleau](/wiki/Duncan_Rouleau "Duncan Rouleau") likes him.[[DR 2]](#cite_note-24)
