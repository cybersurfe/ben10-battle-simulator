Appearance

### Ben as Brainstorm


Brainstorm has a light orange [crab](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crab "wikipedia:Crab")-like appearance with pincers for hands. He can open his skull plates at will to reveal his huge brain, which is pink in color.


In **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")** and **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Brainstorm had six legs and wore a silver and black neck brace with the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on it to help him hold up his head.


In *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, Brainstorm looked mostly the same except his shell was a more vibrant orange and his skull plates were black.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Brainstorm has a black loincloth with a green stripe in the center of it on the bottom of his body. He also has a black stripe in the center of his head with a green stripe running across it. He now has four legs instead of six, and each leg has a single joint instead of two, with a black stripe on each one. The hair-like spikes on Brainstorm's face and forehead are now longer, and his eyes connect to a large black strip that extends around his forehead. He also has two spikes on each elbow, and he wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on a white and green neck brace.



### Albedo as Negative Brainstorm


In **Ultimate Alien**, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Brainstorm looked just like [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s Brainstorm in **Alien Force** and **Ultimate Alien** except his eyes were recolored red and he lacked an Ultimatrix symbol.


In **Omniverse**, Negative Brainstorm looks just like Ben's Brainstorm in the same series, except his shell is recolored pale pink, his teeth are recolored yellowish, his eyes and shell stripes are recolored red, and he wears a red [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") symbol on a red and white neck brace.

Brainstorm's cranium holds an immense brain, giving him super-massive intelligence, much like [Grey Matter](/wiki/Grey_Matter_(Classic) "Grey Matter (Classic)"). He can solve very difficult calculations mentally in roughly seconds, and knows and can visualize equations and movement of objects as if they're actually there.[[6]](#cite_note-GfT-6) He can even determine mechanical values and physical properties by simply looking at them.


With his intellect, Brainstorm can perfectly understand the mindsets and mannerisms of his enemies, take advantage of weaknesses, and any useful objects around him, so he can know exactly what moves to make to take down his enemies. His IQ is shown to be 10^30, or one nonillion.[[4]](#cite_note-TV-4)


Brainstorm can produce and manipulate electricity[[note 1]](#cite_note-8) in a variety of ways, such as electrocuting or launching enemies and disabling or destroying machinery, by opening the exoskeleton plates on his skull. He can also charge up his electrical energy to produce a concentrated beam of electricity strong enough to blast [Ship](/wiki/Ship "Ship") out of the air.[[8]](#cite_note-9) Additionally, he can control the voltage of his electrokinetic attacks.[[4]](#cite_note-TV-4)


Brainstorm can create electric field waves from his brain to block attacks and knock away enemies[[9]](#cite_note-EotB-10) and generate protective force fields powerful enough to topple an [Evolved Arburian Pelarota](/wiki/Evolved_Arburian_Pelarota "Evolved Arburian Pelarota").[[10]](#cite_note-11)


Brainstorm can use his electrokinesis to telekinetically move objects, size and mass ranging from trains to living beings without damaging or hurting them.[[11]](#cite_note-Inferno-12) He can also generate and fire electricity from both his pincers and his brain that can control machinery and other technology, giving him a form of technokinesis.


Brainstorm is strong enough to pin opponents against walls using his pincers[[4]](#cite_note-TV-4) and rip an [Arachnichimp](/wiki/Arachnichimp_(Classic) "Arachnichimp (Classic)")'s webs off his face.[[5]](#cite_note-VoVP2-5) His pincers are also sharp enough to stab through metal.[[4]](#cite_note-TV-4)


Brainstorm is very durable thanks to his shell.[[4]](#cite_note-TV-4)


Brainstorm seems to have enhanced dexterity, as he can use a golf club very easily despite having pincers.[[6]](#cite_note-GfT-6)


Brainstorm is very agile and can perform acrobatics such as somersaults and forward rolls.[[12]](#cite_note-13)


Brainstorm can scale walls[[4]](#cite_note-TV-4) and walk on an Arachnichimp's webs.[[5]](#cite_note-VoVP2-5)


Brainstorm can levitate several stories above the ground.[[11]](#cite_note-Inferno-12)[[9]](#cite_note-EotB-10)


Much like [Ripjaws](/wiki/Ripjaws_(Classic) "Ripjaws (Classic)"), Brainstorm is capable of breathing underwater[[13]](#cite_note-14) thanks to his gills, which he has to keep them moist when he is on land.[[DM 1]](#cite_note-15)


Brainstorm's shell gives him a protective coat-like layer, which can resist the cold temperatures of places such as mountains.[[14]](#cite_note-16)

The sonic screams of a [Sonorosian](/wiki/Sonorosian "Sonorosian") can give Brainstorm a headache and make it hard for him to concentrate, suggesting that concentration is required for him to use his powers.[[15]](#cite_note-17)


As mentioned above, Brainstorm tends to be rather arrogant and egotistical, which can make him forget to do simple things, as shown when he was too busy trying to convince an escaped [Spidermonkey](/wiki/Spidermonkey_(Classic) "Spidermonkey (Classic)") to return to the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") that [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") had to remind him that, being in close proximity, he merely had to reabsorb him.[[5]](#cite_note-VoVP2-5)


Brainstorm's electric attacks are ineffective against weapons that were made specifically for electrokinetic aliens, such as [Aggregor](/wiki/Aggregor "Aggregor")'s [spear](/wiki/Aggregor%27s_Spear "Aggregor's Spear").[[16]](#cite_note-18)


Though Brainstorm can tear it off, he can be immobilized by an [Arachnichimp's](/wiki/Arachnichimp_(Classic) "Arachnichimp (Classic)") webbing if he gets hit with enough of it.[[5]](#cite_note-VoVP2-5)


Brainstorm is vulnerable to being trapped by his species' natural predator, [Vicetopus](/wiki/Vicetopus_(Species) "Vicetopus (Species)"), as demonstrated with [Dr. Psychobos](/wiki/Dr._Psychobos "Dr. Psychobos").[[17]](#cite_note-19)


Even though Brainstorm can breathe underwater, water can short him out.[[18]](#cite_note-20)


If Brainstorm's shell is closed when releasing electricity, it will shock and injure his brain, as also demonstrated with Dr. Psychobos.[[19]](#cite_note-21)

### Alien Force


* Brainstorm first appeared in *[Pier Pressure](/wiki/Pier_Pressure "Pier Pressure")*, where he defeated [Ship](/wiki/Ship "Ship") and protected [Julie](/wiki/Julie_Yamamoto "Julie Yamamoto") from him.
	+ The same scene reoccurred on TV in *[Fame](/wiki/Fame "Fame")*.
* In *[What Are Little Girls Made Of?](/wiki/What_Are_Little_Girls_Made_Of%3F "What Are Little Girls Made Of?")*, Brainstorm defeated [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") in a play-fight.
* In *[Good Copy, Bad Copy](/wiki/Good_Copy,_Bad_Copy "Good Copy, Bad Copy")*, Brainstorm defeated [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill "Big Chill"), but was defeated himself by Negative [Echo Echo](/wiki/Echo_Echo "Echo Echo"), so he switched into [Jetray](/wiki/Jetray_(Classic) "Jetray (Classic)").
* In *[Voided](/wiki/Voided "Voided")*, Brainstorm saved [Manny](/wiki/Manny_Armstrong "Manny Armstrong") from some [Null Guardians](/wiki/Null_Guardians "Null Guardians") and battled [D'Void](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)") before retreating with Manny and [Helen](/wiki/Helen_Wheels "Helen Wheels"). Later, Brainstorm was defeated by D'Void.
* In *[War of the Worlds: Part 2](/wiki/War_of_the_Worlds:_Part_2 "War of the Worlds: Part 2")*, Brainstorm piloted Ship in "Battle Mode" to reach [Augstaka](/wiki/Augstaka "Augstaka"), during which he and [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)") had some banter.
* In *[Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 2](/wiki/Vengeance_of_Vilgax:_Part_2 "Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 2")*, Brainstorm fought and recaptured the escaped [Spidermonkey](/wiki/Spidermonkey_(Classic) "Spidermonkey (Classic)").
* In *[Inferno](/wiki/Inferno "Inferno")*, Brainstorm built a pen for some [Pyroxovores](/wiki/Pyroxovore "Pyroxovore") out of a train and some railway tracks.
* In *[Singlehanded](/wiki/Singlehanded "Singlehanded")*, Brainstorm battled [Sunder](/wiki/Sunder "Sunder") before switching into [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)").

### [Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks](/wiki/Ben_10_Alien_Force:_Vilgax_Attacks "Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks")


Brainstorm is a playable alien in the game. He can launch energy bolts and create large energy blasts. 



#### Xbox 360, Nintendo Wii, PlayStation 2, and PlayStation Portable Versions


Due to the Omnitrix rebooting after Ben's abuse of the quick-change feature during his battle with an animated [Mr. Smoothy](/wiki/Mr._Smoothy "Mr. Smoothy") mascot, Brainstorm was temporarily locked until Ben's arrival on [Encephalonus IV](/wiki/Encephalonus_IV "Encephalonus IV").


Brainstorm is able to use his vast intellect to interact with computers for a variety of purposes, such as uploading a virus or unlocking a sealed gate. Similar to [Chromastone](/wiki/Chromastone "Chromastone"), Brainstorm's unique block animation allows him to redirect focused energy beams to provide power to structures.


Lightning Storm is an unlockable combo move for Brainstorm.


Brainstorm is vital for progression on the [Bellwood](/wiki/Bellwood "Bellwood"), Encephalonus IV, and [MorOtesi](/wiki/MorOtesi "MorOtesi") levels of the game.



#### Nintendo DS Version


Brainstorm and [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill "Big Chill") are reunlocked upon Ben's arrival on Encephalonus IV.


Brainstorm is able to provide power to lifts scattered around Encephalonus IV.



### [Ben 10 Alien Force: The Rise of Hex](/wiki/Ben_10_Alien_Force:_The_Rise_of_Hex "Ben 10 Alien Force: The Rise of Hex")


Brainstorm is a playable alien in the game.


Due to the Omnitrix shorting out after Ben fiddled with it, Brainstorm and [Swampfire](/wiki/Swampfire "Swampfire") were the only aliens available on Ben's arsenal at the beginning of the game.


Brainstorm is able to power generators, in order to move platforms or to open sealed gates.


Brainstorm's small size allows him to navigate areas that most of Ben's aliens and Ben himself are unable to access.


Brainstorm is the only alien form capable of defeating [Techadon](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots") [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)").

Etymology
---------


Brainstorm's name comes from the word "brainstorm", which means a spontaneous group discussion to create new ideas and solve problems. It references how he always creates new ideas and solves problems with his IQ. It also refers to the electrical powers in his brain, as electricity is usually produced from "storm" clouds.



Trivia
------


* Brainstorm had concept art drawn by Eric Canete,[[EC 1]](#cite_note-23) whereas his final design was created by Glenn Wong, with color styling by Chris Hooten.[[GW 1]](#cite_note-24)
* In **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")** and **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Brainstorm originally spoke with a [British](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom "wikipedia:United Kingdom") accent. As of **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Brainstorm now speaks with an [Austrian](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Austria "wikipedia:Austria") accent.
	+ The reason Brainstorm has a British accent in **Alien Force** and **Ultimate Alien** is because the British are stereotyped to being smart and royal.
	+ Brainstorm's accent in **Omniverse** is based on that of [Ludwig Von Drake](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ludwig_Von_Drake "wikipedia:Ludwig Von Drake").[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-25)
		- Coincidentally, Corey Burton voiced Von Drake in numerous Disney projects in the modern era alongside Brainstorm in **Omniverse**.
* Brainstorm's mouth does not move except to show emotion. He does not speak through his mouth because his thoughts are projected as sound waves.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-26)
	+ In *[War of the Worlds: Part 2](/wiki/War_of_the_Worlds:_Part_2 "War of the Worlds: Part 2")*, his mouth opened when he accidentally vomited on [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)"). It opens and closes depending on his motion.
* Bandai cards incorrectly state Brainstorm is a [cetacean](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cetacea "wikipedia:Cetacea"), though he is more based off a [crustacean](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crustacean "wikipedia:Crustacean").
* Brainstorm was originally one of two aliens featured in **Ultimate Alien** who generate yellow electricity; the other being [Shocksquatch](/wiki/Shocksquatch "Shocksquatch").
	+ Brainstorm is currently one of five aliens featured in **Omniverse** who generate green electricity; the others being [Upgrade](/wiki/Upgrade_(Classic) "Upgrade (Classic)"), [Buzzshock](/wiki/Buzzshock_(Classic) "Buzzshock (Classic)"), [Frankenstrike](/wiki/Frankenstrike "Frankenstrike"), and [Nanomech](/wiki/Nanomech "Nanomech").
* In [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt")'s opinion, Brainstorm is a brain alien with some minorly electric side powers, hence why he did not appear in *[Max's Monster](/wiki/Max%27s_Monster "Max's Monster")* alongside the rest of Ben's electric-based aliens.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-27)
* Unlike [Servantis](/wiki/Servantis "Servantis"), Brainstorm is unable to tamper with the thoughts and memories of other beings.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-28)
* [Madison](/wiki/Madison "Madison") likes Brainstorm better than [Rath](/wiki/Rath_(Classic) "Rath (Classic)") and refers to him as "the brain crab."[[20]](#cite_note-29)
* Brainstorm was considered to appear in the [reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)") but did not make the cut as an electric alien for [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Reboot) "Ben Tennyson (Reboot)") because [Shock Rock](/wiki/Shock_Rock "Shock Rock") was created for plot purposes.[[DR 1]](#cite_note-30)
