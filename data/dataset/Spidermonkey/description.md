Appearance
----------


### Ben as Spidermonkey


Spidermonkey is a small [monkey](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Monkey "wikipedia:Monkey")-like alien with four arms, two legs, three fingers on each hand and three toes on each foot, blue fur over a dark blue center, and a tail with two stripes near the tip.


In **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")** and **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Spidermonkey had two pairs of green eyes (making a total of four), wore the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, Spidermonkey looked mostly the same except his eyes were completely black like a spider, although he retained the green pupils. The shape of the fur surrounding the bottom of his face is also slightly different.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Spidermonkey has three pairs of green eyes (making a total of six), his hair is flipped up, the two stripes on his tail have turned white, and the fur on his head is spiky with white stripes. His nose has now shifted to his forehead and its shape has changed. The [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his chest is now connected to two green sashes worn across his chest.



### Albedo as Negative Spidermonkey


In *[Good Copy, Bad Copy](/wiki/Good_Copy,_Bad_Copy "Good Copy, Bad Copy")*, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Spidermonkey looked exactly like Ben's Spidermonkey. In *[The Final Battle: Part 1](/wiki/The_Final_Battle:_Part_1 "The Final Battle: Part 1")*, Negative Spidermonkey's eyes and Ultimatrix symbol were recolored red.


In **Omniverse**, Negative Spidermonkey looks mostly the same as Ben's Spidermonkey except his fur is recolored black, his skin is recolored greyish, the stripes in his hair and on his tail are recolored light grey, and his eyes, sashes, and [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") symbol are recolored red.

1/13
Spidermonkey has the ability to expel webbing from his tail, which was once stated to be as strong as steel. His webbing is very sticky, meaning he can use it to trap opponents or blind them by shooting webbing in their face. Spidermonkey can also shape his webs into slings, or to swing across vast distances.[[2]](#cite_note-2)[[3]](#cite_note-ATTWN-3)


Spidermonkey is highly skilled in hand-to-hand combat.


Spidermonkey's paws can stick to and be used to scale vertical surfaces.


Spidermonkey has enhanced strength, as he was capable of using a lamp post to hit [Ultimate Kevin](/wiki/Ultimate_Kevin "Ultimate Kevin") up a few feet,[[4]](#cite_note-APP2-4) carry [Queen Voratia Rumbletum](/wiki/Queen_Voratia_Rumbletum "Queen Voratia Rumbletum") over his back,[[5]](#cite_note-5) and carry [No Watch Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(No_Watch_Timeline) "Ben Tennyson (No Watch Timeline)") while web-slinging without any difficulty.[[3]](#cite_note-ATTWN-3)


Spidermonkey has enhanced agility and enhanced acrobatic skills.


Spidermonkey is very durable considering his size, as shown when he was able to survive seemingly unharmed after several large pieces of concrete fell on him.[[6]](#cite_note-VoVP2-6)[[1]](#cite_note-TMTCP1-1)


Spidermonkey's feet and tail are both prehensile. They also possess enhanced dexterity, enabling him to hold and carry multiple objects as if they are extra hands.[[7]](#cite_note-7)


Spidermonkey has hearing superior to a [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)")'s; this feat was demonstrated by [Simian](/wiki/Simian "Simian") when he heard [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") and [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") talking about him from several feet away.[[8]](#cite_note-8)


Spidermonkey can show bipedalism for short period of time, such as when he was seen standing on two legs after hitting Ultimate Kevin.

Spidermonkey can get tangled by his own webs.[[9]](#cite_note-9)[[10]](#cite_note-10) Furthermore, enemies can easily use his webs to their advantage.[[11]](#cite_note-11)


Spidermonkey's webs can be frozen and made brittle by the ice breath of a [Polar Manzardill](/wiki/Polar_Manzardill "Polar Manzardill").[[12]](#cite_note-12) They can also be dissolved by the energy generated by a [Crystalsapien](/wiki/Crystalsapien "Crystalsapien").[[13]](#cite_note-13)


Spidermonkey's webs can be torn apart by species with as much or more strength than a [Cerebrocrustacean](/wiki/Cerebrocrustacean "Cerebrocrustacean").[[6]](#cite_note-VoVP2-6)


Spidermonkey is vulnerable to being blown away by a [Geochelone Aerio](/wiki/Geochelone_Aerio "Geochelone Aerio")'s tornadoes.[[4]](#cite_note-APP2-4)


Being an [Arachnichimp](/wiki/Arachnichimp_(Classic) "Arachnichimp (Classic)"), Spidermonkey is vulnerable to his natural predator, the [Root Shark](/wiki/Root_Shark "Root Shark").[[14]](#cite_note-14)


Spidermonkey can be rendered unconscious if hit by a quill from a member of [Argit](/wiki/Argit "Argit")'s [species](/wiki/Argit%27s_Species "Argit's Species"), as demonstrated with [Simian](/wiki/Simian "Simian").[[15]](#cite_note-15)



History
-------


### Alien Force


* Spidermonkey first appeared in *[Be-Knighted](/wiki/Be-Knighted "Be-Knighted")*, where he replaced the [Dragon](/wiki/Dragon "Dragon")'s broken [Universal Translator](/wiki/Universal_Translator "Universal Translator") with a new one.
* In *[What Are Little Girls Made Of?](/wiki/What_Are_Little_Girls_Made_Of%3F "What Are Little Girls Made Of?")*, Spidermonkey was defeated by [Verdona](/wiki/Verdona "Verdona").
* In *[Darkstar Rising](/wiki/Darkstar_Rising "Darkstar Rising")*, Spidermonkey briefly fought [Darkstar](/wiki/Michael_Morningstar_(Classic) "Michael Morningstar (Classic)") before switching into [Jetray](/wiki/Jetray_(Classic) "Jetray (Classic)").
* In *[Good Copy, Bad Copy](/wiki/Good_Copy,_Bad_Copy "Good Copy, Bad Copy")*, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Spidermonkey defeated Jetray and was about to fight [Chromastone](/wiki/Chromastone "Chromastone") before the latter ended up reverting.
* In *[Birds of a Feather](/wiki/Birds_of_a_Feather "Birds of a Feather")*, Spidermonkey battled some [DNAliens](/wiki/DNAliens "DNAliens") and was saved by [Simian](/wiki/Simian "Simian").
* In *[Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 2](/wiki/Vengeance_of_Vilgax:_Part_2 "Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 2")*, Spidermonkey escaped the Omnitrix and battled [Brainstorm](/wiki/Brainstorm "Brainstorm") before getting recaptured.
* In *[Inferno](/wiki/Inferno "Inferno")*, Spidermonkey fought [Vulkanus](/wiki/Vulkanus "Vulkanus") before switching into Jetray to stop a bomb from exploding.
* In *[Simple](/wiki/Simple "Simple")*, Spidermonkey tried to stop the Blue Team, but failed and was captured by them for execution before being rescued by [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") and [Probity](/wiki/Probity "Probity").
* In *[Fool's Gold](/wiki/Fool%27s_Gold "Fool's Gold")*, Spidermonkey stopped some [aliens](/wiki/Decka%27s_Species "Decka's Species") from causing trouble in [Walton](/wiki/Walton "Walton").
* In *[Singlehanded](/wiki/Singlehanded "Singlehanded")*, Spidermonkey fought and tried to escape from [Sunder](/wiki/Sunder "Sunder"), but was eventually trapped by him, so he switched into [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)").
* In *[If All Else Fails](/wiki/If_All_Else_Fails "If All Else Fails")*, Spidermonkey battled a [Highbreed](/wiki/Highbreed "Highbreed").
* In *[Busy Box](/wiki/Busy_Box "Busy Box")*, Spidermonkey was defeated by the [Naljian Destructor](/wiki/Naljian_Destructor "Naljian Destructor").
* In *[The Final Battle: Part 1](/wiki/The_Final_Battle:_Part_1 "The Final Battle: Part 1")*, Negative Spidermonkey failed to capture [Grandpa Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)").
* In *[The Final Battle: Part 2](/wiki/The_Final_Battle:_Part_2 "The Final Battle: Part 2")*, Spidermonkey was briefly used by [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)")'s [Bioids](/wiki/Bioids "Bioids").

* In *[Fame](/wiki/Fame "Fame")*, Spidermonkey was seen on TV. Later, Spidermonkey fought [Bivalvan](/wiki/Bivalvan "Bivalvan") before going [Ultimate](/wiki/Ultimate_Spidermonkey "Ultimate Spidermonkey"). Spidermonkey appeared again when he was changing back to [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)").
* In *[Video Games](/wiki/Video_Games "Video Games")*, Spidermonkey failed to defeat the [Stalker](/wiki/Stalker "Stalker"), so he switched into [Swampfire](/wiki/Swampfire "Swampfire").
* In *[Andreas' Fault](/wiki/Andreas%27_Fault "Andreas' Fault")*, Spidermonkey battled some [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights") in [Mr. Smoothy](/wiki/Mr._Smoothy "Mr. Smoothy").
* In *[Hero Time](/wiki/Hero_Time "Hero Time")*, Spidermonkey saved [Jennifer Nocturne](/wiki/Jennifer_Nocturne "Jennifer Nocturne") from some criminals.
* In *[Reflected Glory](/wiki/Reflected_Glory "Reflected Glory")*, Spidermonkey fought [Psyphon](/wiki/Psyphon "Psyphon") before going Ultimate.
* In *[Perplexahedron](/wiki/Perplexahedron_(Episode) "Perplexahedron (Episode)")*, Spidermonkey escaped an acid room in the [Perplexahedron](/wiki/Perplexahedron "Perplexahedron") with [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)").
* In *[Absolute Power: Part 2](/wiki/Absolute_Power:_Part_2 "Absolute Power: Part 2")*, Spidermonkey was defeated by [Ultimate Kevin](/wiki/Ultimate_Kevin "Ultimate Kevin").
* In *[The Transmogrification of Eunice](/wiki/The_Transmogrification_of_Eunice "The Transmogrification of Eunice")*, Spidermonkey battled [Sunder](/wiki/Sunder "Sunder") before going Ultimate. Spidermonkey appeared again later when he was changing back to Ben.
* In *[It's Not Easy Being Gwen](/wiki/It%27s_Not_Easy_Being_Gwen "It's Not Easy Being Gwen")*, Spidermonkey met up with [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)").
* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")* Spidermonkey fought some of [Eon](/wiki/Eon "Eon")'s [servants](/wiki/Eon%27s_Servants "Eon's Servants").
* In *[The Purge](/wiki/The_Purge "The Purge")*, Spidermonkey went Ultimate to battle [Driscoll](/wiki/Driscoll "Driscoll").
* In *[Simian Says](/wiki/Simian_Says "Simian Says")*, Spidermonkey fought [Mizaru](/wiki/Mizaru "Mizaru") before going Ultimate. Spidermonkey appeared again later when he was changing back to Ben.
* In *[Double or Nothing](/wiki/Double_or_Nothing "Double or Nothing")*, Spidermonkey went Ultimate to battle [Hugh](/wiki/Hugh "Hugh"). Later, he went Ultimate to battle [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo").
* In *[The Perfect Girlfriend](/wiki/The_Perfect_Girlfriend "The Perfect Girlfriend")*, Spidermonkey tried to fix an elevator and was saved by "[Julie](/wiki/Elena_Validus "Elena Validus")" when it nearly fell on him.
* In *[The Mother of All Vreedles](/wiki/The_Mother_of_All_Vreedles "The Mother of All Vreedles")*, Spidermonkey went to battle [the](/wiki/Ma_Vreedle "Ma Vreedle") [Vre](/wiki/Octagon_Vreedle "Octagon Vreedle")[edl](/wiki/Rhomboid_Vreedle "Rhomboid Vreedle")[es](/wiki/Pretty_Boy_Vreedle "Pretty Boy Vreedle") before going Ultimate. Spidermonkey appeared again later when he was changing back to Ben.
* In *[Ben 10/Generator Rex: Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, Spidermonkey appeared as one of the many aliens when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was explaining his story to [Rex](/wiki/Rex_Salazar "Rex Salazar").


### Omniverse


* In *[The More Things Change: Part 1](/wiki/The_More_Things_Change:_Part_1 "The More Things Change: Part 1")*, Spidermonkey fought [Crabdozer](/wiki/Crabdozer "Crabdozer").
* In *[So Long, and Thanks for All the Smoothies](/wiki/So_Long,_and_Thanks_for_All_the_Smoothies "So Long, and Thanks for All the Smoothies")*, Spidermonkey defeated two [Incursean](/wiki/Incursean "Incursean") soldiers.
* In *[Have I Got a Deal for You](/wiki/Have_I_Got_a_Deal_for_You "Have I Got a Deal for You")*, Spidermonkey and [Pax](/wiki/Pax "Pax") snuck into [Professor Hokestar](/wiki/Blarney_T._Hokestar "Blarney T. Hokestar")'s chamber and found a [Screegit](/wiki/Screegit "Screegit").
* In *[Malefactor](/wiki/Malefactor "Malefactor")*, a kid demanded that [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") turned into Spidermonkey so he could dunk him in the Dunk Ben 10 game.
* In *[Tummy Trouble](/wiki/Tummy_Trouble "Tummy Trouble")*, Spidermonkey carried [Queen Rumbletum](/wiki/Queen_Voratia_Rumbletum "Queen Voratia Rumbletum") to safety and battled [Attea](/wiki/Attea "Attea").
* In *[Store 23](/wiki/Store_23 "Store 23")*, Spidermonkey was one of several aliens [Ben Prime](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was showcasing for [Ben 23](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Dimension_23) "Ben Tennyson (Dimension 23)").
* In *[And Then There Were None](/wiki/And_Then_There_Were_None "And Then There Were None")*, Spidermonkey saved [No Watch Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(No_Watch_Timeline) "Ben Tennyson (No Watch Timeline)") from the evil Bens and travelled to the latter's [version](/wiki/No_Watch_Timeline "No Watch Timeline") of [Los Soledad](/wiki/Los_Soledad "Los Soledad") with him so they could meet up with [Professor Paradox](/wiki/Professor_Paradox "Professor Paradox").
* In *[Cough It Up](/wiki/Cough_It_Up "Cough It Up")*, Spidermonkey battled [Thunderpig](/wiki/Thunderpig "Thunderpig") and [EightEight](/wiki/EightEight "EightEight").
* In *[Malgax Attacks](/wiki/Malgax_Attacks "Malgax Attacks")*, Negative Spidermonkey briefly appeared before going Ultimate.
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Spidermonkey appeared for a short time when Ben was cycling through his aliens.


### [Gwen Alters the Past Timeline](/wiki/Gwen_Alters_the_Past_Timeline "Gwen Alters the Past Timeline")


* In *[Time Heals](/wiki/Time_Heals "Time Heals")*, Spidermonkey was found by the Prime Timeline Gwen in [Hex](/wiki/Hex_(Classic) "Hex (Classic)")'s torture chamber.


### Future (Ultimate Alien)


* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, [Ultimate Ben](/wiki/Ultimate_Ben "Ultimate Ben") used Spidermonkey's agility to battle some of [Eon's servants](/wiki/Eon%27s_Servants "Eon's Servants") on the [Rust Bucket 3](/wiki/Rust_Bucket_3 "Rust Bucket 3").

### [Ben 10: Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force_(Video_Game) "Ben 10: Alien Force (Video Game)")


Spidermonkey is a playable alien in the Nintendo Wii, PlayStation 2, and PlayStation Portable versions of the game.


Spidermonkey is initially unavailable, but is unlocked at the beginning of The Forest Mid-evil/Medieval level.


Spidermonkey is able to scale certain surfaces, as well as web himself up anchor points, thereby reaching otherwise inaccessible areas.


Spidermonkey is vital for progression on The Forest Mid-evil/Medieval, Bombs Away!, A Few Bad Eggs, Plumber Trouble, Rural Rumble, and Running on Autopilot levels of the game.



### [Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks](/wiki/Ben_10_Alien_Force:_Vilgax_Attacks "Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks")


Spidermonkey is a playable alien in the game.



#### Xbox 360, Nintendo Wii, PlayStation 2, and PlayStation Portable Versions


Due to the Omnitrix rebooting after Ben's abuse of the quick-change feature during his battle with an animated [Mr. Smoothy](/wiki/Mr._Smoothy "Mr. Smoothy") mascot, Spidermonkey, along with [Swampfire](/wiki/Swampfire "Swampfire"), [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill "Big Chill"), [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)"), and [Cannonbolt](/wiki/Cannonbolt_(Classic) "Cannonbolt (Classic)") were the only aliens available on Ben's arsenal upon his arrival on [Vulpin](/wiki/Vulpin_(Classic) "Vulpin (Classic)").


Spidermonkey is able to grab onto ledges and climb pipes, as well as web swing using anchor points, thereby reaching otherwise inaccessible areas.


Flying Monkey Kick is an unlockable combo move for Spidermonkey.


Spidermonkey is vital for progression on the [Bellwood](/wiki/Bellwood_(Classic) "Bellwood (Classic)"), Vulpin, [Terradino](/wiki/Terradino_(Classic) "Terradino (Classic)"), [Encephalonus IV](/wiki/Encephalonus_IV "Encephalonus IV"), [Anur Phaetos](/wiki/Anur_Phaetos_(Classic) "Anur Phaetos (Classic)"), and [MorOtesi](/wiki/MorOtesi "MorOtesi") levels of the game.



#### Nintendo DS Version


Spidermonkey and Humungousaur are re-unlocked upon Ben's arrival on Terradino.


Spidermonkey is able to scale vertical surfaces and web swing from tree branches and floating rocks.


Spidermonkey is vital for progression on the Bellwood, Terradino, Encephalonus IV and [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)") levels of the game.



### [Ben 10 Alien Force: The Rise of Hex](/wiki/Ben_10_Alien_Force:_The_Rise_of_Hex "Ben 10 Alien Force: The Rise of Hex")


Spidermonkey is a playable alien in the game.


Due to the Omnitrix shorting out after Ben fiddled with it, Spidermonkey was temporarily locked until the beginning of Level 5.


Spidermonkey is able to scale rough surfaces, as well as web himself up specially designated platforms, thus reaching otherwise inaccessible areas.


Spidermonkey's small size allows him to navigate areas most of Ben's aliens and Ben himself are unable to access.



### [Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction](/wiki/Ben_10_Ultimate_Alien:_Cosmic_Destruction "Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction")


Spidermonkey is a playable alien in the game.


Similar to [Echo Echo](/wiki/Echo_Echo "Echo Echo"), Spidermonkey's small size allows him to fit into small passages other aliens cannot.


Spidermonkey is vital for progression on the Devil's Tower, Tokyo Nights, and The Final Battle levels of the game.



### [Ben 10: Galactic Racing](/wiki/Ben_10:_Galactic_Racing "Ben 10: Galactic Racing")


Spidermonkey is a playable alien in the PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, and Nintendo Wii versions of the game.

Etymology
---------


Spidermonkey is named after the [spider monkey](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/spider_monkey "wikipedia:spider monkey"), an endangered species of monkeys, as well as being an amalgamation of words "[spider](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spider "wikipedia:Spider")" and "monkey", referencing his spider-based powers and monkey-like appearance.



Trivia
------


* Spidermonkey's powers and personality are similar to those of [Marvel Comics](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marvel_Comics "wikipedia:Marvel Comics") superhero [Spider-Man](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spider-Man "wikipedia:Spider-Man").
* Spidermonkey is one of [Man of Action](/wiki/Man_of_Action "Man of Action")'s favorite aliens.[[DR 1]](#cite_note-16)
* Spidermonkey is one of the five aliens who escaped the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)"); the others are [Ghostfreak](/wiki/Ghostfreak "Ghostfreak"), [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)"), [Goop](/wiki/Goop_(Classic) "Goop (Classic)"), and [Chromastone](/wiki/Chromastone_(Classic) "Chromastone (Classic)").
* Spidermonkey was conceived by [Dwayne McDuffie](/wiki/Dwayne_McDuffie "Dwayne McDuffie") and [Glen Murakami](/wiki/Glen_Murakami "Glen Murakami").[[DR 2]](#cite_note-17)
* In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Spidermonkey's voice has become higher-pitched, and now talks with monkey sounds and chirps in between words.
* During the pre-production stage of **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")**, Spidermonkey had concept art drawn by Eric Canete.[[EC 1]](#cite_note-18)
* [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") came up with Spidermonkey's **Omniverse** hairstyle from Marvel Comics' [Beast](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beast_(comics) "wikipedia:Beast (comics)") from the **[X-Men](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/X-Men "wikipedia:X-Men")** series.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-19)
	+ Negative Spidermonkey's color scheme in **Omniverse** is similar to that of Negative Ultimate Spidermonkey's design in the same show.
* Spidermonkey is one of Dee Bradley Baker's favorite aliens to voice, the other being [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill "Big Chill").[[DBB 1]](#cite_note-20)[[DBB 2]](#cite_note-21)
