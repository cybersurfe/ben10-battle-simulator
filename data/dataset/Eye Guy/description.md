Appearance
----------


Eye Guy's entire upper-body is covered with green eyes, although he ironically has no eyes on his face, which consists of a mouth and a very large pair of bat-like ears. He has dark yellow skin with lighter yellow skin on his chest. His entire skeleton is made of cartilage, not bones.[[OV Brief 1]](#cite_note-link-4)


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Eye Guy wore black pants with white bands on them and a white belt with the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol on it. He had light green eyes with dark green pupils, and one giant primary eye in the center of his chest.


His feet were light in colour, but in all following designs his feet match the same dark yellow as on his body.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Eye Guy looked the same as before, except the big eye on his chest was replaced by his [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, 16-year-old Eye Guy's eyes are now bigger, recolored slightly brighter, and placed symmetrically on his body. There are also now eyes on his palms. His pants now have green cuffs replacing the white bands, the main belt where the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol is located is recolored green with a white stripe, and he additionally wears green cuffs on his hands.


11-year-old Eye Guy looks mostly the same as he did in the original series, albeit he has the palm eyes, eye placement, and pupil color of his 16-year-old self. He wears white bands respective to the green cuffs and the original Omnitrix symbol on his belt is recolored green.

1/10
From any of the eyes on his torso, Eye Guy can launch green[[note 2]](#cite_note-8) energy beams. By closing all of his other eyes, or by merging all of his other eyes into one area, a larger eye will open on Eye Guy's chest and from it, he can launch a more powerful optic blast. He can also stretch his bigger eye out of his chest on a stalk to better aim it towards targets.


Eye Guy's eyes can move throughout his body and merge to create different types of beams, such as merging three on his shoulder to fire a freeze ray that encases and immobilizes a target in ice on contact.[[5]](#cite_note-B10vstN10P2-9)


Besides energy beams and freeze rays, Eye Guy's eyes can shoot green bolts of electricity capable of overloading and frying machinery,[[6]](#cite_note-SotO-10) fire blasts,[[7]](#cite_note-TUH-11) and sticky goop.[[OV Brief 1]](#cite_note-link-4)


Similar to the compound vision of an insect, Eye Guy's eyes give him 360-degree vision,[[note 3]](#cite_note-14) making it extremely difficult to sneak up on him.[[5]](#cite_note-B10vstN10P2-9) Furthermore, he can "smell" and see trails of certain chemicals with his eyes, as shown when he was able to see the trail of [Mr. Baumann](/wiki/Ignacius_Baumann "Ignacius Baumann")'s cologne.[[10]](#cite_note-15)


Eye Guy is capable of performing acrobatic and gymnastic feats, as well as jumping several feet high.[[6]](#cite_note-SotO-10)[[11]](#cite_note-16)


Eye Guy has good reflexes, capable of dodging incoming attacks.[[1]](#cite_note-TaMMMBW2-1)

History
-------


### Ben 10


* Eye Guy was unlocked offscreen prior to *[Ben 10 vs. the Negative 10: Part 1](/wiki/Ben_10_vs._the_Negative_10:_Part_1 "Ben 10 vs. the Negative 10: Part 1")*.
* Chronologically, Eye Guy first appeared in *[Ben 10 vs. the Negative 10: Part 2](/wiki/Ben_10_vs._the_Negative_10:_Part_2 "Ben 10 vs. the Negative 10: Part 2")*, where he battled the [Negative 10](/wiki/Negative_10 "Negative 10").
* In the Gold Edition of ***[Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix](/wiki/Ben_10:_Secret_of_the_Omnitrix "Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix")***, Eye Guy fought a [Mutant Hornet](/wiki/Mutant_Hornet "Mutant Hornet") and destroyed [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)")'s DNA bomb, accidentally activating the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")'s Self-Destruct Mode in the process.


### Five Years Prior to Omniverse


* In *[Evil's Encore](/wiki/Evil%27s_Encore "Evil's Encore")*, Eye Guy battled [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)") and his [Mutant Giraffe](/wiki/Mutant_Giraffe "Mutant Giraffe"), but failed to defeat them when he reverted.
* In *[Weapon XI: Part 2](/wiki/Weapon_XI:_Part_2 "Weapon XI: Part 2")*, Eye Guy was defeated by [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") when he [absorbed some power](/wiki/Kevin%27s_Omniverse_Flashback_Mutation "Kevin's Omniverse Flashback Mutation") from the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)").
* In *[From Hedorium to Eternity](/wiki/From_Hedorium_to_Eternity "From Hedorium to Eternity")*, Eye Guy defeated a [possessed](/wiki/Ectonurite "Ectonurite") [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)").


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, Eye Guy was re-unlocked by [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Ultimate_Alien) "Ben 10,000 (Ultimate Alien)").


### Omniverse


* In *[Rad](/wiki/Rad "Rad")*, Eye Guy was defeated by the [Gracklflint](/wiki/Gracklflint "Gracklflint").
* In *[Mud Is Thicker Than Water](/wiki/Mud_Is_Thicker_Than_Water "Mud Is Thicker Than Water")*, Eye Guy helped [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") stabilize the power core of the [Plumber Headquarters](/wiki/Plumber_Headquarters "Plumber Headquarters").
* In *[The Ultimate Heist](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Heist "The Ultimate Heist")*, Eye Guy appeared before [Blukic](/wiki/Blukic "Blukic") and [Driba](/wiki/Driba "Driba"). Later, Eye Guy battled [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") [Ultimate Humungousaur](/wiki/Ultimate_Humungousaur "Ultimate Humungousaur"), but was defeated by Negative [Ultimate Echo Echo](/wiki/Ultimate_Echo_Echo "Ultimate Echo Echo").
* In *[A Fistful of Brains](/wiki/A_Fistful_of_Brains "A Fistful of Brains")*, Eye Guy was defeated by [Ultimate Albedo](/wiki/Ultimate_Albedo "Ultimate Albedo").
* In *[Something Zombozo This Way Comes](/wiki/Something_Zombozo_This_Way_Comes "Something Zombozo This Way Comes")*, Eye Guy was frightened by [Zombozo](/wiki/Zombozo_(Classic) "Zombozo (Classic)") and accidentally switched himself into [Toepick](/wiki/Toepick "Toepick").
* In *[Mystery, Incorporeal](/wiki/Mystery,_Incorporeal "Mystery, Incorporeal")*, Eye Guy battled some [Stone Creatures](/wiki/Stone_Creatures "Stone Creatures").
* In *[Universe vs. Tennyson](/wiki/Universe_vs._Tennyson "Universe vs. Tennyson")*, Eye Guy was defeated by the [Galactic Gladiator](/wiki/Galactic_Gladiator "Galactic Gladiator").
* In *[Charm School](/wiki/Charm_School "Charm School")*, Eye Guy looked for [Charmcaster](/wiki/Charmcaster_(Classic) "Charmcaster (Classic)") in the campus of [Friedkin University](/wiki/Friedkin_University "Friedkin University") before being scared by Gwen.
* In *[The Ballad of Mr. Baumann](/wiki/The_Ballad_of_Mr._Baumann "The Ballad of Mr. Baumann")*, Eye Guy followed [Mr. Baumann](/wiki/Ignacius_Baumann "Ignacius Baumann")'s cologne trail to find him and [Sheelane](/wiki/Sheelane "Sheelane").
* In *[It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 2](/wiki/It%27s_a_Mad,_Mad,_Mad_Ben_World:_Part_2 "It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 2")*, Eye Guy defeated [Mad Rook](/wiki/Mad_Rook "Mad Rook") with the help of [Freezeghost](/wiki/Freezeghost "Freezeghost").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Eye Guy appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

Trivia
------


* Eye Guy is the first alien to be unlocked off-screen; this trend would later be followed by [Arctiguana](/wiki/Arctiguana "Arctiguana"), [Feedback](/wiki/Feedback "Feedback"), and [Fasttrack](/wiki/Fasttrack "Fasttrack").
* In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Eye Guy has a tendency to make puns involving the word "eye" or organs related to the eye.
* Eye Guy shares the same name with a [**Power Rangers** villain](https://powerrangers.fandom.com/wiki/Eye_Guy "powerrangers:Eye Guy").
* 11-year-old Eye Guy replaces 11-year-old Feedback in the opening intro as of **Omniverse**'s Arc 4, specifically in the part where the latter originally appeared alongside the 11-year-old versions of [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)"), [Grey Matter](/wiki/Grey_Matter_(Classic) "Grey Matter (Classic)"), and [Wildmutt](/wiki/Wildmutt "Wildmutt").
* Eye Guy is the only alien used by [Ben Prime](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") and Ben 23 to share the same name. [Feedback](/wiki/Feedback_(Dimension_23) "Feedback (Dimension 23)") does not count because he was originally named "Plug Man".[[12]](#cite_note-17)
	+ This leads to Ben Prime admitting that the name is "pretty lame".[[1]](#cite_note-TaMMMBW2-1)
