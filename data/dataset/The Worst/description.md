Appearance
----------


The Worst is a yellow, fat, round blob-like alien who wears a pair of green underwear with white stripes on it. He has elephant-like feet with three toes and bear-like hands with three claws. He also has wart-like bumps on his forehead and both arms.


The Worst's [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol is located on his belly button.



Powers and Abilities
--------------------


* ![]()Unbreakable Skin
* ![]()Enhanced Durability/Heat Resistance
* ![]()Malleable Skin
1/3
The Worst is indestructible on the outside of his body, as he is able to withstand heavy physical attacks due to his soft, blob-like body.[[2]](#cite_note-3)[[3]](#cite_note-NHAB-4)[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-5)


The Worst has malleable skin, allowing him to absorb hits.[[3]](#cite_note-NHAB-4)


The Worst can survive energy attacks, such as [Exo-Skull](/wiki/Exo-Skull "Exo-Skull")'s laser.[[1]](#cite_note-IaMMMBWP1-1)


The Worst is resistant against extreme heat and immune to acids.[[1]](#cite_note-IaMMMBWP1-1)


The Worst has claws that are like thick blunt nails.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-claws-6)



Weaknesses
----------


 [![](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/e/ee/NHAB_%28171.1%29.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/218?cb=20160211145642)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/e/ee/NHAB_%28171.1%29.png/revision/latest?cb=20160211145642)  Pain Feeling

  


| “
 | *I said you were indestructible. I didn't say it wouldn't hurt.* | ”
 |
|  – [Rook](/wiki/Rook_Blonko "Rook Blonko") to The Worst during the latter's fight with [Malice](/wiki/Malice "Malice").[[3]](#cite_note-NHAB-4) |


Although he is indestructible, The Worst still feels pain. 


Sharpened [Taydenite](/wiki/Taydenite "Taydenite") can possibly break The Worst's skin.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-7)


The Worst lacks any offensive options, basically becoming a punching bag for any capable enemy. In general, he has many attributes that could hinder him in battle.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-8)


The Worst needs oxygen to breathe, meaning that he can neither breathe underwater nor survive in the vacuum of space.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-9)


The Worst's claws are not really sharp.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-claws-6)


Like other organic beings, The Worst is vulnerable to being aged to dust by the time rays of a [Chronosapien](/wiki/Chronosapien "Chronosapien") or a [Chronian](/wiki/Chronian "Chronian"), though it would take a while due to all the dead skin and calluses.[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-10)



History
-------


### Omniverse


* The Worst first appeared in *[Outbreak](/wiki/Outbreak "Outbreak")*, where he was accidentally unlocked by [Blukic](/wiki/Blukic "Blukic") and [Driba](/wiki/Driba "Driba") when they were trying to fix the Omnitrix and tried to stop [Liam](/wiki/Liam "Liam"), [Fistina](/wiki/Fistina "Fistina"), [Bubble Helmet](/wiki/Bubble_Helmet "Bubble Helmet"), and [Psyphon](/wiki/Psyphon "Psyphon") along with a group of [Plumbers](/wiki/Plumbers "Plumbers").
* In *[No Honor Among Bros](/wiki/No_Honor_Among_Bros "No Honor Among Bros")*, The Worst defeated [Malice](/wiki/Malice "Malice") by taking so much of a beating that the latter became exhausted and lost.
* In *[It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 1](/wiki/It%27s_a_Mad,_Mad,_Mad_Ben_World:_Part_1 "It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 1")*, The Worst caused [Maltruant](/wiki/Maltruant "Maltruant"), [Dr. Psychobos](/wiki/Dr._Psychobos "Dr. Psychobos"), [Exo-Skull](/wiki/Exo-Skull "Exo-Skull"), [Subdora](/wiki/Subdora "Subdora"), [Toolboxx](/wiki/Toolboxx "Toolboxx") and himself to be transported to [Mad Ben](/wiki/Mad_Ben "Mad Ben")'s [timeline](/wiki/Mad_Ben%27s_Dimension "Mad Ben's Dimension").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, The Worst appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

Trivia
------


* The Worst comes from the same planet as [Ball Weevil](/wiki/Ball_Weevil "Ball Weevil").[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-11)
* The Worst is Ben's least favorite alien.[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-12)
* The Worst is one of four aliens unlocked by [Driba](/wiki/Driba "Driba") and [Blukic](/wiki/Blukic "Blukic"); the other three being [Walkatrout](/wiki/Walkatrout "Walkatrout"), [Pesky Dust](/wiki/Pesky_Dust "Pesky Dust"), and [Mole-Stache](/wiki/Mole-Stache "Mole-Stache").[[4]](#cite_note-13)
* In his first appearance, The Worst shared the same voice as Ben due to the Omnitrix malfunctioning. The out-of-universe reason was because the crew originally did not envision him as a usable alien for later episodes.[[DJW 10]](#cite_note-14) The Worst's voice was eventually changed in *No Honor Among Bros* because the crew liked how he turned out.[[DJW 11]](#cite_note-15)
* [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo")'s [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") does not contain The Worst's DNA.[[DJW 12]](#cite_note-16)
* Like all the other Atrocians, The Worst was based on [Jae Hong Kim](/wiki/Jae_Hong_Kim "Jae Hong Kim")'s storyboards, as that's the way he would draw aliens.[[DJW 13]](#cite_note-17)
* The Worst was among the aliens considered to be featured in the [reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)").[[DR 1]](#cite_note-18)
