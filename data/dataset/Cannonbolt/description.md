Appearance

### Ben as Cannonbolt


Cannonbolt is a hulking, broad-shouldered alien covered in natural, yellow armor plating on his back, shoulders, and the backs of his arms. He is mostly white with black on his upper half and has four claws on both of his hands, having a color scheme and claws similar to that of a panda. Cannonbolt is covered in fur across the thickest parts of his body, while every other part except for his elbows and knees is short fur.[[DR 1]](#cite_note-3)


When Cannonbolt turns into a sphere, he rolls up into a ball, with his backplates and the plates on his hands covering him. His sphere form has semi-circular stripes across his body, with black lines around the circular plates, and his side plates are the plates on his shoulders. It is described as a position similar to a [roly poly](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Armadillidiidae "wikipedia:Armadillidiidae").[[2]](#cite_note-4)[[3]](#cite_note-HU-5)


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Cannonbolt had sharp teeth, yellow eyes, and wore the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol on his forehead.


In *[Game Over](/wiki/Game_Over "Game Over")*, while inside [Sumo Slammers Smackdown](/wiki/Sumo_Slammers_(Classic) "Sumo Slammers (Classic)"), he wore black underwear with a brown outline and had skin sticking out of his head.


In **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")** and **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Cannonbolt's teeth became fuller and human-like, the black stripe on his chest was gone, his eyes were recolored green, and he wore the original Omnitrix/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, Cannonbolt looks exactly the same as his **Alien Force/Ultimate Alien** design, but his colors were altered to fit **Generator Rex**'s art style.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Cannonbolt regains his black stripe from the original series, which now goes down to his belly/chest. His eyes are no longer connected to his shoulders, his palms are now flat instead of bulging, the yellow bolts on his body are now hexagonal and there are three plates on his back instead of two. Depending on the episode, Cannonbolt's teeth is either dull or sharp like his previous series' designs.


16-year-old Cannonbolt wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his chest, while 11-year-old Cannonbolt wears the original Omnitrix symbol (which has been recolored green) on his forehead to reflect his original appearance.



### Ben 10,000 as Cannonbolt


[Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)")'s Cannonbolt looked similar to his 10-year-old self, but with a different color scheme for the legs, which sported a black pants-like design. His claws were longer and outlined in grey, his eyes were closer to his shoulder armor, and the Omnitrix symbol was higher up, being on the top of his head rather than his forehead.



### Gwen 10 as Cannonbolt


[Gwen 10](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Gweniverse) "Gwen Tennyson (Gweniverse)") as Cannonbolt looks similar to [Rollaway](/wiki/Rollaway "Rollaway"), albeit the markings on her eyes are upturned, similar to eyeliner. She has feminine lips, freckles, a hair clip on her left side, pink eyes and a cat symbol-shaped Omnitrix on her chest. Her skin is lavender, while her bolts and lips are purple. She has black markings on her torso and pelvis in a similar design to a bikini. Her bolts are also smaller than Ben's Cannonbolt and Rollaway.


When curled into her shell, Cannonbolt looks just like Rollaway in his shell.

1/17
Cannonbolt can curl into a ball, encasing himself in his plating. In general, the Arburian Pelarota species mostly used this maneuver for defense, like an [armadillo](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Armadillo "wikipedia:Armadillo"). However, since Ben did not know about this when discovering Cannonbolt, he weaponized Cannonbolt's originally defensive capabilities.


When rolled up, Cannonbolt becomes like a motorized wheel that can move at great speed (because of chemicals in his exo-plates that reduce friction)[[pop-up 1]](#cite_note-6)[[4]](#cite_note-Game_Over-7) and can increase his rotational momentum to slam into and/or run over enemies. Cannonbolt is able to maneuver around his surroundings in ball form thanks to the short fur on his body, registering shifts in air and motion around him. Between that and his excellent hearing, Cannonbolt receives something akin to echolocation - giving him "eyeless" vision.[[DR 2]](#cite_note-8)[[DR 3]](#cite_note-9)


Cannonbolt can ricochet and bounce off surfaces like a pinball in sphere form, suffering little-to-no loss in momentum.


Cannonbolt can use his sphere form to increase his agility by quickly opening up his ball shape while airborne, create shockwaves, and spin into a fierce tornado.


Despite his top-heavy body, Cannonbolt has enhanced reflexes, dexterity, and balance, allowing him to quickly change in and out of ball form to suit his immediate needs.


Cannonbolt can house people and fairly large objects within his spheroid form and provide them the same level of protection that he have.


Cannonbolt possesses enhanced strength, being able to fight on par with enemies about his size.[[5]](#cite_note-10) He can also break free from traps made of [Lepidopterran](/wiki/Lepidopterran_(Classic) "Lepidopterran (Classic)") slime.[[6]](#cite_note-11)


From inside his shell, Cannonbolt can withstand twice the heat of reentry into the Earth's atmosphere.[[pop-up 2]](#cite_note-12)[[7]](#cite_note-BAotD-13) Similarly, his shell is resistant to acids and lava,[[8]](#cite_note-14) as well as cold temperatures of places such as [Mikd'lty](/wiki/Mikd%27lty_(Classic) "Mikd'lty (Classic)"),[[9]](#cite_note-15) and can withstand the plasma generated by members of [Ball Weevil](/wiki/Ball_Weevil "Ball Weevil")'s [species](/wiki/Ball_Weevil%27s_Species "Ball Weevil's Species").[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-16)


Cannonbolt's shell can deflect lasers, even when not in sphere form.[[1]](#cite_note-TBT-1) Because of this, he is practically nigh-invulnerable[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-17) when curled into a ball.


Cannonbolt is capable of withstanding the vacuum of space, but he still needs to breathe. Without an oxygen supply, Cannonbolt is limited to holding his breath.[[10]](#cite_note-FaFBM-18)


Cannonbolt can be empowered by electricity, such as those generated by a [Gimlinopithecus](/wiki/Gimlinopithecus "Gimlinopithecus"). This way, he can gain much more power and momentum when in sphere form.[[11]](#cite_note-ATTWB-19)

1/7
Cannonbolt is subjected to the physical laws of reflection and conservation of momentum, meaning that he has difficulty stopping or controlling his direction if he picks up too much speed.


Cannonbolt's bipedal form is top-heavy, making him very unbalanced and clumsy. This is because Arburian Pelarotas are made to spin, not walk.[[pop-up 3]](#cite_note-20)[[4]](#cite_note-Game_Over-7) However, he can still manage to use his sphere form to enhance his agility.


Cannonbolt's large body makes it hard for him to move in small spaces, such as in vehicles like the [Proto-TRUK](/wiki/Proto-TRUK "Proto-TRUK").[[10]](#cite_note-FaFBM-18)


Cannonbolt can emit some sort of odor, which people housed in his spheroid form can find repulsive.[[7]](#cite_note-BAotD-13)[[3]](#cite_note-HU-5)


Cannonbolt can be trapped by strong adhesives, such as those used by the [Stalker](/wiki/Stalker "Stalker").[[12]](#cite_note-21) Similarly, he can be held in place by beings with as much strength as a [Chimera Sui Generis](/wiki/Chimera_Sui_Generis_(Classic) "Chimera Sui Generis (Classic)").[[13]](#cite_note-22)


Cannonbolt cannot roll on slippery surfaces, such as puddles made of a [Murk Gourmand](/wiki/Gourmand "Gourmand")'s slime.[[14]](#cite_note-23)


Despite his shell being resistant to extreme heat while reentering Earth's atmosphere, Cannonbolt can still suffer a mild amount of pain from the force of impact.[[7]](#cite_note-BAotD-13)[[11]](#cite_note-ATTWB-19)


Unlike his shell, Cannonbolt's skin is not heat resistant.[[15]](#cite_note-24)


Cannonbolt can be incapacitated by electricity, such as that generated by a [Transylian](/wiki/Transylian "Transylian").[[16]](#cite_note-25)


In his uncurled form, Cannonbolt can be controlled by a [Vladat](/wiki/Vladat "Vladat") via their [Corrupturas](/wiki/Corruptura "Corruptura").[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-26)



History
-------


### Ben 10


* Cannonbolt first appeared in *[The Big Tick](/wiki/The_Big_Tick "The Big Tick")*, where he was accidentally unlocked by [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)"), and [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") could not figure out what powers he had aside from rolling around and decided Cannonbolt was useless in battle. Later, however, he realized Cannonbolt can be rather useful by defeating the [Great One](/wiki/Great_One "Great One") when all of his other aliens failed.
* In *[Grudge Match](/wiki/Grudge_Match "Grudge Match")*, Cannonbolt fought [Kevin 11](/wiki/Kevin_11 "Kevin 11") and escaped the [Megacruiser](/wiki/Megacruiser "Megacruiser") with [Technorg](/wiki/Technorg "Technorg")'s help.
* In *[The Galactic Enforcers](/wiki/The_Galactic_Enforcers_(Episode) "The Galactic Enforcers (Episode)")*, Cannonbolt battled [SixSix](/wiki/SixSix_(Classic) "SixSix (Classic)") and [Vulkanus](/wiki/Vulkanus "Vulkanus").
* In *[Camp Fear](/wiki/Camp_Fear "Camp Fear")*, Cannonbolt saved [Gilbert](/wiki/Gilbert "Gilbert") from falling.
* In *[Back with a Vengeance](/wiki/Back_with_a_Vengeance "Back with a Vengeance")*, Cannonbolt hid from Kevin 11 and [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") and later fought a [Null Guardian](/wiki/Null_Guardians "Null Guardians").
* In *[Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Episode) "Ben 10,000 (Episode)")*, Cannonbolt battled [future Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Future) "Vilgax (Future)") before [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)") switched him into [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)").
* In *[A Change of Face](/wiki/A_Change_of_Face "A Change of Face")*, Gwen accidentally transformed into Cannonbolt while in Ben's body and battled [Charmcaster](/wiki/Charmcaster_(Classic) "Charmcaster (Classic)") and her [Stone Creatures](/wiki/Stone_Creatures "Stone Creatures"). After Gwen and Ben were put back in their own respective bodies, Ben as Cannonbolt defeated Charmcaster.
* In *[Benwolf](/wiki/Benwolf_(Episode) "Benwolf (Episode)")*, Cannonbolt defeated the [Werewolf](/wiki/Werewolf "Werewolf").
* In *[Game Over](/wiki/Game_Over "Game Over")*, Cannonbolt defeated some of [Kenko](/wiki/Kenko "Kenko")'s minions and accidentally made [Ishiyama](/wiki/Ishiyama "Ishiyama") lose a life in the process. Upon reaching level 13, Cannonbolt prepared to fight Kenko, but reverted before he could.
* In *[The Return](/wiki/The_Return "The Return")*, Cannonbolt fought the Werewolf, the [Mummy](/wiki/Mummy "Mummy"), and [Viktor](/wiki/Viktor "Viktor").
* In *[Be Afraid of the Dark](/wiki/Be_Afraid_of_the_Dark "Be Afraid of the Dark")*, Cannonbolt fell onto [Earth](/wiki/Earth_(Classic) "Earth (Classic)") from space with [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") and Gwen.
* In ***[Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix](/wiki/Ben_10:_Secret_of_the_Omnitrix "Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix")***, Cannonbolt broke into [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)")'s lab with enough force after he failed to open the door twice.
* In *[Don't Drink the Water](/wiki/Don%27t_Drink_the_Water "Don't Drink the Water")*, Cannonbolt battled [Hex](/wiki/Hex_(Classic) "Hex (Classic)").
* In *[Big Fat Alien Wedding](/wiki/Big_Fat_Alien_Wedding "Big Fat Alien Wedding")*, Cannonbolt stopped a truck from crashing into the wedding stage and accidentally set the stage chairs on fire, but managed to put out the flames by jumping into the lake, causing a massive splash. After escaping [Gordon](/wiki/Gordon_Tennyson "Gordon Tennyson") and [Betty Jean](/wiki/Betty_Jean_Tennyson "Betty Jean Tennyson") with Gwen's help, he followed and was defeated by [Camille](/wiki/Camille_Mann-Tennyson "Camille Mann-Tennyson")'s [ex-boyfriend](/wiki/Camille%27s_Ex-Boyfriend "Camille's Ex-Boyfriend") just before he reverted.

### [Ben 10: Protector of Earth](/wiki/Ben_10:_Protector_of_Earth "Ben 10: Protector of Earth")


Cannonbolt is a playable alien in the game.


He has the Dark Heroes and the DNA Force skin. Cannonbolt is needed for defeating the [Snap Dragon](/wiki/Snap_Dragon "Snap Dragon").


Cannonbolt can curl into a ball to encase himself in his plating, turning into a sphere. He can then use this form to roll into enemies to attack. In addition to the average repelling of walls, he was capable of ricocheting off enemies as well.


By compressing himself tightly, Cannonbolt can release the pressure to create a sonic blast.


Cannonbolt can flop onto the ground to create a shockwave, as well as rotate quickly to generate a miniature tornado.



### [Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks](/wiki/Ben_10_Alien_Force:_Vilgax_Attacks "Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks")


Cannonbolt is a playable alien in the game, replacing [Alien X](/wiki/Alien_X_(Classic) "Alien X (Classic)") in the Xbox 360, Nintendo Wii, PlayStation 2, and PlayStation Portable versions of the game. However, Cannonbolt is replaced by [Murk Upchuck](/wiki/Upchuck "Upchuck") in the Nintendo DS version of the game.


Due to the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") rebooting after Ben's abuse of the quick-change feature during his battle with an animated [Mr. Smoothy](/wiki/Mr._Smoothy "Mr. Smoothy") mascot, Cannonbolt, along with [Swampfire](/wiki/Swampfire "Swampfire"), [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill_(Classic) "Big Chill (Classic)"), [Spidermonkey](/wiki/Spidermonkey_(Classic) "Spidermonkey (Classic)"), and [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)") were the only aliens available on Ben's arsenal upon his arrival on [Vulpin](/wiki/Vulpin_(Classic) "Vulpin (Classic)").


Cannonbolt can curl into a ball, encasing himself in his plating. Cannonbolt is able to launch himself from ramps located all over the alien worlds to cross long gaps, as well as insert himself in special slots and use his rotation to power or destroy machinery and reveal hidden structures.


Superball is an unlockable combo move for Cannonbolt.


Cannonbolt is vital for progression on the Vulpin, [Terradino](/wiki/Terradino_(Classic) "Terradino (Classic)"), [Encephalonus IV](/wiki/Encephalonus_IV "Encephalonus IV"), [Anur Phaetos](/wiki/Anur_Phaetos_(Classic) "Anur Phaetos (Classic)"), [MorOtesi](/wiki/MorOtesi "MorOtesi"), and [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)") levels of the game.

Trivia
------


* Cannonbolt was the first alien to be unlocked in the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)"). He was also the only alien to be manually (and accidentally) unlocked by [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)").
* After Season 2 of the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), when [Zs'Skayr](/wiki/Zs%27Skayr "Zs'Skayr") successfully escapes the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") and thus removing Ghostfreak from it, Cannonbolt replaces the latter in the intro for the third and fourth seasons.
* Cannonbolt was initially meant to be one of the original 10 aliens, but ended up being replaced with [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)").[[pop-up 4]](#cite_note-27)
* Cannonbolt is one of the five aliens who have appeared in all series in the Classic Continuity; the other four are [Upchuck](/wiki/Upchuck "Upchuck"), [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)"), [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)"), and [Ghostfreak](/wiki/Ghostfreak "Ghostfreak").
	+ Currently, Cannonbolt, Diamondhead, and Way Big are the only three of these aliens to have appeared in all series of the franchise.
* Cannonbolt was originally planned to be one of the playable aliens in the *[Game Creator](/wiki/Game_Creator "Game Creator")*, but was soon removed from the game entirely when **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")** aired.
	+ However, he was eventually brought back for the **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")** version of the game.
* Cannonbolt tends to make puns based on his abilities.
* Cannonbolt's ball form is similar to [Samus Aran](https://metroid.fandom.com/wiki/Samus_Aran "w:c:metroid:Samus Aran")'s [Morph Ball](https://metroid.fandom.com/wiki/Morph_Ball "w:c:metroid:Morph Ball") ability from the *[Metroid](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Metroid "wikipedia:Metroid")* video game series.
* Cannonbolt's spin attacks are based off the Godzilla monster, Anguirus.
* [Dave Johnson](/wiki/Dave_Johnson "Dave Johnson") did the initial concepts for Cannonbolt, which had knobs that activated rocket blasts to bounce around and later became [Vilgax's](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") [droids](/wiki/Drones_(Classic) "Drones (Classic)"), while the final design was made by [Tom Perkins](/wiki/Tom_Perkins "Tom Perkins").[[TP 1]](#cite_note-28)[[DJ 1]](#cite_note-29)
