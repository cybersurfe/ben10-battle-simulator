Appearance
----------


Astrodactyl resembles a cross between a [Pterodactyl](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pterodactylus "wikipedia:Pterodactylus") and a glider. He has a large yellow beak-like mouth, green eyes, red skin, black and green clothing on his shoulders and waist, green braces around his wrists and ankles, and small, yellow, retractable wings that resemble a glider in appearance which are connected to a 'jetpack'-like structure on his back.


Astrodactyl wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his left shoulder.

1/13
Astrodactyl is capable of flying through the aid of his 'jetpack', with his retractable wings proving stability for this ability. He can create a propulsion blast from his jetpack[[2]](#cite_note-3)[[3]](#cite_note-ATTWN-4) using some kind of internalized star power to enhance his flight.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-Archive-5)[[note 1]](#cite_note-7)


Astrodactyl's star power can be used to create different forms of weapons,[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-Archive-5) the most common weapon being energy whips.


Besides energy weaponry, Astrodactyl can also shoot out stars.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-8)


Astrodactyl can use his star power to fire an energy beam from his mouth.[[1]](#cite_note-AFoB-1)


Astrodactyl can survive in the vacuum of space.[[5]](#cite_note-9)[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-10)


Astrodactyl possesses enhanced strength, as he was able to carry [No Watch Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(No_Watch_Timeline) "Ben Tennyson (No Watch Timeline)") in the air without any trouble[[3]](#cite_note-ATTWN-4), rip off goo that was too difficult for [Ben Prime](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") and [Rook](/wiki/Rook_Blonko "Rook Blonko") to break free from[[6]](#cite_note-11) and lasso a [Techadon Robot](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots") into a wall.[[7]](#cite_note-C5-12)


Astrodactyl can combine his strength with his energy weaponry, as seen when he pulled and swung around a [Techadon robot](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots") that was tied up in his energy whips.[[7]](#cite_note-C5-12)


Astrodactyl's strength also extends to his prehensile feet, as he can carry and lift a [Cerebrocrustacean](/wiki/Cerebrocrustacean "Cerebrocrustacean")[[1]](#cite_note-AFoB-1) and a [Segmentasapien](/wiki/Segmentasapien "Segmentasapien")[[3]](#cite_note-ATTWN-4) using his feet.



Weaknesses
----------


* ![]()Vulnerability to Electricity
* ![]()Durability Limit
1/2
Astrodactyl is very reliant on his jetpack for flying, meaning if it somehow got damaged, he would be at a disadvantage.


Astrodactyl is vulnerable to electricity, such as that generated by a Cerebrocrustacean.[[1]](#cite_note-AFoB-1)


Astrodactyl has a durability limit, as his head hurt after he crashed it against a ship.[[1]](#cite_note-AFoB-1)


Despite his flight, propulsion blasts, and space survivability, Astrodactyl cannot jump into hyperspace.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-13)



History
-------


### Omniverse


* Astrodactyl first appeared in *[While You Were Away](/wiki/While_You_Were_Away "While You Were Away")*, where he battled [Dr. Psychobos](/wiki/Dr._Psychobos "Dr. Psychobos") and helped destroy the supply of [Amber Ogia](/wiki/Amber_Ogia "Amber Ogia").[[note 2]](#cite_note-15)
* In *[The Frogs of War: Part 1](/wiki/The_Frogs_of_War:_Part_1 "The Frogs of War: Part 1")*, Astrodactyl battled the [Incurseans](/wiki/Incursean "Incursean") before the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)")'s randomizer function made him switch into [Snare-oh](/wiki/Snare-oh "Snare-oh").
* In *[Mud Is Thicker Than Water](/wiki/Mud_Is_Thicker_Than_Water "Mud Is Thicker Than Water")*, Astrodactyl battled [Psyphon](/wiki/Psyphon "Psyphon") and [his gang](/wiki/Psyphon%27s_Gang "Psyphon's Gang").
* In *[A Fistful of Brains](/wiki/A_Fistful_of_Brains "A Fistful of Brains")*, Astrodactyl escaped from [Khyber](/wiki/Khyber "Khyber")'s hunting grounds and briefly fought [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") [Brainstorm](/wiki/Brainstorm "Brainstorm") until they both reverted.
* In *[The Vampire Strikes Back](/wiki/The_Vampire_Strikes_Back "The Vampire Strikes Back")*, Astrodactyl flew back to [Anur Transyl](/wiki/Anur_Transyl "Anur Transyl") after [Rad](/wiki/Rad_Dudesman "Rad Dudesman") ejected him from the [Lovely Duck](/wiki/Lovely_Duck "Lovely Duck").
* In *[And Then There Were None](/wiki/And_Then_There_Were_None "And Then There Were None")*, Astrodactyl battled [Bad](/wiki/Bad_Ben "Bad Ben") [Gravattack](/wiki/Gravattack "Gravattack") and saved [No Watch Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(No_Watch_Timeline) "Ben Tennyson (No Watch Timeline)") from [Eon](/wiki/Eon "Eon").
* In *[Collect This](/wiki/Collect_This "Collect This")*, Astrodactyl freed himself and [Rook](/wiki/Rook_Blonko "Rook Blonko") before going after [Collectimus](/wiki/Collectimus "Collectimus").
* In *[Clyde Five](/wiki/Clyde_Five "Clyde Five")*, Astrodactyl chased after [Clyde](/wiki/Clyde_Fife "Clyde Fife") and battled some [Techadon robots](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Astrodactyl appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

Etymology
---------


Astrodactyl's name is a portmanteau of the prefix "astro-", meaning star, referencing his abilities, and "pterodactyl", a flying reptile, referencing his appearance.



Trivia
------


* The crew came up with Astrodactyl's name first before figuring out his appearance and powers from there.[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-16) His appearance was eventually designed by Steven Choi.[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-17)
* Despite both of them being flying animal-inspired aliens with green energy-related abilities, [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") does not consider Astrodactyl to be [Jetray](/wiki/Jetray_(Classic) "Jetray (Classic)")'s "replacement" in **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**.[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-18)[[DJW 10]](#cite_note-19)
	+ The reason for this is because the crew never intended or even wanted to replace any aliens.[[DJW 11]](#cite_note-20)
* From Arc 4 of **Omniverse** onwards, Astrodactyl replaces [Bloxx](/wiki/Bloxx "Bloxx") in the opening intro of the show, particularly in the part where the latter used to appear alongside [Shocksquatch](/wiki/Shocksquatch "Shocksquatch"), [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)"), and [Crashhopper](/wiki/Crashhopper "Crashhopper").
* Whenever Astrodactyl talks or becomes excited, he "squawks" like a parrot.
