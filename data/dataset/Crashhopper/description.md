Appearance
----------


Crashhopper greatly resembles a mix between a grasshopper and a praying mantis with significantly large three-jointed hind limbs. He has a green film connected to the tibia and tarsus of his legs. He has green skin, which has a hue similar to [Stinkfly](/wiki/Stinkfly_(Classic) "Stinkfly (Classic)"), a large segmented horn on top of his head, and two smaller horns on the sides of his head where the eyes are placed. Each horn has three tiny spikes behind them. There are also three spikes on each forearm and the back of both tarsi on his legs. He has two sharp fingers, a thumb on each hand, and two light-colored claw-like toes on his feet along with one on the back. His chin is quite small with two lines, he has gill-like structures under his armpits, and his segmented neck extends from a green collar that is fused with the torso.


Crashhopper's suit represents green overalls with three black stripes and a green tip. The green stripe on his stomach has a large green hexagonal patch where the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol is located and a smaller white stripe.



Personality
-----------


Crashhopper appears to be very eager to fight and sometimes, even excited. He is one to dive headfirst into a situation without much consideration, making him somewhat cocky.[[1]](#cite_note-2)

1/12
Due to his relatively long and muscular legs, Crashhopper is able to jump extremely long distances. His legs can also kick with considerable force, making them offensive weapons in close-quarters.


The resulting shock waves created by Crashhopper's jumping are strong enough to push through a large number of [Mutant Ants](/wiki/Mutant_Ants "Mutant Ants") should they get on top of him.[[2]](#cite_note-IWT-3) They are also powerful enough to crack concrete.[[3]](#cite_note-4)


Crashhopper's head is hard as steel and full of shock-absorbing viscous matter that cushions the impact on his internal organs.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-5)


Crashhopper possesses enhanced strength, as he is capable of lifting and throwing a [Muroid](/wiki/Muroid "Muroid").[[4]](#cite_note-6)


Crashhopper can combine his strength and jumping, as seen when he bounced [Milleous](/wiki/Milleous_(Classic) "Milleous (Classic)") around.[[5]](#cite_note-7)


Crashhopper can propel himself forward by pushing himself off walls, effectively jumping off walls.[[6]](#cite_note-8) He is also quite agile.[[7]](#cite_note-AFoB-9)


The claws on Crashhopper's arms and legs can dig into hard surfaces, allowing him to scale walls and other tall structures.[[8]](#cite_note-10)



Weaknesses
----------


* ![]()Trapped by Mucilator
* ![]()Durability Limit
* ![]()Jumping Limit
1/3
Being an [Orthopterran](/wiki/Orthopterran "Orthopterran"), Crashhopper is vulnerable to being trapped by a member of [Mucilator](/wiki/Mucilator "Mucilator")'s [species](/wiki/Mucilator%27s_Species "Mucilator's Species"), as shown when he got himself in and was unable to escape Mucilator's sticky goo sacs on his own.[[2]](#cite_note-IWT-3)[[9]](#cite_note-OPaPP2-11)


Crashhopper's body is not as shielded as it looks; when a [Contumelia](/wiki/Contumelia "Contumelia") ship missile struck him, he started to stroke his head and feel great discomfort from the impact.[[10]](#cite_note-12) Furthermore, he was easily hurt by punches from [Princess Looma](/wiki/Looma_Red_Wind "Looma Red Wind").[[11]](#cite_note-13)


Crashhopper cannot jump from high altitudes, as he was unable to jump from [Khyber](/wiki/Khyber "Khyber")'s [ship](/wiki/Khyber%27s_Ship "Khyber's Ship") to [Bellwood](/wiki/Bellwood_(Classic) "Bellwood (Classic)") because it was too high up in the sky.[[9]](#cite_note-OPaPP2-11)


If his legs are hit correctly, Crashhopper will move backward automatically, as demonstrated with [17-year-old Mutant Kevin](/wiki/Kevin%27s_Omniverse_Mutation "Kevin's Omniverse Mutation").[[12]](#cite_note-14)



History
-------


### Omniverse


* Chronologically, Crashhopper first appeared in *[It Was Them](/wiki/It_Was_Them "It Was Them")*, where he battled [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)")'s [Mutant Ants](/wiki/Mutant_Ants "Mutant Ants") and [Mucilator](/wiki/Mucilator "Mucilator").
* In *[So Long, and Thanks for All the Smoothies](/wiki/So_Long,_and_Thanks_for_All_the_Smoothies "So Long, and Thanks for All the Smoothies")*, Crashhopper destroyed some [Contumelia](/wiki/Contumelia "Contumelia") ship missiles that were about to crash onto [Earth](/wiki/Earth_(Classic) "Earth (Classic)").
* In *[Hot Stretch](/wiki/Hot_Stretch "Hot Stretch")*, Crashhopper failed to catch [Ester](/wiki/Ester "Ester").
* In *[Of Predators and Prey: Part 1](/wiki/Of_Predators_and_Prey:_Part_1 "Of Predators and Prey: Part 1")*, Crashhopper was crushed by Mucilator, so he switched into [Armodrillo](/wiki/Armodrillo "Armodrillo").
* In *[Of Predators and Prey: Part 2](/wiki/Of_Predators_and_Prey:_Part_2 "Of Predators and Prey: Part 2")*, Crashhopper fought [Buglizard](/wiki/Buglizard "Buglizard") until she switched into Mucilator.
* In *[Many Happy Returns](/wiki/Many_Happy_Returns "Many Happy Returns")*, Crashhopper was defeated by [Princess Looma](/wiki/Looma_Red_Wind "Looma Red Wind").
* In *[Gone Fishin'](/wiki/Gone_Fishin%27 "Gone Fishin'")*, Crashhopper defeated the [Krakken](/wiki/Krakken "Krakken").
* In *[Bros in Space](/wiki/Bros_in_Space "Bros in Space")*, Crashhopper helped scare away the [Muroids](/wiki/Muroid "Muroid").
* In *[The Frogs of War: Part 1](/wiki/The_Frogs_of_War:_Part_1 "The Frogs of War: Part 1")*, Crashhopper briefly battled [Milleous](/wiki/Milleous_(Classic) "Milleous (Classic)") until he reverted.
* In *[Mud Is Thicker Than Water](/wiki/Mud_Is_Thicker_Than_Water "Mud Is Thicker Than Water")*, Crashhopper defeated [Psyphon](/wiki/Psyphon "Psyphon") and [his gang](/wiki/Psyphon%27s_Gang "Psyphon's Gang") in a [Plumber Tank](/wiki/Plumber_Tank "Plumber Tank").
* In *[A Fistful of Brains](/wiki/A_Fistful_of_Brains "A Fistful of Brains")*, Crashhopper was defeated by [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") [Ultimate Humungousaur](/wiki/Ultimate_Humungousaur "Ultimate Humungousaur").
* In *[Rad Monster Party](/wiki/Rad_Monster_Party "Rad Monster Party")*, Crashhopper defeated [Kuphulu](/wiki/Kuphulu "Kuphulu").
* In *[Clyde Five](/wiki/Clyde_Five "Clyde Five")*, Crashhopper defeated [Liam](/wiki/Liam "Liam").
* In *[Breakpoint](/wiki/Breakpoint "Breakpoint")*, Crashhopper fought [Fistrick](/wiki/Fistrick "Fistrick"), [Corvo](/wiki/Corvo "Corvo"), Liam, and [Solid Plugg](/wiki/Solid_Plugg "Solid Plugg").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Crashhopper appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

Etymology
---------


Crashhopper's name is a portmanteau of "crash", the impact that he makes upon landing, and "[grasshopper](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grasshopper "wikipedia:Grasshopper")", the creature he is based on.



Trivia
------


* [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") did not come up with Crashhopper.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-15)
* [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") enjoys jumping as Crashhopper.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-16)
* When Crashhopper jumps or speaks, a cricket-like sound is heard.
* Crashhopper's abilities are similar to those of [Cricket](https://generatorrex.fandom.com/wiki/Cricket "w:c:generatorrex:Cricket") from **[Generator Rex](https://generatorrex.fandom.com/wiki/Generator_Rex "w:c:generatorrex:Generator Rex")**, another series [Man of Action](/wiki/Man_of_Action "Man of Action") worked on.
* The film connected to the tibia and tarsus of Crashhopper's legs is similar to both [Jetray](/wiki/Jetray_(Classic) "Jetray (Classic)") and Stinkfly's wings.
* From Arc 4 of **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")** onwards, Crashhopper is replaced with [Bullfrag](/wiki/Bullfrag "Bullfrag") in the opening intro of the show, specifically in the part where the former initially appeared alongside [Shocksquatch](/wiki/Shocksquatch "Shocksquatch"), [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)"), and [Bloxx](/wiki/Bloxx "Bloxx").
* [Skurd](/wiki/Skurd "Skurd") could give Ben Crashhopper's legs while in human form. However, the legs would be pretty dangerous for Ben's more fragile human form.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-17)
