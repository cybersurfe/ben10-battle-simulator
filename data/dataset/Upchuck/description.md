Appearance
----------


### Ben as Perk Upchuck


Perk Upchuck has green skin, six sprout-like growths on the back of his head, a dark-green patch of skin on his head, light-green skin on his face and belly and a crooked teeth overbite. He has whisker like lines on his face, and a green tail.


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Perk Upchuck had white eyes and wore a black open-front suit with white over-sleeves and white bands around his legs and white padding under his three-toed feet. He wore the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol on his left hand.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, 16-year-old Perk Upchuck does not wear clothes and his eyes are recolored green. He wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") on his stomach.


11-year-old Perk Upchuck looks almost the same as the original series, but his eyes and original Omnitrix symbol are recolored green and he now has bare feet with short claws.



### Ben as Murk Upchuck


Murk Upchuck has swampy-green skin, a tanned stomach and muzzles, black sprouts and black-green spots on his tail, limbs, and back. His eyes are sharp and green, he has whisker-like lines, his teeth are more aligned with visibly sharp canine teeth and his tail is short and straightened, as opposed to Perk Upchuck's curled tail. Gwen as Murk Upchuck shares these traits.


In *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, Murk Upchuck's black stripes on his face were longer, reaching his mouth, and he only had two lines instead of three. His claws were attached to his hands through finger stubs.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Murk Upchuck resembles his previous design, except that his claws are longer and bigger, his sprouts are brighter, and he has less spots on his body. He now has only two teeth in the shape of an underbite, and the markings on his face are thinner and contained by the face, no longer spanning the head.


Murk Upchuck wears the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)")/[Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his stomach.



### Mad Ben as Mad Perk Upchuck


[Mad Ben](/wiki/Mad_Ben "Mad Ben")'s Perk Upchuck has skin coloring that looks more like that of a Murk [Gourmand](/wiki/Gourmand "Gourmand"). His teeth are now mirrored, and he wears a black helmet with a spike and orange glasses on it and spiked shoulder pads that are connected. He has orange bandages around his wrists, the spikes on the pads are orange, and the strap connecting the pads goes around the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Power_Watch "Power Watch"), which is on his chest. His eyes and Omnitrix are orange.

Both Upchucks have three very strong, adhesive tongues that can stretch to great lengths, allowing them to latch onto large objects and reel them in with relative ease.


Both Upchucks have developed jaws, diamond-hard teeth, and a gastrointestinal tract that allows them to can eat almost any material,[[merch 1]](#cite_note-Murk-4) including virtually any object all the way up to [Ascalon](/wiki/Ascalon "Ascalon").[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-5)


Besides objects and matter, both Upchucks can eat other beings such as tigers,[[3]](#cite_note-TT-6) [Crabdozers](/wiki/Crabdozer_(Species) "Crabdozer (Species)"),[[1]](#cite_note-FaFBM-1) [To'kustars](/wiki/To%27kustar_(Classic) "To'kustar (Classic)"),[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-To.27kustar-7) and even [Celestialsapiens](/wiki/Celestialsapien "Celestialsapien").[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-Celestialsapien-8)


In the case of To'kustars, they would first have to be chopped up into bite-size chunks.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-To.27kustar-7) In the case of Celestialsapiens, they would either be digested or spat back out.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-Celestialsapien-8)


Both Upchucks' mouths can stretch, allowing them to swallow objects larger than themselves. They are connected with a trans-space bladder dimension where they store all the "food" they eat. Both Upchucks are connected to the Gourmand species and can sense any danger to their people through their stomachs.[[3]](#cite_note-TT-6)


By breathing air out of their mouths, both Upchucks are able to fly and even survive in the vacuum of space, as demonstrated by various [Gourmands](/wiki/Gourmand "Gourmand").[[3]](#cite_note-TT-6)


The items ingested by either Upchuck are converted into explosive balls of liquids that can be expelled with great accuracy.[[note 1]](#cite_note-10) The explosive power of these "loogies" depends on the chemical composition and overall size of what Upchuck ate to create them.[[pop-up 1]](#cite_note-11)[[5]](#cite_note-12)


Both Upchucks can carry heavy objects in their stomach, such as the [Alpha](/wiki/Alpha "Alpha") Nanite's container, and release them without having to turn them into a "loogie."[[6]](#cite_note-13)


Both Upchucks can eat and spit out energy, such as lasers and a [Crystalsapien](/wiki/Crystalsapien "Crystalsapien")'s ultraviolet beams,[[7]](#cite_note-14) in the same way they eat and spit out acid matter.


Both Upchucks can make their "loogies" curve like a boomerang, shuriken, or Frisbee. They can also fire powerful streams of their explosive vomit like an energy beam by building up digested matter and releasing it all at once.[[8]](#cite_note-15)


Either Upchuck is able to take a surprising amount of punishment for a creature of their size, taking the force of a moving car impact, as well as surviving after being thrown through a wall.[[9]](#cite_note-B4GB-16)


Both Upchucks can spit out slime, which can be slippery for [Arburian Pelarotas](/wiki/Arburian_Pelarota_(Classic) "Arburian Pelarota (Classic)"). This slime is also capable of melting ice.[[10]](#cite_note-Duped-17)

1/5
Both Upchucks have a limit to how many big objects they can swallow at once. They also become slow and excessively when they eat a lot of things. Otherwise, they are somewhat quick, considering their small size.


If either Upchuck does not spit out something they eat, they will get very fat and rendered immobile until they spit it out.[[11]](#cite_note-18)


Either Upchuck's hunger can be used against them, as they can easily be swayed from any task with the simple promise of a tasty treat.[[merch 1]](#cite_note-Murk-4)


Upchuck cannot eat Ultimate Panuncians as they would simply thrash in their stomach until they regurgitate them safely.[[1]](#cite_note-FaFBM-1)


There is a limit to the kinds of energy either Upchuck can ingest, as the energy beams of an [Evolved Galvan](/wiki/Evolved_Galvan "Evolved Galvan") made them inflate into a ball.[[12]](#cite_note-19)


Either Upchuck can be temporarily immobilized by a [Necrofriggian](/wiki/Necrofriggian "Necrofriggian")'s ice breath until they thaw themselves free using their slime.[[10]](#cite_note-Duped-17)


Despite being able to eat a Celestialsapien, neither Upchuck can gain the latter's powers in any form or way.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-20)



History
-------


### Ben 10


* Perk Upchuck first appeared in *[The Visitor](/wiki/The_Visitor "The Visitor")*, where he was unlocked by [Xylene](/wiki/Xylene "Xylene") and defeated the [Mechadroid](/wiki/Drones "Drones").
* In ***[Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix](/wiki/Ben_10:_Secret_of_the_Omnitrix "Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix")***, Perk Upchuck was defeated by [Myaxx](/wiki/Myaxx "Myaxx").
* In *[Ben 4 Good Buddy](/wiki/Ben_4_Good_Buddy "Ben 4 Good Buddy")*, Perk Upchuck battled the [Road Crew](/wiki/Road_Crew "Road Crew") and was defeated by [Turbine](/wiki/Turbine "Turbine").
* In *[Ben 10 vs. the Negative 10: Part 2](/wiki/Ben_10_vs._the_Negative_10:_Part_2 "Ben 10 vs. the Negative 10: Part 2")*, Perk Upchuck defeated the [Forever Ninja](/wiki/Forever_Ninja "Forever Ninja") and [Driscoll](/wiki/Driscoll "Driscoll") and accidentally destroyed [Mt. Rushmore](/wiki/Mt._Rushmore "Mt. Rushmore") in the process.


### Five Years Prior to Omniverse


* In *[Evil's Encore](/wiki/Evil%27s_Encore "Evil's Encore")*, Perk Upchuck stopped [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)")'s control of the Plumber Base and accidentally destroyed [Mt. Rushmore](/wiki/Mt._Rushmore "Mt. Rushmore") in the process for the second time in a row.
* In *[From Hedorium to Eternity](/wiki/From_Hedorium_to_Eternity "From Hedorium to Eternity")*, Perk Upchuck accidentally broke one of [Mr. Baumann](/wiki/Ignacius_Baumann "Ignacius Baumann")'s car windows.


### Alien Force


* Murk Upchuck first appeared in *[War of the Worlds: Part 1](/wiki/War_of_the_Worlds:_Part_1 "War of the Worlds: Part 1")*, ​where he was unlocked by the [Master Control](/wiki/Master_Control "Master Control"). Murk Upchuck battled an army of [DNAliens](/wiki/DNAliens "DNAliens") and unsuccessfully attempted to destroy the [Hyperspace Jump Gate](/wiki/Hyperspace_Jump_Gate "Hyperspace Jump Gate") alongside [his teammates](/wiki/Alien_Force "Alien Force") before switching into [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)").
* In *[Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 2](/wiki/Vengeance_of_Vilgax:_Part_2 "Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 2")*, Murk Upchuck fought and captured the escaped [Chromastone](/wiki/Chromastone "Chromastone").
* In *[In Charm's Way](/wiki/In_Charm%27s_Way "In Charm's Way")*, Murk Upchuck defeated [Charmcaster](/wiki/Charmcaster_(Classic) "Charmcaster (Classic)")'s giant animated [Mr. Smoothy](/wiki/Mr._Smoothy "Mr. Smoothy").


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Duped](/wiki/Duped "Duped")*, Arrogant Ben used Murk Upchuck to battle [Urien](/wiki/Urien "Urien"), but was defeated.
* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, Perk Upchuck was re-unlocked by [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Ultimate_Alien) "Ben 10,000 (Ultimate Alien)").
* In *[The Purge](/wiki/The_Purge "The Purge")*, Murk Upchuck was defeated by the [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights").
* In *[Ben 10/Generator Rex: Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, Murk Upchuck ate the [Alpha](/wiki/Alpha "Alpha") Nanite's container to transport it out of [Rex](/wiki/Rex_Salazar "Rex Salazar")'s dimension.
* In *[Inspector #13](/wiki/Inspector_13_(Episode) "Inspector 13 (Episode)")*, [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") as Murk Upchuck helped rescue [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)").
* In *[Night of the Living Nightmare](/wiki/Night_of_the_Living_Nightmare "Night of the Living Nightmare")*, Murk Upchuck was seen confronting [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") in a dream.


### Omniverse


* In *[Tummy Trouble](/wiki/Tummy_Trouble "Tummy Trouble")*, Upchuck appeared four times. First, Perk Upchuck defeated [Nyancy Chan](/wiki/Nyancy_Chan "Nyancy Chan")'s tigers. Later, both Upchucks helped settle peace negotiations between the Perk and Murk [Gourmands](/wiki/Gourmand "Gourmand"). Lastly, Perk Upchuck helped eat [Peptos XI](/wiki/Peptos_XI "Peptos XI") with the other Gourmands.
* In *[Store 23](/wiki/Store_23 "Store 23")*, Murk Upchuck was one of several aliens [Ben Prime](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was showcasing for [Ben 23](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Dimension_23) "Ben Tennyson (Dimension 23)").
* In *[A Fistful of Brains](/wiki/A_Fistful_of_Brains "A Fistful of Brains")*, Perk Upchuck was defeated by [Ultimate Albedo](/wiki/Ultimate_Albedo "Ultimate Albedo").
* In *[For a Few Brains More](/wiki/For_a_Few_Brains_More "For a Few Brains More")*, Perk Upchuck ate [Crabdozer](/wiki/Crabdozer "Crabdozer") before spitting out [Ultimate Panuncian](/wiki/Ultimate_Panuncian "Ultimate Panuncian").
* In *[The Rooters of All Evil](/wiki/The_Rooters_of_All_Evil "The Rooters of All Evil")*, Perk Upchuck fought the [Rooters](/wiki/Rooters "Rooters").
* In *[No Honor Among Bros](/wiki/No_Honor_Among_Bros "No Honor Among Bros")*, Perk Upchuck defeated [Rook](/wiki/Rook_Blonko "Rook Blonko") at the Golden Fist Tournament.
* In *[Universe vs. Tennyson](/wiki/Universe_vs._Tennyson "Universe vs. Tennyson")*, Perk Upchuck defeated [Thunderpig](/wiki/Thunderpig "Thunderpig").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Perk Upchuck appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.


### Mad Ben's Timeline


* In *[It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 1](/wiki/It%27s_a_Mad,_Mad,_Mad_Ben_World:_Part_1 "It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 1")*, [Mad](/wiki/Mad_Ben "Mad Ben") Perk Upchuck defeated Ben Prime, Ben 23, and [Dr. Psychobos](/wiki/Dr._Psychobos "Dr. Psychobos").

Perk Upchuck is a playable alien character in Protector of Earth; he is unlockable with a cheat on the DS version. He deals heavy damage to regular enemies and mini-bosses, but not to boss enemies.



### [Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks](/wiki/Ben_10_Alien_Force:_Vilgax_Attacks "Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks")


Murk Upchuck is a playable alien in the game, replacing both [Alien X](/wiki/Alien_X_(Classic) "Alien X (Classic)") and [Cannonbolt](/wiki/Cannonbolt_(Classic) "Cannonbolt (Classic)") in the Nintendo DS version.


Due to the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") rebooting after Ben's abuse of the quick change feature during his battle against [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)")'s forces, Murk Upchuck was temporarily locked until Ben's arrival in the [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)"), where both Murk Upchuck and [Jetray](/wiki/Jetray_(Classic) "Jetray (Classic)") are re-unlocked.


Murk Upchuck is able to consume any enemy projectile and spit it back as a green glowing energy ball. He is the recommended alien during the battle with the animated [Mr. Smoothy](/wiki/Mr._Smoothy "Mr. Smoothy") mascot.



### [Ben 10: Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse_(Video_Game) "Ben 10: Omniverse (Video Game)")


Perk Upchuck is a playable alien in the game (by 11-year-old Ben only). He is an exclusive for the DS and 3DS version.



### [Ben 10: Omniverse 2](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse_2 "Ben 10: Omniverse 2")


Perk Upchuck is a playable alien in the game (only on 3DS).

Etymology
---------


Upchuck's name is a pun of the verb "upchuck", which means to vomit, referencing his abilities.


The Perk prefix is named after [Tom Perkins](/wiki/Tom_Perkins "Tom Perkins"), who design Upchuck's appearance in the original series.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-21)[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-namesakes-22)


The Murk prefix is named after [Glen Murakami](/wiki/Glen_Murakami "Glen Murakami"), who redesigned Upchuck for **Alien Force** and **Ultimate Alien**.[[TP 1]](#cite_note-23)[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-namesakes-22)



Trivia
------


* Upchuck is the only known Omnitrix alien who can switch between the different races of his species.[[3]](#cite_note-TT-6)[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-24) Azmuth's reasons for including both subspecies are unknown.[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-25)
	+ The out-of-universe reason for this is because [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") liked both of Upchuck's designs, even though he preferred Perks, and wanted to use them together on the show.[[DJW 10]](#cite_note-26)[[DJW 11]](#cite_note-27)
* Unlike most aliens featured in **Omniverse** that had clothes in the original series, Perk Upchuck was unclothed as he appears cuter and more uncivilized naked.[[DJW 12]](#cite_note-28)
* Upchuck does not have a natural predator because his species is at the top of the food chain.[[1]](#cite_note-FaFBM-1)[[DJW 13]](#cite_note-29)
* Both Perk Upchuck and Murk Upchuck have the same abilities; neither of them have abilities exclusive to any one Upchuck.[[DJW 14]](#cite_note-30)
* Upchuck would have been the real winner of the loogie contest in *[Back with a Vengeance](/wiki/Back_with_a_Vengeance "Back with a Vengeance")* had Ben unlocked him prior to the events of the episode.[[pop-up 2]](#cite_note-31)[[13]](#cite_note-32)
* In the original series, Perk Upchuck was unable to consume human food.[[9]](#cite_note-B4GB-16)[[pop-up 3]](#cite_note-33)[[14]](#cite_note-TV-34) This was retconned in **Omniverse**, as he can now eat normal food as well as everything else.[[DJW 15]](#cite_note-35)
	+ The out-of-universe reason for this change was because the crew felt that Upchuck not eating Earth food was too limiting.[[DR 1]](#cite_note-36)
* Some of Perk Upchuck's prototypes were used to create [Spitter](/wiki/Spitter "Spitter"), [Arctiguana](/wiki/Arctiguana "Arctiguana"), [Sploot](/wiki/Sploot "Sploot"), [Mot Snikrep](/wiki/Mot_Snikrep "Mot Snikrep"), and [Zzzakt](/wiki/Zzzakt "Zzzakt").[[TP 2]](#cite_note-Upchuck-37)
* Like Arctiguana, [Buzzshock](/wiki/Buzzshock_(Classic) "Buzzshock (Classic)"), [Frankenstrike](/wiki/Frankenstrike "Frankenstrike"), and [Snare-oh](/wiki/Snare-oh "Snare-oh"), Murk Upchuck appeared in **Omniverse**, but he did not appear in *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*.
	+ The out-of-universe reason reason Murk Upchuck had fewer appearances than Perk Upchuck in **Omniverse** is because Derrick believed "Perks are the best Upchucks".[[DJW 16]](#cite_note-38)
* Upchuck is one of the five aliens who have appeared in every series in the Classic Timeline; the other four are [Cannonbolt](/wiki/Cannonbolt_(Classic) "Cannonbolt (Classic)"), [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)"), [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)"), and [Ghostfreak](/wiki/Ghostfreak "Ghostfreak").
* Murk Upchuck is the first alien in **Alien Force** to not have the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") on his chest. Instead, the symbol was on his stomach.
* Perk Upchuck and [Bullfrag](/wiki/Bullfrag "Bullfrag") are the only aliens to have been manually unlocked by entering a code into an Omnitrix.
	+ Murk Upchuck is one of four aliens to have been unlocked by [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)"); the others being Way Big, Bullfrag and [Atomix](/wiki/Atomix "Atomix").
* 11-year-old Perk Upchuck is replaced with [Juryrigg](/wiki/Juryrigg "Juryrigg") in **Omniverse**'s Arc 5.
* Upchuck was among the aliens considered to be featured in the [reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)").[[DR 2]](#cite_note-39)[[DR 3]](#cite_note-40)
