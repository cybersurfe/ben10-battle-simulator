Appearance
----------


Pesky Dust is a short, [fairy](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fairy "wikipedia:Fairy")-like alien with blue skin. He has purple fairy-like wings, pink spots and blue hair that sticks straight up. Pesky Dust wears a green dress-like outfit with black zigzags. His hands have three fingers with white gloves on them and his feet have two toes. His eyes are green with feminine eyelines and has pink markings on his cheeks and chin.


Pesky Dust wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his chest.

Pesky Dust is capable of flight and possesses enhanced speed and agility thanks to his fairy-like wings. He can also leave behind a green trail[[note 1]](#cite_note-3) while flying.


Pesky Dust can release a special kind of dust that makes his victims fall asleep, such as [Zaroffians](/wiki/Zaroffian "Zaroffian"),[[3]](#cite_note-FaFBM-4) [Pugnavores](/wiki/Pugnavore "Pugnavore"),[[1]](#cite_note-TMDGS-1) [Polymorphs](/wiki/Polymorph_(Classic) "Polymorph (Classic)"), [Galvanic Mechamorphs](/wiki/Galvanic_Mechamorph_(Classic) "Galvanic Mechamorph (Classic)"),[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-5) [Chronosapiens](/wiki/Chronosapien "Chronosapien"), [Ectonurites](/wiki/Ectonurite_(Classic) "Ectonurite (Classic)"),[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-6) and even [Celestialsapiens](/wiki/Celestialsapien_(Classic) "Celestialsapien (Classic)")[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-7) and [Ultimate Kevin](/wiki/Ultimate_Kevin "Ultimate Kevin").[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-8)


Pesky Dust can enter and examine the dreams of other beings, which can be used as a form of interrogation or extracting information from a victim.[[3]](#cite_note-FaFBM-4) However, they must be affected by Pesky Dust's sleep dust in order for him to enter their dreams.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-9)


Pesky Dust can control the dreams of multiple victims at once.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-10)


If Pesky Dust were to keep dousing a victim in his sleeping dust, they would end up sleeping forever.[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-11)


Pesky Dust can manipulate a victim's dreams, causing them to have nightmares instead.[[1]](#cite_note-TMDGS-1)



Weaknesses
----------


Pesky Dust's abilities would be useless against technological or robotic opponents, who cannot fall asleep or have dreams. He can also be affected by the abilities of other [Nemuinas](/wiki/Nemuina "Nemuina").[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-12)


Members of [Gutrot](/wiki/Gutrot "Gutrot")'s [species](/wiki/Gutrot%27s_Species "Gutrot's Species") are immune to Pesky Dust's sleeping dust.[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-13)


Pesky Dust is quite small and is not suited for melee combat.[[4]](#cite_note-Outbreak-14)


Being a Nemuina, Pesky Dust is weak against his natural predator.[[DJW 10]](#cite_note-15) His predator have a small secondary brain cluster which allows it to continue hunting even when the primary brain is asleep.[[DJW 11]](#cite_note-16)



History
-------


### Omniverse


* Pesky Dust first appeared in *[Outbreak](/wiki/Outbreak "Outbreak")*, where he was accidentally unlocked by [Driba](/wiki/Driba "Driba") and [Blukic](/wiki/Blukic "Blukic") when they tried to fix the malfunctioning [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") before they switched him into [Mole-Stache](/wiki/Mole-Stache "Mole-Stache").
* In *[For a Few Brains More](/wiki/For_a_Few_Brains_More "For a Few Brains More")*, Pesky Dust put [Khyber](/wiki/Khyber "Khyber") to sleep and before entering his dream to find out where [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") was taking [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)")'s brain.
* In *[The Most Dangerous Game Show](/wiki/The_Most_Dangerous_Game_Show "The Most Dangerous Game Show")*, Pesky Dust defeated [Charles Zenith](/wiki/Charles_Zenith "Charles Zenith").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Pesky Dust appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

Etymology
---------


Pesky Dust's name is a play on the words "pesky" and "pixie dust". 



Trivia
------


* The design for Pesky Dust came from [Lillimusha DiForestini](/wiki/Lillimusha_DiForestini "Lillimusha DiForestini"), drawn by Steven Choi and Chap Yaep. Meanwhile, [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") came up with his name.[[DJW 12]](#cite_note-17)
* Pesky Dust's appearance is similar to that of [Tinker Bell](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tinker_Bell "wikipedia:Tinker Bell")'s from *[Peter Pan](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peter_Pan "wikipedia:Peter Pan")*.
* Pesky Dust's abilities are similar to those of [Jigglypuff](http://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Jigglypuff_(Pok%C3%A9mon) "bulbapedia:Jigglypuff (Pokémon)") from Pokémon.[[DJW 13]](#cite_note-18)
* Pesky Dust is one of four aliens unlocked by [Driba](/wiki/Driba "Driba") and [Blukic](/wiki/Blukic "Blukic"); the other three being [Walkatrout](/wiki/Walkatrout "Walkatrout"), [Mole-Stache](/wiki/Mole-Stache "Mole-Stache"), and [The Worst](/wiki/The_Worst "The Worst").[[4]](#cite_note-Outbreak-14)
* In his first appearance, Pesky Dust shared the same voice as Ben due to the Omnitrix malfunctioning. The out-of-universe reason was because the crew originally did not envision him as a usable alien for later episodes.[[DJW 14]](#cite_note-19)
	+ Pesky Dust's voice was eventually changed to a high-pitched, trilling, feminine voice in *For a Few Brains More* because the crew liked how he turned out.[[DJW 15]](#cite_note-20)
* [Khyber](/wiki/Khyber "Khyber") is afraid of Nemuinas like Pesky Dust[[3]](#cite_note-FaFBM-4) and considers the dream he was entrapped in during *For a Few Brains More* to be his worst nightmare.[[DJW 16]](#cite_note-21)
	+ While [Kai](/wiki/Kai_Green "Kai Green") thinks Pesky Dust is cute, [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") considers his Nemuina transformation to be extremely dangerous and "nobody to mess with".[[1]](#cite_note-TMDGS-1)
* Similar to [Astrodactyl](/wiki/Astrodactyl "Astrodactyl"), Pesky Dust makes a sound when he talks. This sound is like a trill, chirp, whistle, or purr.
* [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo")'s [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") does not contain Pesky Dust's DNA.[[DJW 17]](#cite_note-22)
	+ Despite this, [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") drew some art of Negative Pesky Dust in 2020, which can be found in the [gallery](/wiki/Pesky_Dust/Gallery "Pesky Dust/Gallery").[[DJW 18]](#cite_note-23)
