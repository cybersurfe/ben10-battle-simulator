Appearance
----------


### Ben as Ultimate Humungousaur


Ultimate Humungousaur is a humanoid dinosaur-like creature, with dark blue armor similar to that of an Ankylosaurus. He is more burly and muscular than his pre-evolved form.


He has camo green skin and his helmet[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-1) has three silver spikes running down the top, and two downward pointing black horns on the sides. His back armor has long spikes running down each side, his plated chest and torso are dark blue with five spikes on each end and his tail has a blue mace on the end of it, with silver spikes.


Ultimate Humungousaur's knuckles have silver barrels on them, which he can transform into four-barreled missile launchers. He has black claws.


Ultimate Humungousaur wears the evolved [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.



### Albedo as Negative Ultimate Humungousaur


In **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")**, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Ultimate Humungousaur looked exactly like Ben's Ultimate Humungousaur, except his eyes and Ultimatrix symbol were red.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Negative Ultimate Humungousaur has greyish-tan skin, his spikes are yellowish-brown, and his shoulder sport armor plating. His overall appearance is more techno-organic, almost like a cyborg. He has exposed metallic tubes on his skin and black "veins" on his skin. He wears the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") symbol on his chest.

1/9
Ultimate Humungousaur is exponentially stronger than [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)"), even when at full size. He is strong enough to lift and throw a [To'kustar](/wiki/To%27kustar_(Classic) "To'kustar (Classic)") in record timing[[1]](#cite_note-TUS-2) and make a giant crater in the ground by punching it.[[2]](#cite_note-3)


Ultimate Humungousaur has very durable armor plating throughout his body, giving him enhanced durability combined with his thick skin.


Ultimate Humungousaur can transform his hands into bio-gatling guns that shoot missiles,[[note 1]](#cite_note-5) which deal major damage and can even damage and knock out a [Petrosapien](/wiki/Petrosapien_(Classic) "Petrosapien (Classic)").[[3]](#cite_note-AFoB-6)


Ultimate Humungousaur's tail has a mace-like structure at the tip, which can be used as a flail.


Like Humungousaur, Ultimate Humungousaur can survive in the vacuum of space for a short time and can create shock waves by clapping his hands.

History
-------


### Alien Force


* Ultimate Humungousaur first appeared in *[The Final Battle: Part 1](/wiki/The_Final_Battle:_Part_1 "The Final Battle: Part 1")*, where he was used by [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") to defeat [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)").
* In *[The Final Battle: Part 2](/wiki/The_Final_Battle:_Part_2 "The Final Battle: Part 2")*, Negative Ultimate Humungousaur was defeated by an army of [Bioids](/wiki/Bioids "Bioids") as Humungousaurs.


### Ultimate Alien


* Ultimate Humungousaur was first used by [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") in *[Too Hot to Handle](/wiki/Too_Hot_to_Handle "Too Hot to Handle")*, where he briefly battled [P'andor](/wiki/P%27andor "P'andor"), [Buzz](/wiki/Buzz_(Human) "Buzz (Human)"), [Surgeon](/wiki/Surgeon "Surgeon"), and [Hammer](/wiki/Hammer "Hammer") and accidentally caused a cave in.
* In *[Hero Time](/wiki/Hero_Time "Hero Time")*, Ultimate Humungousaur fought [Overlord](/wiki/Carl_Nesmith "Carl Nesmith") before switching into [Goop](/wiki/Goop_(Classic) "Goop (Classic)").
* In *[Where the Magic Happens](/wiki/Where_the_Magic_Happens "Where the Magic Happens")*, Ultimate Humungousaur briefly battled [Addwaitya](/wiki/Addwaitya "Addwaitya") and some [Scrutins](/wiki/Scrutin "Scrutin") before escaping [Legerdomain](/wiki/Legerdomain "Legerdomain").
* In *[The Enemy of My Enemy](/wiki/The_Enemy_of_My_Enemy "The Enemy of My Enemy")*, Ultimate Humungousaur was defeated by [Ultimate Kevin](/wiki/Ultimate_Kevin "Ultimate Kevin").
* In *[Girl Trouble](/wiki/Girl_Trouble "Girl Trouble")*, Ultimate Humungousaur defeated [Antonio](/wiki/Antonio "Antonio").
* In *[The Ultimate Sacrifice](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Sacrifice "The Ultimate Sacrifice")*, Ultimate Humungousaur battled the [Red Robot](/wiki/Red_Robot "Red Robot") until [Sentient Ultimate Humungousaur](/wiki/Sentient_Ultimate_Humungousaur "Sentient Ultimate Humungousaur") took over his body. Ultimate Humungousaur's personality alternated between his sentient counterpart's and Ben's until he reverted in a way that trapped Ben inside the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)").
* In *[Solitary Alignment](/wiki/Solitary_Alignment "Solitary Alignment")*, Ultimate Humungousaur battled [Sir George](/wiki/Sir_George "Sir George") until they made an agreement over what will happen regarding the showdown with [Dagon](/wiki/Dagon "Dagon").
* In *[Couples Retreat](/wiki/Couples_Retreat "Couples Retreat")*, Ultimate Humungousaur fought [Darkstar](/wiki/Michael_Morningstar_(Classic) "Michael Morningstar (Classic)").
* In *[Night of the Living Nightmare](/wiki/Night_of_the_Living_Nightmare "Night of the Living Nightmare")*, Ultimate Humungousaur was seen confronting [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") in a dream.


### Omniverse


* In *[The Ultimate Heist](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Heist "The Ultimate Heist")*, Negative Ultimate Humungousaur attacked [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") and was pinned down by [Eye Guy](/wiki/Eye_Guy "Eye Guy") before switching into Negative [Ultimate Echo Echo](/wiki/Ultimate_Echo_Echo "Ultimate Echo Echo").
* In *[A Fistful of Brains](/wiki/A_Fistful_of_Brains "A Fistful of Brains")*, ​Negative Ultimate Humungousaur defeated [Crashhopper](/wiki/Crashhopper "Crashhopper") and [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)"), but was defeated by [Feedback](/wiki/Feedback "Feedback").
* In *[For a Few Brains More](/wiki/For_a_Few_Brains_More "For a Few Brains More")*, Negative Ultimate Humungousaur defeated [Bloxx](/wiki/Bloxx "Bloxx"), but was quickly defeated by [Atomix](/wiki/Atomix "Atomix") and switched into Negative [Ultimate Spidermonkey](/wiki/Ultimate_Spidermonkey "Ultimate Spidermonkey") off-screen.
* In *[And Then There Were None](/wiki/And_Then_There_Were_None "And Then There Were None")*, Negative Ultimate Humungousaur chased after [No Watch Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(No_Watch_Timeline) "Ben Tennyson (No Watch Timeline)") before [Spidermonkey](/wiki/Spidermonkey_(Classic) "Spidermonkey (Classic)") got the latter to safety.


### Future (Ultimate Alien)


* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, [Ultimate Ben](/wiki/Ultimate_Ben "Ultimate Ben") altered his size using Ultimate Humungousaur's powers twice to fight [Eon](/wiki/Eon "Eon")'s [servants](/wiki/Eon%27s_Servants "Eon's Servants").

### [Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction](/wiki/Ben_10_Ultimate_Alien:_Cosmic_Destruction "Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction")


Ultimate Humungousaur is a playable alien in Cosmic Destruction, on all platforms. But only during a brief period of time during the first level, the [Catacombs](/wiki/Catacombs "Catacombs"), against [Vulkanus](/wiki/Vulkanus "Vulkanus")' Pickaxe minions. Ultimate Humungousaur can do a stronger version of Humungousaur's shoulder charge and punch the ground to create large shockwaves. He can shoot missiles.



### [Ben 10: Galactic Racing](/wiki/Ben_10:_Galactic_Racing "Ben 10: Galactic Racing")


Ultimate Humungousaur is a playable racer in the game on all consoles.



### [Punch Time Explosion](/wiki/Punch_Time_Explosion "Punch Time Explosion")


Ultimate Humungousaur is Ben's Punch Time Explosion in the game.



### [Project Exonaut](/wiki/Project_Exonaut "Project Exonaut")


Ultimate Humungousaur is a playable Exosuit of the Atlas Brigade on Project Exonaut. His Weapon Mod, similar to Finn, increases the Tridex's Shots to five instead of just three. Ultimate Humongosaur is available for purchase at level 10 for 750 coins.



### [TKO](/wiki/TKO "TKO")


Ultimate Humungousaur is a playable character.



### Toon Football


Ultimate Humungousaur is a playable character. He is the third Ultimate form in the game.

Trivia
------


* Negative Ultimate Humungousaur is the first Ultimate alien to appear on-screen.
* Ultimate Humungousaur is the only Ultimate form that appeared in three different shows in the Classic Timeline.
* Ultimate Humungousaur was the [Alien of the Month](/wiki/Alien_of_the_Month "Alien of the Month") in April 2011.
* Ultimate Humungousaur's shell resembles Bowser's shell from the *[Mario](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mario_(franchise) "wikipedia:Mario (franchise)")* franchise.
* Ultimate Humungousaur was supposed to appear in *[Catch a Falling Star](/wiki/Catch_a_Falling_Star "Catch a Falling Star")*, serving the same purpose his [devolved form](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)") did in the final version of the episode.[[AA 1]](#cite_note-12)
