Appearance
----------


Because his true form is made of living sound waves,[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-link-1) Echo Echo's appearance is actually a small, white containment suit[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-link2-2) made of silicon,[[1]](#cite_note-3)[[2]](#cite_note-4) making his body a living amplifier.


One of his most noticeable features is a square appendage resembling an MP3 player behind his back with a port on it, decorated with a 10 symbol and what looks like cassette tapes on his lower legs. The green circle-shaped indents on the back of Echo Echo's hands are sound holes,[[MW 1]](#cite_note-hole-5) and the inside of his mouth is also green with lines that give it the appearance of an amplifier. He also wears headphones so he would not be hurt by his own sonic screams.[[DM 1]](#cite_note-6)The headphones are attached to a backpack.


In **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")** and **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Echo Echo wore the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, Echo Echo looked mostly the same as before, except his eyes were bigger and his mouth was recolored black.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Echo Echo's eyes bulge out, he has two black stripes on both of his earplugs, which are more pronounced, his legs are stubby, he now has three toes, and the green circles on his hands are now bigger. He is also shorter and has a circular shape on his stomach and the tapes on his legs are now gone. His head is less round and is wider and the outlines of his mouth and eyes are thicker as well as his wires. He wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his forehead.

1/15
Echo Echo can scream at audible to ultrasonic frequencies capable of overloading machinery, shattering steel, stopping projectiles in mid-flight, destroying multiple [Laser Lances](/wiki/Laser_Lance "Laser Lance") at once if concentrated for enough time and even damaging [Taydenite](/wiki/Taydenite "Taydenite") at the right frequency.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-7)


Echo Echo has the ability to duplicate himself, but unlike [Ditto](/wiki/Ditto "Ditto"), the clones are not linked to each other in any way, allowing entire groups to be knocked out without harming the rest and he can clone himself much faster than Ditto could. Even if the clones are defeated, Echo Echo can be fine as long as at least one is still intact.


By combining these two aspects, Echo Echo can create a wall of sound, where he and his clones can redirect projectiles back to their source by bouncing them off of strategically placed clones, as seen during his battle with [Argit](/wiki/Argit "Argit").[[3]](#cite_note-KBS-8)


Echo Echo can create an echo chamber, where his clones attack from all directions and keep screaming until the target is knocked out or defeated, as demonstrated in his fights with [Helen](/wiki/Helen_Wheels "Helen Wheels")[[4]](#cite_note-9) and [Ultimate Kevin](/wiki/Ultimate_Kevin "Ultimate Kevin").[[5]](#cite_note-APP2-10)


Echo Echo's sound holes help him resonate.[[MW 1]](#cite_note-hole-5) He can also detect vibrations in the ground.


Using his sonic screams, Echo Echo can locate objects/people, giving him the ability of echolocation.[[6]](#cite_note-AT-11)


Echo Echo can channel vibrations to enhance his screams, as seen when he touched [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)"), who had absorbed metal that caused him to vibrate when struck by [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)")'s [mana](/wiki/Mana "Mana").[[7]](#cite_note-12)


Echo Echo can transmit sound waves through speakers, such as phones.[[8]](#cite_note-13)


Echo Echo can use his sonic screams to create a force field.[[9]](#cite_note-14)


Echo Echo is quite agile, as he is capable of jumping exceptionally high.[[10]](#cite_note-B10RP2-15)[[3]](#cite_note-KBS-8)


Echo Echo seems to have more strength than his size suggests, as he can carry a power coupling completely on his own,[[11]](#cite_note-16) and kick off the trunk door from the [DX Mark 10](/wiki/DX_Mark_10 "DX Mark 10") with one foot.[[5]](#cite_note-APP2-10)


With the help of multiple clones, Echo Echo can throw a [Plumber Ship](/wiki/Plumber_Ship "Plumber Ship") about a meter,[[12]](#cite_note-17) carry a Murk [Gourmand](/wiki/Gourmand "Gourmand"),[[13]](#cite_note-18) and hold his own against larger opponents such as the [dragon robot](/wiki/Dragon_Robot "Dragon Robot"),[[10]](#cite_note-B10RP2-15) a [Highbreed](/wiki/Highbreed "Highbreed"),[[6]](#cite_note-AT-11) and Ultimate Kevin.[[5]](#cite_note-APP2-10)


Echo Echo also appears to be rather durable, as he and his clones were able to withstand being thrown against a wall.[[10]](#cite_note-B10RP2-15)


Echo Echo can levitate in the air.[[14]](#cite_note-19)[[15]](#cite_note-20)[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-21)


Echo Echo can create copies of any object he is holding while making clones.[[16]](#cite_note-22)[[GT 1]](#cite_note-23)[[note 1]](#cite_note-26)


When under the effects of a cold virus, Echo Echo can accidentally unleash sonic sneezes rather than intentional sonic screams.[[17]](#cite_note-27)

Echo Echo clones are only durable to a certain extent, as they can be destroyed by [Sunder](/wiki/Sunder "Sunder")'s power axe,[[18]](#cite_note-28) an [Aerophibian](/wiki/Aerophibian_(Classic) "Aerophibian (Classic)")'s neuroshock blasts, either a [Methanosian](/wiki/Methanosian "Methanosian") or [Pyronite](/wiki/Pyronite_(Classic) "Pyronite (Classic)")'s fire attacks, or a [Cerebrocrustacean](/wiki/Cerebrocrustacean "Cerebrocrustacean")'s electric attacks.[[5]](#cite_note-APP2-10)


Echo Echo can be rendered unconscious if hit by a quill from a member of [Argit](/wiki/Argit "Argit")'s [species](/wiki/Argit%27s_Species "Argit's Species").[[3]](#cite_note-KBS-8)


Echo Echo can be immobilized by a [Necrofriggian](/wiki/Necrofriggian_(Classic) "Necrofriggian (Classic)")'s ice breath.[[5]](#cite_note-APP2-10)


Echo Echo's sonic screams can easily hurt his teammates' ears.[[10]](#cite_note-B10RP2-15)[[19]](#cite_note-29) However, technically speaking, they are not as powerful as a [Loboan](/wiki/Loboan_(Classic) "Loboan (Classic)")'s sonic howls.[[DR 1]](#cite_note-30)


There is a limit to the number of duplicates Echo Echo can make, which depends on the amount of energy he has access to.[[DM 2]](#cite_note-archive-31)


Echo Echo can be controlled by a [Vladat](/wiki/Vladat "Vladat") via their [Corrupturas](/wiki/Corruptura "Corruptura").[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-32)



History

### Alien Force


* Echo Echo first appeared in *[Ben 10 Returns: Part 2](/wiki/Ben_10_Returns:_Part_2 "Ben 10 Returns: Part 2")*, where he defeated a [dragon robot](/wiki/Dragon_Robot "Dragon Robot") and destroyed a large pile of [Laser Lances](/wiki/Laser_Lance "Laser Lance").
* In *[Kevin's Big Score](/wiki/Kevin%27s_Big_Score "Kevin's Big Score")*, Echo Echo defeated [Argit](/wiki/Argit "Argit").
	+ The same scene reoccurred on TV in *[Fame](/wiki/Fame "Fame")*.
* In *[Alone Together](/wiki/Alone_Together "Alone Together")*, Echo Echo fought [Reinrassig III](/wiki/Reinrassig_III "Reinrassig III"), accidentally got teleported to the planet [Turrawuste](/wiki/Turrawuste "Turrawuste"), defeated a [Dravek](/wiki/Dravek "Dravek"), and located a [Teleporter Pod](/wiki/Teleporter_Pod "Teleporter Pod") by using his echolocation.
* In *[Darkstar Rising](/wiki/Darkstar_Rising "Darkstar Rising")*, Echo Echo briefly battled [Darkstar](/wiki/Michael_Morningstar_(Classic) "Michael Morningstar (Classic)") before switching into [Spidermonkey](/wiki/Spidermonkey_(Classic) "Spidermonkey (Classic)").
* In *[Good Copy, Bad Copy](/wiki/Good_Copy,_Bad_Copy "Good Copy, Bad Copy")*, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Echo Echo battled [Brainstorm](/wiki/Brainstorm "Brainstorm") and [Jetray](/wiki/Jetray_(Classic) "Jetray (Classic)") before switching into Negative [Spidermonkey](/wiki/Spidermonkey_(Classic) "Spidermonkey (Classic)").
* In *[Undercover](/wiki/Undercover "Undercover")*, Echo Echo defeated some [DNAliens](/wiki/DNAliens "DNAliens").
* In *[Grounded](/wiki/Grounded "Grounded")*, Echo Echo was caught by [Carl](/wiki/Carl_Tennyson "Carl Tennyson") and [Sandra](/wiki/Sandra_Tennyson "Sandra Tennyson") and sent back to his room. Despite being unable to help [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") and [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") in person, however, he screamed into [his phone](/wiki/Ben%27s_Cell_Phone "Ben's Cell Phone") to defeat some DNAliens until Sandra took his phone away.
* In *[Unearthed](/wiki/Unearthed "Unearthed")*, Echo Echo defeated some DNAliens.
* In *[Don't Fear the Repo](/wiki/Don%27t_Fear_the_Repo "Don't Fear the Repo")*, Echo Echo scared the [Vreedle Brothers](/wiki/Vreedle_Brothers "Vreedle Brothers") away.
* In *[Fool's Gold](/wiki/Fool%27s_Gold "Fool's Gold")*, Echo Echo and his clones defeated [primal Decka](/wiki/Decka "Decka") by being eaten by him and making him sick before being vomited out.
* In *[Singlehanded](/wiki/Singlehanded "Singlehanded")*, Echo Echo defeated [Sunder](/wiki/Sunder "Sunder").
* In *[Trade Off](/wiki/Trade_Off "Trade Off")*, Echo Echo created some clones to look for Gwen before opting to get into Kevin's [car](/wiki/Kevin%27s_Car_(Original) "Kevin's Car (Original)") instead.
* In *[Above and Beyond](/wiki/Above_and_Beyond "Above and Beyond")*, Echo Echo defeated [Helen](/wiki/Helen_Wheels "Helen Wheels").
* In *[The Secret of Chromastone](/wiki/The_Secret_of_Chromastone "The Secret of Chromastone")*, Echo Echo took out [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") with a sonic sneeze.

* In *[Viktor: The Spoils](/wiki/Viktor:_The_Spoils "Viktor: The Spoils")*, Echo Echo destroyed some missiles before going Ultimate.
* In *[Basic Training](/wiki/Basic_Training "Basic Training")*, Ultimate Echo Echo made a cameo, suggesting that Ben had transformed into Echo Echo first.
* In *[Prisoner Number 775 is Missing](/wiki/Prisoner_Number_775_is_Missing "Prisoner Number 775 is Missing")*, Echo Echo threw a [Plumber Ship](/wiki/Plumber_Ship "Plumber Ship") into a lake.
* In *[Greetings from Techadon](/wiki/Greetings_from_Techadon "Greetings from Techadon")*, Echo Echo fought a [Techadon robot](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots") before going Ultimate. Echo Echo appeared again later when he was changing back to Ben.
* In *[The Flame Keepers' Circle](/wiki/The_Flame_Keepers%27_Circle "The Flame Keepers' Circle")*, Echo Echo battled some [Esoterica](/wiki/Esoterica "Esoterica") and shattered [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)")'s tank.
* In *[Double or Nothing](/wiki/Double_or_Nothing "Double or Nothing")*, Echo Echo fought [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") [Rath](/wiki/Rath_(Classic) "Rath (Classic)") before going Ultimate.
* In *[The Perfect Girlfriend](/wiki/The_Perfect_Girlfriend "The Perfect Girlfriend")*, Echo Echo went Ultimate to fight [Elena](/wiki/Elena_Validus "Elena Validus"). Echo Echo appeared again later when he was changing back to Ben.

### [Ben 10: Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force_(Video_Game) "Ben 10: Alien Force (Video Game)")


Echo Echo is a playable alien character in the Nintendo DS version of the game.


Echo Echo is initially unavailable, but is unlocked upon completion of the Shipyard 1-2 level.


Echo Echo is able to use sonic screams and up to six duplicates to chain various combo attacks.


Echo Echo is not required to be used at all in order to complete the game.



### [Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks](/wiki/Ben_10_Alien_Force:_Vilgax_Attacks "Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks")


Echo Echo is a playable alien in the game.



#### Xbox 360, Nintendo Wii, PlayStation 2 and PlayStation Portable Versions


Due to the Omnitrix rebooting after Ben's abuse of the quick-change feature during his battle with an animated [Mr. Smoothy](/wiki/Mr._Smoothy "Mr. Smoothy") mascot, Echo Echo was temporarily locked until Ben's arrival on [Anur Phaetos](/wiki/Anur_Phaetos_(Classic) "Anur Phaetos (Classic)").


Echo Echo is able to duplicate himself up to two times, for a total of three Echo Echos at a time, in order to activate multiple pressure-sensitive platforms simultaneously.


Echo Echo is able to use his sonic screams to shatter obstructing crystals.


Wall of Sound is an unlockable combo move for Echo Echo.


Echo Echo is vital for progression on the Anur Phaetos and [MorOtesi](/wiki/MorOtesi "MorOtesi") levels of the game.



#### Nintendo DS Version


Echo Echo and [Chromastone](/wiki/Chromastone "Chromastone") are reunlocked upon Ben's arrival on MorOtesi.


Echo Echo is able to use his sonic screams to shatter MorOtesi diamonds.



### [Ben 10 Alien Force: The Rise of Hex](/wiki/Ben_10_Alien_Force:_The_Rise_of_Hex "Ben 10 Alien Force: The Rise of Hex")


Echo Echo is a playable alien in the game.


Due to the Omnitrix sorting out after Ben fiddled with it, Echo Echo was temporarily locked until the beginning of Level 6.


Echo Echo is able to duplicate himself up to two times, for a total of three Echo Echoes at a time, in order to activate linked switches simultaneously for access to further areas. Each duplicate can be controlled separately.


Echo Echo's small size allows him to navigate areas that most of Ben's aliens and Ben himself are unable to access.



### [Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction](/wiki/Ben_10_Ultimate_Alien:_Cosmic_Destruction "Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction")


Echo Echo is a playable alien in the game.


Similar to [Spidermonkey](/wiki/Spidermonkey_(Classic) "Spidermonkey (Classic)"), Echo Echo's small size allows him to fit into small passages other aliens cannot. Like in *Vilgax Attacks*, Echo Echo can only clone himself to a maximum of three clones at a time. He can also go [Ultimate](/wiki/Ultimate_Echo_Echo#Video_Games "Ultimate Echo Echo").


Echo Echo is vital for progression on The Catacombs and The Amazon levels of the game.

Etymology
---------


An [echo](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Echo "wikipedia:Echo") is the reflection of a sound that arrives at a listener with a delay after the direct sound, referencing Echo Echo's audio-kinetic powers. The repetition of the word "echo" in his name references both the nature of an echo and his self-duplication power.



Trivia
------


* Like [Rath](/wiki/Rath_(Classic) "Rath (Classic)") and [Atomix](/wiki/Atomix "Atomix"), Echo Echo gives names to his attacks and moves. The names of the attacks are notably less hammy than the former two. This trait is shared with Ultimate Echo Echo.
* Originally, Echo Echo was a robot-like alien.[[DM 2]](#cite_note-archive-31) This was later changed in **Omniverse**, as he is actually living sound waves wearing a containment suit.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-link-1)[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-link2-2) It is unknown who came up with the latter concept, as [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") heard it from someone else.[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-33)
	+ Echo Echo and [NRG](/wiki/NRG "NRG") are the only aliens whose appearance are actually containment suits.
* If Ben were to use Echo Echo with [Skurd](/wiki/Skurd "Skurd") attached to the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)"), Echo Echo's clones would have to remain huddled to use Skurd's power.[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-34)
* Echo Echo's singing sounds like auto-tune, though he is really good at dubstep.[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-35)
* Echo Echo's voice has changed throughout his appearances in the franchise:
	+ In *[Ben 10 Returns: Part 2](/wiki/Ben_10_Returns:_Part_2 "Ben 10 Returns: Part 2")* and *[Kevin's Big Score](/wiki/Kevin%27s_Big_Score "Kevin's Big Score")*, it originally sounded very high-pitched and tinny, and had a strong robotic filter.
	+ In *[Alone Together](/wiki/Alone_Together "Alone Together")*, it became higher-pitched, and the robotic filter was less prevalent.
	+ From *[Good Copy, Bad Copy](/wiki/Good_Copy,_Bad_Copy "Good Copy, Bad Copy")* until his final appearance in **Ultimate Alien** (*[Inspector #13](/wiki/Inspector_13_(Episode) "Inspector 13 (Episode)")*; excluding *[Undercover](/wiki/Undercover "Undercover")*), the robotic filter was missing.
	+ In *Undercover*, it sounded like it did in *Alone Together*, but slightly deeper.
	+ In **Omniverse**, it is high-pitched and more robotic, and it echoes more, akin to speaking through a portable microphone.
	+ At some point in early production of **Alien Force**, Echo Echo's voice was supposed to sound like his most frequently-used voice in the series, but with an echo effect.[[merch 1]](#cite_note-36)
* [Dee Bradley Baker](/wiki/Dee_Bradley_Baker "Dee Bradley Baker") did Echo Echo's voice by talking while inhaling and squinching his upper throat shut.[[DBB 1]](#cite_note-37)[[DBB 2]](#cite_note-38)
* Echo Echo originally had the ability to extend his neck and arms in the pre-production stage of **Alien Force**.[[GM 1]](#cite_note-39)
* Echo Echo had concept art drawn by Eric Canete[[EC 1]](#cite_note-twitter-40) and [Glen Murakami](/wiki/Glen_Murakami "Glen Murakami"), with his final design created by Glen Murakami, with color styling by Chris Hooten.[[GW 1]](#cite_note-https:.2F.2Fwww.webstagramsite.com.2Fmedia.2FB3vioYgjHZw-41)
	+ Echo Echo's design was approved rather quickly.[[EC 1]](#cite_note-twitter-40)
* Echo Echo's design was based on an [Apple](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apple_Inc. "wikipedia:Apple Inc.") [iPod](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/iPod "wikipedia:iPod").[[GW 1]](#cite_note-https:.2F.2Fwww.webstagramsite.com.2Fmedia.2FB3vioYgjHZw-41)
* Echo Echo was supposed to retain the Omnitrix symbol on his chest in the pre-production stage of **Omniverse**.[[DJW 10]](#cite_note-42)
* Echo Echo was [Dwayne McDuffie](/wiki/Dwayne_McDuffie "Dwayne McDuffie")'s favorite Omnitrix alien.[[DM 3]](#cite_note-43)
* From Arc 4 of **Omniverse** onwards, Echo Echo is replaced with [Juryrigg](/wiki/Juryrigg "Juryrigg") in the opening intro of the show.
* Had [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Echo Echo appeared in **Omniverse**, he would have been either a red recolor of Echo Echo's appearance in said series or at least have some red markings.[[DJW 11]](#cite_note-44)
	+ Derrick eventually drew a version of Negative Echo Echo in the **Omniverse** art style in 2020.[[DJW 12]](#cite_note-45)
* In *[FusionFall](/wiki/FusionFall "FusionFall")*, there is a [fusion of Echo Echo](/wiki/Fusion_Echo_Echo "Fusion Echo Echo") that, when defeated, releases [Forever Ninja](/wiki/Forever_Ninja "Forever Ninja") sets.
* Echo Echo is similar in some ways to [Echoes](https://jojo.fandom.com/wiki/Echoes), the Stand of Koichi Hirose in Part 4 of the manga and anime series *[Jojo's Bizarre Adventure](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jojo%27s_Bizarre_Adventure_(2012_TV_series) "wikipedia:Jojo's Bizarre Adventure (2012 TV series)")*.
	+ Both Echo Echo and Echoes ACT 3 are short, white humanoids with green as a secondary color.
	+ Echoes (specifically ACT 1 and ACT 2) and Echo Echo are able to unleash powerful sound attacks from multiple angles simultaneously (Echo Echo via his duplication, and Echoes through writing onomatopoeia on surfaces).
