Appearance

### Ben as Ditto


Ditto is about the height of an average human child. He has white skin, a black and white head and face, black shoulders, large hands with four digits and feet which are merely stubs. Ditto also has three fin-like growths on his head, and gem-like extended sensory nodes[[TP 1]](#cite_note-DeviantArt-4) on his arms and waist.


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Ditto's clothes were black and had an overall-like design. He had green eyes with light green shines and wore the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol on his forehead.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Ditto looked exactly the same as he did in the original series, except the white section outlining the hourglass symbol across his chest and arms was blue. He wore the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, 11-year-old Ditto's design is roughly similar to his original appearance except he appears smaller and stubbier. All of his fins are recolored white, with the ones on the sides of his head now connected to his eyes. He has two small whiskers on each of his cheeks, his fingers are sharper, and the sensory nodes on his shoulders have been relocated to his shoulders and wrists. His jumpsuit has been altered as well, with his neck being completely black and the feet being cut off to reveal three cat-like toes on each foot. The Omnitrix symbol on his forehead has been recolored green.


16-year-old Ditto looks exactly like his 11-year-old self, except he his fins are recolored green and his jumpsuit is now green with black patches under his arms, green sleeves, and black ankles. This jumpsuit seems to have a design based on the Omnitrix's insignia, with black triangles on the shoulders and green in the center. The three sensory nodes on Ditto's waist are now on a black belt. Like his younger self, Ditto wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his forehead.

1/7
Ditto has the ability to clone himself without limits through binary fission.[[pop-up 2]](#cite_note-image-5)[[2]](#cite_note-DWS-3)[[note 1]](#cite_note-6)


Each Ditto operates independently, regardless of what the others want them to do and each clone is indistinguishable from the others. This allows Ditto to do multiple tasks simultaneously or to cover a large area. An example of this is when Ditto is able to extend his reach across a vast distance by simply cloning himself in a straight line, thereby forming a chain of Dittos.[[3]](#cite_note-OMWAT-7)


The extended sensory nodes on Ditto's body[[note 2]](#cite_note-9) assist in the sensory web that connects all of his clones[[TP 1]](#cite_note-DeviantArt-4) by coordinating the hive mind communication between them.[[TP 2]](#cite_note-10) This allows the clones to share senses and physical sensations, such as pain.


Ditto is more flexible than a [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)"), and can breathe underwater.[[2]](#cite_note-DWS-3)[[1]](#cite_note-AFoB-1)


Ditto is quite agile and can burrow into the ground and pop out like a [mole](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mole_(animal) "wikipedia:Mole (animal)").[[1]](#cite_note-AFoB-1)


Ditto can also run very fast.[[1]](#cite_note-AFoB-1)


Ditto can jump surprisingly high for his size; while playing basketball, Ditto slam-dunked the ball through a hoop in a way that would be difficult for an average human to jump high enough to do.[[2]](#cite_note-DWS-3)

1/6
In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Ditto's clones shared a link to keep track of each other,[[pop-up 2]](#cite_note-image-5)[[2]](#cite_note-DWS-3) making it so that if one Ditto was hurt, all the others would feel the same pain and if one Ditto got killed, the others were automatically killed as well, thus negating the ability to form an army of clones. However, this is no longer true as of **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, as each clone is independent from each other's pain and death.[[1]](#cite_note-AFoB-1)


Ditto's clones can be fused back together with enough force.[[3]](#cite_note-OMWAT-7)


If Ditto's clones cannot agree on one plan, an argument might break out between them.[[2]](#cite_note-DWS-3)


If the Dittos are too far apart from each other, he cannot change back into [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") until all the duplicates are close enough together. It seems that this requires more power from the Omnitrix than normal, as seen later when he could only transform into [Upgrade](/wiki/Upgrade_(Classic) "Upgrade (Classic)") for a fraction of the normal time limit.[[2]](#cite_note-DWS-3)


Ditto is not resistant to cold temperatures.[[1]](#cite_note-AFoB-1)


Ditto can be harmed by electricity while in water, such as when he was tortured with an electrical prod by Dr. Animo while he was held captive in a container filled with water.[[2]](#cite_note-DWS-3)


Ditto can be controlled by [Vladats](/wiki/Vladat "Vladat") via their [Corrupturas](/wiki/Corruptura "Corruptura"). However, the latter cannot control all of the Ditto clones as each of them are independent.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-11) Furthermore, the Corrupturas will not be copied across his clones.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-12)


Being a [Splixson](/wiki/Splixson "Splixson"), Ditto is vulnerable to being trapped by a [Panuncian](/wiki/Panuncian "Panuncian"), as they also possess the ability to clone themselves with no limit.[[5]](#cite_note-13)[[1]](#cite_note-AFoB-1)


Ditto can be forced to duplicate if pulled with enough force.[[2]](#cite_note-DWS-3)



History
-------


### Ben 10


* Ditto first appeared in *[Divided We Stand](/wiki/Divided_We_Stand "Divided We Stand")*, where he was accidentally unlocked by [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") and fought [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)")'s [mutant seagull](/wiki/Mutant_Seagull "Mutant Seagull"). Then, Ditto played basketball with his clones and, eventually, one of the Dittos was caught by Animo to have his DNA copied. Later, Ditto defeated Animo's army of [Mutant Lepidopterrans](/wiki/Mutant_Lepidopterran "Mutant Lepidopterran").
* In *[Ready to Rumble](/wiki/Ready_to_Rumble "Ready to Rumble")*, Ditto saved [Mrs. Fang](/wiki/Mrs._Fang "Mrs. Fang") and defeated [Mr. Beck](/wiki/Mr._Beck "Mr. Beck").


### Five Years Prior to Omniverse


* In *[Ben Again](/wiki/Ben_Again "Ben Again")*, 16-year-old Ben in 11-year-old Ben's body used Ditto to battle [Eon](/wiki/Eon "Eon") until the latter retreated.


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, Ditto was re-unlocked by [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Ultimate_Alien) "Ben 10,000 (Ultimate Alien)").


### Omniverse


* In *[O Mother, Where Art Thou?](/wiki/O_Mother,_Where_Art_Thou%3F "O Mother, Where Art Thou?")*, Ditto fought some [Pretty Boy Vreedle](/wiki/Pretty_Boy_Vreedle "Pretty Boy Vreedle") clones and chased after [Ma Vreedle](/wiki/Ma_Vreedle "Ma Vreedle").
* In *[A Fistful of Brains](/wiki/A_Fistful_of_Brains "A Fistful of Brains")*, Ditto and his clones tried to escape from [Panuncian](/wiki/Panuncian "Panuncian"), but eventually failed in the end.
* In *[Animo Crackers](/wiki/Animo_Crackers "Animo Crackers")*, Ditto tried to chase [Spanner](/wiki/Ken_Tennyson "Ken Tennyson"), but was caught off guard and defeated by [Future Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Future) "Aloysius Animo (Future)").
* In *[Universe vs. Tennyson](/wiki/Universe_vs._Tennyson "Universe vs. Tennyson")*, Ditto fought [Fistrick](/wiki/Fistrick "Fistrick"), but reverted.
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Ditto appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.


### Future (Original)


* In *[Ken 10](/wiki/Ken_10 "Ken 10")*, [Ken](/wiki/Ken_Tennyson "Ken Tennyson") as Ditto and [Devlin](/wiki/Devlin_Levin "Devlin Levin") found the [Null Void Projector](/wiki/Null_Void_Projector "Null Void Projector").

Etymology
---------


"Ditto" is a word that means "the same thing again", referencing Ditto's self-duplication ability.



Trivia
------


* [Tom Perkins](/wiki/Tom_Perkins "Tom Perkins") designed Ditto for the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"); the first design he made was approved as the crew was very busy when Ditto was conceived during development.[[TP 3]](#cite_note-14)
* According to [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s fansites on the [Extranet](/wiki/Extranet "Extranet"), Ditto is his cutest alien.[[6]](#cite_note-15)[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-16)
* If Ben were to use Ditto with [Skurd](/wiki/Skurd "Skurd") attached to the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)"), only the original Ditto would have Skurd.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-17) If all the clones had to use Skurd's power, they would have to remain huddled together.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-18)
* [Ben 23](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Dimension_23) "Ben Tennyson (Dimension 23)") may not have an alternate counterpart to Ditto in his [Omnitrix](/wiki/Hero_Watch "Hero Watch"). If he did, however, then the alien's name would be Copy Copy 2.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-19)
* Three clones of 11-year-old Ditto replace 11-year-old [Feedback](/wiki/Feedback "Feedback") at the end of the opening intro of **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Arc 4 onwards.
* Ditto seems to be a homage to Yakko Warner from *[Animaniacs](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Animaniacs "wikipedia:Animaniacs")*, as they share the same voice actor ([Rob Paulsen](/wiki/Rob_Paulsen "Rob Paulsen")) and similar mannerisms.
	+ Likewise, 10/11-year-old Ditto shares a similar black and white color layout to Yakko.
* Ditto and [Wildvine](/wiki/Wildvine_(Classic) "Wildvine (Classic)") are the only aliens to be manually (and accidentally) unlocked by Ben.
* Ditto's design in **Omniverse** has some elements of *[Pikmin](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pikmin "wikipedia:Pikmin")*.[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-20)
* Had Ditto appeared in the [reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)"), the crew would have ideally had Rob Paulsen return to voice him.[[DR 1]](#cite_note-21)
