Appearance
----------


Walkatrout is a small, blue fish-like alien with stubby fins for arms, a shark-like dorsal fin, and a pair of thin, spindly legs jutting from his underside. He has a wide mouth with an underbite, and gills on his cheeks with bulging green eyes.


Walkatrout wears a green belt-like strap around his "waist" and back, with the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol connecting the front.

--------------------

1/5
Walkatrout has a slippery body, making him hard to grab[[2]](#cite_note-2)[[1]](#cite_note-IaMMMBWP1-1) and allowing him to slide on the [Plumber HQ](/wiki/Plumber_Headquarters "Plumber Headquarters") floor.[[3]](#cite_note-Outbreak-3)


Walkatrout has gills and can breathe underwater.[[4]](#cite_note-TFC-4)


Walkatrout has a degree of enhanced durability, as is shown when he was knocked onto the ground along with [Speedyquick](/wiki/Speedyquick "Speedyquick") without serious damage.[[1]](#cite_note-IaMMMBWP1-1)


Although his body is quite weak, his tail is somewhat strong, as he was able to hurt [Liam](/wiki/Liam "Liam")'s [Vaxasaurian](/wiki/Vaxasaurian_(Classic) "Vaxasaurian (Classic)") form with it and escape.[[3]](#cite_note-Outbreak-3)


Walkatrout is somewhat acrobatic, as he can perform cartwheels.[[3]](#cite_note-Outbreak-3)


Being an [Ickthyperambuloid](/wiki/Ickthyperambuloid "Ickthyperambuloid"), a [Vladat](/wiki/Vladat "Vladat")'s [Corrupturas](/wiki/Corruptura "Corruptura") would not stick to Walkatrout due to his slippery skin.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-5)



Weaknesses
----------


* ![]()Small Size
* ![]()Slippery Body Hindering Teammates
* ![]()Vulnerable Tail

1/6
Walkatrout is physically very weak, being unable to break through a museum's doors[[5]](#cite_note-FaTM-6) and he is not suited for hand-to-hand combat. He also has no hands, which makes grabbing things impossible for him.


Walkatrout is not very fast, as [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was disappointed transforming into him when he was trying to run away.[[6]](#cite_note-7)


Walkatrout is very small, giving him a disadvantage against larger opponents. He is only as tall as a [Vaxasaurian](/wiki/Vaxasaurian_(Classic) "Vaxasaurian (Classic)")'s foot.[[3]](#cite_note-Outbreak-3) Despite this, Walkatrout is still unable to fit into cramped spaces such as toilets without assistance.[[4]](#cite_note-TFC-4)


While Walkatrout's slippery body can be used well as a strength, it also prevents him from being held up by his teammates,[[1]](#cite_note-IaMMMBWP1-1) unless he were to be held up by his tail.[[3]](#cite_note-Outbreak-3)


Being an Ickthyperambuloid, Walkatrout is vulnerable to his natural predator.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-8)



History
-------


### Omniverse


* Walkatrout first appeared in *[Outbreak](/wiki/Outbreak "Outbreak")*, where he was accidentally unlocked by [Blukic](/wiki/Blukic "Blukic") and [Driba](/wiki/Driba "Driba") when they tried to fix the Omnitrix. After escaping from [Liam](/wiki/Liam "Liam"), Blukic and Driba switched him into [Pesky Dust](/wiki/Pesky_Dust "Pesky Dust").
* In *[Charm School](/wiki/Charm_School "Charm School")*, Ben accidentally transformed into Walkatrout and was disappointed.
* In *[Fight at the Museum](/wiki/Fight_at_the_Museum "Fight at the Museum")*, Walkatrout failed to escape the exhibit room he and [Kai](/wiki/Kai_Green "Kai Green") were trapped in.
* In *[It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 1](/wiki/It%27s_a_Mad,_Mad,_Mad_Ben_World:_Part_1 "It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 1")*, Walkatrout traveled to [Bellwood](/wiki/Bellwood "Bellwood") with [Speedyquick](/wiki/Speedyquick "Speedyquick")'s help.
* In *[The Final Countdown](/wiki/The_Final_Countdown "The Final Countdown")*, Walkatrout got in the locked [Plumber HQ](/wiki/Plumber_Headquarters "Plumber Headquarters") through the toilet.
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Walkatrout appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

Etymology
---------


Walkatrout's name is derived from "walk about" and "[trout](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trout "wikipedia:Trout")", the latter of which is the common name for a number of certain species of freshwater fish.



Trivia
------


* The design for Walkatrout came from [Gil Steptoe](/wiki/Gil_Steptoe "Gil Steptoe"), drawn by Steven Choi and Chap Yaep.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-9)[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-10)
* Walkatrout's name was coined by Driba;[[3]](#cite_note-Outbreak-3) Ben starts using that name in *[An American Benwolf in London](/wiki/An_American_Benwolf_in_London "An American Benwolf in London")*.
* Walkatrout is one of four aliens unlocked by [Driba](/wiki/Driba "Driba") and [Blukic](/wiki/Blukic "Blukic"); the other three being [Pesky Dust](/wiki/Pesky_Dust "Pesky Dust"), [Mole-Stache](/wiki/Mole-Stache "Mole-Stache"), and [The Worst](/wiki/The_Worst "The Worst").[[3]](#cite_note-Outbreak-3)
* [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo")'s [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") does not contain Walkatrout's DNA.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-11)
	+ Despite this, [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") drew some art of Negative Walkatrout in 2020, which can be found in the [gallery](/wiki/Walkatrout/Gallery "Walkatrout/Gallery").[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-12)
