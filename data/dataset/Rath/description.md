Appearance
----------


### Ben as Rath


Rath is about nine feet tall. He resembles an orange and white bipedal [tiger](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tiger "wikipedia:Tiger") with one black claw coming out of each wrist and no tail. He is also muscular and has green eyes and black stripes on his shoulders, head, legs, and upper body as well as a white jaw, neck, chest, stomach, hands, and feet. The [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") is located in the center of his chest. Both Gwen and Azmuth take these appearance cues as Rath.


In *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, fur stuck out of Rath's arms, legs, chin, and head, and his muscles now have protruding veins when using his strength, making his appearance more pronounced and realistic in Generator Rex Art Style.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Rath looked similar to his **Alien Force** and **Ultimate Alien** appearance, but was more muscular, with longer and more pointed eyebrows which were completely black (except in his [first reappearance](/wiki/It_Was_Them "It Was Them")). His fur appeared darker as well.


In *[The Secret of Dos Santos](/wiki/The_Secret_of_Dos_Santos "The Secret of Dos Santos")*, [Skurd](/wiki/Skurd "Skurd") fixes the Omnitrix error that made Rath naked by giving him an outfit similar to that of a luchador (a Mexican masked wrestler). Rath's luchador outfit has a green and black color scheme, consisting of black boots, black gloves, and a luchador mask. Rath's claws are now greyish-silver instead of black, and the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") is now on his luchador belt.



### Albedo as Negative Rath


In **Alien Force**, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Rath looked exactly like [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s Rath but had red eyes and a red Ultimatrix symbol. In **Ultimate Alien**, Negative Rath lacked an Ultimatrix symbol.


In **Omniverse**, Negative Rath looks mostly the same as Ben Prime's Rath except his fur is recolored white, his stripes and skin are recolored dark pink, and his eyes are recolored red. In concept art, Negative Rath is naked yet wears a red [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") on his chest.


In *[Malgax Attacks](/wiki/Malgax_Attacks "Malgax Attacks")*, Negative Rath has clothing similar to the one Ben Prime's Rath gains in *The Secret of Dos Santos*. However, he lacks the mask and the tank top. He wears black shorts, red gloves and boots, a red belt with the Ultimatrix symbol on it.



### Mad Ben as Mad Rath


[Mad Ben](/wiki/Mad_Ben "Mad Ben")'s Rath has darker fur, orange eyes, and a hairstyle like his [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)") form. He also has Mad Ben's [Mad Max](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Max_Rockatansky "wikipedia:Max Rockatansky") style silver plates with orange spikes on his shoulders, lower arms, and calves. The [Omnitrix](/wiki/Power_Watch "Power Watch") symbol is on a black panel on his chest and is colored orange. He is also slightly thinner than [Ben Prime](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s Rath.

Personality
-----------


Rath's personality resembles that of a comic book vigilante, a stereotypical professional wrestler, and a stereotypical high school jerk.


Rath almost always speaks in third person. Sometimes, when he becomes extremely angry, he mixes up metaphors (such as "a man's food is his castle"[[1]](#cite_note-CoR-1) or "[you](/wiki/Manny_Armstrong "Manny Armstrong") just bit off an eye that's bigger than your stomach can chew").[[2]](#cite_note-AaB-4) He is self-aware that they do not make sense, but still continues to use them anyway.


Rath's muscle-over-brains mindset makes him needlessly argue with everyone around him. He even shouts at inanimate objects, like a hole he made in the ground, a stolen spaceship, gravity,[[3]](#cite_note-PN775IM-5) water,[[4]](#cite_note-GF-6) fire, the Omnitrix[[5]](#cite_note-MHR-7) and Ultimatrix[[6]](#cite_note-8) for their malfunctions, the [Proto-Tool](/wiki/Proto-Tool "Proto-Tool")'s laser pointer, a giant ball of yarn,[[7]](#cite_note-Catfight-9) and a paper bag on his head, arguments of which were ridiculous and that occasionally lead him to harm both himself and others. Rath's big mouth often gets him and his teammates into trouble, such as when [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") tried to reach an agreement with [Vulkanus](/wiki/Vulkanus "Vulkanus"), Rath began mocking the latter's defeats from them and called him "baby man", resulting in the negotiation downgrading into a fight.[[1]](#cite_note-CoR-1)


When Rath is angry at someone or something, he usually yells a sentence starting with, "Let me tell ya something" followed by the full name of the person, calling Kevin by this,[[1]](#cite_note-CoR-1) or their position, labeling [Khyber](/wiki/Khyber "Khyber") as "self-proclaimed greatest huntsman in the galaxy".[[8]](#cite_note-OPaP2-10)


Rath is very fearless to the point of recklessness, as he didn't hesitate to literally jump down [Jarett](/wiki/Jarett "Jarett")'s throat to rescue the [Tiffin](/wiki/Tiffin "Tiffin") or about firing the [Vreedle Brothers](/wiki/Vreedle_Brothers "Vreedle Brothers") out into space after they blasted him and his tray of food. Rath also does not make empty threats, as shown after saving the Tiffin and threatening to mutilate Jarett with rearrangement of his organs into a sweater if he started a war with the [Lewodans](/wiki/Lewodan "Lewodan"), his low snarls hinted he meant it.[[1]](#cite_note-CoR-1) He also once outright admitted he intended to hit [Rojo](/wiki/Rojo "Rojo") in the head during a train robbery.[[9]](#cite_note-HEWTL-11) [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") even agreed with [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)")'s premonition that Rath would do something unthinkable if he ever went after [J.T.](/wiki/J.T._(Classic) "J.T. (Classic)") and [Cash](/wiki/Cash_Murray_(Classic) "Cash Murray (Classic)") upon learning about them taking credit for his heroics.[[10]](#cite_note-12)


Rath has a lot of pride in his capabilities and himself, expressing bluntness or anger instead of gratitude when Gwen and [Rook](/wiki/Rook_Blonko "Rook Blonko") saved him on several occasions.[[9]](#cite_note-HEWTL-11)[[11]](#cite_note-13)[[5]](#cite_note-MHR-7) Similarly, he denied needing enhancements from [Skurd](/wiki/Skurd "Skurd") against [Exo-Skull](/wiki/Exo-Skull "Exo-Skull").[[12]](#cite_note-SoDS-14)


Over time, Ben has gained slight control of himself as Rath. He once lowered his voice when talking to Gwen on the phone, even though he had two [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights") at his ferocious mercy, and mildly called her unempathetic response "harsh".[[13]](#cite_note-Duped-15)


Because of Rath's extremely low intellect, Kevin can't help but mock or make fun of him.


Despite the fact that Rath is supposed to know nothing but anger and fighting, he is shown to be friendly towards the Tiffin, though not at first.[[1]](#cite_note-CoR-1)


Rath does not have a very good sense of humor, as he gets mad whenever someone mistakes him for a "kitty".


Rath lacked the strong sense of shame his kind have, as he was always naked but had no shame.[[14]](#cite_note-FAtC-16)


Rath sometimes yells out his special moves while fighting. These include Polaris Piledriver, Antarian Arm Bar, Ophiuchus Face-plant, Sirius Butt-Kicking, Cosmic Drop and [Incursean](/wiki/Incursean_(Classic) "Incursean (Classic)") Ambassador.


Ever since Skurd fixed the error with the Omnitrix that caused Rath to go naked, Rath is capable of speaking in Spanish, though it should be noted that this is a feature granted by the Omnitrix, and is not something that Rath naturally knows.[[12]](#cite_note-SoDS-14)

Rath possesses enhanced strength and agility, enabling him to jump great distances and lift objects heavier than himself. This was shown by how he was able to easily dismember the [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights")' paramilitary tank via brute force and toss the still extremely large pieces around (with one hand and later with two for the tank's body) with minimal to no effort.[[13]](#cite_note-Duped-15) Rath's strength also gives him an advantage in combat, allowing him to power his way through many opponents and smash through tough defenses.


Rath's unbridled aggression gives him a psychological edge in combat. There are very few things that Rath fears and many opponents find it extremely difficult to beat him if he is fully enraged.


Rath possesses a large, retractable black claw on each wrist, which are sharp enough to cut through [Attea](/wiki/Attea "Attea")'s Incursean Blaster and a box.[[7]](#cite_note-Catfight-9) These claws can be used to stab and slice opponents or to create a shock wave when stabbed into the ground. Whenever Rath gets determined or angry, his claws seem to grow larger.[[1]](#cite_note-CoR-1)


Rath's also has a very strong bite force, as he was able to easily shatter [SevenSeven](/wiki/SevenSeven "SevenSeven")'s blade with his sharp teeth.[[16]](#cite_note-25)


Rath is extremely durable, being able to take a point-blank laser blast with no apparent effect and survive a free fall from several hundred feet in the air with only a bit of dizziness as a result[[3]](#cite_note-PN775IM-5) and break through a concrete wall without showing any pain.[[5]](#cite_note-MHR-7)


Rath can run very fast,[[17]](#cite_note-26) and run on walls.[[18]](#cite_note-27)


Rath can release a loud roar, although this seems to be more of an intimidation tactic rather than an offensive feature.[[1]](#cite_note-CoR-1)


Rath can balance on a giant yarn ball[[7]](#cite_note-Catfight-9) and a unicycle.[[19]](#cite_note-28)


Even though he is not one of the smartest of Ben's aliens, Rath can perform fighting moves with expert execution, as seen when he used wrestling moves to defeat [Manny](/wiki/Manny_Armstrong "Manny Armstrong")[[2]](#cite_note-AaB-4) and a [Techadon robot](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots").[[20]](#cite_note-29) Rath either made up the moves himself or learned them from someone.[[ES 1]](#cite_note-30)


Rath is also strong enough to overpower female tetramands as he overpowered Looma on two occasions.

Rath's brain is hardwired for aggression and as such, Rath's main weakness is his lack of intelligence and highly aggressive temperament. This causes Rath to become easily distracted and difficult to stop once he starts fighting. He is also not quite adept for diplomatic approaches, particularly negotiations, and can make bad or difficult situations worse.[[1]](#cite_note-CoR-1)


Rath's apparent lack of intelligence also often convinces him to needlessly argue with inanimate objects.[[3]](#cite_note-PN775IM-5)


Rath is scared of water, like most actual cats, but unlike actual tigers.[[4]](#cite_note-GF-6)


Rath can be subdued by the Hephestan Neuro Grip.[[8]](#cite_note-OPaP2-10)


Rath has very sensitive hearing, which makes him vulnerable to loud piercing sounds, such as a certain [sonic frequency signal](/wiki/Sonic_Frequency_Device "Sonic Frequency Device").[[14]](#cite_note-FAtC-16)


Being a feline-based alien, Rath can be controlled by people that have the ability to control cats, like [Nyancy Chan](/wiki/Nyancy_Chan "Nyancy Chan"). He can also be easily distracted by a laser pointer.[[7]](#cite_note-Catfight-9)


Rath can be trapped in a mana bubble. A lack of oxygen will cause him to pass out.[[1]](#cite_note-CoR-1)


Rath can easily be scared by [Toepick](/wiki/Toepick "Toepick")'s [species](/wiki/Toepick%27s_Species "Toepick's Species").[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-31)


Despite his enhanced strength, Rath is physically weaker than [Tetramands](/wiki/Tetramand_(Classic) "Tetramand (Classic)").[[DR 1]](#cite_note-32)



History
-------


### Alien Force


* Rath first appeared in *[Con of Rath](/wiki/Con_of_Rath "Con of Rath")*, where he was accidentally unlocked by [Tiffin](/wiki/Tiffin "Tiffin"), and [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") remained as said transformation until [Zaw-Veenull](/wiki/Zaw-Veenull "Zaw-Veenull") and [Cicely](/wiki/Cicely "Cicely") took Tiffin away at the end. Ben then transformed into him a second time, telling [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") that "Rath wanted a word with him".
* In *[Primus](/wiki/Primus_(Episode) "Primus (Episode)")*, [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)") as Rath was defeated by [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)").
* In *[Above and Beyond](/wiki/Above_and_Beyond "Above and Beyond")*, Rath defeated [Manny](/wiki/Manny_Armstrong "Manny Armstrong").
* In *[Vendetta](/wiki/Vendetta "Vendetta")*, Rath battled some [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights").
* In *[The Final Battle: Part 1](/wiki/The_Final_Battle:_Part_1 "The Final Battle: Part 1")*, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Rath jumped out of the [Rust Bucket 2](/wiki/Rust_Bucket_2 "Rust Bucket 2") to find [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)").

* In *[The Big Story](/wiki/The_Big_Story "The Big Story")*, Rath defeated [SevenSeven](/wiki/SevenSeven "SevenSeven").
* In *[Prisoner Number 775 is Missing](/wiki/Prisoner_Number_775_is_Missing "Prisoner Number 775 is Missing")*, Rath failed to stop [Prisoner 775](/wiki/Prisoner_775 "Prisoner 775") from escaping in a stolen [Plumber Ship](/wiki/Plumber_Ship "Plumber Ship"), but was able to make it crash by taking pieces of the ship apart.
* In *[Greetings from Techadon](/wiki/Greetings_from_Techadon "Greetings from Techadon")*, Rath defeated a [Techadon robot](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots").
* In *[Double or Nothing](/wiki/Double_or_Nothing "Double or Nothing")*, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Rath battled [Echo Echo](/wiki/Echo_Echo "Echo Echo"), but was defeated when the latter went [Ultimate](/wiki/Ultimate_Echo_Echo "Ultimate Echo Echo").

Etymology
---------


Rath's name is a misspelled version of "wrath", meaning fury and rage, referencing his attitude and fury-based powers.



Trivia
------


* [Dwayne McDuffie](/wiki/Dwayne_McDuffie "Dwayne McDuffie") came up with both Rath's personality,[[DM 1]](#cite_note-33) and the wrestling moves he performed in *[Above and Beyond](/wiki/Above_and_Beyond "Above and Beyond")*.[[ES 2]](#cite_note-34)
	+ Rath's personality is similar to that of [Marvel Comics](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marvel_Comics "wikipedia:Marvel Comics") superheroes [Wolverine](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wolverine_(character) "wikipedia:Wolverine (character)") and the Hulk due to all three of them having aggressive personalities. Additionally, Rath shares Hulk's tendency to refer to himself in third person and low intelligence.
* Rath's catchphrase, "LEMME TELL YA SOMETHIN'!", appears to be a reference to Hulk Hogan's signature catch phrase, "Let me tell you something, brother".[[Bandai 1]](#cite_note-Bandai-35)
* Rath is [Eugene Son](/wiki/Eugene_Son "Eugene Son"), [Paul Eiding](/wiki/Paul_Eiding "Paul Eiding"), and [Yuri Lowenthal](/wiki/Yuri_Lowenthal "Yuri Lowenthal")'s favorite Omnitrix alien,[[ES 3]](#cite_note-36)[[ES 4]](#cite_note-37)[[AC 1]](#cite_note-38)[[YL 1]](#cite_note-39) the latter of whom states that his love for the [Appoplexian](/wiki/Appoplexian_(Classic) "Appoplexian (Classic)") transformation is mostly because of [John DiMaggio](/wiki/John_DiMaggio "John DiMaggio")'s voice acting for the character.
* Appoplexians have an advanced sense of shame and typically wear clothes. However, Rath used to be naked all the time[[14]](#cite_note-FAtC-16) because of "a horrible error on the part of the Omnitrix" that was eventually fixed by Skurd.[[12]](#cite_note-SoDS-14) This change eventually became permanent.[[DJW 10]](#cite_note-40)[[DJW 11]](#cite_note-41)
* Out of all of [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s aliens, [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") and [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") find Rath the most entertaining and amusing due to his mediocre intelligence, occasionally joking about him behind his back or out of his earsight.[[3]](#cite_note-PN775IM-5)
* The [Vreedle Brothers](/wiki/Vreedle_Brothers "Vreedle Brothers") state that Appoplexians such as Rath are much more aggressive and dimmer than [they](/wiki/Vreedle "Vreedle") are.[[1]](#cite_note-CoR-1)
* Similar to [Echo Echo](/wiki/Echo_Echo "Echo Echo") and [Atomix](/wiki/Atomix "Atomix"), whenever Rath executes wrestling moves, he names them after stars.[[2]](#cite_note-AaB-4)
	+ When Rath is about to fight in certain episodes, the sound of a ringside bell can be heard.
* In September 2011, *[FusionFall](/wiki/FusionFall "FusionFall")* released [Rath Nano](/wiki/Rath_Nano "Rath Nano").
* Rath is replaced with 11-year-old [Blitzwolfer](/wiki/Blitzwolfer "Blitzwolfer") in the opening intro of **Omniverse**'s Arc 5.
* Rath is the only alien to speak another human language other than English; in this case, it is Spanish.
* Rath is the third alien in **Alien Force** and **Ultimate Alien** to not be voiced by [Dee Bradley Baker](/wiki/Dee_Bradley_Baker "Dee Bradley Baker"); the first being [Alien X](/wiki/Alien_X_(Classic) "Alien X (Classic)") and the second being [Ghostfreak](/wiki/Ghostfreak "Ghostfreak").
* Rath is the second alien to stay in a whole episode due to an [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") malfunction; the first being [Grey Matter](/wiki/Grey_Matter_(Classic) "Grey Matter (Classic)").
* Rath and [Buzzshock](/wiki/Buzzshock_(Classic) "Buzzshock (Classic)") are the only aliens to have been unlocked by electromagnetic interference.
