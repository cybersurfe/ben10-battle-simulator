Appearance
----------


Kickin Hawk resembles a combination of a [rooster](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rooster "wikipedia:Rooster") and a [hawk](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hawk "wikipedia:Hawk"). He has sharp black claws on his toes and talons on his hands, as well as two larger talons protruding from his arms. He has green eyes and a short yellow beak and is covered in brown feathers. He also has a white-brown mohawk and belly. He wears a green mask, a green belt with a white stripe, and black briefs.


Kickin Hawk wears a strap vest with the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on the vest hooker.

Kickin Hawk has retractable talons on his fingers and scythe-like blades on his elbows which he can use to cut objects with. He also has enhanced strength, durability, agility and reflexes and can use a special ability where he spins around very fast and knocks enemies away with his feet.[[2]](#cite_note-BiS-2)


Kickin Hawk is recognized by [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") as his best close-range (hand-to-hand) fighter, or "foot-to-head fighter", fighting almost exclusively with his legs.[[1]](#cite_note-Rook_Tales-1)


Kickin Hawk's legs have surprising flexibility and dexterity, as he is able to use them to reach out and grab objects much like an arm.[[3]](#cite_note-3) He is also capable of pulling the trigger of a blaster with his three-fingered hands.[[2]](#cite_note-BiS-2)


Kickin Hawk's kicks can generate powerful shockwaves that send his enemies flying. He is also able to kick an object with enough force equivalent to a cannonball, and can also run very fast.[[4]](#cite_note-4)


Kickin Hawk is capable of withstanding the vacuum of space, but he still needs to breathe to survive. Without an oxygen-supply, Kickin Hawk is limited to holding his breath.[[5]](#cite_note-TT-5)



Weaknesses
----------


 [![](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/2/26/OMWAT_%28357%29.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/218?cb=20160203181153)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/2/26/OMWAT_%28357%29.png/revision/latest?cb=20160203181153)  Dexterity Limit

  
There is a limit to Kickin Hawk's dexterity regarding fine-manipulation, as he is unable to hold and use [his cellphone](/wiki/Ben%27s_Cell_Phone "Ben's Cell Phone") properly.[[6]](#cite_note-6)


Kickin Hawk is weak against energy-based attacks, such as those generated by a [Pugnavore](/wiki/Pugnavore "Pugnavore").[[7]](#cite_note-7)


Kickin Hawk requires an oxygen supply to survive. Without it, he is limited to holding his breath while in space.[[5]](#cite_note-TT-5)


Kickin Hawk is vulnerable to electricity, as demonstrated with [Liam](/wiki/Liam "Liam").[[8]](#cite_note-Outbreak-8)



History
-------


### Omniverse


* Kickin Hawk first appeared in *[Outbreak](/wiki/Outbreak "Outbreak")*, where he was added to the active playlist after the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") "sampled" [Liam](/wiki/Liam "Liam")'s DNA[[note 1]](#cite_note-10) and defeated the latter.
* In *[Bros in Space](/wiki/Bros_in_Space "Bros in Space")*, Kickin Hawk defeated [Fistrick](/wiki/Fistrick "Fistrick") and his [Muroid](/wiki/Muroid "Muroid") army.
* In *[T.G.I.S.](/wiki/T.G.I.S. "T.G.I.S.")*, Kickin Hawk and [Zak](/wiki/Zak_Saturday "Zak Saturday") battled [Argost](/wiki/V.V._Argost "V.V. Argost").
* In *[Tummy Trouble](/wiki/Tummy_Trouble "Tummy Trouble")*, Kickin Hawk fought [Attea](/wiki/Attea "Attea") until he reverted.
* In *[Vilgax Must Croak](/wiki/Vilgax_Must_Croak "Vilgax Must Croak")*, Kickin Hawk entered [Incarcecon](/wiki/Incarcecon "Incarcecon") with the other [Plumbers](/wiki/Plumbers "Plumbers") before briefly attacking [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") out of anger.
* In *[O Mother, Where Art Thou?](/wiki/O_Mother,_Where_Art_Thou%3F "O Mother, Where Art Thou?")*, Kickin Hawk knocked some TNT out of the [Vreedle Brothers](/wiki/Vreedle_Brothers "Vreedle Brothers")' hands and kicked it away before it exploded.
* In *[Collect This](/wiki/Collect_This "Collect This")*, Kickin Hawk played soccer with [Rook](/wiki/Rook_Blonko "Rook Blonko") and the [Kineceleran Kids](/wiki/Kineceleran_Kids "Kineceleran Kids") and accidentally wrecked [Pakmar](/wiki/Pakmar "Pakmar")'s lamp store after scoring a goal.
* In *[Weapon XI: Part 1](/wiki/Weapon_XI:_Part_1 "Weapon XI: Part 1")*, Kickin Hawk tried to fight [Servantis](/wiki/Servantis "Servantis"), but was stopped and defeated by [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)"), forcing Kickin Hawk to escape the [Rooter Headquarters](/wiki/Rooter_Headquarters "Rooter Headquarters") with the rest of [his team](/wiki/Ben%27s_Team/OV "Ben's Team/OV").
* In *[Rook Tales](/wiki/Rook_Tales "Rook Tales")*, Kickin Hawk was defeated by [Kundo](/wiki/Kundo "Kundo").
* In *[Charm School](/wiki/Charm_School "Charm School")*, Kickin Hawk "protected" [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") from [Hex](/wiki/Hex_(Classic) "Hex (Classic)") by pinning the latter against a tree.
* In *[Vreedlemania](/wiki/Vreedlemania "Vreedlemania")*, Kickin Hawk fought the [Vreedles](/wiki/Vreedle "Vreedle") and was defeated by the [Krakken](/wiki/Krakken "Krakken").
* In *[Third Time's a Charm](/wiki/Third_Time%27s_a_Charm "Third Time's a Charm")*, Kickin Hawk battled and tricked [Charmcaster](/wiki/Charmcaster_(Classic) "Charmcaster (Classic)") with [Skurd](/wiki/Skurd "Skurd")'s help before kicking her into [her bag](/wiki/Charmcaster%27s_Bag "Charmcaster's Bag").
* In *[The Most Dangerous Game Show](/wiki/The_Most_Dangerous_Game_Show "The Most Dangerous Game Show")*, Kickin Hawk escaped the [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)") with all the contestants and was defeated by [Charles Zenith](/wiki/Charles_Zenith "Charles Zenith").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Kickin Hawk appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

Etymology
---------


Kickin Hawk's name is a portmanteau of "kicking", his main fighting technique; and "[chickenhawk](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chickenhawk_(bird) "wikipedia:Chickenhawk (bird)")", referencing his hawk-like appearance.



Trivia
------


* Kickin Hawk's existence was confirmed on the [Entertainment Earth](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Entertainment_Earth "wikipedia:Entertainment Earth") website.
* [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") possibly came up with Kickin Hawk, but cannot remember if that is true.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-12) He was conceived after the creation of Liam.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-13)
* Kickin Hawk appears to make a screeching sound when he talks, much like how [Liam](/wiki/Liam "Liam") often makes chicken noises when he speaks.
	+ The same sound can be heard during Kickin Hawk's transformation sequence and whenever he kicks.
* Kickin Hawk went by multiple working names, such as Ka-Chickin, Chi-Kickin, Ch'Kickin, and KiChicken, before his final name was coined by [Matt Wayne](/wiki/Matt_Wayne "Matt Wayne").[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-14)[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-15)
* Kickin Hawk's mask resembles that of [Hawkman](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hawkman "wikipedia:Hawkman"). The rest of his outfit bears a close resemblance to the 1980's design of [Marvel](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marvel_Comics "wikipedia:Marvel Comics")'s [Hercules](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hercules_(Marvel_Comics) "wikipedia:Hercules (Marvel Comics)").
* [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo")'s [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") does not contain Kickin Hawk's DNA.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-16)
