Appearance
----------


Bullfrag is a humanoid frog-like alien with three-fingered hands and two-toed feet. He is, however, considerably taller, more muscular and paler-skinned than a typical Incursean and has a more defined jawline.


In *[The Frogs of War: Part 2](/wiki/The_Frogs_of_War:_Part_2 "The Frogs of War: Part 2")*, Bullfrag first wore a typical black-and-purple Incursean uniform as a disguise. He also wore an Incursean helmet and purple sunglasses.


As of *[Bengeance is Mine](/wiki/Bengeance_is_Mine "Bengeance is Mine")*, Bullfrag wears green sunglasses, a black bodysuit with a green stripe down the middle, fingerless black gloves, toeless black boots with green cuffs above them, and a green belt with a white buckle.


Bullfrag wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his upper chest, which was hidden under his suit during *The Frogs of War: Part 2*.

Powers and Abilities
--------------------


* ![]()Long Prehensile Tongue
* ![]()Strong Tongue
* ![]()Sticky Tongue
* ![]()Chest Inflation
* ![]()Enhanced Jumping
* ![]()Enhanced Strength
1/6
Bullfrag has powerful jumping abilities and is very agile, much like an ordinary [Earth](/wiki/Earth_(Classic) "Earth (Classic)") frog.


Bullfrag has impressive hand-to-hand combat abilities, as he was able to take down Attea and a squadron of average Incurseans[[2]](#cite_note-TFoWP2-2) and hold his own against a mob of [Squid Monsters](/wiki/Squid_Monsters "Squid Monsters").[[1]](#cite_note-BIM-1)


Bullfrag's physique makes him stronger than an average Incursean, he was able to carry [Bill Gacks](/wiki/Bill_Gacks "Bill Gacks") in the air without any difficulty.[[1]](#cite_note-BIM-1)


Bullfrag is more durable than other Incurseans, as he can endure being kicked by a [Tetramand](/wiki/Tetramand_(Classic) "Tetramand (Classic)").[[3]](#cite_note-Catfight-3)


Bullfrag possesses a long, sticky and prehensile tongue, which he can use as a grappling line or to hit a distant target. It is also very strong, as it can smash boxes.[[3]](#cite_note-Catfight-3) Its tensile strength is high enough to slow down the [Billions Tower](/wiki/Billions_Tower "Billions Tower") sign to a near standstill after falling from the top of a skyscraper.[[4]](#cite_note-Vreedlemania-4)


Bullfrag can inflate his chest to repel enemies and gain momentum to jump.[[1]](#cite_note-BIM-1)


Bullfrag is capable of withstanding the vacuum of space for a limited amount of time, as demonstrated by [Attea](/wiki/Attea "Attea").[[5]](#cite_note-TT-5)


Bullfrag can hold his breath underwater for four to seven hours thanks to his respirators.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-6)



Weaknesses
----------


* ![]()Overstretched Tongue
1/1
Bullfrag has a very delicate nose[[DM 1]](#cite_note-7) and is sensitive to strong smells, such as that of a [Methanosian](/wiki/Methanosian "Methanosian")'s swampy stench, as demonstrated with Attea and many Incursean soldiers.[[6]](#cite_note-8)


Bullfrag's tongue will hurt if it stretches too long.[[4]](#cite_note-Vreedlemania-4)


Bullfrag requires an oxygen supply to survive. Without it, he is limited to holding his breath while in space, as demonstrated with Attea.[[5]](#cite_note-TT-5)


Bullfrag can be immobilized by a [Necrofriggian](/wiki/Necrofriggian_(Classic) "Necrofriggian (Classic)")'s ice breath, as demonstrated with [Milleous](/wiki/Milleous_(Classic) "Milleous (Classic)").[[7]](#cite_note-9)


Bullfrag is vulnerable to pressurized water blasts, such as those generated by an [Orishan](/wiki/Orishan "Orishan"), as demonstrated with Attea.[[5]](#cite_note-TT-5)



History
-------


### Omniverse


* Bullfrag first appeared in *[The Frogs of War: Part 2](/wiki/The_Frogs_of_War:_Part_2 "The Frogs of War: Part 2")*, where he was unlocked by [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)") offscreen and infiltrated the Incursean war fleet to help [his team](/wiki/Ben%27s_Team/OV "Ben's Team/OV").
* In *[Bengeance is Mine](/wiki/Bengeance_is_Mine "Bengeance is Mine")*, Bullfrag battled some [Squid Monsters](/wiki/Squid_Monsters "Squid Monsters") and saved [Bill Gacks](/wiki/Bill_Gacks "Bill Gacks") from them.
* In *[Catfight](/wiki/Catfight "Catfight")*, [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was forcibly transformed into Bullfrag four times by [Attea](/wiki/Attea "Attea"), who used the [Omnitrix Remote Control](/wiki/Omnitrix_Remote_Control "Omnitrix Remote Control") to do so.
* In *[Vreedlemania](/wiki/Vreedlemania "Vreedlemania")*, Bullfrag prevented the [Billions Tower](/wiki/Billions_Tower "Billions Tower") logo from harming the nearby civilians.
* In *[It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 1](/wiki/It%27s_a_Mad,_Mad,_Mad_Ben_World:_Part_1 "It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 1")*, Bullfrag saved some slaves from the falling drill machine with the help of [Mr. Monkey](/wiki/Mr._Monkey "Mr. Monkey").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Bullfrag appeared for a short time when Ben was cycling through his aliens.

### [Ben 10: Omniverse 2](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse_2 "Ben 10: Omniverse 2")


Being the title alien, Bullfrag is playable in the game.


Before arriving on Earth, Ben, at Azmuth's instruction, needs to acquire Incursean DNA for the Omnitrix in order to prevent the Incurseans from discovering his return from exile. To ensure capable combat prowess, Ben had to acquire a sample from larger Incurseans. Upon unlocking his new Incursean transformation, [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") tries a few names such as "Crazy Legs" and "Aquattack" before settling on Bullfrag.

Etymology
---------


Bullfrag's name is a play on "[bullfrog](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_bullfrog "wikipedia:American bullfrog")", referencing his appearance, and "frag", which means to kill.



Trivia
------


* [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") came up with Bullfrag.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-10) During production, he started out looking more like a regular [Incursean](/wiki/Incursean_(Classic) "Incursean (Classic)").[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-11)[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-12)
	+ The original idea for Bullfrag's sunglasses were that they were part of his disguise to hide his green eyes. However, the crew liked them so much, they decided to make the sunglasses a permanent part of his appearance.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-13)
* Bullfrag is more muscular and developed than other Incurseans because most members of the species have very poor diets. Similar to the other [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix "Omnitrix") aliens, Bullfrag is an example of an optimal Incursean.[[MW 1]](#cite_note-14)
* Like most other Incurseans, Bullfrag speaks with a [Brooklyn](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brooklyn "wikipedia:Brooklyn") accent.
* Bullfrag is [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s most handsome alien.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-15)
* Bullfrag and Perk [Upchuck](/wiki/Upchuck "Upchuck") are the only aliens to have been manually unlocked by entering a code into an Omnitrix.
	+ Bullfrag is also one of four aliens to have been unlocked by [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)"); the others being [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)"), Murk Upchuck, and [Atomix](/wiki/Atomix "Atomix").
* Like frogs, Bullfrag makes a croaking sound after inflating his chest.
* While Bullfrag is attracted to [Attea](/wiki/Attea "Attea"), the latter has a strong crush on Bullfrag as well.[[2]](#cite_note-TFoWP2-2)[[3]](#cite_note-Catfight-3)[[MW 2]](#cite_note-16) Although he thinks she is cute, he is not into her personality.[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-17)
	+ Ben blaming the attraction on Bullfrag's DNA is similar to [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") blaming his cravings for [Chili Fries](/wiki/Chili_Fries "Chili Fries") on Ben's DNA. Curiously, [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") even made a reference to the similarity, claiming that it is the excuse all boys use.[[2]](#cite_note-TFoWP2-2)
* From Arc 4 of **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")** onwards, Bullfrag replaces [Crashhopper](/wiki/Crashhopper "Crashhopper") in the opening intro of the show, specifically in the part where the latter originally appeared alongside [Shocksquatch](/wiki/Shocksquatch "Shocksquatch"), [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)"), and [Bloxx](/wiki/Bloxx "Bloxx").
* Bullfrag's voice and mannerisms are based on [Sylvester Stallone](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sylvester_Stallone "wikipedia:Sylvester Stallone").
* Bullfrag's design seems similar to Rash from *[Battletoads](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battletoads "wikipedia:Battletoads")*, as both were amphibian superheroes that wore sunglasses.
* Bullfrag's sunglasses greatly resemble [Kamina](https://gurrenlagann.fandom.com/wiki/Kamina "w:c:gurrenlagann:Kamina")'s sunglasses from **[Gurren Lagann](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gurren_Lagann "wikipedia:Gurren Lagann")**. However, the design is not a reference to it.[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-18)
	+ Coincidentally, Bullfrag's sunglasses are similar to the visor of [Soundwave](https://tfwiki.net/wiki/Soundwave_(Animated)) from **[Transformers Animated](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Transformers_Animated "wikipedia:Transformers Animated")**. Therefore, they are a reference to [2000 AD](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2000_AD_(comic) "wikipedia:2000 AD (comic)")'s *[ABC Warriors](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ABC_Warriors "wikipedia:ABC Warriors")*.
