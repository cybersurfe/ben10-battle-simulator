Appearance
----------


Ultimate Big Chill looks mostly the same as his devolved counterpart in **Alien Force** and **Ultimate Alien** except his skin is recolored red, his wings and antennae have a flame design, and he had crests over his eyes with the same design. Similar to Big Chill, Ultimate Big Chill can make his wings and antennae fold into a cloak.


Ultimate Big Chill wears the evolved [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.

Ultimate Big Chill can become intangible, manipulate ice, fly, and breathe ice like Big Chill, but can create "ice flames", which he describes as "fire so cold it burns".[[1]](#cite_note-HEWTL-1)


These flames are plasma beams that consume the heat of whatever they touch and use that energy to burn.[[DM 1]](#cite_note-2)[[MW 1]](#cite_note-3) Ultimate Big Chill's breath and beams are both stronger and colder, capable of instantly turning the air around him and his enemies into solid ice.[[2]](#cite_note-EfA-4) In fact, his ice breath is comparable to blizzard winds and it spreads farther than the breath of his normal counterpart.


Ultimate Big Chill can breathe or shoot flames from his hands that turn into blue ice when they hit a target.[[2]](#cite_note-EfA-4) This form of ice freezes the enemy at a much faster rate than Big Chill's ice breath.


By inhaling deeply, Ultimate Big Chill can lower the temperature of the air he breathes in, because he sucks the heat out of objects, freezing anything that happens to be in its path.


Ultimate Big Chill is also capable of faster flight than his previous form.


Like Big Chill, Ultimate Big Chill can also survive in a variety of environments, including the vacuum of space, extreme heat, intense cold, and deep oceans.[[3]](#cite_note-5)



Weaknesses
----------


 [![](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/2/22/VTS_%28279%29.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/218?cb=20150906221726)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/2/22/VTS_%28279%29.png/revision/latest?cb=20150906221726)  Vulnerability to Electricity

  
Ultimate Big Chill's intangibility is useless against opponents who can also turn intangible, as it will cancel out their abilities to do so.[[4]](#cite_note-6)


Like his base form, Ultimate Big Chill is vulnerable to electricity, such as that generated by a [Transylian](/wiki/Transylian "Transylian").[[5]](#cite_note-7)


Ultimate Big Chill's ice flames cannot hold [Pyronites](/wiki/Pyronite_(Classic) "Pyronite (Classic)") because of the heat they generate, as demonstrated with his [sentient counterpart](/wiki/Sentient_Ultimate_Big_Chill "Sentient Ultimate Big Chill").[[6]](#cite_note-TUS-8)


Ultimate Big Chill can be knocked out of the sky by species with as much strength as a [To'kustar](/wiki/To%27kustar_(Classic) "To'kustar (Classic)"), as also demonstrated by his sentient counterpart.[[6]](#cite_note-TUS-8)



History
-------


### Ultimate Alien


* Ultimate Big Chill first appeared in *[Hit 'Em Where They Live](/wiki/Hit_%27Em_Where_They_Live "Hit 'Em Where They Live")*, where he defeated [Vulkanus](/wiki/Vulkanus "Vulkanus") and [his minions](/wiki/Pickaxe_Aliens "Pickaxe Aliens").
* In *[Escape from Aggregor](/wiki/Escape_from_Aggregor "Escape from Aggregor")*, Ultimate Big Chill battled [Galapagus](/wiki/Galapagus "Galapagus").
* In *[Ultimate Aggregor](/wiki/Ultimate_Aggregor_(Episode) "Ultimate Aggregor (Episode)")*, Ultimate Big Chill defended himself from some fans.
* In *[Deep](/wiki/Deep "Deep")*, Ultimate Big Chill chased [Ultimate Aggregor](/wiki/Ultimate_Aggregor "Ultimate Aggregor") and defeated some [Piscciss Volanns](/wiki/Piscciss_Volann "Piscciss Volann") before switching into [Goop](/wiki/Goop_(Classic) "Goop (Classic)") to save [Piscciss](/wiki/Piscciss_(Classic) "Piscciss (Classic)").
* In *[Viktor: The Spoils](/wiki/Viktor:_The_Spoils "Viktor: The Spoils")*, Ultimate Big Chill was defeated by an undead [Viktor](/wiki/Viktor "Viktor").
* In *[Greetings from Techadon](/wiki/Greetings_from_Techadon "Greetings from Techadon")*, Ultimate Big Chill battled a [Techadon robot](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots") before switching into [Echo Echo](/wiki/Echo_Echo "Echo Echo").
* In *[The Flame Keepers' Circle](/wiki/The_Flame_Keepers%27_Circle "The Flame Keepers' Circle")*, Ultimate Big Chill defeated some [Esoterica](/wiki/Esoterica "Esoterica").

### [Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction](/wiki/Ben_10_Ultimate_Alien:_Cosmic_Destruction "Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction")


Ultimate Big Chill is a playable alien in the game.


He can make large plasma blasts on the ground, which either freeze, damage, or both, as well as hurl fiery ice spikes at opponents from just above the ground.


Unlike his original counterpart, Ultimate Big Chill is unable to double jump or glide.



### [Project Exonaut](/wiki/Project_Exonaut "Project Exonaut")


Ultimate Big Chill is a playable Exosuit of the Banzai Squadron on Project Exonaut. His Weapon Mod enhances the Tridex's firing rate and accuracy. He is available for purchase at level 10 for 750 coins.

Trivia
------


* Just like Big Chill, Ultimate Big Chill's mouth doesn't move when he speaks.
* Ultimate Big Chill's voice is more gravelly than Big Chill's, having a deeper tone behind it.
* Ultimate Big Chill was the [Alien of the Month](/wiki/Alien_of_the_Month "Alien of the Month") in July 2011.
* Ultimate Big Chill is the first Ultimate to have a Nano in *[FusionFall](/wiki/FusionFall "FusionFall")*.
* Ultimate Big Chill was replaced by his devolved form for the **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")** version of *[Game Creator](/wiki/Game_Creator "Game Creator")*.
* Had Ultimate Big Chill appeared in **Omniverse** as one of [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo")'s transformations, [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") would have given him some kind of cold weather-controlling powers to make him more unique compared to what was shown in **Ultimate Alien**.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-9)
	+ Derrick has also considered giving Ultimate Big Chill some [Mothra](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mothra "wikipedia:Mothra")-inspired powers.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-10)
