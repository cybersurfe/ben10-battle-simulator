Appearance
----------


Ball Weevil is a small yellow insect-like alien with black eyes and green pupils. He has four stick legs and a beak along with a big antenna.


Ball Weevil wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on a green and white brace around his neck.

Ball Weevil can spit round, green, sticky balls of plasma[[4]](#cite_note-4)[[5]](#cite_note-WYWA-5)[[note 1]](#cite_note-7) that absorb matter and explode upon impact, or whenever Ball Weevil wants it to.[[7]](#cite_note-RoE-8) The size of these balls depends on how much matter they absorb.


Ball Weevil's plasma balls can be in more shapes than just spheres. The bigger the ball he creates, the larger the explosion.


Ball Weevil can spew out plasma in a controllable stream to act as a rope to tie up an enemy. The plasma is strong enough to momentarily hold a [Tetramand](/wiki/Tetramand_(Classic) "Tetramand (Classic)").[[7]](#cite_note-RoE-8)


Ball Weevil's plasma balls can absorb energy, as seen when he absorbed both a [Prypiatosian-B](/wiki/Prypiatosian-B "Prypiatosian-B")'s radioactive energy blasts and an [Aerophibian](/wiki/Aerophibian_(Classic) "Aerophibian (Classic)")'s neuroshock blasts into one of his globules.[[8]](#cite_note-9)


Ball Weevil has enhanced agility and balance, being able to roll on his plasma balls and can climb on walls with ease.


Ball Weevil seems to be pretty strong for his size, as shown when he stopped his giant plasma ball rolling at a high speed.[[5]](#cite_note-WYWA-5)


Ball Weevil's plasma balls are buoyant enough to float on water.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-10)



Weaknesses
----------

Ball Weevil can be hurt by his own plasma balls if they are reflected at him.[[1]](#cite_note-OPaP1-1) He can also get stuck in his own plasma.[[7]](#cite_note-RoE-8)


Ball Weevil is vulnerable to being trapped by a member of [Terroranchula](/wiki/Terroranchula "Terroranchula")'s [species](/wiki/Terroranchula%27s_Species "Terroranchula's Species"), his natural predator.[[3]](#cite_note-SP1-3)


Ball Weevil's plasma can be neutralized by a Terroranchula's webs.[[1]](#cite_note-OPaP1-1) It is also useless against a species capable of withstanding it, such as [Arburian Pelarotas](/wiki/Arburian_Pelarota_(Classic) "Arburian Pelarota (Classic)").[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-11)


Ball Weevil cannot breathe underwater.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-12) He also cannot use his plasma balls as a helmet because he can't breathe in slime.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-13)


While he can absorb matter and energy using his plasma, Ball Weevil cannot absorb gases.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-14)



History
-------


### Omniverse


* Ball Weevil first appeared in *[Of Predators and Prey: Part 1](/wiki/Of_Predators_and_Prey:_Part_1 "Of Predators and Prey: Part 1")*, where he was defeated by [Khyber](/wiki/Khyber "Khyber").[[note 2]](#cite_note-16)
* In *[Arrested Development](/wiki/Arrested_Development "Arrested Development")*, 11-year-old Ben as Ball Weevil defeated [Computron](/wiki/Computron "Computron")'s [minions](/wiki/Computron%27s_Minions "Computron's Minions").
* In *[Rules of Engagement](/wiki/Rules_of_Engagement "Rules of Engagement")*, Ball Weevil defeated [Princess Looma](/wiki/Looma_Red_Wind "Looma Red Wind") with [Julie](/wiki/Julie_Yamamoto "Julie Yamamoto")'s help.
* In *[Showdown: Part 1](/wiki/Showdown:_Part_1 "Showdown: Part 1")*, Ball Weevil battled [Tyrannopede](/wiki/Tyrannopede "Tyrannopede") until she turned into [Terroranchula](/wiki/Terroranchula "Terroranchula") and trapped him.
* In *[While You Were Away](/wiki/While_You_Were_Away "While You Were Away")*, Ball Weevil escaped the brainwashed [Revonnahganders](/wiki/Revonnahgander "Revonnahgander").
* In *[Cough It Up](/wiki/Cough_It_Up "Cough It Up")*, Ball Weevil fought [Sunder](/wiki/Sunder "Sunder") and was saved by [Spanner](/wiki/Ken_Tennyson "Ken Tennyson").
* In *[The Rooters of All Evil](/wiki/The_Rooters_of_All_Evil "The Rooters of All Evil")*, Ball Weevil defeated [Swift](/wiki/Swift "Swift") and [Leander](/wiki/Leander "Leander").
* In *[Weapon XI: Part 2](/wiki/Weapon_XI:_Part_2 "Weapon XI: Part 2")*, Ball Weevil defeated a [Way Bad](/wiki/Way_Bads "Way Bads").
* In *[The Most Dangerous Game Show](/wiki/The_Most_Dangerous_Game_Show "The Most Dangerous Game Show")*, Ball Weevil battled a [Slammoid](/wiki/Slammoid "Slammoid").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Ball Weevil appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

Etymology
---------


Ball Weevil's name is a combination of the words "ball" and "[boll weevil](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boll_weevil "wikipedia:Boll weevil")".



Trivia
------


* [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") came up with and designed Ball Weevil before he worked on **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**; this included coming up with his powers.[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-18)[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-name-19)
	+ Ball Weevil was named by [Matt Wayne](/wiki/Matt_Wayne "Matt Wayne").[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-name-19)
* Ball Weevil comes from the same planet as [The Worst](/wiki/The_Worst "The Worst"), where they act as a natural sanitation solution.[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-20)
* Ball Weevil's design was inspired by the *[Pokémon](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon "wikipedia:Pokémon")* franchise.[[DJW 10]](#cite_note-21)
* Ball Weevil's concept names were Turd Burglar[[DJW 11]](#cite_note-22) and Collectiball.[[MW 1]](#cite_note-link-23)
	+ The latter name was changed when it was found that there was a product with the same name.[[MW 1]](#cite_note-link-23)[[MW 2]](#cite_note-24)
* Out of all the aliens who were introduced in **Omniverse**, Ball Weevil is Matt Wayne's favorite.[[MW 3]](#cite_note-25)
* Ball Weevil's powers bear a resemblance to the Katamaris' from *[Katamari Damacy](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Katamari_Damacy "wikipedia:Katamari Damacy")*.
* If [Ben 23](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Dimension_23) "Ben Tennyson (Dimension 23)") were to have his own version of Ball Weevil in his [Omnitrix](/wiki/Hero_Watch "Hero Watch"), the transformation's name would be Sticky Cricket.[[DJW 12]](#cite_note-26)
