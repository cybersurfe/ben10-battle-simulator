### Ben as Frankenstrike


Frankenstrike has an appearance similar to the classic depiction of [Boris Karloff](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boris_Karloff "wikipedia:Boris Karloff")'s version of Victor Frankenstein's monster. He has a muscular body with a human nose, black hair with sideburns, and a pair of bolts on each side of his neck that are green in the crease. He wears metal gloves with his fingers showing and metal pants with lines on them with glowing green triangle dashes in the middle. While he does have two tower-shaped conductor coil-like generators on his back, they are not machines. Rather, these generators are metallic-organic growths.[[pop-up 1]](#cite_note-2)[[1]](#cite_note-BAotD-1)


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Frankenstrike had dark greyish yellow skin, his hair had a mullet hairstyle with brown at the end, and stitch-like lines on his arms (right arm: half of his shoulder and left arm: halfway from his glove). His gloves were brown, his pants were black, and the bolts were black. He had three bolts on his chest in addition to the ones on his neck and white lines on his waist that connected to the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol like a belt clasp.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Frankenstrike looked mostly the same except his skin, gloves, back generators, belt, bolts, and the bottom of his shoes were all recolored grey. The original Omnitrix symbol has been replaced with a grey hexagon and the bolts had no green lines on them. The [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol was located on his chest, replacing the top bolt in the triangular shape made by the bolts on his chest.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Frankenstrike's hair is shorter, lacking bangs and a mullet while retaining the evident sideburns. His skin has several uneven seams, combining grayish-yellow with darker grey. He also has large pink scars across his chest and right arm. Though he retains the bolts on his neck, the bolts on his chest have been relocated to his back. He has also gained more bolts, one on each shoulder and elbow. The bolts are bronze and resemble flathead screws. His belt is green with white stripes and bolts in them, one of these bolts being a buckle. His turrets are green with screws on them, and the coils are white and feature green bulbs at the tips. His gloves are green with bolts on their knuckles, his pants are a greenish-black color, and his "boots" are copper with three green slits in them. Frankenstrike's teeth are now yellowed and uneven, and bear out when he speaks. His [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol is embedded in the flesh of his right pectoral, with several deep scars protruding from where it resides.

Frankenstrike has an electromagnetic body with two conductor coils on his back that allow him to generate and manipulate green[[note 1]](#cite_note-7) lightning and electromagnetism.


Frankenstrike can emit powerful lightning bolts and emit magnetism from his hands or his feet, allowing him to attach to metal structures.[[1]](#cite_note-BAotD-1)


Frankenstrike can emit his lightning in the form of waves that can travel along flat surfaces.[[2]](#cite_note-ATTWN-3)


Frankenstrike can alternatively shoot concentrated beams of electricity.[[5]](#cite_note-CIU-8)


Frankenstrike possesses enhanced strength, as he was able to punch through a [Techadon robot](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots") and knock its arm off.[[6]](#cite_note-9) He is also strong enough to carry an [Appoplexian](/wiki/Appoplexian_(Classic) "Appoplexian (Classic)") over his shoulders.[[4]](#cite_note-IaMMMBW2-5)


Frankenstrike has enhanced jumping, as shown when he was able to jump and hold onto a high ladder when waiting to ambush [Phil](/wiki/Phil_Billings_(Classic) "Phil Billings (Classic)").[[7]](#cite_note-MM-10)


Frankenstrike is durable enough to withstand being blasted by one of [Phil](/wiki/Phil_Billings_(Classic) "Phil Billings (Classic)")'s electric blasts, as well as having enhanced reflexes, as he was able to dodge the same blasts easily.[[7]](#cite_note-MM-10)


Frankenstrike can enhance his physical attacks by giving them an electrical charge.[[7]](#cite_note-MM-10)


Like all Anur System aliens, Frankenstrike is immune to the effects of [Corrodium](/wiki/Corrodium "Corrodium")[[1]](#cite_note-BAotD-1) and can see in the dark.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-11)


Frankenstrike can survive in the vacuum of space.[[1]](#cite_note-BAotD-1)


Despite his claims, Frankenstrike is actually quite fast, as demonstrated when [Lightning Volt](/wiki/Lightning_Volt "Lightning Volt") was able to keep up with [Rath](/wiki/Rath_(Classic) "Rath (Classic)").[[4]](#cite_note-IaMMMBW2-5)


Frankenstrike has a form of technokinesis by connecting himself to machines with his conductor coils to control them. He can do so with his electricity as well.


Frankenstrike is resistant against extreme heat and immune to an [Anodite](/wiki/Anodite "Anodite")'s [mana](/wiki/Mana "Mana") attacks, as demonstrated by [Viktor](/wiki/Viktor "Viktor").[[8]](#cite_note-12)

History
-------


### Ben 10


* Frankenstrike first appeared in *[Be Afraid of the Dark](/wiki/Be_Afraid_of_the_Dark "Be Afraid of the Dark")*, where he was added to the active playlist after the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") "sampled" [Viktor](/wiki/Viktor "Viktor")'s DNA.[[note 2]](#cite_note-17) Frankenstrike battled [Zs'Skayr](/wiki/Zs%27Skayr "Zs'Skayr") and the [Mummy](/wiki/Mummy "Mummy") on the exterior of the space station and personally sent Viktor into an ill-created portal to the [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)") before it closed.


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, Frankenstrike was re-unlocked by [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Ultimate_Alien) "Ben 10,000 (Ultimate Alien)").


### Omniverse


* In *[Max's Monster](/wiki/Max%27s_Monster "Max's Monster")*, Frankenstrike fought and trapped [Phil](/wiki/Phil_Billings_(Classic) "Phil Billings (Classic)") in the streets of [Bellwood](/wiki/Bellwood_(Classic) "Bellwood (Classic)").
* In *[Something Zombozo This Way Comes](/wiki/Something_Zombozo_This_Way_Comes "Something Zombozo This Way Comes")*, Frankenstrike fought some [Zombie Clowns](/wiki/Zombie_Clowns "Zombie Clowns") and defeated the [Circus Freaks](/wiki/Circus_Freak_Trio_(Classic) "Circus Freak Trio (Classic)").
* In *[Cough It Up](/wiki/Cough_It_Up "Cough It Up")*, Frankenstrike defeated [Kraab](/wiki/Kraab_(Classic) "Kraab (Classic)").
* In *[The Color of Monkey](/wiki/The_Color_of_Monkey "The Color of Monkey")*, Frankenstrike chased [Rojo](/wiki/Rojo "Rojo") and fought a [Techadon robot](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots").


### Benzarro's Timeline


* In *[And Then There Were None](/wiki/And_Then_There_Were_None "And Then There Were None")*, [Benzarro](/wiki/Benzarro "Benzarro") as Frankenstrike chased after [No Watch Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(No_Watch_Timeline) "Ben Tennyson (No Watch Timeline)") before [Spidermonkey](/wiki/Spidermonkey_(Classic) "Spidermonkey (Classic)") got the latter to safety.
* In *[And Then There Was Ben](/wiki/And_Then_There_Was_Ben "And Then There Was Ben")*, Benzarro as Frankenstrike battled [Vomit Man](/wiki/Vomit_Man "Vomit Man") and [Gwen 10](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Gweniverse) "Gwen Tennyson (Gweniverse)") as [Wildvine](/wiki/Wildvine_(Classic) "Wildvine (Classic)") until he was sent back to his own timeline by No Watch Ben as [Clockwork](/wiki/Clockwork "Clockwork").


### Comics


* In *[What Lies Ben-eath](/wiki/What_Lies_Ben-eath "What Lies Ben-eath")*, Frankenstrike tried to get rid of the Shadow Monster.

Etymology
---------


His placeholder name, Benvicktor, is a combination of "Ben" and "Vicktor".


The name Frankenstrike is a combo of the name of Frankenstein, referencing his appearance, and the word "strike", as in a lightning strike, which alludes to his electrokinetic abilities.



Trivia
------


* Frankenstrike's appearance is based off of [Mary Shelley](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mary_Shelley "wikipedia:Mary Shelley")'s [Frankenstein's monster](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frankenstein%27s_monster "wikipedia:Frankenstein's monster").
* Frankenstrike's placeholder name "Benvicktor" was assigned to him by the credits for *[Be Afraid of the Dark](/wiki/Be_Afraid_of_the_Dark "Be Afraid of the Dark")* and toys during the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"). Therefore, Frankenstrike is one of the four aliens whose working name had Ben's name in it, the other three being [Blitzwolfer](/wiki/Blitzwolfer "Blitzwolfer"), [Snare-oh](/wiki/Snare-oh "Snare-oh"), and [Nanomech](/wiki/Nanomech "Nanomech").
	+ In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, the name "Benvicktor" was replaced with Frankenstrike. In fact, Ben came up with Frankenstrike's name when he was 11 years old.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-18)
* In the original series, Frankenstrike was considered to be Ben's second smartest alien.[[pop-up 2]](#cite_note-19)[[10]](#cite_note-20) As such, he was credited as one of the aliens [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)") used to build [Ken](/wiki/Ken_Tennyson "Ken Tennyson")'s [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Replica) "Omnitrix (Replica)").[[pop-up 3]](#cite_note-21)[[11]](#cite_note-22) This was retconned in **Omniverse**, as hyper-intelligence is not a common trait among Transylians. Therefore, Ben does not become any smarter as Frankenstrike.[[MW 1]](#cite_note-23)[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-24)
* Despite all of his electrokinetic abilities, [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") states that Frankenstrike cannot absorb electricity.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-25)
* From Arc 4 of **Omniverse** onward, Frankenstrike replaces [Gravattack](/wiki/Gravattack "Gravattack") in the opening intro of the show. He also replaces [Shocksquatch](/wiki/Shocksquatch "Shocksquatch") in the opening intro of Arc 5.
* Similar to [Arctiguana](/wiki/Arctiguana "Arctiguana"), [Snare-oh](/wiki/Snare-oh "Snare-oh"), [Murk Upchuck](/wiki/Upchuck "Upchuck"), and [Buzzshock](/wiki/Buzzshock_(Classic) "Buzzshock (Classic)"), Frankenstrike appeared in **Omniverse**, but he did not appear in *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*.
* Frankenstrike is one of five aliens featured in **Omniverse** who generate green electricity; the others being [Upgrade](/wiki/Upgrade_(Classic) "Upgrade (Classic)"), Buzzshock, [Brainstorm](/wiki/Brainstorm "Brainstorm"), and Nanomech.
	+ Unlike the others, however, Frankenstrike's bio-electricity discharge is naturally colored green for other Transylians.[[pop-up 4]](#cite_note-26)[[1]](#cite_note-BAotD-1)
* Frankenstrike was considered to appear in the [reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)") but did not make the cut as an electric alien for [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Reboot) "Ben Tennyson (Reboot)") because [Shock Rock](/wiki/Shock_Rock "Shock Rock") was created for plot purposes.[[DR 1]](#cite_note-27)
