**Stink Arms** is the combination of [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)") and [Stinkfly](/wiki/Stinkfly_(Classic) "Stinkfly (Classic)")'s DNA. He cannot be accessed unless the faceplate of the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix "Omnitrix") is broken or by using the [Biomnitrix](/wiki/Biomnitrix "Biomnitrix").[[note 1]](#cite_note-fusions-1)

Appearance
----------


Stink Arms mostly resembled Four Arms with a few Stinkfly characteristics. Four Arms' eyes were replaced by Stinkfly's eyestalks, and he had Stinkfly's tail and wings.


Stink Arms wore a shirt based on Stinkfly's suit and Four Arms' pants, and also wore the Omnitrix symbol on his face much like Stinkfly.



Powers and Abilities
--------------------


* ![]()Enhanced Strength
* ![]()Flight
1/2
Being part-Tetramand, Stink Arms has enhanced strength, enough to send the [Mutant Frog](/wiki/Mutant_Frog "Mutant Frog") flying by hitting it with a metal alligator from an establishment. He is also as durable as Four Arms.


Being part-Lepidopterran, Stink Arms has a sharp tail similar to Stinkfly's. He also possesses limited flight.



Weaknesses
----------


Stink Arms' huge mass makes it hard for him to fly properly.


Despite his enhanced strength, Stink Arms is not as strong as Four Arms because his strength level is affected by that of Stinkfly.

Trivia
------


* Stink Arms can be created in one of Bandai's Ben 10 Games, *[DNA Lab](/wiki/DNA_Lab "DNA Lab")*, along with many other fusions. He is the only fusion from the show to appear in this game.
* Stink Arms is the first fusion alien in the [series](/wiki/Ben_10_(Franchise) "Ben 10 (Franchise)").
* Stink Arms' voice is the same as Stinkfly's.
