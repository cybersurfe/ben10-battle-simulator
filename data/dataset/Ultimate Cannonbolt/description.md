Appearance
----------


Ultimate Cannonbolt looks exactly like [Cannonbolt](/wiki/Cannonbolt_(Classic) "Cannonbolt (Classic)") does in **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")** and **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, but he has hard light metallic blue skin. His shell has cylindrical studs that turn into spikes when he goes into his ball form. He has two on each back segment and one on each shoulder, wrist and leg plate. His teeth are similar to Cannonbolt's, but he has two sharp fangs.


Ultimate Cannonbolt's sphere form is similar to Cannonbolt, but grey. The spikes on the armor become pointed when in sphere form.


Ultimate Cannonbolt wears the evolved [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.



Powers and Abilities
--------------------


* ![]()Sphere Transformation
* ![]()Enhanced Speed
* ![]()Strong/Spiked Shell
* ![]()Enhanced Durability
* ![]()Enhanced Dexterity
* ![]()Enhanced Strength
* ![]()Radiation Immunity
1/7
Ultimate Cannonbolt has powers similar to Cannonbolt's, but the former's shell has spikes on it and increased defense and offensive abilities.


Ultimate Cannonbolt has increased speed, momentum, and can bounce higher.


Ultimate Cannonbolt is able to take hits from [Sunder](/wiki/Sunder "Sunder")'s energy axe, only being pushed back.[[2]](#cite_note-TToE-2)


Ultimate Cannonbolt has enhanced strength, as shown when he smashed Sunder to the ground with his fists with ease.[[2]](#cite_note-TToE-2)


Ultimate Cannonbolt seems to be able to withstand radiation, as he was able to hold [P'andor](/wiki/P%27andor "P'andor") while [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") got him back in his [containment suit](/wiki/Prypiatosian-B_Containment_Suit "Prypiatosian-B Containment Suit").[[1]](#cite_note-THtH-1)


Unlike Cannonbolt, Ultimate Cannonbolt cannot be trapped by sticky adhesives due to his spiked shell.


Ultimate Cannonbolt does not lose momentum in his ball form because his spikes give him additional traction.[[DM 1]](#cite_note-3)


While Ultimate Cannonbolt has never trained himself to do so, he can fire his spikes as projectiles.[[ES 1]](#cite_note-4)



Weaknesses
----------


* ![]()Durability Limit
* ![]()Sticky Surfaces
1/2
Ultimate Cannonbolt's metal shell makes him more top-heavy than Cannonbolt, meaning that he is somewhat slower and more unbalanced when walking.


Ultimate Cannonbolt can be stuck by sticky surfaces, like mud.[[2]](#cite_note-TToE-2)


Ultimate Cannonbolt has a durability limit, as a shot from [Ultimate Aggregor](/wiki/Ultimate_Aggregor "Ultimate Aggregor") threw him into a wall.[[3]](#cite_note-5)


A [Cerebrocrustacean](/wiki/Cerebrocrustacean "Cerebrocrustacean")'s electrical force field can topple Ultimate Cannonbolt, as demonstrated with his [sentient counterpart](/wiki/Sentient_Ultimate_Cannonbolt "Sentient Ultimate Cannonbolt").[[4]](#cite_note-6)



History
-------


### Ultimate Alien


* Ultimate Cannonbolt first appeared in *[Too Hot to Handle](/wiki/Too_Hot_to_Handle "Too Hot to Handle")*, where he ​held [P'andor](/wiki/P%27andor "P'andor") while [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") placed the former's [containment suit](/wiki/Prypiatosian-B_Containment_Suit "Prypiatosian-B Containment Suit") back on him.
* In *[Perplexahedron](/wiki/Perplexahedron_(Episode) "Perplexahedron (Episode)")*, Ultimate Cannonbolt nearly defeated [Ultimate Aggregor](/wiki/Ultimate_Aggregor "Ultimate Aggregor") only to lose the piece of the [Map of Infinity](/wiki/Map_of_Infinity "Map of Infinity") to him.
* In *[The Transmogrification of Eunice](/wiki/The_Transmogrification_of_Eunice "The Transmogrification of Eunice")*, Ultimate Cannonbolt battled [Sunder](/wiki/Sunder "Sunder") and rescued [Eunice](/wiki/Eunice "Eunice").
* In *[It's Not Easy Being Gwen](/wiki/It%27s_Not_Easy_Being_Gwen "It's Not Easy Being Gwen")*, Ultimate Cannonbolt defeated [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)").

### [Project Exonaut](/wiki/Project_Exonaut "Project Exonaut")


Ultimate Cannonbolt is a playable Exosuit of the Atlas Brigade on Project Exonaut. His Weapon Mod, similar to the Ballista, increases the Bulldog's shots to five instead of just one and adds 4 more shots making it 19 instead of 15. Ultimate Cannonbolt is available for purchase at level 30 for 2000 coins.

Trivia
------


* Ultimate Cannonbolt was designed by Glenn Wong, with color styling by Chris Hooten.[[GW 1]](#cite_note-7)
* Ultimate Cannonbolt is the only [Ultimate Form](/wiki/Ultimate_Forms "Ultimate Forms") introduced in the first two seasons of **Ultimate Alien** that is not playable in *[Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction](/wiki/Ben_10_Ultimate_Alien:_Cosmic_Destruction "Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction")*.
* Ultimate Cannonbolt was replaced by his devolved form for the **Omniverse** version of *[Game Creator](/wiki/Game_Creator "Game Creator")*.
* Ultimate Cannonbolt was the [Alien of the Month](/wiki/Alien_of_the_Month "Alien of the Month") in May 2011.
* Ultimate Cannonbolt was originally not supposed to appear in *[It's Not Easy Being Gwen](/wiki/It%27s_Not_Easy_Being_Gwen "It's Not Easy Being Gwen")*. After transforming into [Cannonbolt](/wiki/Cannonbolt_(Classic) "Cannonbolt (Classic)") for the second time, Ben was supposed to change into [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)") off-screen and defeat [Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)") as him.[[JHK 1]](#cite_note-8)
* Had Ultimate Cannonbolt appeared in **Omniverse** as one of [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo")'s transformations, [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") would have redesigned him into something more unique compared to what was seen in **Ultimate Alien**.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-9)
