Appearance
----------


### Ben as XLR8


XLR8 resembles a semi-armored [Velociraptor](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Velociraptor "wikipedia:Velociraptor"). He has black orbs on his feet and wears a black cone-like helmet with a sliding protective visor (that is part of his [Kineceleran](/wiki/Kineceleran_(Classic) "Kineceleran (Classic)") biology and not mechanical).[[pop-up 1]](#cite_note-2)[[2]](#cite_note-3) His visor is made up of his skin and part of his skull.[[DR 1]](#cite_note-4) Whenever his visor is lifted, one can see his blue face, green eyes, black lips, and stripes running under and in between his eyes; the rest of his head's features are unknown. XLR8 has five blue stripes on his tail and wears black bodysuit and a turtleneck-like shirt with a white stripe on the center.


In *[Back with a Vengeance](/wiki/Back_with_a_Vengeance "Back with a Vengeance")*, XLR8 wore a yellow raincoat.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")** and *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, XLR8 looked exactly the same as he did in the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), albeit his skin was slightly darker and he was more muscular.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, XLR8 is now taller and the white color on his shirt is replaced by green, which now covers his whole neck. He has four blue stripes on his tail, the black balls on his feet are bigger, his helmet is longer in the back and front, his claws are longer and his visor is darker.


11-year-old XLR8 in **Omniverse** looks exactly the same as his 16-year-old self, except that he is shorter, and the green color of his shirt is white. He has three stripes on his tail.


XLR8 wears the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)")/[Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his chest.



### Ben 10,000 as XLR8


[Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)")'s XLR8 was taller and more muscular-looking than his 10-11/16-year-old self. His arms, legs and tail stripes were white instead of blue, and there were less stripes on his tail. XLR8's clothing had longer sleeves with a pair of stripes under the shoulders and has an arrow-shaped symbol on his lower torso. He wore the Omnitrix symbol on his chest.



### Ken as XLR8


[Ken](/wiki/Ken_Tennyson "Ken Tennyson")'s XLR8 looked the same as 10-year-old XLR8 in the original series.



### Gwen 10 as XLR8


[Gwen 10](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Gweniverse) "Gwen Tennyson (Gweniverse)")'s XLR8 is more upright. Her face lacks the black markings that Ben's has, as they are replaced with freckles. She has pink eyes and Gwen 10's hair-clips, as well as a black helmet. The majority of her outfit is dark blue with a light blue section around her neck that goes down her chest in a V-formation, light blue stripes on her forearms and wrists, two light blue stripes on the end of her tail, and a light blue stripe on each of her legs where the outfit ends. Her visor now has pink markings, and her hands are more human-shaped with claws for fingers. She wears the Omnitrix symbol on her chest with a cat shape surrounding it.

Using the orbs on his feet, XLR8 can reach running speeds of 500 miles per hour[[merch 1]](#cite_note-Bandai-5) within two seconds. He can accelerate so quickly that time appears to completely stop to him, allowing him to change the positions of things around him mid-motion, while only being able to be seen by a camera with a very high shutter speed.[[3]](#cite_note-The_Unnaturals-6)


XLR8 is fast enough to run up walls[[4]](#cite_note-7) and run on water.[[5]](#cite_note-The_Krakken-8)[[6]](#cite_note-BwaV-9)


XLR8 is capable of creating tornadoes by generating a centripetal vacuum powerful enough to lift a [To'kustar](/wiki/To%27kustar_(Classic) "To'kustar (Classic)")[[7]](#cite_note-10), either by running in a circular pattern or spinning at supersonic speeds.[[8]](#cite_note-MW-11)[[9]](#cite_note-MC-12) He can also combine this with water running to create water vortexes by running in front of[[5]](#cite_note-The_Krakken-8) or around his enemies.[[10]](#cite_note-13)


Even without his orbs, XLR8 naturally has a very high dexterity, capable of thinking, acting and reacting at superhuman speed. His sharp reflexes allow him to dodge attacks with little to no effort,[[11]](#cite_note-TR-14)[[12]](#cite_note-B10VtN10P1-15) and change direction very easily while running. He is able to type on keyboards or keypads at high speeds. When using this skill to bypass a multiple-digit code, he can think of and input every single possible combination instead of guessing, such as when he was trying to remove [SevenSeven 23](/wiki/SevenSeven_(Dimension_23) "SevenSeven (Dimension 23)")'s bomb from [Ben 23](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Dimension_23) "Ben Tennyson (Dimension 23)")'s arm.[[13]](#cite_note-Store_23-16)


Compared to his fellow speedster alien [Fasttrack](/wiki/Fasttrack "Fasttrack"), XLR8 seems to have more control over his speed; he can immediately end a run by stopping in place, while Fasttrack can have trouble stopping if he runs too fast.[[14]](#cite_note-17) Furthermore, he is implied to be faster than [Citrakayahs](/wiki/Citrakayah "Citrakayah")[[15]](#cite_note-18)[[16]](#cite_note-Solitary-19) and confirmed to be faster than [Aerophibians](/wiki/Aerophibian_(Classic) "Aerophibian (Classic)") on the ground.[[DM 1]](#cite_note-20)


XLR8 can deliver attacks in rapid succession, such as excessively kicking, or stabbing with his claws. The strength of his attacks also get enhanced when he applies more speed to them.


XLR8's scissor-like claws can cut through many materials with relative ease.[[17]](#cite_note-21)


XLR8 has enhanced strength, as he was able to carry a multitude of people, including [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)"), to safety without slowing down at age 10.[[5]](#cite_note-The_Krakken-8)[[8]](#cite_note-MW-11)


XLR8 can recover from injuries faster than any other species,[[12]](#cite_note-B10VtN10P1-15) and is durable enough to withstand being thrown against a wall.[[18]](#cite_note-MHR-22)


XLR8's tail is prehensile, as he held a [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)") egg with it when he was about to trap [Devlin 11](/wiki/Devlin_Levin "Devlin Levin").[[19]](#cite_note-Ken_10-23) This feat was first demonstrated when [Kevin 11](/wiki/Kevin_11 "Kevin 11") used his tail to carefully pick up a tray of slop and shove it in [Technorg](/wiki/Technorg "Technorg")'s face when he wasn't looking.[[20]](#cite_note-24)


XLR8 is able to cling to surfaces, either due to his claws, his agility, or a combination of the two.[[8]](#cite_note-MW-11)


XLR8 has the potential to become even faster with age, seeing how XLR8 was capable of travelling globally in just a few seconds when used by Ben 10,000.[[pop-up 2]](#cite_note-25)[[21]](#cite_note-Ben_10.2C000-26) This also boosts his strength, shown when his XLR8 was able to greatly damage [Exo-Skull](/wiki/Exo-Skull "Exo-Skull") with speed-enhanced physical attacks.[[21]](#cite_note-Ben_10.2C000-26)


XLR8 can make opponents dizzy to the point of falling unconscious by grabbing them by their arms and spinning them around in a circle at a fast pace; this feat was exclusively demonstrated by [Helen](/wiki/Helen_Wheels "Helen Wheels") on Gwen.[[22]](#cite_note-27)


XLR8 is completely frictionless.[[DR 2]](#cite_note-28)

1/10
XLR8's biggest weakness is magnets and charged pulses, as they can completely throw him off-course and cause him to lose his footing and spin out of control.[[merch 1]](#cite_note-Bandai-5)


XLR8's speed does not work on all surfaces; running on ice can slow him down,[[9]](#cite_note-MC-12) whereas running on mud leaves him stuck in place.[[19]](#cite_note-Ken_10-23)


XLR8 can also be held in place if his tail is either stepped on[[23]](#cite_note-29) or held down.[[24]](#cite_note-30)


Opponents with good reflexes and reaction time can counter XLR8's speed and land a blow on him, such as [Transylians](/wiki/Transylian "Transylian"),[[11]](#cite_note-TR-14) [Tetramands](/wiki/Tetramand_(Classic) "Tetramand (Classic)"),[[18]](#cite_note-MHR-22) and [Vulpimancers](/wiki/Vulpimancer_(Classic) "Vulpimancer (Classic)").[[25]](#cite_note-31)


XLR8 is unable to avoid multiple objects being thrown at him all at once.[[21]](#cite_note-Ben_10.2C000-26)


Despite his enhanced strength, XLR8 felt discomfort on his back from carrying [Mr. Baumann](/wiki/Ignacius_Baumann "Ignacius Baumann") over his shoulders, though this was because Mr. Baumann had rocks in his pockets.[[26]](#cite_note-32) Technically speaking, XLR8 is physically weaker than Citrakayahs.[[MW 1]](#cite_note-33)


XLR8 has sensitive hearing, meaning he is vulnerable to being knocked out by a [Sonorosian](/wiki/Sonorosian "Sonorosian")'s sonic screams, as demonstrated with Helen.[[27]](#cite_note-34)



History
-------


### 1773


* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, 16-year-old Ben as XLR8 was defeated by young [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)").


### Ben 10


* XLR8 first appeared in *[And Then There Were 10](/wiki/And_Then_There_Were_10 "And Then There Were 10")*, where he got revenge on [J.T.](/wiki/J.T._(Classic) "J.T. (Classic)") and [Cash](/wiki/Cash_Murray_(Classic) "Cash Murray (Classic)") before helping [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") and [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") pack their belongings into the [Rust Bucket](/wiki/Rust_Bucket_(Classic) "Rust Bucket (Classic)").
* In *[The Krakken](/wiki/The_Krakken "The Krakken")*, XLR8 saved some people from the [Krakken](/wiki/Krakken "Krakken"), but failed to stop her from stealing a crate from [Jonah Melville](/wiki/Jonah_Melville "Jonah Melville")'s boat.
* In *[Permanent Retirement](/wiki/Permanent_Retirement "Permanent Retirement")*, XLR8 was used offscreen to take the people back to their houses.
* In *[Kevin 11](/wiki/Kevin_11_(Episode) "Kevin 11 (Episode)")*, XLR8 saved [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") from some bullies.
* In *[The Alliance](/wiki/The_Alliance "The Alliance")*, XLR8 battled [Rojo](/wiki/Rojo "Rojo") until he was forced to retreat with Gwen.
* In *[Lucky Girl](/wiki/Lucky_Girl "Lucky Girl")*, XLR8 defeated [Hex](/wiki/Hex_(Classic) "Hex (Classic)") in a museum.
* In *[Secrets](/wiki/Secrets "Secrets")*, XLR8 appeared while he was captured. Later, XLR8 fought [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") on [Mt. Rushmore](/wiki/Mt._Rushmore "Mt. Rushmore") until the malfunctioning [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") made him switch into [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)").
	+ Also, XLR8 appeared as one of the many alien templates used for Vilgax's army in an imaginary sequence.
* In *[Truth](/wiki/Truth "Truth")*, XLR8 traveled to the Mt. Rushmore [Plumber](/wiki/Plumbers_(Classic) "Plumbers (Classic)") base with Gwen and was defeated by a [Wigsilian Org Beast](/wiki/Wigsilian_Org_Beast "Wigsilian Org Beast").
* In *[The Big Tick](/wiki/The_Big_Tick "The Big Tick")*, XLR8 failed to defeat the [Great One](/wiki/Great_One "Great One").
* In *[The Galactic Enforcers](/wiki/The_Galactic_Enforcers_(Episode) "The Galactic Enforcers (Episode)")*, XLR8 battled [SixSix](/wiki/SixSix_(Classic) "SixSix (Classic)") and [Vulkanus](/wiki/Vulkanus "Vulkanus").
* In *[Camp Fear](/wiki/Camp_Fear "Camp Fear")*, XLR8 searched the underground level of [Camp Opinicon](/wiki/Camp_Opinicon "Camp Opinicon") for Max and found him, but reverted before he could free him.
* In *[Back with a Vengeance](/wiki/Back_with_a_Vengeance "Back with a Vengeance")*, XLR8 appeared six times. Firstly, XLR8 was used by the [Master Control](/wiki/Master_Control "Master Control"). Later, he stole Max's food. Then, he ran on the river in order to catch a falling cart. After that, XLR8 went after Vilgax and [Kevin 11](/wiki/Kevin_11 "Kevin 11"). Lastly, in the [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)"), XLR8 tried to escape from Vilgax twice.
* In *[Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Episode) "Ben 10,000 (Episode)")*, XLR8 saved Gwen from her [future self](/wiki/Gwendolyn_Tennyson "Gwendolyn Tennyson") and later fought [Exo-Skull](/wiki/Exo-Skull "Exo-Skull") until he reverted.
* In *[Merry Christmas](/wiki/Merry_Christmas "Merry Christmas")*, XLR8 defeated some toy Nutcracker soldiers.
* In *[Under Wraps](/wiki/Under_Wraps "Under Wraps")*, XLR8 defeated a pair of [mutated](/wiki/Corrodium "Corrodium") cows and battled the [Mummy](/wiki/Mummy "Mummy") until he reverted. Later, XLR8 collected some chicken eggs.
* In *[Monster Weather](/wiki/Monster_Weather "Monster Weather")*, XLR8 played the drums in a band and protected some people from [S.A.M.](/wiki/S.A.M. "S.A.M.")'s lightning. He then chased the latter through the city and eventually defeated him.
* In *[The Unnaturals](/wiki/The_Unnaturals "The Unnaturals")*, XLR8 intervened with the [Squires](/wiki/Squires "Squires") to help the Cannons win the baseball game and humiliated J.T. and Cash in the process. Later, XLR8 hung J.T. and Cash from a sign by their shirts.
* In *[The Return](/wiki/The_Return "The Return")*, XLR8 fought [Viktor](/wiki/Viktor "Viktor") and witnessed [Zs'Skayr](/wiki/Zs%27Skayr "Zs'Skayr") being revived.
* In *[Perfect Day](/wiki/Perfect_Day "Perfect Day")*, XLR8 defeated [Enoch](/wiki/Enoch "Enoch") and some [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights") and trapped the former in a dream world. In Enoch's dream, XLR8 failed to trap him in a dream world and quickly reverted.
* In the Blue Edition of ***[Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix](/wiki/Ben_10:_Secret_of_the_Omnitrix "Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix")***, XLR8 fought a [Mutant Hornet](/wiki/Mutant_Hornet "Mutant Hornet") and destroyed [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)")'s DNA bomb, accidentally activating the Omnitrix's Self-Destruct Mode in the process.
* In *[Ben 4 Good Buddy](/wiki/Ben_4_Good_Buddy "Ben 4 Good Buddy")*, XLR8 fixed the [Rust Bucket](/wiki/Rust_Bucket "Rust Bucket").
* In *[Ready to Rumble](/wiki/Ready_to_Rumble "Ready to Rumble")*, XLR8 road down a hill while sitting on an ice block.
* In *[Ben 10 vs. the Negative 10: Part 1](/wiki/Ben_10_vs._the_Negative_10:_Part_1 "Ben 10 vs. the Negative 10: Part 1")*, XLR8 battled some [hypnotized](/wiki/Sublimino "Sublimino") guards.
* In *[Let the Games Begin](/wiki/Let_the_Games_Begin "Let the Games Begin")*, XLR8 looked for certain objects on the road so he could beat Gwen in a game of bingo.

Etymology
---------


XLR8's name is a [gramogram](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/gramogram "wikipedia:gramogram") of the word "accelerate", referencing his enhanced speed. A few translations of the show use a gramogram of their own, like the French *"AXLR"*, but most others simply use XLR8 in the official dubbing, in their respective accent. However, XLR8 is gibberish in any language other than English, so it ends up making no sense.



Trivia
------


* A common misconception about XLR8 is that the balls on his feet are referred to as wheels. This even occurs within the [Reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)").[[28]](#cite_note-35)
* In his [original design](/wiki/Ben_10_(Pre-Production)#XLR8 "Ben 10 (Pre-Production)") XLR8 had the same name, powers, and skin color, but was much more humanoid before the crew switched from superheroes to aliens. [Dave Johnson](/wiki/Dave_Johnson "Dave Johnson") designed him but noted that like [Ripjaws](/wiki/Ripjaws_(Classic) "Ripjaws (Classic)") and [Wildmutt](/wiki/Wildmutt "Wildmutt"), it was hard for outsourced animators to draw him, particularly his hands.[[DJ 1]](#cite_note-36)
* Originally, XLR8 was the only Kineceleran on [Earth](/wiki/Earth_(Classic) "Earth (Classic)") because the slow rotation of said planet made other Kinecelerans sick after a few days.[[pop-up 3]](#cite_note-37)[[21]](#cite_note-Ben_10.2C000-26) This was retconned in **Omniverse**, as [several Kinecelerans](/wiki/Kineceleran_Kids "Kineceleran Kids") reside in [Undertown](/wiki/Undertown "Undertown").[[29]](#cite_note-38)
* XLR8's voice has changed over the course of his appearances, though a buzzing-like sound effect can be heard whenever XLR8 speaks nonetheless:
	+ In the original series, XLR8 spoke with a raspy voice.
	+ In **Omniverse**, XLR8's voice sounds like a fast-talking, higher-pitched version of 16-year-old [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s voice.
* XLR8 is the final alien used onscreen in the original series, despite said appearance being a non-canon "What If?" episode.
* XLR8 and [NRG](/wiki/NRG "NRG") are the only aliens whose names are gramograms.
* XLR8 was the winner of a [Cartoon Network](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cartoon_Network "wikipedia:Cartoon Network") **[Ben 10](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10")** alien poll that only had the first twelve aliens.
* XLR8's name was used in a [Pop dance group](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/XLR8 "wikipedia:XLR8") in the [Philippines](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "wikipedia:Philippines"), as well as on a CD about different technical institutes by the [Universal Technical Institute](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Universal_Technical_Institute "wikipedia:Universal Technical Institute").
* In the online game *[Ben to the Rescue](/wiki/Ben_to_the_Rescue "Ben to the Rescue")*, XLR8's visor is miscolored green.
