Appearance
----------


Ampfibian is a blue [jellyfish](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jellyfish "wikipedia:Jellyfish")-like alien with white swipes on his body resembling lightning streaks, a main body with three-segmented skirt-like parts for his head, chest and abdomen, and six long tentacles that function as two pairs of arms and a pair of legs.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Ampfibian had pupil-less eyes and wore the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest. His eyes were colored blue in *[Fused](/wiki/Fused "Fused")* due to being fused with [Ra'ad](/wiki/Ra%27ad "Ra'ad"), but were changed to green for the rest of his appearances.


In *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, Ampfibian looked mostly the same as in **Ultimate Alien** except most of the white swipes on his body were missing while the ones that remained were slightly darker and shaped differently.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Ampfibian looks almost the same as before, except the lightning streak swipes on his body have been altered, his tentacles are now pointed instead of curved, and his green eyes are larger with the black outlining in them becoming much more prominent. He wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his mouth.

Ampfibian can manipulate, produce, and absorb electrical energy, such as lightning and the [Piscciss Volann](/wiki/Piscciss_Volann_(Classic) "Piscciss Volann (Classic)") guards' energy net.[[3]](#cite_note-Deep-4) That energy is then launched out of his tentacles as attacks.


Similar to [Ghostfreak](/wiki/Ghostfreak "Ghostfreak") and [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill_(Classic) "Big Chill (Classic)"), Ampfibian can make himself intangible.[[4]](#cite_note-Fused-5) Correlating with this, he is capable of electrically shocking any objects and beings he phases through.[[5]](#cite_note-6)


Ampfibian can fly and is agile enough to dodge attacks while flying.[[6]](#cite_note-Special_Delivery-7)


Ampfibian can breathe underwater, the latter of which allows him to swim at superhuman speeds. He can also safely produce and discharge electricity underwater.[[DM 1]](#cite_note-8)[[3]](#cite_note-Deep-4)


Ampfibian's tentacles can extend multiple times their length.[[7]](#cite_note-9)[[6]](#cite_note-Special_Delivery-7)


Similar to [Upgrade](/wiki/Upgrade_(Classic) "Upgrade (Classic)") and [Buzzshock](/wiki/Buzzshock_(Classic) "Buzzshock (Classic)"), Ampfibian can become pure energy in order to enter electrical cables and generate a large energy blast that surrounds him, as demonstrated by Ra'ad.[[4]](#cite_note-Fused-5)


Ampfibian has degree of enhanced strength, enough to restrain [Phil](/wiki/Phil_Billings_(Classic) "Phil Billings (Classic)") with his tentacles.[[8]](#cite_note-Max.27s_Monster-10)


Even though his body is frail, Ampfibian is durable enough to withstand being thrown against a wall by [Solid Plugg](/wiki/Solid_Plugg "Solid Plugg")[[9]](#cite_note-Have_I_Got_a_Deal_for_You-11) and getting hit by an [Aerophibian](/wiki/Aerophibian_(Classic) "Aerophibian (Classic)")'s neuroshock blasts.[[10]](#cite_note-The_Rooters_of_All_Evil-12)


Ampfibian can read both [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)") and [Osmosians](/wiki/Osmosian "Osmosian")' thoughts by sensing the electrical pulses of their minds;[[DM 2]](#cite_note-13) this feat was only demonstrated by Ra'ad on [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)").[[4]](#cite_note-Fused-5)


Ampfibian can survive in the vacuum of space and generate a protective energy shield around himself; these feats were both exclusively demonstrated by [Ultimate Aggregor](/wiki/Ultimate_Aggregor "Ultimate Aggregor") during his visit to the [Perplexahedron](/wiki/Perplexahedron "Perplexahedron").[[11]](#cite_note-14)

Ampfibian's intangibility is useless against other beings that can also become intangible, as it will cancel out their abilities to do so.[[12]](#cite_note-15)


Ampfibian is weak against enemies who can drain electricity, like Phil.[[8]](#cite_note-Max.27s_Monster-10) Furthermore, Ampfibian's electric attacks are useless against Solid Plugg[[9]](#cite_note-Have_I_Got_a_Deal_for_You-11) because he is made out of super dense rubber.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-16)


Since his body is very frail, heavy objects falling on Ampfibian can make him faint.[[10]](#cite_note-The_Rooters_of_All_Evil-12)


Despite his durability, Ampfibian can still be harmed by an Aerophibian's neuroshock blasts.[[10]](#cite_note-The_Rooters_of_All_Evil-12)


Ampfibian is vulnerable to magic, as shown when he got trapped in [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)")'s [mana](/wiki/Mana "Mana") shields.[[4]](#cite_note-Fused-5)


Despite being able to use his electricity underwater, Ampfibian can accidentally shock himself if he is entering or partially in water, as demonstrated with Ra'ad.[[4]](#cite_note-Fused-5)



History
-------


### Ultimate Alien


* Ampfibian first appeared in *[Fused](/wiki/Fused "Fused")*, where his DNA was sampled and put in the [Codon Stream](/wiki/Codon_Stream "Codon Stream") when the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") scanned [Ra'ad](/wiki/Ra%27ad "Ra'ad")'s DNA. [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") remained stuck as Ampfibian due to Ra'ad being trapped in the Ultimatrix until [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") eventually put the Ultimatrix in Reset Mode.
* In *[Deep](/wiki/Deep "Deep")*, Ampfibian swam to [Piscciss](/wiki/Piscciss_(Classic) "Piscciss (Classic)")' core, along with [the team](/wiki/Alien_Force "Alien Force") and [Magister Pyke](/wiki/Magister_Pyke "Magister Pyke").
* In *[Viktor: The Spoils](/wiki/Viktor:_The_Spoils "Viktor: The Spoils")*, an unconscious Ampfibian's electric abilities were forcibly used to put [King Xarion](/wiki/King_Xarion "King Xarion")'s mind in [Viktor](/wiki/Viktor "Viktor")'s body.
* In *[Ben 10/Generator Rex: Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, Ampfibian appeared as one of the many aliens when Ben was explaining his story to [Rex](/wiki/Rex_Salazar "Rex Salazar").
* In *[Inspector #13](/wiki/Inspector_13_(Episode) "Inspector 13 (Episode)")*, [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") as Ampfibian battled several incomplete [Techadon robots](/wiki/Techadon_Robots "Techadon Robots").
* In *[Night of the Living Nightmare](/wiki/Night_of_the_Living_Nightmare "Night of the Living Nightmare")*, Ampfibian defeated [Ultimate Kevin](/wiki/Ultimate_Kevin "Ultimate Kevin") in a dream.


### Omniverse


* In *[Have I Got a Deal for You](/wiki/Have_I_Got_a_Deal_for_You "Have I Got a Deal for You")*, Ampfibian electrocuted [Pax](/wiki/Pax "Pax"), [Blarney T. Hokestar](/wiki/Blarney_T._Hokestar "Blarney T. Hokestar"), fought [Solid Plugg](/wiki/Solid_Plugg "Solid Plugg") and saved the [Screegit](/wiki/Screegit "Screegit").
* In *[Special Delivery](/wiki/Special_Delivery "Special Delivery")*, Ampfibian defeated [Seebik](/wiki/Seebik "Seebik") and the [Kraaho](/wiki/Kraaho "Kraaho").
* In *[Rad](/wiki/Rad "Rad")*, Ampfibian caught Pax.
* In *[Return to Forever](/wiki/Return_to_Forever "Return to Forever")*, Ampfibian tried to fight the [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights"), but was reverted by their [DNA Scanner](/wiki/DNA_Scanner "DNA Scanner").
* In *[Max's Monster](/wiki/Max%27s_Monster "Max's Monster")*, Ampfibian battled [Phil](/wiki/Phil_Billings_(Classic) "Phil Billings (Classic)").
* In *[The Rooters of All Evil](/wiki/The_Rooters_of_All_Evil "The Rooters of All Evil")*, Ampfibian was defeated by [Swift](/wiki/Swift "Swift").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Ampfibian appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

Etymology
---------


Ampfibian's name is a portmanteau of the words "amper" and "[amphibian](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amphibian "wikipedia:Amphibian")" (with the "ph" changed to a "pf"). It is also a pun of the word "amplify" ("amp" for short), which means to charge and increase energy, referencing Ampfibian's abilities to absorb, generate, and redirect electrical energy. The word "amphibian" is also used since most are either adapted to water, or generate their own electricity.



Trivia
------


* Ampfibian was designed by Steven Choi and Chap Yaep,[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-17) and was named by [Len Wein](/wiki/Len_Wein "Len Wein").[[MW 1]](#cite_note-18)
* Ampfibian was the [Alien of the Month](/wiki/Alien_of_the_Month "Alien of the Month") in September 2011.
* Ampfibian and the other Andromeda aliens have powers linked to a certain element; for Ampfibian, it is electricity.
* Ampfibian is the only Andromeda alien that [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") used in the same episode he was scanned in.[[4]](#cite_note-Fused-5)
* As of **Omniverse**, Ampfibian and [Feedback](/wiki/Feedback "Feedback") are the only aliens who generate blue electricity.
