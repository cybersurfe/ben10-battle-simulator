Appearance
----------


### Ben as Fasttrack


Fasttrack is a feline-like alien with a black suit-like color scheme, and his hands, legs, chest, and part of his head are colored blue. He has black mask-like fins around his green eyes, a triangle-shaped nose, a thunderbolt-like design down his chest, and a triangle one down his back. Similar to [Water Hazard](/wiki/Water_Hazard "Water Hazard"), he also has spike-like blades on his arms and legs.


Fasttrack wears the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.



### Albedo as Negative Fasttrack


[Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Fasttrack looks just like [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s Fasttrack, but with red eyes and no Ultimatrix symbol.

1/11
Much like [XLR8](/wiki/XLR8_(Classic) "XLR8 (Classic)"), Fasttrack has enhanced speed and agility, and is shown to have enhanced reflexes as well.


Fasttrack is capable of giving himself a boost of speed while running; this is demonstrated when [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)")'s missile flew past him, and Fasttrack ran at a higher speed, allowing him to outrun it for a short time and forcing Animo to increase the missile's power.[[2]](#cite_note-TEC-3) Furthermore, Fasttrack can also jump to great heights while running.[[1]](#cite_note-EoMF-1)


Fasttrack is durable enough to withstand being thrown against the [Seal](/wiki/Seal "Seal").[[3]](#cite_note-SA-4)


Fasttrack can recover quickly, as he was able to immediately get back on his feet and continue running when he saw that [Sir George](/wiki/Sir_George "Sir George") was about to attack him again.[[3]](#cite_note-SA-4) Similarly, Fasttrack was able to recover from falling to the ground after he jumped towards Animo's missile but failed to catch it.[[2]](#cite_note-TEC-3)


Fasttrack possesses enhanced strength, as he was able to carry both [Magister Hulka](/wiki/Magister_Hulka "Magister Hulka") and [Tack](/wiki/Tack "Tack") to safety, [[4]](#cite_note-5) as well as [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") and [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") while in Legerdomain.[[1]](#cite_note-EoMF-1) Technically speaking, Fasttrack is physically stronger than [Kinecelerans](/wiki/Kineceleran_(Classic) "Kineceleran (Classic)").[[MW 1]](#cite_note-6)


Fasttrack has enhanced dexterity, as seen when he disarmed a group of [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knight "Forever Knight") by taking their weapons away and was able to carry them all in his hands before dropping them on the floor.[[5]](#cite_note-7)


Fasttrack seems to have an exceptional level of balance, as he was easily able to run out of [Kevin's car](/wiki/Kevin%27s_Car_(Original) "Kevin's Car (Original)") without falling down and stay on his feet even while it was speeding through the desert.[[2]](#cite_note-TEC-3)


Like XLR8, Fasttrack has an enhanced sense of direction, as he could easily knock down several [Esoterica](/wiki/Esoterica "Esoterica") in seconds while running in sharp zigzags and circles.[[6]](#cite_note-8)



Weaknesses
----------


* ![]()Slippery Surfaces
* ![]()Exploitable Speed
1/2
Fasttrack can have a hard time stopping if he runs too quickly.[[2]](#cite_note-TEC-3)


It is implied that, despite his enhanced speed, Fasttrack is physically slower than [Kinecelerans](/wiki/Kineceleran_(Classic) "Kineceleran (Classic)").[[7]](#cite_note-9)[[3]](#cite_note-SA-4)


Being a speed-based alien, Fasttrack shares similar weaknesses with XLR8. For example, he is being unable to run on certain surfaces like ice, which can make him slip very easily.[[2]](#cite_note-TEC-3)


Similarly, opponents with good reflexes and reaction time can counter Fasttrack's speed and land a blow on him, as seen when Fasttrack ran straight into [Sir George](/wiki/Sir_George "Sir George")'s outstretched arm, badly stunning him.[[3]](#cite_note-SA-4)


Being a feline-based alien, Fasttrack can be controlled by people who have the ability to control cats, like [Nyancy Chan](/wiki/Nyancy_Chan "Nyancy Chan"), depending on how prevalent his feline attributes are.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-10)


Unlike [XLR8](/wiki/XLR8_(Classic) "XLR8 (Classic)"), Fasttrack is not completely frictionless.[[DR 1]](#cite_note-11)



History
-------


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, Fasttrack was unlocked by [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Ultimate_Alien) "Ben 10,000 (Ultimate Alien)").
* Fasttrack first appeared in *[Basic Training](/wiki/Basic_Training "Basic Training")*, where he saved [Tack](/wiki/Tack "Tack") and [Magister Hulka](/wiki/Magister_Hulka "Magister Hulka") from an explosion caused by a [Nuclear Fusion Grenade](/wiki/Nuclear_Fusion_Grenade "Nuclear Fusion Grenade").
* In *[A Knight to Remember](/wiki/A_Knight_to_Remember "A Knight to Remember")*, Fasttrack disarmed the [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights") by taking all of their weapons away.
* In *[Solitary Alignment](/wiki/Solitary_Alignment "Solitary Alignment")*, Fasttrack tried to take the [Ascalon](/wiki/Ascalon "Ascalon") from [Sir George](/wiki/Sir_George "Sir George"), but failed and was defeated by him, forcing Fasttrack to switch into [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)").
* In *[Enemy of My Frenemy](/wiki/Enemy_of_My_Frenemy "Enemy of My Frenemy")*, Fasttrack was tied to [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") and [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") (with [mana](/wiki/Mana "Mana")) so they could all escape from the [Scrutins](/wiki/Scrutin "Scrutin") together. He and [the team](/wiki/Alien_Force "Alien Force") then met [Ignaceous](/wiki/Ignaceous "Ignaceous").
* In *[The Eggman Cometh](/wiki/The_Eggman_Cometh "The Eggman Cometh")*, Fasttrack trained with Kevin until they were stopped by Gwen. Later, Fasttrack failed to stop [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)")'s missile from destroying a weather balloon experiment.
* In *[Couples Retreat](/wiki/Couples_Retreat "Couples Retreat")*, Fasttrack failed to catch [Darkstar](/wiki/Michael_Morningstar_(Classic) "Michael Morningstar (Classic)") and stop him from going to the [Door to Anywhere](/wiki/Door_to_Anywhere "Door to Anywhere"). He then looked around for the stolen page from Gwen's [spellbook](/wiki/Gwen%27s_Spellbook "Gwen's Spellbook") before reverting.
* In *[Night of the Living Nightmare](/wiki/Night_of_the_Living_Nightmare "Night of the Living Nightmare")*, Fasttrack appeared twice in a dream. Firstly, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Fasttrack fled from [Swampfire](/wiki/Swampfire "Swampfire"). Seconds later, Ben as Fasttrack chased Negative Fasttrack all the way to [Mr. Smoothy](/wiki/Mr._Smoothy_(Classic) "Mr. Smoothy (Classic)").
* In *[The Beginning of the End](/wiki/The_Beginning_of_the_End "The Beginning of the End")*, Fasttrack defeated the [Esoterica](/wiki/Esoterica "Esoterica") who were spying on him, Gwen, and Kevin.

Etymology
---------


"Fast track" is an informal phrase meaning "the quickest and most direct route to achievement of a goal", as in competing for professional advancement.



Trivia
------


* Although Fasttrack did not appear in **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, he was mentioned in *[Have I Got a Deal for You](/wiki/Have_I_Got_a_Deal_for_You "Have I Got a Deal for You")*, *[The Ultimate Heist](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Heist "The Ultimate Heist")*, and *[Breakpoint](/wiki/Breakpoint "Breakpoint")*.
	+ There were no plans for Fasttrack to appear during the early seasons of the show.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-12)
* Fasttrack is the only alien introduced in **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")** to have been unlocked off-screen.
* Fasttrack's voice sounds very similar to [Spidermonkey](/wiki/Spidermonkey_(Classic) "Spidermonkey (Classic)")'s.
* Fasttrack's name was first revealed in a *[Ben 10: Galactic Racing](/wiki/Ben_10:_Galactic_Racing "Ben 10: Galactic Racing")* press release and confirmed by writer [Peter David](/wiki/Peter_David "Peter David"), who revealed the name of Fasttrack's [species](/wiki/Citrakayah "Citrakayah") and [home planet](/wiki/Chalybeas "Chalybeas") as well.[[PD 1]](#cite_note-revelation-2)
* The reason why Fasttrack was absent in **Omniverse** was because the crew was unable to figure out how to make him more interesting compared to what was shown in **Ultimate Alien**.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-13)
	+ Had he appeared, Fasttrack would have worn clothes, similar to many other aliens.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-14) [Dee Bradley Baker](/wiki/Dee_Bradley_Baker "Dee Bradley Baker") would have also reprised his role, but with some feline attributes added to his voice.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-15)
* A concept idea for Fasttrack's **Omniverse** design depicted him with wheeled feet inspired by the Wheelers from ***[Return to Oz](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Return_to_Oz "wikipedia:Return to Oz")***. However, it was rejected because the design made him look even more similar to XLR8.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-16)
	+ [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") eventually drew a version of Fasttrack in the **Omniverse** art style, as well as an alternate version depicting him with sunglasses similar to [Bullfrag](/wiki/Bullfrag "Bullfrag")'s, as part of a GoFundMe in 2021.[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-17) The alternate design was made after Derrick received several fan requests regarding Fasttrack wearing sunglasses.[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-18)
