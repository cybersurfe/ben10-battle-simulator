Appearance
----------


Alien X is a humanoid alien whose entire body is solid black, excluding his hands, which are white. He has tiny white star-like spots all over his body, giving him the appearance of a starry night sky. His eyes are green and lack pupils, and his forehead bears three horn-like protrusions.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Alien X is largely the same as before, but with minor changes. His chin is now bigger, his body is more muscular, his horns are smaller, and the stars on his body are brighter than before.


Alien X wears the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)")/[Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his chest.



Personality
-----------


 [![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/1/1f/SLaTfAtS_%28574%29.png/revision/latest?cb=20160105171015) [![](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/1/1f/SLaTfAtS_%28574%29.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20160105171015)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/1/1f/SLaTfAtS_%28574%29.png/revision/latest?cb=20160105171015)  Bellicus, Serena, and Ben, the three personalities of Alien X

  
Alien X has three personalities: [Serena](/wiki/Serena "Serena"), the "voice of love and compassion", [Bellicus](/wiki/Bellicus "Bellicus"), the "voice of rage and aggression", and [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)"), the "voice of reason".[[1]](#cite_note-X.3DB.2B2-2) All three of these voices combine to form Alien X's speech. Otherwise, Alien X is silent. The only things he will say are what he will be doing - what Ben, Bellicus, and Serena have agreed to do.


As Serena and Bellicus have been arguing for eternity and before they had no tiebreaker, getting them to focus on any present task is difficult at best. This leaves Alien X's body trapped in place, floating about a foot above the ground until an agreement is made.

History
-------


### Alien Force


* Alien X first appeared in *[X = Ben + 2](/wiki/X_%3D_Ben_%2B_2 "X = Ben + 2")*, where he stopped a flood caused by a broken dam. After much fruitless arguing, [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") convinces [Serena](/wiki/Serena "Serena") and [Bellicus](/wiki/Bellicus "Bellicus") to transform him into another alien (mostly by insulting them). Ben swore that he won't use Alien X again, as it is not worth the price.
	+ The same scene reoccurred in a flashback in *[The Ultimate Enemy: Part 2](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Enemy:_Part_2 "The Ultimate Enemy: Part 2")*.


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[The Forge of Creation](/wiki/The_Forge_of_Creation "The Forge of Creation")*, [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") half-heartedly turned into Alien X in hopes of learning the location of the [Forge of Creation](/wiki/Forge_of_Creation "Forge of Creation") from [Bellicus](/wiki/Bellicus "Bellicus") and [Serena](/wiki/Serena "Serena") to no avail until [Professor Paradox](/wiki/Professor_Paradox "Professor Paradox") suddenly appeared. Bellicus and Serena almost came to a vote in having Paradox destroyed for being within 500 light-years of a [Celestialsapien](/wiki/Celestialsapien_(Classic) "Celestialsapien (Classic)") before he reverted Alien X.


### Omniverse


* In *[So Long, and Thanks for All the Smoothies](/wiki/So_Long,_and_Thanks_for_All_the_Smoothies "So Long, and Thanks for All the Smoothies")*, Alien X was unable to stop the [Annihilargh](/wiki/Annihilargh "Annihilargh") from destroying the universe. Fortunately, [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") managed to convince [Bellicus](/wiki/Bellicus "Bellicus") and [Serena](/wiki/Serena "Serena") to recreate a near-identical copy.
* In *[Universe vs. Tennyson](/wiki/Universe_vs._Tennyson "Universe vs. Tennyson")*, Alien X defeated the [Galactic Gladiator](/wiki/Galactic_Gladiator "Galactic Gladiator"), winning the trial for recreating the universe.
* In *[Weapon XI: Part 1](/wiki/Weapon_XI:_Part_1 "Weapon XI: Part 1")*, Alien X battled the [Rooters](/wiki/Rooters "Rooters") and the [brainwashed](/wiki/Servantis "Servantis") [Amalgam Kids](/wiki/Amalgam_Kids "Amalgam Kids").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, [Skurd](/wiki/Skurd "Skurd") used Alien X's DNA to create a sword for [Chromastone](/wiki/Chromastone "Chromastone"), which managed to pierce the Annihilargh's barrier. Later, Alien X appeared for a short time when Ben was cycling through his aliens.


### Alternate Timeline


* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, Negative Alien X was mentioned by [Professor Paradox](/wiki/Professor_Paradox "Professor Paradox") when speaking of an alternate timeline in which [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") turned into said transformation and was trapped motionless for nearly a year.

Etymology
---------


Alien X's name is made up of "[alien](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alien "wikipedia:Alien")", a reference to him being an extraterrestrial life form, as well as a transformation, and "X", the roman numeral for 10, which refers to both his mysteriousness and the fact that he is Ben's tenth alien in **Alien Force**. "X" is also used as an unknown variable, which references how Alien X's appearance and powers were kept secret until his debut.



Trivia
------


* Ben, Azmuth, and [Professor Paradox](/wiki/Professor_Paradox "Professor Paradox") all acknowledge that Alien X is the most powerful being in the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix "Omnitrix").[[3]](#cite_note-TFoC-4)[[note 6]](#cite_note-30)
	+ However, Ben has conceded that transforming into Alien X is not worth the cost, as the risk is simply too great for him to use the transformation.[[1]](#cite_note-X.3DB.2B2-2)[[merch 1]](#cite_note-31) Simply put, Alien X is too much of a pain for Ben to use unless there is no other option.[[DJW 12]](#cite_note-32)
* Alien X's starred body is not simply "aesthetic". Like all [Celestialsapiens](/wiki/Celestialsapien_(Classic) "Celestialsapien (Classic)"), Alien X contains his own "debate dimension", which exists inside his head.[[DM 2]](#cite_note-33) But rather than being a real place, it is a visual interpretation of what is happening mentally.[[DJW 13]](#cite_note-34)
	+ This explains how Paradox was able to travel inside Alien X and speak to Ben whilst he was arguing with Serena and Bellicus before reversing the transformation.[[3]](#cite_note-TFoC-4)
* Much like his birthplace, Alien X exists outside the universe (i.e. time and space). Beings existing outside time and space are simultaneously everywhere and nowhere at the same time.[[DR 2]](#cite_note-tweet1-35)
	+ This also implies that since Ben Prime and [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)") share the same Celestialsapien DNA in their Omnitrices, the Alien X transformation used by them is the same Alien X, not just genetically, but literally.[[DR 2]](#cite_note-tweet1-35)

The only way to access Alien X was to use a pair of [keys](/wiki/Ultimatrix_Security_Keys "Ultimatrix Security Keys") in Gwen and Kevin's possession. This is not the case for the new [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)"), in which Alien X is accessible without any keys, as the threat of Albedo turning into Alien X has been neutralized.[[note 7]](#cite_note-38)
* In *[Universe vs. Tennyson](/wiki/Universe_vs._Tennyson "Universe vs. Tennyson")*, Ben offers to let the two personalities argue between themselves for eternity if they give him full control over Alien X. Serena agrees, followed by an initially reluctant Bellicus, giving Ben full control over Alien X. This is seen again in *[Weapon XI: Part 1](/wiki/Weapon_XI:_Part_1 "Weapon XI: Part 1")*.

Ben tries to convince him that Alien X is real, but Rook doesn't believe him and asks Ben to transform and show him, which Ben refused.
	+ This conversation happens again in *[So Long, and Thanks for All the Smoothies](/wiki/So_Long,_and_Thanks_for_All_the_Smoothies "So Long, and Thanks for All the Smoothies")*, and by the end of the episode, Rook sees Alien X for real, but after the universe is restored by Ben, his memory of Alien X no longer remains and he disbelieves Ben when the latter says that he used Alien X to recreate the Universe.
	+ Finally, on seeing Celestialsapiens and Alien X in *Universe vs. Tennyson*, Rook believes Ben.
* In *[Andreas' Fault](/wiki/Andreas%27_Fault "Andreas' Fault")*, a fan of Ben refers to the Alien X smoothie as a disappointment as well as saying it tastes like "filler". This is a reference to Alien X's constant standing in the series.
* In many promotional images and the openings to **Alien Force** and **Ultimate Alien**, Alien X is posing dramatically. This ironic, considering that he is frozen in a standing-up position for most of the time in the series proper.
* Alien X had concept art drawn by Eric Canete,[[EC 1]](#cite_note-40) whereas his final design was created by Glenn Wong, with color styling by Chris Hooten.[[GW 1]](#cite_note-41)
* The original plan for Alien X had him with two faces talking to represent two personalities in one body. However, because of animation challenges and the fact that Alien X has one male and one female personality, which was not reflected by the faces, the idea was scrapped.[[GM 1]](#cite_note-42)
* Alien X was the first alien featured in **Alien Force** and **Ultimate Alien** to not be voiced by [Dee Bradley Baker](/wiki/Dee_Bradley_Baker "Dee Bradley Baker"); the second being [Rath](/wiki/Rath_(Classic) "Rath (Classic)").
* During the entirety of Season 1 of **Alien Force**, the opening of the show only displayed a silhouette of Alien X because he was meant to be a surprise alien. However, part of his face could be seen on the bottom right of the screen. From Season 2 onwards, the intro began showing Alien X's full image.
* Alien X is a Nano and a former NPC in the game **[FusionFall](/wiki/FusionFall "FusionFall")**.
* In **Project Exonaut**, Alien X is a level 10 suit.
* Alien X's celestial abilities and appearance are similar to the hero [Captain Universe](https://marvel.fandom.com/wiki/Captain_Universe "w:c:marvel:Captain Universe") from [Marvel Comics](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marvel_Comics "wikipedia:Marvel Comics").
