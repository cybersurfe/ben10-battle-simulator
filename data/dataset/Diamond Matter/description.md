**Diamond Matter** is the combination of [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)") and [Grey Matter](/wiki/Grey_Matter_(Classic) "Grey Matter (Classic)")'s DNA. He cannot be accessed unless the faceplate of the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") is broken or by using the [Biomnitrix](/wiki/Biomnitrix "Biomnitrix").[[note 1]](#cite_note-fusions-1)

Appearance
----------


Diamond Matter is slightly bigger than Grey Matter. He has Diamondhead's crystalline skin, as well as the spikes on his back and head, and Grey Matter's eyes and mouth.


Diamond Matter wore a uniform that was white with black cuffs on the left and black with white cuffs on the right, and wore the Omnitrix symbol on his chest.



Powers and Abilities
--------------------


* ![]()Crystal Shard Projectiles/Enhanced Intelligence
* ![]()Metamorphic Arms
* ![]()Shard Extension
* ![]()Enhanced Durability
1/4
Being part-Petrosapien, Diamond Matter can alter the shape of his limbs and fire diamond shard projectiles much like Diamondhead.


Being part-Galvan, Diamond Matter's intelligence is at the same level of Grey Matter, allowing him to fire his diamond shard projectiles with excellent precision.



Weaknesses
----------


Unlike Diamondhead, Diamond Matter's enhanced strength is reduced to a level similar to Grey Matter because of his size.



History
-------


### Ben 10


* Diamond Matter first appeared in *[Dr. Animo and the Mutant Ray](/wiki/Dr._Animo_and_the_Mutant_Ray "Dr. Animo and the Mutant Ray")*, where he battled [Heatbat](/wiki/Heatbat "Heatbat") and briefly got eaten, then thrown up by an alligator. He then sliced his way through some swamp trees to clear the [gang's](/wiki/Ben%27s_Team/OS "Ben's Team/OS") airboat's path until he reverted.
