Appearance
----------


Atomix has a humanoid robotic appearance. He has a green and white body with large cylinders on his arms near his wrists, that are full of green energy with dark green bubble-like spheres, which constantly move. He has the same energy on his Omnitrix-shaped chest. His head is a pointed, armored helmet. He is mouthless.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-2)


Atomix wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his green and white belt.



Personality
-----------


Atomix acts with a kind of "righteous" personality and speaks to others with proper titles, such as referring to [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") as "grandfather" and [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") as "cousin".[[1]](#cite_note-FaFBM-1)


Atomix names his special attacks, much like a character from an anime show.



### Nega Ben as Atomix


[Nega](/wiki/Nega_Ben "Nega Ben") Atomix would retain Nega Ben's dour personality.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-3)

As his name implies, Atomix can create and manipulate atomic or nuclear energy using his hands, which can do a massive amount of damage to its surroundings. Fundamentally, he is a walking nuclear reactor.


The way Atomix creates his nuclear blasts is by chanting "HAA-MEE-NA HAA-MEE-NA HAA-MEE-NA-HA!"[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-4) It is not crucial for him to chant every attack, but doing so will focus an attack's power and cause substantially more damage.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-5)


Atomix is able to activate his cylinders on his arms and his Omnitrix-shaped chest to melt nearby objects, such as an [Evolved Arachnichimp](/wiki/Evolved_Arachnichimp "Evolved Arachnichimp")'s webs.[[1]](#cite_note-FaFBM-1)


Atomix is capable of flight and, as such, he can perform a flying ramming attack called the Fissile Whistle.[[1]](#cite_note-FaFBM-1)


Atomix is strong enough to take out an [Evolved Vaxasaurian](/wiki/Evolved_Vaxasaurian "Evolved Vaxasaurian") with a single punch, and kick an object up nearly past a planet's atmosphere.[[1]](#cite_note-FaFBM-1)


Atomix can generate a massive blast of energy dubbed "Nuclear Winner". This move can easily knock out enemies, as well as devastate the surrounding area.[[1]](#cite_note-FaFBM-1)


Atomix's hard body gives him substantial protection against physical attacks.[[2]](#cite_note-MA-6)


Atomix can generate an orb of nuclear energy called a Fusion Cuisine, which can generate a very bright light capable of burning up [Ectonurites](/wiki/Ectonurite_(Classic) "Ectonurite (Classic)") and greatly harming [Vladats](/wiki/Vladat "Vladat"). He can also make the attack continue even after he reverts to [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)").[[3]](#cite_note-7) With enough work, Atomix can generate a Fusion Cuisine orb the size of the sun.[[DR 1]](#cite_note-8)


Atomix can glow in the dark.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-9)


Although he has never trained himself to do so, Atomix can reproduce gamma radiation.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-10)

History
-------


### Omniverse


* Atomix first appeared in *[For a Few Brains More](/wiki/For_a_Few_Brains_More "For a Few Brains More")*, where he was accidentally unlocked and named by [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)"), and defeated [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") [Ultimate Humungousaur](/wiki/Ultimate_Humungousaur "Ultimate Humungousaur") and Negative [Ultimate Spidermonkey](/wiki/Ultimate_Spidermonkey "Ultimate Spidermonkey").
* In *[The Vampire Strikes Back](/wiki/The_Vampire_Strikes_Back "The Vampire Strikes Back")*, Atomix created a light fusion to burn up [Zs'Skayr](/wiki/Zs%27Skayr "Zs'Skayr") and harm [Lord Transyl](/wiki/Lord_Transyl "Lord Transyl"). He then made the 'Fusion Cuisine' stay active just in case Zs'Skayr ever came back to [Z'Castle Zs'Skayr](/wiki/Z%27Castle_Zs%27Skayr "Z'Castle Zs'Skayr").
* In *[Weapon XI: Part 1](/wiki/Weapon_XI:_Part_1 "Weapon XI: Part 1")*, Atomix broke into the [Rooters](/wiki/Rooters "Rooters")' [headquarters](/wiki/Rooter_Headquarters "Rooter Headquarters").
* In *[Malgax Attacks](/wiki/Malgax_Attacks "Malgax Attacks")*, Atomix was defeated by [Mal](/wiki/Malware_Armor "Malware Armor")[gax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Atomix appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.


### Future (Original)


* Atomix was mentioned in *[Ken 10](/wiki/Ken_10 "Ken 10")* as one of [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)")'s aliens. To [Ken](/wiki/Ken_Tennyson "Ken Tennyson")'s disappointment, Atomix was not one of the aliens in the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Replica) "Omnitrix (Replica)") given to him by his father.

Etymology
---------


Atomix's name is a portmanteau of the word "atomic" and "mix", referencing his manipulation of nuclear energy as well as his ability to bring about nuclear fusion.



Trivia
------


* Atomix is Ben's second most powerful alien; the overall most powerful being [Alien X](/wiki/Alien_X_(Classic) "Alien X (Classic)").[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-12)
* Atomix was first mentioned in *[Ken 10](/wiki/Ken_10 "Ken 10")*; at the time, there were never any plans to design him during the original series as characters without screen time would be an undesirable financial expense to the studio.[[TP 1]](#cite_note-13)
	+ Incorporating Atomix into **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")** and giving him a physical form and powers was [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt")'s idea.[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-14) He was first revealed in a sneak peek of Arc 4.
	+ Atomix was designed by Dou Hong,[[DJW 10]](#cite_note-15) based on Derrick's sketches.[[DJW 11]](#cite_note-16)[[TP 2]](#cite_note-17) Had [Tom Perkins](/wiki/Tom_Perkins "Tom Perkins") designed him when his name was first conceptualized, he would have likely looked different.[[TP 3]](#cite_note-18)
* Similar to [Rath](/wiki/Rath_(Classic) "Rath (Classic)"), [Echo Echo](/wiki/Echo_Echo "Echo Echo"), and [Ultimate Echo Echo](/wiki/Ultimate_Echo_Echo "Ultimate Echo Echo") , Atomix gives names to his attacks and moves.
* Atomix's design bears a lot of resemblance to the bio-suit [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)") wore in ***[Secret of the Omnitrix](/wiki/Ben_10:_Secret_of_the_Omnitrix "Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix")***.
* Atomix is one of [Ken](/wiki/Ken_Tennyson "Ken Tennyson")'s favorite aliens. In fact, Ken seemed to be most excited to turn into Atomix than any other alien when he got his [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Replica) "Omnitrix (Replica)").[[4]](#cite_note-19)
* Atomix's powers are largely unexplored.[[DJW 12]](#cite_note-20)
* Atomix is the only alien to have been unlocked by accident in **Omniverse**, and was the only one overall to have been done so by [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)").
	+ Atomix is also one of four aliens to have been unlocked by Azmuth; the others being [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)"), [Murk Upchuck](/wiki/Upchuck "Upchuck"), and [Bullfrag](/wiki/Bullfrag "Bullfrag").
* Even though Atomix has a [Galvan](/wiki/Galvan_(Classic) "Galvan (Classic)") tech symbol on his chest, it does not necessarily mean that [his species](/wiki/Atomix%27s_Species "Atomix's Species") is related to the Galvan in any way, shape or form.[[DJW 13]](#cite_note-21)
* The way Atomix charges his attacks is based on Goku's Kamehameha chant in the **[Dragon Ball](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dragon_Ball "wikipedia:Dragon Ball")** franchise.[[DJW 14]](#cite_note-22)
	+ However, it sounds like the "Homina Homina Homina" exclamation of Ralph Kramden from **[The Honeymooners](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Honeymooners "wikipedia:The Honeymooners")**.
* Atomix's voice is similar to those of Aquaman from **[Batman: The Brave and the Bold](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Batman:_The_Brave_and_the_Bold "wikipedia:Batman: The Brave and the Bold")** and [Captain LaserBeam](https://thrilling-adventure-hour.fandom.com/wiki/The_Adventures_of_Captain_Laserbeam "w:c:thrilling-adventure-hour:The Adventures of Captain Laserbeam") from **[Thrilling Adventure Hour](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thrilling_Adventure_Hour "wikipedia:Thrilling Adventure Hour")**, both of which are traditional superheroes voiced by [John DiMaggio](/wiki/John_DiMaggio "John DiMaggio").
* Atomix cannot transmute matter.[[DJW 15]](#cite_note-23)
* A hypothetical [Ultimate](/wiki/Ultimate_Forms "Ultimate Forms") version of Atomix would be very dangerous, emitting so much radiation that not only must one stand at least 40 feet away from him, but he would also necessitate a [Containment Suit](/wiki/Prypiatosian-B_Containment_Suit "Prypiatosian-B Containment Suit") similar to [NRG](/wiki/NRG "NRG")'s.[[DJW 16]](#cite_note-24)
* There were no plans for Atomix to appear in the [reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)"), not even for Season 5.[[DR 2]](#cite_note-25)
