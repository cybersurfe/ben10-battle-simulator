Appearance
----------


### Ben as Ultimate Spidermonkey


Ultimate Spidermonkey has a large gorilla-like body with purple skin, black fur and three pairs of eyes. He lacks Spidermonkey's tail and extra pair of arms, but they are replaced with two pairs of retractable spider legs that can fold into his waist. Ultimate Spidermonkey wears the evolved [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.



### Albedo as Negative Ultimate Spidermonkey


[Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Ultimate Spidermonkey has red eyes, pale-purple skin, and yellow spider fangs on each side of his mouth. Instead of the purple spider legs, Negative Ultimate Spidermonkey has two extra sets of gorilla arms, and he wears the evolved [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") symbol, which has eight spikes now instead of four, on his stomach.

1/11
Ultimate Spidermonkey is stronger than his devolved form, being strong enough to take down a [Tetramand](/wiki/Tetramand_(Classic) "Tetramand (Classic)").[[1]](#cite_note-TUH-1)


By splitting his lower jaw into two halves, Ultimate Spidermonkey can shoot webs from his mouth, much more than Spidermonkey can shoot from his tail. His webs are strong enough to keep a [Vaxasaurian](/wiki/Vaxasaurian_(Classic) "Vaxasaurian (Classic)")[[2]](#cite_note-2) and an [Orishan](/wiki/Orishan "Orishan")[[3]](#cite_note-3) from escaping. They can also insulate and cancel out a [Gimlinopithecus](/wiki/Gimlinopithecus "Gimlinopithecus")' electrical abilities.[[1]](#cite_note-TUH-1)[[4]](#cite_note-4)


Ultimate Spidermonkey's webbing appears to be somewhat unlimited, as he can create enough webbing to make a soft cushion to land on if falling.[[5]](#cite_note-TMoAV-5)


Ultimate Spidermonkey is extremely durable, being able to survive an incredibly high fall after being thrown into the air by [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)"), although he was badly stunned as a result.[[6]](#cite_note-6)


Ultimate Spidermonkey is highly agile, as he can jump extremely high[[7]](#cite_note-TP-7) and can run very fast despite his large stature.


In **Ultimate Alien**, Ultimate Spidermonkey's spider legs could pull things and break them almost like he was using his hands. He could also fold his spider legs into his hips, and attack with the tips of his spider legs as they were sharp enough to pierce a concrete wall.[[7]](#cite_note-TP-7)


In **Omniverse**, Ultimate Spidermonkey is capable of climbing walls, much like his devolved form.[[8]](#cite_note-8)


Ultimate Spidermonkey can survive in space for a short time, but he still needs to breathe. Also, considering that he once fell through space and down to earth, Ultimate Spidermonkey may also be immune to radiation, as he was completely unharmed after falling through the atmosphere.[[5]](#cite_note-TMoAV-5)

* ![]()Meltable Webs

1/4
Despite his enhanced durability, Ultimate Spidermonkey can be harmed and discomforted by certain weapons, such as a [Forever Knight](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights") energy sword, which was able to leave a scar on his chest.[[7]](#cite_note-TP-7)


Ultimate Spidermonkey can be put to sleep by [Argit](/wiki/Argit "Argit")'s quills.[[1]](#cite_note-TUH-1)


Despite his enhanced strength, Ultimate Spidermonkey is physically weaker than [Atomix](/wiki/Atomix "Atomix"), as he was overpowered by the latter's Nuclear Winner.[[9]](#cite_note-FaFBM-9)


Ultimate Spidermonkey's webbing is ineffective against a species capable of becoming intangible, such as an [Ectonurite](/wiki/Ectonurite "Ectonurite").[[10]](#cite_note-TUS-10)


Ultimate Spidermonkey's webs can be torn apart by species with as much strength as an [Anodite](/wiki/Anodite "Anodite")[[10]](#cite_note-TUS-10) or melted by Atomix's nuclear energy.[[9]](#cite_note-FaFBM-9)


Ultimate Spidermonkey is sensitive to high-pitched noises, such as those of an [Evolved Sonorosian](/wiki/Evolved_Sonorosian "Evolved Sonorosian")'s [sonic disks](/wiki/Sonic_Disk "Sonic Disk"), as demonstrated with his [sentient counterpart](/wiki/Sentient_Ultimate_Spidermonkey "Sentient Ultimate Spidermonkey").[[10]](#cite_note-TUS-10)



History
-------


### Ultimate Alien


* Ultimate Spidermonkey first appeared in *[Fame](/wiki/Fame "Fame")*, where he defeated [Bivalvan](/wiki/Bivalvan "Bivalvan").
* In *[Reflected Glory](/wiki/Reflected_Glory "Reflected Glory")*, Ultimate Spidermonkey was defeated by [Psyphon](/wiki/Psyphon "Psyphon") only to be saved by [Cash](/wiki/Cash_Murray_(Classic) "Cash Murray (Classic)") and [J.T.](/wiki/J.T._(Classic) "J.T. (Classic)")
* In *[The Transmogrification of Eunice](/wiki/The_Transmogrification_of_Eunice "The Transmogrification of Eunice")*, Ultimate Spidermonkey was defeated by [Sunder](/wiki/Sunder "Sunder").
* In *[The Purge](/wiki/The_Purge "The Purge")*, Ultimate Spidermonkey defeated [Driscoll](/wiki/Driscoll "Driscoll").
* In *[Simian Says](/wiki/Simian_Says "Simian Says")*, Ultimate Spidermonkey defeated [Mizaru](/wiki/Mizaru "Mizaru").
* In *[Double or Nothing](/wiki/Double_or_Nothing "Double or Nothing")*, Ultimate Spidermonkey defeated [Hugh](/wiki/Hugh "Hugh"). Later, Ultimate Spidermonkey readied to battle [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo"), but reverted to Ben when going through a force-field.
* In *[The Mother of All Vreedles](/wiki/The_Mother_of_All_Vreedles "The Mother of All Vreedles")*, Ultimate Spidermonkey failed to defeat the [Vreedle Brothers](/wiki/Vreedle_Brothers "Vreedle Brothers"), [Ma Vreedle](/wiki/Ma_Vreedle "Ma Vreedle"), and [Pretty Boy Vreedle](/wiki/Pretty_Boy_Vreedle "Pretty Boy Vreedle") in space due to the lack of oxygen and fell back to [Earth](/wiki/Earth "Earth").
* In *[Night of the Living Nightmare](/wiki/Night_of_the_Living_Nightmare "Night of the Living Nightmare")*, Ultimate Spidermonkey was defeated by [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") in a dream.


### Omniverse


* In *[The Ultimate Heist](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Heist "The Ultimate Heist")*, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Ultimate Spidermonkey defeated [Shocksquatch](/wiki/Shocksquatch "Shocksquatch") and battled [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)") before being put to sleep by [Argit](/wiki/Argit "Argit")'s quills.
* In *[A Fistful of Brains](/wiki/A_Fistful_of_Brains "A Fistful of Brains")*, Negative Ultimate Spidermonkey defeated Shocksquatch.
* In *[For a Few Brains More](/wiki/For_a_Few_Brains_More "For a Few Brains More")*, Negative Ultimate Spidermonkey was defeated by [Atomix](/wiki/Atomix "Atomix").
* In *[And Then There Were None](/wiki/And_Then_There_Were_None "And Then There Were None")*, Negative Ultimate Spidermonkey chased after [No Watch Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(No_Watch_Timeline) "Ben Tennyson (No Watch Timeline)") only to be ambushed by [Gwen 10](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Gweniverse) "Gwen Tennyson (Gweniverse)") as [Wildvine](/wiki/Wildvine_(Classic) "Wildvine (Classic)").
* In *[Malgax Attacks](/wiki/Malgax_Attacks "Malgax Attacks")*, Negative Ultimate Spidermonkey defeated three [Galvan Soldiers](/wiki/Galvan_Soldiers "Galvan Soldiers") and managed to break into [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)")'s laboratory.


### Comics


* In *[Going Viral](/wiki/Going_Viral "Going Viral")*, Ultimate Spidermonkey defeated Dragon Monster.
* In *[Breaking News](/wiki/Breaking_News "Breaking News")*, Ultimate Spidermonkey defeated [DNAliens](/wiki/DNAliens "DNAliens").
* In *[Remote Control](/wiki/Remote_Control "Remote Control")*, Ultimate Spidermonkey avoided [Driscoll](/wiki/Driscoll "Driscoll")'s lasers.
* In *[Christmas Clown](/wiki/Christmas_Clown "Christmas Clown")*, Ultimate Spidermonkey defeated [Zombozo](/wiki/Zombozo_(Classic) "Zombozo (Classic)") animatronic.
* In *[Hero Times Two](/wiki/Hero_Times_Two "Hero Times Two")*, Ultimate Spidermonkey along with [Rex Salazar](/wiki/Rex_Salazar "Rex Salazar") pervented [The Pack](/wiki/The_Pack "The Pack") from getting Promethium.

### [Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction](/wiki/Ben_10_Ultimate_Alien:_Cosmic_Destruction "Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction")


 [![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/e/e3/Ultimate_Spidermonkey_Cosmic_Destruction.png/revision/latest?cb=20200902025514) [![](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/e/e3/Ultimate_Spidermonkey_Cosmic_Destruction.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/218?cb=20200902025514)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/e/e3/Ultimate_Spidermonkey_Cosmic_Destruction.png/revision/latest?cb=20200902025514)   Ultimate Spidermonkey in Cosmic Destruction

  
Ultimate Spidermonkey is a playable alien in the game.


Ultimate Spidermonkey is the most used ultimate form in the game, as he is used twice while the rest of the other [Ultimate Forms](/wiki/Ultimate_Forms "Ultimate Forms") are only used once.


Ultimate Spidermonkey can pound the ground to make small shockwaves and spin his spider legs around to damage enemies around him.

Trivia
------


* Ultimate Spidermonkey was the first Ultimate form to be used in **Ultimate Alien**.
* Ultimate Spidermonkey's **Ultimate Alien** appearance was designed by Glenn Wong, with color styling by Chris Hooten.[[GW 1]](#cite_note-11)
* In *[Reflected Glory](/wiki/Reflected_Glory "Reflected Glory")*, Ultimate Spidermonkey's jaw kept splitting whenever he spoke; this did not happen in any of his other appearances.
* Ultimate Spidermonkey was the [Alien of the Month](/wiki/Alien_of_the_Month "Alien of the Month") in February 2011.
* Ultimate Spidermonkey is the only Ultimate Form to have a complete redesign in **Omniverse**.
* When Ultimate Spidermonkey shoots webs from his mouth in **Omniverse**, his mouth bears resemblance to the [Predators](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Predator_(fictional_species) "wikipedia:Predator (fictional species)") from the franchise of the same name.
* Ultimate Spidermonkey cannot grow spider legs out from the spikes on his shoulders.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-12)
* If Ben had access to Ultimate Spidermonkey in **Omniverse**, he would still have six arms similar to Negative Ultimate Spidermonkey.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-13)
