Appearance
----------


Clockwork is a robot-like alien with skin that is made of golden metal, with black stripes and a transparent piece of green glass on his chest, revealing the gears inside of him.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, the piece of glass on his chest was circular.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, 16-year-old Clockwork has a larger body and holes on the key on his somewhat restructured head. The piece of glass on his chest is now in the shape of a hexagon, and there is a black stripe on his back that splits into two stripes with a keyhole between them.


11-year-old Clockwork has a thinner and rounder head, a white and black shirt, a smiley face, and a circular piece of glass similar to his original appearance.


Clockwork wears the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)")/[Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)")/[original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.

Clockwork can fire green time rays from his hands or chest, which have various effects like sending people back in time,[[3]](#cite_note-B10KR-3) aging an object/person to dust,[[1]](#cite_note-CaFS-1) restoring individuals who have been erased from time, sending them into a [different timeline](/wiki/Alternate_Timelines "Alternate Timelines"),[[2]](#cite_note-ATTWB-2) and reverting them to a previous state while also immobilizing them.[[4]](#cite_note-IaMMMBW1-4)


Clockwork can slow down time around himself, making it look like to others that Clockwork is moving at very high speeds.[[5]](#cite_note-BIM-5)


By rotating the key on his head, Clockwork can create hologram-like projections of what happened in the past.[[6]](#cite_note-6)[[7]](#cite_note-7)


Clockwork has a degree of enhanced strength, as he was able to throw a pipe so hard it became heavily embedded into a wall.[[5]](#cite_note-BIM-5)


Clockwork is capable of reversing the effects of a [Chronosapien Time Bomb](/wiki/Chronosapien_Time_Bomb "Chronosapien Time Bomb") using his time powers.[[2]](#cite_note-ATTWB-2)


Because he is robotic (rather than biological) in nature, Clockwork can survive in the vacuum of space, as demonstrated by [Maltruant](/wiki/Maltruant "Maltruant").[[8]](#cite_note-TEoaE-8)


Clockwork can rotate his head 360° degrees.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-9)


Clockwork possesses chronopathy, which grants him a fine-tuned sense of precise time and determines the exact time an event has taken or will take place and for how long, as demonstrated by Maltruant.[[8]](#cite_note-TEoaE-8)


Because he is a technological being, it is possible for a [Galvanic Mechamorph](/wiki/Galvanic_Mechamorph_(Classic) "Galvanic Mechamorph (Classic)") to merge with Clockwork, thus making his time rays stronger and increasing his speed and stamina to superhuman levels. While in this state, Clockwork can jump extremely high, morph his hands into drills, fire rockets out of shoulder-mounted launchers, and sense another [Chronosapien](/wiki/Chronosapien "Chronosapien")'s head key.[[4]](#cite_note-IaMMMBW1-4)


Although he has never trained himself to do so, Clockwork can travel through time.[[9]](#cite_note-10)[[3]](#cite_note-B10KR-3)


Clockwork can sense the use of a fellow Chronosapien's powers and undo the resulting effects if needed.[[2]](#cite_note-ATTWB-2)


With enough power and skill, Clockwork can perform a temporal consciousness conversion in which a person's consciousness is swapped with that of a younger or older version of the target in question,[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-11) much like what [Eon](/wiki/Eon "Eon") did to [Ben Prime](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s 16 and 11-year-old selves.[[10]](#cite_note-12)



Weaknesses
----------


* ![]()Below Average Stamina
* ![]()Vulnerable Head Key
* ![]()Overpowered Time Rays
* ![]()Durability Limit
1/4
Clockwork is not very durable, as a laser blast from [Carl Nesmith](/wiki/Carl_Nesmith "Carl Nesmith")'s glove damaged Clockwork's right shoulder and caused him to shut down. When Clockwork reverted to [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)") form in this state, [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s right arm was broken.[[1]](#cite_note-CaFS-1)


If something hits Clockwork's head key, he can be badly stunned; this shows that Clockwork is not very agile, as he admits that he "is not good for dodging."[[5]](#cite_note-BIM-5)


Because of his large body and short legs, Clockwork cannot run very fast and is low on stamina, meaning he tires out quickly.[[11]](#cite_note-13)


Clockwork's time rays are ineffective against anything diamond-related such as [Petrosapiens](/wiki/Petrosapien_(Classic) "Petrosapien (Classic)"), due to them being incapable of aging.[[12]](#cite_note-14)[[3]](#cite_note-B10KR-3) Similarly, the time rays are ineffective on gumballs because time has no effect on them.[[13]](#cite_note-15)[[14]](#cite_note-16)


The use of Clockwork's time powers creates a ripple effect in time that can be sensed or reversed by another Chronosapien.


Clockwork's time powers are completely useless in a place where there is no time at all, such as the beginning of the [universe](/wiki/Universe "Universe"), as demonstrated with [Maltruant](/wiki/Maltruant "Maltruant").[[15]](#cite_note-AND-17)


There is a limit to how strong Clockwork is, as he is unable to penetrate through the [Contumelia](/wiki/Contumelia "Contumelia")'s machine's extra-dimensional field, as also demonstrated by Maltruant.[[15]](#cite_note-AND-17)


Unlike Maltruant, Clockwork is unable to travel through dimensions, even when he's upgraded by a Galvanic Mechamorph.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-18)[[16]](#cite_note-IaMMMBW2-19)


Because he is a mechanical-based being, Clockwork can be magnetized by a [Biot-savartian](/wiki/Biot-savartian "Biot-savartian").[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-20)


Clockwork can be temporarily disabled if he is exposed to a large electromagnetic pulse, such as that from a solar pulse.[[17]](#cite_note-21)[[8]](#cite_note-TEoaE-8)


Clockwork can be affected by a [Nemuina](/wiki/Nemuina "Nemuina")'s sleeping dust.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-22)


Clockwork is vulnerable to having his body dismantled by a [Planchaküle](/wiki/Planchak%C3%BCle "Planchaküle").[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-23) Despite that, he can still function if he loses some of his parts.[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-24)



History
-------


### 5 Years Prior to Omniverse


* In *[Ben Again](/wiki/Ben_Again "Ben Again")*, 16-year-old [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") as 11-year-old Clockwork helped fix the time stream and defeat [Eon](/wiki/Eon "Eon").


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, Clockwork was unlocked by [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Ultimate_Alien) "Ben 10,000 (Ultimate Alien)").
* Clockwork was first used by Ben offscreen prior to *[Inspector #13](/wiki/Inspector_13_(Episode) "Inspector 13 (Episode)")*.[[GT 1]](#cite_note-25)
* In *[Inspector #13](/wiki/Inspector_13_(Episode) "Inspector 13 (Episode)")*, [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") turned into Clockwork during a car ride to the [Techadon Factory](/wiki/Techadon_Factory "Techadon Factory").
* In *[Catch a Falling Star](/wiki/Catch_a_Falling_Star "Catch a Falling Star")*, Clockwork disarmed [Carl Nesmith](/wiki/Carl_Nesmith "Carl Nesmith"), but was caught off guard and defeated by [Jennifer Nocturne](/wiki/Jennifer_Nocturne "Jennifer Nocturne").
* In *[The Eggman Cometh](/wiki/The_Eggman_Cometh "The Eggman Cometh")*, Clockwork found out what happened in [Natalie](/wiki/Natalie_Tennyson "Natalie Tennyson")'s kitchen and realized [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)") was up to no good again.
* In *[The Beginning of the End](/wiki/The_Beginning_of_the_End "The Beginning of the End")*, Clockwork found out where the [Flame Keepers' Circle](/wiki/Flame_Keepers%27_Circle "Flame Keepers' Circle") had gone.


### Omniverse


* In *[A Jolt from the Past](/wiki/A_Jolt_from_the_Past "A Jolt from the Past")*, Clockwork chased some [Megawhatts](/wiki/Nosedeenian_(Classic) "Nosedeenian (Classic)") to [Fistrick](/wiki/Fistrick "Fistrick")'s lair until he grew too tired.
* In *[Many Happy Returns](/wiki/Many_Happy_Returns "Many Happy Returns")*, Clockwork was defeated by [Princess Looma](/wiki/Looma_Red_Wind "Looma Red Wind").
* In *[Ben Again](/wiki/Ben_Again "Ben Again")*, 11-year-old [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") as 16-year-old Clockwork helped fix the time stream and defeat [Eon](/wiki/Eon "Eon").
* In *[Bengeance is Mine](/wiki/Bengeance_is_Mine "Bengeance is Mine")*, Clockwork defeated a [Squid Monster](/wiki/Squid_Monsters "Squid Monsters") by slowing down time.
* In *[And Then There Was Ben](/wiki/And_Then_There_Was_Ben "And Then There Was Ben")*, [No Watch Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(No_Watch_Timeline) "Ben Tennyson (No Watch Timeline)") as Clockwork learned about his abilities from [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)"). Later, No Watch Ben as Clockwork reversed the effects of the [Chronosapien Time Bomb](/wiki/Chronosapien_Time_Bomb "Chronosapien Time Bomb") and sent [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") [Ultimate Echo Echo](/wiki/Ultimate_Echo_Echo "Ultimate Echo Echo"), [Mad Ben](/wiki/Mad_Ben "Mad Ben"), [Bad](/wiki/Bad_Ben "Bad Ben") [Buzzshock](/wiki/Buzzshock_(Classic) "Buzzshock (Classic)"), [Benzarro](/wiki/Benzarro "Benzarro")'s [Frankenstrike](/wiki/Frankenstrike "Frankenstrike"), [Nega Ben](/wiki/Nega_Ben "Nega Ben"), and [Eon](/wiki/Eon "Eon") back to their respective timelines.
* In *[It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 1](/wiki/It%27s_a_Mad,_Mad,_Mad_Ben_World:_Part_1 "It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 1")*, Clockwork defeated [Mad](/wiki/Mad_Ben "Mad Ben") [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)") before having his powers enhanced by [Techno-Bubble](/wiki/Techno-Bubble "Techno-Bubble"). He then defeated [Exo-Skull](/wiki/Exo-Skull "Exo-Skull") and [Subdora](/wiki/Subdora "Subdora"), but was defeated himself by [Maltruant](/wiki/Maltruant "Maltruant").
* In *[It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 2](/wiki/It%27s_a_Mad,_Mad,_Mad_Ben_World:_Part_2 "It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 2")*, Clockwork fused with Techno-Bubble again in a failed attempt to leave Mad Ben's [home dimension](/wiki/Mad_Ben%27s_Dimension "Mad Ben's Dimension") and return to their [respective](/wiki/Classic_Timeline "Classic Timeline") [timelines](/wiki/Dimension_23 "Dimension 23").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Clockwork appeared for a short time as [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

Etymology
---------


The term "clockwork" refers to a mechanism with a spring and toothed gearwheels, used to drive a mechanical clock and other types of devices; this refers to Clockwork being a mechanical alien with gears and time-based powers.



Trivia
------


 [![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/9/93/Haywire_Clockwork_No_Box.png/revision/latest?cb=20210305104042) [![](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/9/93/Haywire_Clockwork_No_Box.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/218?cb=20210305104042)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/9/93/Haywire_Clockwork_No_Box.png/revision/latest?cb=20210305104042)   The Haywire Clockwork toy

  
* Clockwork's appearance when he is fused with [Techno-Bubble](/wiki/Techno-Bubble "Techno-Bubble") bears a strong resemblance to the [Haywire](/wiki/Toy_Line/4%22_Collection/Haywire "Toy Line/4\" Collection/Haywire") Clockwork action figure, which has [Eatle](/wiki/Eatle "Eatle")'s **Ultimate Alien** color scheme.
* Clockwork makes gear sounds when he moves, which is similar to [Armodrillo](/wiki/Armodrillo "Armodrillo") when he moves his arms.
	+ In **Omniverse**, Clockwork ticks like a clock for the entirety of his transformation sequence, and there is a sound similar to the tolling of [Big Ben](/wiki/Big_Ben "Big Ben") that sometimes appears at the end.
	+ The tolling clock sound also occurs when Clockwork's time rays strike their target or when he is struck on his head key.
* Clockwork speaks with a thick German accent because [Germany](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany "wikipedia:Germany") is known for inventing the watch, a portable timepiece that could tell time no matter wherever someone was in the world. Whenever Clockwork spoke in **Omniverse**, the reverb in his voice would make a cuckoo clock sound.
* [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") thinks that 11-year-old Ben kept Clockwork after the events of *[Ben Again](/wiki/Ben_Again "Ben Again")*, but [Matt Youngberg](/wiki/Matt_Youngberg "Matt Youngberg") always said he did not.[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-26)
	+ Concept art of 12-year old [Kevin's](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") [mutated form](/wiki/Kevin%27s_Omniverse_Flashback_Mutation "Kevin's Omniverse Flashback Mutation") featured Clockwork's key on the sides of his head. However, this was removed in the final version.[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-27)[[DJW 10]](#cite_note-28)
* Clockwork is the only alien used by 11-year-old Ben whose [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol has a gold outer casing, rather than a silver one like his other aliens.
* Unlike [Buzzshock](/wiki/Buzzshock_(Classic) "Buzzshock (Classic)"), [Spitter](/wiki/Spitter "Spitter"), and [Arctiguana](/wiki/Arctiguana "Arctiguana"), Clockwork was first revealed by a [different Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Ultimate_Alien) "Ben 10,000 (Ultimate Alien)") from an alternate timeline rather than the [original one](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)").
	+ Regardless, all four of these aliens would later be unlocked by present-day Ben.
* There were no plans to feature Clockwork in the [reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)"), a fact that upsets [Duncan Rouleau](/wiki/Duncan_Rouleau "Duncan Rouleau").[[DR 1]](#cite_note-29)
