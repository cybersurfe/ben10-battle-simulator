### Ben as Ripjaws


Ripjaws shares a number of traits with [Earth](/wiki/Earth_(Classic) "Earth (Classic)")'s aquatic life, such as an [alligator](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alligator "wikipedia:Alligator"), an [eel](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eel "wikipedia:Eel"), an [anglerfish](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anglerfish "wikipedia:Anglerfish"), and a [shark](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shark "wikipedia:Shark"). He can change between a fishtail with triangular lines or legs at will and has a phosphorescent light on his head.


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Ripjaws' body was mostly grey and he had four-fingered hands. He wore the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol on a black patch over his left pectoral.


In *[Back with a Vengeance](/wiki/Back_with_a_Vengeance "Back with a Vengeance")*, Ripjaws wore a yellow raincoat.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Ripjaws had a catfish-like face, green eyes with black pupils, chest plates similar to [Humungosaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)"), three-fingered hands, a green lure, and green lips. His tail was colored grey like his skin and his fins were a darker shade of green than in the original series. He wore the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In the **Ultimate Alien** intro, Ripjaws looked almost the same as in the series proper except his tail was black, reflecting his appearance in the original series. His lips were also colored black and his lure was white.


In *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, Ripjaws looked mostly the same as in **Ultimate Alien** except one of his eyes was pink due to a coloring error.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, 16-year-old Ripjaws mostly resembles his original appearance, except that his eyes are green, he now has arm and neck braces, his antenna light is green, he has a big fin on his back, the fins on his arms are longer, he is a little more hunched back, his claws are black, and his tail is bigger and drags behind him. He now has a belt in place of the strap that holds the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on it.


11-year-old Ripjaws appears almost exactly the same as he did in the original series, except his eyes and the Omnitrix symbol on his left upper torso are recolored green.

Ripjaws' main ability is to breathe underwater and swim at high speeds with amazing agility. He has great endurance and can withstand strong water pressure, meaning he possesses the nature of abyssal aquatic life, hence his blank eyes and the anglerfish light in his forehead.


Ripjaws displays the distinctive feature of turning his legs into a fish's tail and vice-versa, giving him a merman-like appearance. While outside the water environment, he displays a loincloth with a similar pattern to his tail (implying the loincloth wraps around his legs, fusing with it and becoming the tail). While his legs are formed into a tail, Ripjaws can swim very well at great speeds.


Ripjaws has enhanced strength, being able to easily punch a hole in a dock,[[3]](#cite_note-TK-4)[[4]](#cite_note-LL-5) tear a door of the [Perplexahedron](/wiki/Perplexahedron "Perplexahedron") open,[[5]](#cite_note-6) lasso an anchor,[[6]](#cite_note-GF-7) and hold his own against [Vulkanus](/wiki/Vulkanus "Vulkanus") while underwater.[[7]](#cite_note-TBoMB-8) He is also capable of lifting more than his own weight.


Ripjaws' large jaws and sharp teeth are his most deadly weapon, being able to shred even metal from submarines. He also possesses sharp claws that can damage metal[[8]](#cite_note-9) and mountains.[[9]](#cite_note-SC-10)


Ripjaws is rather durable, as shown when he was able to withstand being thrown against [Sheelane](/wiki/Sheelane "Sheelane")'s ship.[[7]](#cite_note-TBoMB-8)


Ripjaws can unhinge his jaws,[[DM 1]](#cite_note-11) allowing him to fit a mass supply of objects in his mouth.[[3]](#cite_note-TK-4)[[6]](#cite_note-GF-7)


Much like an ordinary anglerfish, Ripjaws' lure can serve as a light in dark areas.[[10]](#cite_note-12) His eyes also glow.[[4]](#cite_note-LL-5)

At age 10, Ripjaws' greatest weakness was his inability to stay out of water for too long, requiring it to keep him from dehydrating. However, he has started to rely less on water as he got older, as he had no trouble surviving outside of water at age 16.


Exposure to extreme heat will cause Ripjaws to dehydrate quickly.[[4]](#cite_note-LL-5)


Ripjaws is vulnerable to electricity.[[3]](#cite_note-TK-4)


Ripjaws can be immobilized by ice, such as the ice breath of an [Evolved Necrofriggian](/wiki/Evolved_Necrofriggian "Evolved Necrofriggian"), as demonstrated with several [Piscciss Volann](/wiki/Piscciss_Volann_(Classic) "Piscciss Volann (Classic)") guards.[[11]](#cite_note-13)


Ripjaws is vulnerable to magic, as shown when the [Piscciss Volann Prisoner](/wiki/Piscciss_Volann_Prisoner_(Classic) "Piscciss Volann Prisoner (Classic)") was knocked out by one of [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)")'s [mana](/wiki/Mana "Mana") projectiles.[[12]](#cite_note-14)

### Ben 10


* Ripjaws first appeared in *[The Krakken](/wiki/The_Krakken "The Krakken")*, where he helped the [Krakken](/wiki/Krakken "Krakken") defeat [Jonah Melville](/wiki/Jonah_Melville "Jonah Melville") and retrieve her eggs.
* In *[Last Laugh](/wiki/Last_Laugh "Last Laugh")*, Ripjaws defeated some crooks.
* In *[Secrets](/wiki/Secrets "Secrets")*, Ripjaws suffered from dehydration on [Mt. Rushmore](/wiki/Mt._Rushmore "Mt. Rushmore") and was nearly caught by [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") before the malfunctioning [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") turned him into [XLR8](/wiki/XLR8_(Classic) "XLR8 (Classic)").
	+ Also, Ripjaws appeared as one of the many alien templates used for Vilgax's army in an imaginary sequence.
* In *[Truth](/wiki/Truth "Truth")*, Ripjaws defeated a [Havoc Beast](/wiki/Havoc_Beast "Havoc Beast") and met [Phil](/wiki/Phil_Billings_(Classic) "Phil Billings (Classic)").
* In *[The Big Tick](/wiki/The_Big_Tick "The Big Tick")*, Ripjaws failed to defeat the [Great One](/wiki/Great_One "Great One").
* In *[Framed](/wiki/Framed "Framed")*, [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") mentioned Ripjaws was used by [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") to terrorize Chicago, Illinois.
* In *[They Lurk Below](/wiki/They_Lurk_Below "They Lurk Below")*, Ripjaws battled some [Cyber Squids](/wiki/Cyber_Squids "Cyber Squids").
* In *[Back with a Vengeance](/wiki/Back_with_a_Vengeance "Back with a Vengeance")*, Ripjaws saved a drowning man before switching into [Stinkfly](/wiki/Stinkfly_(Classic) "Stinkfly (Classic)") to fly him to safety.
* In *[Divided We Stand](/wiki/Divided_We_Stand "Divided We Stand")*, Ripjaws went surfing and ended up scaring some tourists away from the beach.
* In *[Ben 4 Good Buddy](/wiki/Ben_4_Good_Buddy "Ben 4 Good Buddy")*, Ripjaws fought the [Road Crew](/wiki/Road_Crew "Road Crew") and broke into [Lawrence Wainright](/wiki/Lawrence_Wainright "Lawrence Wainright")'s RV to find water.
* In *[Sleepaway Camper](/wiki/Sleepaway_Camper "Sleepaway Camper")*, Ripjaws slept at the bottom of a lake to avoid [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)")'s snoring.


### Ultimate Alien

Trivia
------


* Ripjaws' voice has changed over the course of his appearances:
	+ In the original series, he spoke with a deep, rasping voice.
	+ In **Omniverse**, he speaks in a higher, garbled voice that is slightly similar to how a person sounds underwater.
* 11-year-old Ripjaws replaces 11-year-old [Grey Matter](/wiki/Grey_Matter_(Classic) "Grey Matter (Classic)") in the opening intro for **Omniverse**'s Arc 5, specifically in the part where the latter originally appeared alongside the 11-year-old versions of [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)"), [Eye Guy](/wiki/Eye_Guy "Eye Guy"), and [Snare-oh](/wiki/Snare-oh "Snare-oh").
	+ 11-year-old Ripjaws was not necessarily planned to appear in **Omniverse** flashbacks.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-16)
* Ripjaws was first envisioned as a purple crocodile-like creature under the name [Razorjaws](/wiki/Ben_10_(Pre-Production)#Razorjaws "Ben 10 (Pre-Production)"). [Dave Johnson](/wiki/Dave_Johnson "Dave Johnson") designed his finalized sketch but had many regrets, noting the weird head shape and numerous teeth in place of lips made it difficult for animators to draw and resulted in him having few appearances.[[DJ 1]](#cite_note-17)
* Ripjaws never received a water respirator similar to the one worn by the [Piscciss Volann Prisoner](/wiki/Piscciss_Volann_Prisoner_(Classic) "Piscciss Volann Prisoner (Classic)") during his appearances in the original series because he was never meant to be used outside of water.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-18)
* Ripjaws is the only alien to appear in **Ultimate Alien** outside of the opening that does not having a speaking role.
