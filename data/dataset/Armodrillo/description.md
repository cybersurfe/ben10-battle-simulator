Appearance
----------


### Ben as Armodrillo


Armodrillo is a yellow ten-foot-tall alien[[2]](#cite_note-4)[[3]](#cite_note-TToE-5) with an armored, robotic-like body. His head is similar to a Greek Corinthian helmet with two ears on it, giving him the appearance of an [armadillo](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Armadillo "wikipedia:Armadillo"). Armodrillo has a gray tail and jackhammer parts on his elbows, wrists and hands, creating the impression that he is a digging robot. Despite his mechanical appearance, all of these aspects are, in fact, biological and natural to his race. He also has blood that is grey and partially translucent.[[4]](#cite_note-AF-6)


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Armodrillo looks similar to his **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")** appearance, but his ears are slightly longer, he has grey bolts with black markings on his shoulders and a metal circle on top of his head. His face has been restructured a little bit, his arm-drills are now much larger, and his feet are now yellow instead of grey while his toes remain grey. His fingers now have a small horizontal line design along with his tail and the black parts of his limbs, his arms, upper arms, and thighs are now more chain-like, and his forearms and shoulders are now much bulkier.


Armodrillo wears the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)")/[Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his chest.

Armodrillo has incredible digging capabilities thanks to his jackhammer arms, and can transform his hands into drills to help himself burrow underground and move through it. Armodrillo can even dig through solid rock.[[3]](#cite_note-TToE-5) His drill hands can also be very useful in battle, as seen when Armodrillo fought [Slamworm](/wiki/Slamworm "Slamworm"),[[5]](#cite_note-Malefactor-7) and can also cause a lot of pain to a [Galilean](/wiki/Galilean "Galilean").[[6]](#cite_note-8)


Armodrillo possesses enhanced durability, courtesy of his armor. He is durable enough to withstand attacks from many powerful opponents, as well as being thrown through walls and other solid surfaces.


Armodrillo possesses enhanced strength, as he was able to destroy ten [robots](/wiki/Computron%27s_Minions "Computron's Minions") from [Dimension 12](/wiki/Dimension_12 "Dimension 12"),[[7]](#cite_note-9) lift up a [Mucilator](/wiki/Mucilator%27s_Species "Mucilator's Species"),[[8]](#cite_note-OPaPP1-10) and hold his own against [Trombipulor](/wiki/Trumbipulor "Trumbipulor").[[9]](#cite_note-SD-11)


Armodrillo can create destructive earthquakes by placing his hands on the ground and pumping the jackhammer pistons on his elbows.[[10]](#cite_note-12) He can also do this by slamming his hands onto the ground.


Armodrillo has the capability to cause a magnitude 10 earthquake depending on where he tries it, such as fault lines on tectonic plates.[[DR 1]](#cite_note-earthquake-13)


Using his jackhammer arms, Armodrillo can propel himself very high into the air by punching the ground, as seen when he avoided getting rammed by [Crabdozer](/wiki/Crabdozer "Crabdozer").[[11]](#cite_note-OPaPP2-14)


Armodrillo can extend his hands by pushing in the screws on his arms.[[12]](#cite_note-IaMBW1-15)


Armodrillo is able to create fissures in the ground, although he may have to first dig deep underground to do this.[[13]](#cite_note-16)


Using his jackhammer arms, Armodrillo can create shock waves to blast away larger enemies with a single punch[[5]](#cite_note-Malefactor-7) and launch objects he is holding.[[12]](#cite_note-IaMBW1-15)


Armodrillo can create enough vibrations to collapse buildings into rubble, as demonstrated by [Andreas](/wiki/Andreas "Andreas") when he destroyed a [Forever Knight](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights") castle.[[4]](#cite_note-AF-6)

History
-------


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Andreas' Fault](/wiki/Andreas%27_Fault "Andreas' Fault")*, Armodrillo's DNA was sampled and put in the [Codon Stream](/wiki/Codon_Stream "Codon Stream") when the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") scanned [Andreas](/wiki/Andreas "Andreas")' DNA.
* Armodrillo first appeared in *[Hero Time](/wiki/Hero_Time "Hero Time")*, where he helped [Captain Nemesis](/wiki/Carl_Nesmith "Carl Nesmith") defeat some of [Computron](/wiki/Computron "Computron")'s [minions](/wiki/Computron%27s_Minions "Computron's Minions").
* In *[Reflected Glory](/wiki/Reflected_Glory "Reflected Glory")*, Armodrillo battled some [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights").
* In *[...Nor Iron Bars a Cage](/wiki/...Nor_Iron_Bars_a_Cage "...Nor Iron Bars a Cage")*, Armodrillo was defeated by [Ultimate Kevin](/wiki/Ultimate_Kevin "Ultimate Kevin").
* In *[The Transmogrification of Eunice](/wiki/The_Transmogrification_of_Eunice "The Transmogrification of Eunice")*, Armodrillo rushed to save [Eunice](/wiki/Eunice "Eunice") when he thought she was being attacked by a bear.
* In *[Revenge of the Swarm](/wiki/Revenge_of_the_Swarm "Revenge of the Swarm")*, Armodrillo freed [Julie](/wiki/Julie_Yamamoto "Julie Yamamoto") and fought some [Victor Validus](/wiki/Victor_Validus "Victor Validus") clones, but was defeated by [Elena](/wiki/Elena_Validus "Elena Validus").
* In *[The Creature from Beyond](/wiki/The_Creature_from_Beyond "The Creature from Beyond")*, Armodrillo battled the victims of the [Lucubra](/wiki/Lucubra "Lucubra")'s mind control before switching into [NRG](/wiki/NRG "NRG").
* In *[Double or Nothing](/wiki/Double_or_Nothing "Double or Nothing")*, Armodrillo scared a ticket clerk to let him and [the team](/wiki/Alien_Force "Alien Force") into the theatre.
* In *[Night of the Living Nightmare](/wiki/Night_of_the_Living_Nightmare "Night of the Living Nightmare")*, Armodrillo was seen confronting [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") in a dream.


### Omniverse


* In *[The More Things Change: Part 2](/wiki/The_More_Things_Change:_Part_2 "The More Things Change: Part 2")*, Armodrillo defeated many of [Psyphon](/wiki/Psyphon "Psyphon")'s [henchmen](/wiki/Psyphon%27s_Gang "Psyphon's Gang"), but failed to defeat Psyphon himself.
* In *[Of Predators and Prey: Part 1](/wiki/Of_Predators_and_Prey:_Part_1 "Of Predators and Prey: Part 1")*, Armodrillo was defeated by [Slamworm](/wiki/Slamworm "Slamworm").
* In *[Of Predators and Prey: Part 2](/wiki/Of_Predators_and_Prey:_Part_2 "Of Predators and Prey: Part 2")*, Armodrillo battled Slamworm and [Crabdozer](/wiki/Crabdozer "Crabdozer").
* In *[Malefactor](/wiki/Malefactor "Malefactor")*, Armodrillo battled Slamworm and Crabdozer again.
* In *[Rules of Engagement](/wiki/Rules_of_Engagement "Rules of Engagement")*, Armodrillo failed to defeat [Princess Looma](/wiki/Looma_Red_Wind "Looma Red Wind").
* In *[Ben Again](/wiki/Ben_Again "Ben Again")*, Armodrillo was used by 11-year-old [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") in 16-year-old Ben's body to find coordinates in the basement of [Pakmar](/wiki/Pakmar "Pakmar")'s Fine Teas.
* In *[Special Delivery](/wiki/Special_Delivery "Special Delivery")*, Armodrillo battled [Zombozo](/wiki/Zombozo_(Classic) "Zombozo (Classic)"), [Fistrick](/wiki/Fistrick "Fistrick"), and [Trumbipulor](/wiki/Trumbipulor "Trumbipulor").
* In *[Return to Forever](/wiki/Return_to_Forever "Return to Forever")*, Armodrillo tried to break out of a force field, but was reverted by the [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights")' [DNA Scanner](/wiki/DNA_Scanner "DNA Scanner").
* In *[The Ultimate Heist](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Heist "The Ultimate Heist")*, Armodrillo broke into [Inspector 13](/wiki/Inspector_13 "Inspector 13")'s warehouse.
* In *[For a Few Brains More](/wiki/For_a_Few_Brains_More "For a Few Brains More")*, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Armodrillo defeated [Gravattack](/wiki/Gravattack "Gravattack") before switching into Negative Gravattack.
* In *[And Then There Was Ben](/wiki/And_Then_There_Was_Ben "And Then There Was Ben")*, [No Watch Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(No_Watch_Timeline) "Ben Tennyson (No Watch Timeline)") as Armodrillo caused a small earthquake so a young Ben Prime could fall into the crater where the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") landed during the events of *[And Then There Were 10](/wiki/And_Then_There_Were_10 "And Then There Were 10")*.
* In *[Stuck on You](/wiki/Stuck_on_You "Stuck on You")*, [Skurd](/wiki/Skurd "Skurd") gave [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)") Armodrillo's arm to battle [Khyber](/wiki/Khyber "Khyber") as [Tyrannopede](/wiki/Tyrannopede "Tyrannopede").
* In *[The Secret of Dos Santos](/wiki/The_Secret_of_Dos_Santos "The Secret of Dos Santos")*, Armodrillo tried to find the entrance to the Temple of the Sky until [Kai](/wiki/Kai_Green "Kai Green") did so herself.
* In *[Malgax Attacks](/wiki/Malgax_Attacks "Malgax Attacks")*, Skurd gave [Whampire](/wiki/Whampire "Whampire") Armodrillo's arm to battle [Mal](/wiki/Malware_Armor "Malware Armor")[gax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Armodrillo appeared for a short time when Ben was cycling through his aliens.

Etymology
---------


Armodrillo's name is a combination of the words "armor", "drill", and "armadillo".



Trivia
------


* Armodrillo and the other Andromeda aliens have their powers related to a basic element; for Armodrillo, it is earth.
* Armodrillo was the [Alien of the Month](/wiki/Alien_of_the_Month "Alien of the Month") in August 2011, though his name was misspelled as "Armadrillo".
* Armodrillo was designed by Steven Choi and Chap Yaep.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-18)
