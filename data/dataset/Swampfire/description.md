### Ben as Swampfire


Swampfire is a humanoid, plant-like alien that has an overall green and black colored body, mostly with a red flame-patterned head and root-like feet, seemingly holding black rocks. His eyes are oval-shaped with points at each end with pupils copying the shape but are smaller. Also, his shoulders and head have red petals and his elbows and legs have green frills sticking out. Swampfire is taller than an average human and has a distinct rotten stench that worsens with heat.


In **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")** and **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Swampfire wore the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Swampfire's first design was similar to his previous appearance, except for having five fingers on his hands. Swampfire's skin was olive green, and his arms were larger. The red petals on his shoulders, along with the green thorns on his body, were thinner and longer. His torso design changed, splitting the black parts on his waist into two. The [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol also moved from his chest to his stomach.


Swampfire's first stage of "blossoming" was more muscular than his earlier design. He also lost his pupils, one of his red petals on each shoulder, and a green frill from each leg. The lines on his neck thinned and the lines on his chest moved upward and into a pointed down angle. His green color was also a shade lighter than his olive green.


Swampfire's second stage of "blossoming" had more muscle with the black lines being removed from his chest entirely. His red petals formed into a red thorn shape, with two being on each arm. The necklines were triangular and his shade of green was lime green. His face resembled his fully matured form except that instead of orange surrounding the eyes, there was black surrounding them.


As of *[Charmed, I'm Sure](/wiki/Charmed,_I%27m_Sure "Charmed, I'm Sure")*, Swampfire's second appearance, or his "blossomed"/"matured" form, is plant-like, but with major changes. He looks more muscular and taller, his skin color is now light green, his face is now colored yellow, red, and orange, his pupils are gone and his chin is more visible. His hands are now colored black, with four yellow claws (with spikes on the knuckles), and yellow holes in the palms, used to unleash his fire powers. He has six red horns with yellow spots (aesthetically resembling flames); two on each forearm, and one on each shoulder, as well as a green horn on each foot, knee, and elbow. He now has a large collar raised up around his neck, which is yellow on the inside. The Omnitrix symbol is still on his stomach.

Swampfire has the ability to ignite methane in his palms similar to a flamethrower. After his blossoming, he ignites methane from yellow holes in his palms. His fire blasts are precise enough for him to shoot a cage with [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") and [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") in it, while the cage melted and Kevin and Gwen were left unharmed.[[1]](#cite_note-XB2-1)


Before "blossoming", Swampfire's fire blasts were powerful enough to harm [Vaxasaurians](/wiki/Vaxasaurian_(Classic) "Vaxasaurian (Classic)")[[2]](#cite_note-GCBC-2) and [Sonorosians](/wiki/Sonorosian "Sonorosian").[[3]](#cite_note-3) In his "blossomed" form, his fire blasts have become more powerful than before.[[4]](#cite_note-CIS-4)


Swampfire is capable of limited flight through means of launching fire from his palms as jet propulsion, much like [Heatblast](/wiki/Heatblast_(Classic) "Heatblast (Classic)").[[5]](#cite_note-5) He is also capable of breathing fire, as demonstrated by [Swamps](/wiki/Swamps "Swamps").[[6]](#cite_note-6)


Swampfire has a high degree of chlorokinesis,[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-7) being able to telepathically interface with plant life, manipulate and instantly create biological agriculture, and generate a gas that makes exposed plant life elongate, enlarge and ensnare enemies, seemingly being mutated by the gases. Some plants have a brief green aura when he influences them.[[7]](#cite_note-GT-8)


Aside from [Earth](/wiki/Earth_(Classic) "Earth (Classic)") plants, Swampfire uses his telepathic plant interface with alien plant life, which was what allowed him to free [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") from the [Highbreed Tree Monster](/wiki/Highbreed_Tree_Monster "Highbreed Tree Monster").[[8]](#cite_note-9) In his "blossomed" form, his telepathic influence over plants has extended to sentient plant life, such as the [Mutant Pumpkins](/wiki/Mutant_Pumpkins "Mutant Pumpkins") from [Anur Transyl](/wiki/Anur_Transyl "Anur Transyl").[[4]](#cite_note-CIS-4)


Swampfire can generate seeds from his body that phase into or pierce into any ground or flooring before turning to vine tentacles strong enough to restrain a [Highbreed](/wiki/Highbreed "Highbreed")[[9]](#cite_note-10) and a [Chimera Sui Generis](/wiki/Chimera_Sui_Generis_(Classic) "Chimera Sui Generis (Classic)").[[10]](#cite_note-TFBP2-11) They usually are as small as pebbles, but they can sometimes become larger and sharper if used on a metal surface.[[10]](#cite_note-TFBP2-11)


Swampfire's body can generate a type of mud that sticks to everything but him, which he used to blind [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)"), as the latter could not pull it off his eyes and had to change into Negative [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill_(Classic) "Big Chill (Classic)") to get rid of it.[[2]](#cite_note-GCBC-2)


Swampfire is capable of regenerating and regrowing any damaged or destroyed body parts, allowing him to grow back lost limbs, holes in his body, or even his head. He was even able to reform his body from remains when it got splattered to pieces.[[11]](#cite_note-VoVP2-12)


Swampfire is also able to heal other people's wounds as demonstrated when [Reinrassig III](/wiki/Reinrassig_III "Reinrassig III") lost his hand when he saved Ben from a [Rodilia Dentia](/wiki/Rodilia_Dentia "Rodilia Dentia").[[12]](#cite_note-13)


Another method of regeneration involves reattaching detached body parts. This is accomplished by manipulating his vine-like "veins" and tentacles to reach out and meld with the insides of the detached limb and return them to the body, allowing him to reconstitute automatically if damaged.[[13]](#cite_note-B10RP1-14)


Swampfire's methane has a very pungent smell that is intolerable to some species like the [Incurseans](/wiki/Incursean_(Classic) "Incursean (Classic)"), which will make them retch and faint.[[1]](#cite_note-XB2-1) Using his methane abilities, Swampfire can produce "sleeping spores" that can render [humans](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)") unconscious.[[14]](#cite_note-15)


Swampfire has enhanced strength, being able to push over a [Highbreed Weather Array](/wiki/Highbreed_Weather_Array "Highbreed Weather Array") using leverage by turning his feet into roots running deep into the ground.[[15]](#cite_note-ETAtW-16) Similarly, he was able to pull himself out and resist the gravitational force of a [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)") portal,[[16]](#cite_note-17) and even hold his own against [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") with his bare fists.[[10]](#cite_note-TFBP2-11) In his "blossomed" form, he appears to be even stronger than before.[[4]](#cite_note-CIS-4)


Swampfire's body structure allows him to walk straight through thin spaces such as the gaps between iron bars.[[1]](#cite_note-XB2-1) When he was crushed under a large container, his body deformed into vines that burrowed through and then out of the ground to reform him instantly. In the same instance, Kevin punched Swampfire in the torso, resulting in his fist getting stuck in it until Swampfire punched him back. Additionally, Swampfire was shown to be immune to laser bolts, as they simply passed right through him and seemed to tickle him.[[13]](#cite_note-B10RP1-14)


Swampfire is able to extend his fingers and feet into long roots that can dig through rock.[[7]](#cite_note-GT-8)


Swampfire is capable of jumping exceptionally high.[[15]](#cite_note-ETAtW-16)


Swampfire has rather strong teeth that are able to injure another [Methanosian](/wiki/Methanosian "Methanosian").[[4]](#cite_note-CIS-4)


Swampfire can generate vine-like tentacles from his back.[[17]](#cite_note-18)


Swampfire can grow plants from the ground simply by raising his hand, though he only gained this ability after "blossoming".[[4]](#cite_note-CIS-4)


Also after "blossoming", Swampfire gains the ability to grow vines with a flame tip from his hands and use them as whips, which are able to slice the plasma generated by [Ball Weevil](/wiki/Ball_Weevil "Ball Weevil")'s [species](/wiki/Ball_Weevil%27s_Species "Ball Weevil's Species").[[18]](#cite_note-WXIP2-19)

Similar to [Stinkfly](/wiki/Stinkfly_(Classic) "Stinkfly (Classic)")'s odor, Swampfire's swampy stench can make it hard for him to hide from anybody or anything capable of smelling.


Despite his regeneration, Swampfire can be continuously cut up by sharp objects, such as a [Polymorph](/wiki/Polymorph "Polymorph")'s [Anti-Gravity Projector](/wiki/Anti-Gravity_Projector "Anti-Gravity Projector"), preventing him from regenerating.[[11]](#cite_note-VoVP2-12)


Swampfire can be harmed by electricity, such as that generated by a [Conductoid](/wiki/Conductoid "Conductoid").[[18]](#cite_note-WXIP2-19)


Swampfire can be immobilized by ice, such as the ice breath of a [Necrofriggian](/wiki/Necrofriggian_(Classic) "Necrofriggian (Classic)"), though he can thaw himself out by igniting the methane in the palms of his hands.[[15]](#cite_note-ETAtW-16)[[2]](#cite_note-GCBC-2)


Swampfire's fire attacks can be canceled out by fire extinguishers.[[19]](#cite_note-VG-20)


Swampfire is vulnerable to [magic](/wiki/Magic_(Classic) "Magic (Classic)"), as his back was scorched by a magic attack from [Charmcaster](/wiki/Charmcaster_(Classic) "Charmcaster (Classic)") (while empowered by the [Alpha Rune](/wiki/Alpha_Rune "Alpha Rune") armor).[[4]](#cite_note-CIS-4)


Swampfire's body heat can make it difficult for him to fight opponents that are attracted to heat, like reptiles.[[20]](#cite_note-21)


Swampfire's plants can be killed using liquid chemicals, such as a spray used by the [Stalker](/wiki/Stalker "Stalker").[[19]](#cite_note-VG-20)


Originally, Swampfire was unable to control sentient plants, such as the [Plant Clones](/wiki/Plant_Clones "Plant Clones"), let alone weeds.[[21]](#cite_note-22) This weakness is no longer a problem ever since he "blossomed".[[4]](#cite_note-CIS-4)


Despite his enhanced strength, Swampfire is physically weaker than [Crystalsapiens](/wiki/Crystalsapien_(Classic) "Crystalsapien (Classic)"),[[DM 1]](#cite_note-23) [Tetramands](/wiki/Tetramand_(Classic) "Tetramand (Classic)"), and [Vaxasaurians](/wiki/Vaxasaurian_(Classic) "Vaxasaurian (Classic)").[[DM 2]](#cite_note-24)


If Ben turns into Swampfire with a broken limb, his limb will be twisted and shriveled, and he will feel pain from it.[[22]](#cite_note-CaFS-25)


Swampfire can be aged to death with timerays such as that of [Eon](/wiki/Eon "Eon")'s,as showed when Eon aged both Ultimate Swampfire and Base Swampfire,thus removing them from the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)").[[23]](#cite_note-26)

### Alien Force


* Swampfire first appeared in *[Ben 10 Returns: Part 1](/wiki/Ben_10_Returns:_Part_1 "Ben 10 Returns: Part 1")*, where he defeated some [DNAliens](/wiki/DNAliens "DNAliens"), [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights"), and [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)").
* In *[Everybody Talks About the Weather](/wiki/Everybody_Talks_About_the_Weather "Everybody Talks About the Weather")*, Swampfire battled some DNAliens, gave [Alan](/wiki/Alan_Albright "Alan Albright") advice on flying, and destroyed the [Highbreed Weather Array](/wiki/Highbreed_Weather_Array "Highbreed Weather Array") with the latter's help.
* In *[Plumbers' Helpers](/wiki/Plumbers%27_Helpers_(Episode) "Plumbers' Helpers (Episode)")*, Swampfire escaped the [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)") and defeated [Manny](/wiki/Manny_Armstrong "Manny Armstrong") and [Helen](/wiki/Helen_Wheels "Helen Wheels").
* In *[Paradox](/wiki/Paradox "Paradox")*, Swampfire found and met [Professor Paradox](/wiki/Professor_Paradox "Professor Paradox") and briefly chased him around [Los Soledad](/wiki/Los_Soledad "Los Soledad").
* In *[X = Ben + 2](/wiki/X_%3D_Ben_%2B_2 "X = Ben + 2")*, Swampfire defeated [SevenSeven](/wiki/SevenSeven "SevenSeven") and some [Incurseans](/wiki/Incursean_(Classic) "Incursean (Classic)") and stopped [Attea](/wiki/Attea "Attea") from destroying [Earth](/wiki/Earth_(Classic) "Earth (Classic)").
* In *[Alone Together](/wiki/Alone_Together "Alone Together")*, Swampfire defeated a large group of [Dasypodidae](/wiki/Dasypodidae "Dasypodidae"). Later, Swampfire healed [Reinrassig III](/wiki/Reinrassig_III "Reinrassig III")'s detached hand, giving the latter a new [Methanosian](/wiki/Methanosian "Methanosian") arm.
* In *[Good Copy, Bad Copy](/wiki/Good_Copy,_Bad_Copy "Good Copy, Bad Copy")*, Swampfire defeated [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)"), but was defeated himself by Negative [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill_(Classic) "Big Chill (Classic)"), so he switched into [Brainstorm](/wiki/Brainstorm "Brainstorm").
* In *[Undercover](/wiki/Undercover "Undercover")*, Swampfire saved [the team](/wiki/Alien_Force "Alien Force") from an exploding [Teleporter Pod](/wiki/Teleporter_Pod "Teleporter Pod").
* In *[Grounded](/wiki/Grounded "Grounded")*, Swampfire was defeated by a [Highbreed](/wiki/Highbreed "Highbreed").
* In *[Pet Project](/wiki/Pet_Project "Pet Project")*, Swampfire fought [Ship](/wiki/Ship "Ship"), who was being controlled by [Joseph Chadwick](/wiki/Joseph_Chadwick "Joseph Chadwick") and the Forever Knights.
* In *[War of the Worlds: Part 1](/wiki/War_of_the_Worlds:_Part_1 "War of the Worlds: Part 1")*, Swampfire battled an army of [DNAliens](/wiki/DNAliens "DNAliens") before switching into [Murk Upchuck](/wiki/Upchuck "Upchuck").
* In *[War of the Worlds: Part 2](/wiki/War_of_the_Worlds:_Part_2 "War of the Worlds: Part 2")*, Swampfire defeated a [Highbreed Commander](/wiki/Highbreed_Commander "Highbreed Commander").
* In *[Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 2](/wiki/Vengeance_of_Vilgax:_Part_2 "Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 2")*, Swampfire fought and recaptured the escaped [Goop](/wiki/Goop_(Classic) "Goop (Classic)").
	+ The same scene reoccurred on TV during [Will Harangue](/wiki/Will_Harangue "Will Harangue")'s news report in *[Fame](/wiki/Fame "Fame")*.
* In *[Simple](/wiki/Simple "Simple")*, Swampfire captured the [Blue](/wiki/Blue_Leader "Blue Leader") and [Red Leaders](/wiki/Red_Leader "Red Leader") and forced them to work out a peace treaty.
* In *[Singlehanded](/wiki/Singlehanded "Singlehanded")*, Swampfire, with no left hand, battled some [Vulpimancers](/wiki/Vulpimancer_(Classic) "Vulpimancer (Classic)") in the Null Void. He scared them and two kids away with a fire blast.
* In *[If All Else Fails](/wiki/If_All_Else_Fails "If All Else Fails")*, Swampfire took over the [Highbreed Tree Monster](/wiki/Highbreed_Tree_Monster "Highbreed Tree Monster") in order to free [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)").

### [Ben 10: Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force_(Video_Game) "Ben 10: Alien Force (Video Game)")


Swampfire is a playable alien in all versions of the game.



#### Nintendo Wii, PlayStation 2 and PlayStation Portable Versions


Swampfire and [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill "Big Chill") are the only aliens available on Ben's arsenal at the beginning of the game.


Swampfire is able to use his fireballs to ignite stacks of hay blocking the path.


Swampfire is vital for progression on the Rural Rumble level of the game.



#### Nintendo DS Version


Swampfire and Humungousaur are the only aliens available on Ben's arsenal at the beginning of the game.


Swampfire is able to unleash three small or one large fireball, a stream of fire, cause vines to erupt from the ground and imbue his punches and kicks with fire.


Swampfire is not required to be used at all in order to complete the game.



### [Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks](/wiki/Ben_10_Alien_Force:_Vilgax_Attacks "Ben 10 Alien Force: Vilgax Attacks")


Swampfire is a playable alien in the game.


Swampfire could ignite his hands for "flaming fists," create a ring of fire by emitting gas to attack enemies, and cause thorny vines to sprout from the ground.



#### Xbox 360, Nintendo Wii, PlayStation 2 and PlayStation Portable Versions


Due to the Omnitrix rebooting after Ben's abuse of the quick-change feature during his battle with an animated [Mr. Smoothy](/wiki/Mr._Smoothy "Mr. Smoothy") mascot, Swampfire, along with Big Chill, [Spidermonkey](/wiki/Spidermonkey_(Classic) "Spidermonkey (Classic)"), [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)") and [Cannonbolt](/wiki/Cannonbolt_(Classic) "Cannonbolt (Classic)") were the only aliens available on Ben's arsenal upon his arrival on [Vulpin](/wiki/Vulpin_(Classic) "Vulpin (Classic)").


Swampfire can use his fireballs to ignite leaking gas canisters and overgrown vines.


Swampfire is the only alien form that is able to defeat the [Two-Headed Snake](/wiki/Two-Headed_Snake "Two-Headed Snake").


Swampfire is the only playable alien with two unlockable combo moves: Ring of Fire and Flamethrower.


Swampfire is vital for progression on the Vulpin and [Terradino](/wiki/Terradino_(Classic) "Terradino (Classic)") levels of the game.



#### Nintendo DS Version


Only Swampfire and [Goop](/wiki/Goop_(Classic) "Goop (Classic)") are available on Ben's arsenal upon his arrival on Vulpin.


Swampfire is able to safely bypass the dangerous flames produced by the exhaust vents on Vulpin, as well as ignite any plant life on Terradino that is blocking the path.



### [Ben 10 Alien Force: The Rise of Hex](/wiki/Ben_10_Alien_Force:_The_Rise_of_Hex "Ben 10 Alien Force: The Rise of Hex")


Swampfire is a playable alien in the game.


Due to the Omnitrix sorting out after Ben fiddled with it, Swampfire and [Brainstorm](/wiki/Brainstorm "Brainstorm") were the only aliens available on Ben's arsenal at the beginning of the game.


Swampfire is able to spray gas that causes roots to grow into vines, which can be jumped on to reach otherwise inaccessible areas or to cross a hazardous path.


Swampfire is also able to rapidly regenerate his health meter, at the cost of draining the Omnitrix Energy meter at an equally fast pace.



### [Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction](/wiki/Ben_10_Ultimate_Alien:_Cosmic_Destruction "Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction")


Swampfire is a playable alien character in the game.


Swampfire can ignite methane in his palms to project fire. He is able to create plants and ignite them.


Swampfire is vital for progression on the Eiffel Tower, The Great Wall, and The Colosseum levels of the game.



### [Ben 10: Galactic Racing](/wiki/Ben_10:_Galactic_Racing "Ben 10: Galactic Racing")


Swampfire is a playable racer in this game on all platforms (except DS).



### [Ben 10: Omniverse 2](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse_2 "Ben 10: Omniverse 2")


Swampfire is a playable alien in the game (not on 3DS). He has his original **Omniverse** appearance.



### [Punch Time Explosion XL](/wiki/Punch_Time_Explosion_XL "Punch Time Explosion XL")


Swampfire is one of the aliens used by [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") in Battle.



### [TKO](/wiki/TKO "TKO")


Swampfire's special moves are Fireball and Vinespike, and his super move is Inferno Blast.

Trivia
------


* [SevenSeven](/wiki/SevenSeven "SevenSeven") is scared of [Methanosians](/wiki/Methanosian "Methanosian") like Swampfire.[[1]](#cite_note-XB2-1)[[DM 3]](#cite_note-27)
* Swampfire is the reason why Kevin has [an x-shaped scar on his chin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Future) "Kevin Levin (Future)").[[24]](#cite_note-28)[[18]](#cite_note-WXIP2-19)
* Swampfire is one of the four regular aliens to get a complete redesign in **Omniverse**; the others being [Shocksquatch](/wiki/Shocksquatch "Shocksquatch"), [Eatle](/wiki/Eatle "Eatle"), and [Chromastone](/wiki/Chromastone_(Classic) "Chromastone (Classic)"). However, Swampfire and Chromastone are the only two of these aliens to get redesigns mid-series (not counting [Rath](/wiki/Rath_(Classic) "Rath (Classic)") getting clothes).
* Swampfire and [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill_(Classic) "Big Chill (Classic)") are the only aliens whose natural biological behavior has overridden [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s normal state.
	+ Methanosians undergo a kind of growth and "blossoming" phase. In Ben's case, his [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") was unable to select a different alien and he kept developing large facial pustules in his human form until Swampfire had fully blossomed.[[4]](#cite_note-CIS-4)
* Swampfire underwent heavy revisions to his design during the pre-production stage of **Alien Force**, with concept art drawn by Glenn Wong, Eric Canete,[[EC 1]](#cite_note-29) [Tom Perkins](/wiki/Tom_Perkins "Tom Perkins"),[[TP 1]](#cite_note-30) and [Glen Murakami](/wiki/Glen_Murakami "Glen Murakami"). The final design was created by Glen Murakami, with color styling by Chris Hooten.[[GW 1]](#cite_note-31)
* Swampfire's blossoming was [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt")'s idea, as he wanted to use some of Tom Perkins' unused Swampfire concept art.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-redesign-32)
* Swampfire is the first alien used in **Alien Force**. The first alien used in the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10") ([Heatblast](/wiki/Heatblast_(Classic) "Heatblast (Classic)")) also has fire-based powers like Swampfire.
	+ Swampfire is also the first alien that Ben used via the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)").
* Swampfire's voice is very stuffy and nasally because he does not have a nose. It also sounds a lot like Maximus from **[American Dragon: Jake Long](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Dragon:_Jake_Long "wikipedia:American Dragon: Jake Long")**.
	+ Despite not having a nose, however, Swampfire somehow has a sense of smell.[[13]](#cite_note-B10RP1-14)[[22]](#cite_note-CaFS-25)
* Swampfire is one of the four regular aliens to be temporarily destroyed and removed from the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/Ultimatrix; the others being [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)"), Chromastone, and [Feedback](/wiki/Feedback "Feedback").
* Swampfire has a nano in *[FusionFall](/wiki/FusionFall "FusionFall")* after the player beats [Fusion Ben](/wiki/Fusion_Ben "Fusion Ben").
