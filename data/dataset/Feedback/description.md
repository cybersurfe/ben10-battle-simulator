Appearance
----------


Feedback has a black body with one green eye in the middle of his head, two antennae, a tail with round plugs on their tips, four-fingered hands, and feet with only two front toes.


11-year-old Feedback had a white chest color and round plugs on the tips of his fingers.


16-year-old Feedback looks identical to his 11-year-old self except his chest is recolored green and his fingers are now square. He also has gold battery bolts on his shoulders, arms, and hips.


Feedback wears the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/[Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his chest.

Feedback has the ability to absorb and redirect energy attacks,[[CN 1]](#cite_note-3) most commonly electricity.[[note 1]](#cite_note-5) This ability is refined to the point that Feedback can redirect any type of energy and an infinite amount[[DR 1]](#cite_note-6) at near point-blank range. He absorbs energy through the plug-like conductors on his antennae, tail, and fingers.


Feedback can absorb energy out of living beings, as seen when he battled [Argost](/wiki/V.V._Argost "V.V. Argost")[[2]](#cite_note-TGIS-2) and [Phil](/wiki/Phil_Billings_(Classic) "Phil Billings (Classic)") in his [Terroranchula](/wiki/Terroranchula_(Species) "Terroranchula (Species)") form.[[4]](#cite_note-MM-7)[[5]](#cite_note-WXIP1-8)


Feedback can absorb a [Crystalsapien](/wiki/Crystalsapien "Crystalsapien")'s ultraviolet beams,[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-9) tachyon particles fired from a [Tachyon Cannon](/wiki/Tachyon_Cannon "Tachyon Cannon"),[[6]](#cite_note-10) the energy of an [Annihilargh](/wiki/Annihilargh "Annihilargh"),[[7]](#cite_note-AND-11) and multiversal levels of energy (as long as the energy of these other universes have their basis in the same set of physical laws).[[DR 2]](#cite_note-12)


Feedback can use stored energy to generate electrical charges, which can be used as ranged attacks or to empower his punches.[[8]](#cite_note-TH-13)


Feedback can fire energy balls by joining his fingers and absorbing the energy out of electric devices.[[9]](#cite_note-SD1-14)


Feedback has enhanced agility, reflexes and jumping abilities, and possesses enhanced speed but only while running quickly on electrical power lines.[[9]](#cite_note-SD1-14)


Feedback can fly by projecting energy behind him like a jet engine.[[1]](#cite_note-SD2-1)[[10]](#cite_note-15)


Feedback has enhanced strength, as he can punch a boulder apart,[[8]](#cite_note-TH-13) lift Phil in his Terroranchula form[[5]](#cite_note-WXIP1-8) and charge into a building by busting down a wall with ease.[[11]](#cite_note-Breakpoint-16)


Feedback has enhanced durability, as he quickly shrugged off Malware's blast that made him fall into water[[1]](#cite_note-SD2-1) and was unfazed when Terroranchula Phil threw him against a wall.[[4]](#cite_note-MM-7)


Feedback is able to stretch the plugs on his head, hips, and hands for more range.[[4]](#cite_note-MM-7) He can also "smell" radio signals by using his antennae.[[12]](#cite_note-17)


Due to [his species](/wiki/Conductoid "Conductoid") being born in a [nebula](/wiki/Teslavorr_Nebula "Teslavorr Nebula") rather than a planet, it is implied that Feedback can survive in the vacuum of space.[[OV Brief 1]](#cite_note-18)



Weaknesses
----------


* ![]()Vulnerable Antennae
* ![]()Ineffective Electrokinesis
* ![]()Unstoppable Electric Blasts
1/3
Feedback needs to absorb energy from time to time to fight because the energy contained within him will deplete with use.[[1]](#cite_note-SD2-1)


Feedback's antennae can be easily pulled on, causing him great discomfort and leaving him stuck in place.[[11]](#cite_note-Breakpoint-16)


Feedback's electric blasts are occasionally ineffective against opponents who can absorb electricity, such as Phil.[[4]](#cite_note-MM-7)


Once Feedback powers up his electric energy blasts, he cannot stop himself from shooting them, meaning he will have to resort to shooting them all up in the air to avoid any collateral damage if he fails to catch his target.[[11]](#cite_note-Breakpoint-16)


Feedback is weak against energy-based attacks, such as those generated by a [Pugnavore](/wiki/Pugnavore "Pugnavore"). [[13]](#cite_note-19)



History
-------


### Ben 10


* Feedback was unlocked off-screen after ***[Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens](/wiki/Ben_10:_Destroy_All_Aliens "Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens")***.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-20)


### Five Years Prior to Omniverse


* Feedback first appeared in *[The More Things Change: Part 1](/wiki/The_More_Things_Change:_Part_1 "The More Things Change: Part 1")*, where he defeated [Malware](/wiki/Malware "Malware").
* In *[A Jolt from the Past](/wiki/A_Jolt_from_the_Past "A Jolt from the Past")*, Feedback defeated the [Megawhatts](/wiki/Nosedeenian "Nosedeenian").
* In *[Trouble Helix](/wiki/Trouble_Helix "Trouble Helix")*, Feedback battled Malware.
* In *[Of Predators and Prey: Part 2](/wiki/Of_Predators_and_Prey:_Part_2 "Of Predators and Prey: Part 2")*, Feedback fought [Phil](/wiki/Phil_Billings_(Classic) "Phil Billings (Classic)") as [Terroranchula](/wiki/Terroranchula "Terroranchula").
	+ The same scene reoccurred in a flashback in *[Max's Monster](/wiki/Max%27s_Monster "Max's Monster")*.
* In *[Malefactor](/wiki/Malefactor "Malefactor")*, Feedback battled Malware while [Khyber](/wiki/Khyber "Khyber") was retrieving a [Psycholeopterran](/wiki/Smuggled_Psycholeopterran "Smuggled Psycholeopterran") DNA sample for the [Nemetrix](/wiki/Nemetrix "Nemetrix").
* In *[Showdown: Part 1](/wiki/Showdown:_Part_1 "Showdown: Part 1")*, Feedback appeared four times. Firstly, Feedback defeated the [Violet Offenders](/wiki/Violet_Offenders "Violet Offenders"). Afterwards, Feedback somehow saved some people from a burning building. Later, Feedback practiced his powers. Lastly, he briefly fought Malware before he was removed by the latter.[[note 2]](#cite_note-28)


### Omniverse


* In *[Showdown: Part 2](/wiki/Showdown:_Part_2 "Showdown: Part 2")*, [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") regained access to Feedback after forgiving his younger self[[note 3]](#cite_note-29) and, with help from [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)"), destroyed Malware for good.
* In *[T.G.I.S.](/wiki/T.G.I.S. "T.G.I.S.")*, Feedback defeated [Argost](/wiki/V.V._Argost "V.V. Argost").
* In *[Store 23](/wiki/Store_23 "Store 23")*, Feedback defeated [Liam](/wiki/Liam "Liam").
* In *[Max's Monster](/wiki/Max%27s_Monster "Max's Monster")*, Feedback defeated Phil and reverted him from his [Terroranchula](/wiki/Terroranchula%27s_Species "Terroranchula's Species") form.
* In *[A Fistful of Brains](/wiki/A_Fistful_of_Brains "A Fistful of Brains")*, Feedback defeated [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") [Ultimate Humungousaur](/wiki/Ultimate_Humungousaur "Ultimate Humungousaur").
* In *[Rad Monster Party](/wiki/Rad_Monster_Party "Rad Monster Party")*, Feedback fought [Zs'Skayr](/wiki/Zs%27Skayr "Zs'Skayr").
* In *[Weapon XI: Part 1](/wiki/Weapon_XI:_Part_1 "Weapon XI: Part 1")*, Feedback defeated Phil and reverted him from his Terroranchula form.
* In *[Clyde Five](/wiki/Clyde_Five "Clyde Five")*, Feedback located Liam after discovering he was the one who was controlling the [Cincotrix](/wiki/Mark_5_Nanoshift_Enabled_Exo-Armor "Mark 5 Nanoshift Enabled Exo-Armor") while [Clyde](/wiki/Clyde_Fife "Clyde Fife") was using it.
* In *[Breakpoint](/wiki/Breakpoint "Breakpoint")*, Feedback failed to catch [Fistrick](/wiki/Fistrick "Fistrick").
* In *[Vreedlemania](/wiki/Vreedlemania "Vreedlemania")*, Feedback fought the [Pretty Boys](/wiki/Pretty_Boy_Vreedle "Pretty Boy Vreedle") and watched [Ma Vreedle](/wiki/Ma_Vreedle "Ma Vreedle") scare off [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)").
* In *[Let's Do the Time War Again](/wiki/Let%27s_Do_the_Time_War_Again "Let's Do the Time War Again")*, Feedback defeated [Subdora](/wiki/Subdora "Subdora") and accidentally stopped time when he battled the [Time Beast](/wiki/Time_Beast "Time Beast").
* In *[Malgax Attacks](/wiki/Malgax_Attacks "Malgax Attacks")*, [Skurd](/wiki/Skurd "Skurd") gave [Whampire](/wiki/Whampire "Whampire") Feedback's arms to fight [Mal](/wiki/Malware_Armor "Malware Armor")[gax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)").
* In *[The Most Dangerous Game Show](/wiki/The_Most_Dangerous_Game_Show "The Most Dangerous Game Show")*, Feedback attempted to battle [Charles Zenith](/wiki/Charles_Zenith "Charles Zenith") but was immediately reverted by him.
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Feedback absorbed the energy released by [Maltruant](/wiki/Maltruant "Maltruant")'s [Annihilargh](/wiki/Annihilargh "Annihilargh") and defeated him with it, trapping the latter in a time loop for all eternity.

Etymology
---------


[Feedback](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Feedback "wikipedia:Feedback"), in electronics, is the process of returning part of the output of a system to the input; this references Feedback's ability to absorb and then redistribute energy.



Trivia
------


* [Ben 23](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Dimension_23) "Ben Tennyson (Dimension 23)") had originally named [his version of Feedback](/wiki/Feedback_(Dimension_23) "Feedback (Dimension 23)") "Plug Man", but eventually changed it due to liking the name "Feedback" better.[[15]](#cite_note-30)
* According to [Yuri Lowenthal](/wiki/Yuri_Lowenthal "Yuri Lowenthal"), Feedback has the heroic voice that he always wanted for himself.[[AC 1]](#cite_note-31)
	+ Whenever Feedback speaks, an electrical sound is heard.
* Feedback was designed by [Dave Johnson](/wiki/Dave_Johnson "Dave Johnson") at the request of [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") so he could look more like an [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10") alien.[[DJ 1]](#cite_note-32)[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-33)
	+ This explains why 11-year-old Feedback's color scheme is similar that of outfits worn by many of 10/11-year-old Ben's aliens throughout the Classic Timeline, particularly [Buzzshock](/wiki/Buzzshock_(Classic) "Buzzshock (Classic)")'s design in both the original series and **Ultimate Alien**.
* The concept of Feedback came from [Glen Murakami](/wiki/Glen_Murakami "Glen Murakami") and [Dwayne McDuffie](/wiki/Dwayne_McDuffie "Dwayne McDuffie")'s story ideas,[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-history-34) and had already started by the time Derrick joined the crew.[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-35)
	+ Feedback's powers were conceived before Dave came up with his design.[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-36)
* The loss of Feedback, who was Ben's favorite alien at the time, was supposed to be the reason Ben took off the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") and retired from being a hero,[[MW 2]](#cite_note-37)[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-history-34) but it was ultimately Cartoon Network executives who vetoed the idea, which many crew members were thankful for.[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-38)
	+ Due to the loss of Feedback, he is one of the four regular aliens to be temporarily destroyed and removed from the original Omnitrix/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)"); the others being [Chromastone](/wiki/Chromastone "Chromastone"), [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)"), and [Swampfire](/wiki/Swampfire "Swampfire").
* Feedback is the first new alien transformation to appear onscreen in **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**. He is also the final alien used by 16-year-old Ben onscreen in **Omniverse**, as well as the Classic Timeline as a whole.
* Feedback is one of two exceptions to the principle that transformations are not full-blown aliens due to retaining Ben's personality, memories, and consciousness; the other being [Ghostfreak](/wiki/Ghostfreak "Ghostfreak").[[DR 3]](#cite_note-39)[[DR 4]](#cite_note-40)
* Due to his DNA being absent from the Codon Stream during *Double or Nothing*, Albedo's [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") does not have Feedback's DNA.[[DJW 10]](#cite_note-41)
	+ Similarly, [Mad Ben](/wiki/Mad_Ben "Mad Ben") does not have Feedback available in his [Omnitrix](/wiki/Power_Watch "Power Watch").[[DJW 11]](#cite_note-42) However, it is unknown if [Bad Ben](/wiki/Bad_Ben "Bad Ben"), [Benzarro](/wiki/Benzarro "Benzarro"), or [Nega Ben](/wiki/Nega_Ben "Nega Ben") each have Feedback available in their Omnitricies.[[DJW 12]](#cite_note-43)[[DJW 13]](#cite_note-44)
* [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") once claimed that Feedback's appearance is actually a containment suit,[[DJW 14]](#cite_note-45) like [Echo Echo](/wiki/Echo_Echo "Echo Echo") and [NRG](/wiki/NRG "NRG"), but he eventually refuted the statement, admitting that he was wrong.[[DJW 15]](#cite_note-46)
* Feedback, [Grey Matter](/wiki/Grey_Matter_(Classic) "Grey Matter (Classic)"), and [Juryrigg](/wiki/Juryrigg "Juryrigg") are the only aliens with their own theme song.
	+ Feedback's theme sounds extremely similar to the song *[You Need to Stop](https://youtu.be/SwVsTKu9Nx4)* by Osmo Band.
* Feedback and [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)") are the only aliens who have ever defeated Malware.
* As of **Omniverse**, Feedback and [Ampfibian](/wiki/Ampfibian_(Classic) "Ampfibian (Classic)") are the only aliens who generate blue electricity.
* Feedback's places in the **[Omniverse](/wiki/Omniverse "Omniverse")** intros change a lot over the course of the show.
	+ From Arc 4 onwards, 11-year-old Feedback is replaced with his 16-year-old self.
	+ 11-year-old Feedback is also replaced with 11-year-old [Eye Guy](/wiki/Eye_Guy "Eye Guy") in the opening intro of **Omniverse**'s Arc 4, specifically in the part where the former originally appeared alongside the 11-year-old versions of [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)"), [Grey Matter](/wiki/Grey_Matter_(Classic) "Grey Matter (Classic)"), and [Wildmutt](/wiki/Wildmutt "Wildmutt").
	+ Furthermore, as of Arc 4, three clones of 11-year-old [Ditto](/wiki/Ditto "Ditto") take 11-year-old Feedback's place in the part where the latter was originally lined up alongside the 11-year-old versions of Ben, [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)"), [Cannonbolt](/wiki/Cannonbolt_(Classic) "Cannonbolt (Classic)"), [XLR8](/wiki/XLR8_(Classic) "XLR8 (Classic)"), [Wildmutt](/wiki/Wildmutt "Wildmutt"), [Ripjaws](/wiki/Ripjaws_(Classic) "Ripjaws (Classic)"), and [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)") at the end.
	+ Feedback is also replaced with [Snare-oh](/wiki/Snare-oh "Snare-oh") in the opening intro of Arc 5.
* In *[FusionFall Heroes](/wiki/FusionFall_Heroes "FusionFall Heroes")*, Feedback has a yellow eye and a pupil, even though he doesn't have either in the show.
