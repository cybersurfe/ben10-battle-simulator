Appearance
----------


Being an organosillicon-based life form with segmented hardened quasi-organic polymer skin, Bloxx's body is made up of individual red, blue, and yellow building blocks that he can rearrange at will.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-Segmentasapien_Physiology-2)


Bloxx's default appearance looks similar to that of a [gorilla](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gorilla "wikipedia:Gorilla") made of mostly red and yellow building blocks with some blue blocks. He has black lines and studs all over his body. His feet are stubs and his hands are extremely large to balance most of his body. Bloxx wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his chest.

1/18
Bloxx's shapeshifting powers allow him to transform into multiple things, such as a wall, catapult, and dome. If Bloxx's body is damaged or broken, he can easily reform the broken pieces immediately.


Bloxx can generate and form more blocks on his body at will,[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-Segmentasapien_Physiology-2) either to grow in size, create constructs of blocks, or change himself into a larger construct.


Bloxx can willingly detach parts of his body to get out of certain situations.[[1]](#cite_note-AD-3)


Bloxx can extend his arms to great lengths on their own without creating more blocks to make them longer. This allows him to easily transfer big gaps and cover more ground when walking on his hands, as shown when he was crossing over several buildings while chasing the [Screegit](/wiki/Screegit "Screegit") and caught up to it.[[2]](#cite_note-Have_I_Got_a_Deal_for_You-4)


Bloxx can turn his hands into guns dubbed Bloxx-Lobbers[[3]](#cite_note-5)[[4]](#cite_note-6) and fire explosive blocks from them as projectiles.[[5]](#cite_note-RMP-7) The blocks are also painful to step on, as seen when [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)") used them to pain [Khyber](/wiki/Khyber "Khyber") as [Tyrannopede](/wiki/Tyrannopede "Tyrannopede") into releasing him from his mouth.[[6]](#cite_note-8)


Bloxx has enhanced strength, able to punch into a [Dimension 12](/wiki/Dimension_12 "Dimension 12") robot and shut it down,[[1]](#cite_note-AD-3) pull rocks straight out of the ground,[[7]](#cite_note-9) generate a shockwave by slamming his fists into the ground,[[8]](#cite_note-10) and carry [Fistrick](/wiki/Fistrick "Fistrick").[[9]](#cite_note-11)


Bloxx is very dense and durable, being able to survive being shot multiple times,[[10]](#cite_note-TMTCP1-12) beaten down by [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") [Ultimate Humungousaur](/wiki/Ultimate_Humungousaur "Ultimate Humungousaur"),[[11]](#cite_note-13) and crushed by [Ma Vreedle](/wiki/Ma_Vreedle "Ma Vreedle").[[12]](#cite_note-OMWAT-14)


Bloxx can jump exceptionally high, as he was able to leap up to a high platform where Ma Vreedle was. He also has good reflexes, as he could swiftly dodge one of her shots and punch the others with precise timing.[[12]](#cite_note-OMWAT-14)


Similar to [Cannonbolt](/wiki/Cannonbolt_(Classic) "Cannonbolt (Classic)"), Bloxx can encase people or objects inside his body, as shown when he caught the [Gracklflint](/wiki/Gracklflint "Gracklflint"),[[13]](#cite_note-15) and protected [Rook](/wiki/Rook_Blonko "Rook Blonko") from dying in the [Earth](/wiki/Earth_(Classic) "Earth (Classic)")'s atmosphere.[[14]](#cite_note-Breakpoint-16)


Bloxx also gains similar abilities to Cannonbolt when in his sphere form, such as increased speed, and being able to see where he is going when rolling around.


Because he is made of building blocks, Bloxx can survive in the vacuum of space. He is also immune to extremely high amounts of heat and radiation, as seen when falling through Earth's atmosphere.[[14]](#cite_note-Breakpoint-16)

1/5
Despite his shape-changing abilities, Bloxx can be broken when hit with enough force, but can easily rebuild himself right after.


Beings that can become intangible, such as [Ectonurites](/wiki/Ectonurite_(Classic) "Ectonurite (Classic)"), can easily phase through Bloxx if he traps them in his dome form.[[5]](#cite_note-RMP-7)


While Bloxx is highly durable to an extent, he still feels pain, as seen when he was acting as a railway bridge to save a train.[[2]](#cite_note-Have_I_Got_a_Deal_for_You-4) Similarly, plummeting through Earth's atmosphere caused him great discomfort, enough to make his butt feel like it was "on fire".[[14]](#cite_note-Breakpoint-16)


Bloxx is vulnerable to acidic substances, whether it be liquid[[2]](#cite_note-Have_I_Got_a_Deal_for_You-4) or gaseous.[[15]](#cite_note-ATTWN-17)



History
-------


### Omniverse


* Bloxx first appeared in *[The More Things Change: Part 1](/wiki/The_More_Things_Change:_Part_1 "The More Things Change: Part 1")*, where he saved [Mr. Baumann](/wiki/Ignacius_Baumann "Ignacius Baumann")'s shop from being destroyed with [Rook](/wiki/Rook_Blonko "Rook Blonko")'s help.[[note 1]](#cite_note-19)
* In *[A Jolt from the Past](/wiki/A_Jolt_from_the_Past "A Jolt from the Past")*, Bloxx defeated [Fistrick](/wiki/Fistrick "Fistrick")'s [thug](/wiki/Fistrick%27s_Thug "Fistrick's Thug") and [Hoodlum](/wiki/Hoodlum "Hoodlum").
* In *[Have I Got a Deal for You](/wiki/Have_I_Got_a_Deal_for_You "Have I Got a Deal for You")*, Bloxx saved a train from falling off a bridge before battling the [Screegit](/wiki/Screegit "Screegit") and contained it long enough for [Professor Hokestar](/wiki/Blarney_T._Hokestar "Blarney T. Hokestar") to revert the Screegit to its original form.
* In *[Many Happy Returns](/wiki/Many_Happy_Returns "Many Happy Returns")*, Bloxx stopped a [Tetramand](/wiki/Tetramand_(Classic) "Tetramand (Classic)") warship from attacking [his team](/wiki/Ben%27s_Team/OV "Ben's Team/OV").
* In *[Bros in Space](/wiki/Bros_in_Space "Bros in Space")*, Bloxx made the process of extracting the [Amber Ogia](/wiki/Amber_Ogia "Amber Ogia") on [Revonnah](/wiki/Revonnah "Revonnah") easier.
* In *[Arrested Development](/wiki/Arrested_Development "Arrested Development")*, 11-year-old [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") as Bloxx defeated some [Dimension 12](/wiki/Dimension_12 "Dimension 12") robots.
* In *[Rules of Engagement](/wiki/Rules_of_Engagement "Rules of Engagement")*, Bloxx blocked the way of [Julie](/wiki/Julie_Yamamoto "Julie Yamamoto") and [Hervé](/wiki/Herv%C3%A9 "Hervé") so he could explain himself to the former.
* In *[T.G.I.S.](/wiki/T.G.I.S. "T.G.I.S.")*, Bloxx saved [Zak](/wiki/Zak_Saturday "Zak Saturday") from [Argost](/wiki/V.V._Argost "V.V. Argost").
* In *[Rad](/wiki/Rad "Rad")*, Bloxx tried to catch the [Gracklflint](/wiki/Gracklflint "Gracklflint"), but failed and helped [Rad Dudesman](/wiki/Rad_Dudesman "Rad Dudesman") make a trap for it.
* In *[The Frogs of War: Part 1](/wiki/The_Frogs_of_War:_Part_1 "The Frogs of War: Part 1")*, Bloxx saved himself and Rook when they crashed into [Milleous](/wiki/Milleous_(Classic) "Milleous (Classic)")' ship.
* In *[O Mother, Where Art Thou?](/wiki/O_Mother,_Where_Art_Thou%3F "O Mother, Where Art Thou?")*, Bloxx was defeated by [Ma Vreedle](/wiki/Ma_Vreedle "Ma Vreedle").
* In *[For a Few Brains More](/wiki/For_a_Few_Brains_More "For a Few Brains More")*, Bloxx took [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)")'s brain from [Ultimate Albedo](/wiki/Ultimate_Albedo "Ultimate Albedo") and was defeated by [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") [Ultimate Humungousaur](/wiki/Ultimate_Humungousaur "Ultimate Humungousaur").
* In *[Rad Monster Party](/wiki/Rad_Monster_Party "Rad Monster Party")*, Bloxx battled [Viktor](/wiki/Viktor "Viktor") and [Zs'Skayr](/wiki/Zs%27Skayr "Zs'Skayr").
* In *[Universe vs. Tennyson](/wiki/Universe_vs._Tennyson "Universe vs. Tennyson")*, Bloxx saved himself, Mr. Baumann, and Fistrick from crashing before trapping the latter so Rook could arrest him.
* In *[Rook Tales](/wiki/Rook_Tales "Rook Tales")*, Bloxx was defeated by [Kundo](/wiki/Kundo "Kundo").
* In *[Breakpoint](/wiki/Breakpoint "Breakpoint")*, Bloxx saved Rook from being burnt to death in the [Earth](/wiki/Earth_(Classic) "Earth (Classic)")'s atmosphere before he reverted.
* In *[It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 2](/wiki/It%27s_a_Mad,_Mad,_Mad_Ben_World:_Part_2 "It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 2")*, Bloxx and [Handy Man](/wiki/Handy_Man "Handy Man") gave water to the enslaved Benwood citizens.
* In *[Stuck on You](/wiki/Stuck_on_You "Stuck on You")*, [Skurd](/wiki/Skurd "Skurd") gave [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)") the Bloxx-Lobber to fight [Khyber](/wiki/Khyber "Khyber") as [Tyrannopede](/wiki/Tyrannopede "Tyrannopede").
* In *[Let's Do the Time War Again](/wiki/Let%27s_Do_the_Time_War_Again "Let's Do the Time War Again")*, Skurd gave [Gravattack](/wiki/Gravattack "Gravattack") the Bloxx-Lobber to catch the [Time Beast](/wiki/Time_Beast "Time Beast").
* In *[Third Time's a Charm](/wiki/Third_Time%27s_a_Charm "Third Time's a Charm")*, Skurd gave [Shocksquatch](/wiki/Shocksquatch "Shocksquatch") the Bloxx-Lobber to battle [Charmcaster](/wiki/Charmcaster_(Classic) "Charmcaster (Classic)").
* In *[Malgax Attacks](/wiki/Malgax_Attacks "Malgax Attacks")*, Skurd gave [Whampire](/wiki/Whampire "Whampire") a pair of Bloxx-Lobbers to fight [Mal](/wiki/Malware_Armor "Malware Armor")[gax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Bloxx appeared for a short time when Ben was cycling through his aliens.

Etymology
---------


Bloxx's name is a play on the word "blocks", which alludes to what he is made out of.



Trivia
------


* Bloxx was first revealed in a [Bandai](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bandai "wikipedia:Bandai") Toy Fair display for **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**.
* During production, Bloxx's working name was Blox.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-20)
* [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") did not come up with Bloxx because the idea for the transformation had already started by the time he joined the crew.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-21)[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-22)
	+ Derrick, however, made notes on some existing rough concepts of Bloxx before he changed the design of his head.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-23)
* From Arc 4 of **Omniverse** onwards, Bloxx is replaced with [Astrodactyl](/wiki/Astrodactyl "Astrodactyl") in the opening intro of the show, particularly in the part where the former originally appeared alongside [Shocksquatch](/wiki/Shocksquatch "Shocksquatch"), [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Classic) "Way Big (Classic)"), and [Crashhopper](/wiki/Crashhopper "Crashhopper").
* Bloxx's design resembles Gutsman.exe from *[Mega Man Battle Network](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mega_Man_Battle_Network "wikipedia:Mega Man Battle Network")*.
* The blocks that Bloxx's body is made of resemble plastic bricks of the [LEGO](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lego "wikipedia:Lego") construction toy brand. Similarly, they are exceptionally durable and painful to step on.
	+ Related to this, UK fans of Ben 10 won a Guinness World Record in October 2012 after 15,556 people took part in building a huge life-size LEGO statue of Bloxx.[[16]](#cite_note-24) The final statue had taken around 570 hours to complete and used somewhere around 240,000 bricks. It was designed by Kevin Cooper from KC Designs.
