Appearance
----------


### Ben as Arctiguana


As his name suggests, Arctiguana's appearance somewhat resembles an [iguana](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iguana "wikipedia:Iguana"). He is a cold-blooded alien (as normal reptiles) whose blood is literally very cold.[[OV Brief 1]](#cite_note-link-7)


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Arctiguana had small spikes around his face, three dorsal fins reaching down his back and white gills on the sides of his head. He had white pants and a black open-front shirt with white cuffs and he wore the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol on his left hand.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Arctiguana looked exactly the same as before, except his eyes were green, the white part on his left glove became completely black, his mouth was blue, and he wore the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, 16-year-old Arctiguana still retains the clothing from his previous designs, albeit it goes all the way up to his head and his chest is completely covered up with a white underbelly shell. He has four pointed fins instead of three and the black stripes on his face do not go through his mouth anymore, as they now go around his mouth. The spikes on his face are now on his chin and his gills are no longer visible. The claws on his front feet are pointier and less dexterous, and he now has two claws on each of his back feet. He wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his chest.


11-year-old Arctiguana looks mostly the same as his 16-year-old self except he wears the same clothing as in the original series, making his gills visible. His eyes are connected by a black stripe, he has spikes on his face, and the original Omnitrix symbol on his left wrist is recolored green.



### Albedo as Negative Arctiguana


[Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Arctiguana looks the same as 16-year-old Arctiguana do in **Omniverse**, except his skin is recolored pale blue or more of a dark purple shade, the white parts of his clothing are recolored dark red, and his eyes and [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") symbol are recolored red.

1/7
Similar to [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill_(Classic) "Big Chill (Classic)"), Arctiguana is capable of breathing ice by his mouth and firing a long-range freeze ray that isolates targets in ice. This ice breath is cold enough to freeze large bodies of water[[2]](#cite_note-Ben_10.2C000-6) and molten lava,[[3]](#cite_note-8) and can make an [Arachnichimp](/wiki/Arachnichimp_(Classic) "Arachnichimp (Classic)")'s webbing brittle enough such that it can be broken free of.[[4]](#cite_note-CT-9) His ice is also very slippery.[[5]](#cite_note-Store_23-10)


Arctiguana possesses enhanced agility, as seen when skating across an icy path in a snowboard-like fashion. In the same scenario, he can use his ice breath to create structures made of ice.[[1]](#cite_note-BA-1)


Arctiguana possesses enhanced strength, enough to push a [Petrosapien](/wiki/Petrosapien_(Classic) "Petrosapien (Classic)") off a building and break a car.[[5]](#cite_note-Store_23-10)


Arctiguana possesses enhanced durability, being able to withstand being hit by a Petrosapien's shard projectiles.[[5]](#cite_note-Store_23-10)


Arctiguana can use his breath as a means of quickly hovering himself via propulsion (along with anyone holding on to him) across the ground.[[6]](#cite_note-11)


Arctiguana can similarly use his breath as mode of flight via propulsion.[[5]](#cite_note-Store_23-10)


Arctiguana possesses enhanced dexterity thanks to his three-fingered hands and can crawl up walls without struggling.[[5]](#cite_note-Store_23-10)


Although he has never trained himself to do so, Arctiguana can survive in environments with sub-freezing temperatures.[[OV Brief 1]](#cite_note-link-7)

### Ben 10


* Arctiguana was unlocked offscreen after ***[Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens](/wiki/Ben_10:_Destroy_All_Aliens "Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens")***.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-unlock1-12)[[MW 1]](#cite_note-unlock2-13)


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, Arctiguana was re-unlocked by [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Ultimate_Alien) "Ben 10,000 (Ultimate Alien)").


### Omniverse


* In *[Hot Stretch](/wiki/Hot_Stretch "Hot Stretch")*, Arctiguana froze the lava created by the [Kraaho](/wiki/Kraaho "Kraaho").
* In *[Ben Again](/wiki/Ben_Again "Ben Again")*, Arctiguana was used by 11-year-old Ben in 16-year-old Ben's body to battle [Eon](/wiki/Eon "Eon")'s [servants](/wiki/Eon%27s_Servants "Eon's Servants") alongside 16-year-old [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") and [Rook](/wiki/Rook_Blonko "Rook Blonko"). He then helped 16-year-old Ben as 11-year-old [Wildvine](/wiki/Wildvine_(Classic) "Wildvine (Classic)") battle Eon.
* In *[For a Few Brains More](/wiki/For_a_Few_Brains_More "For a Few Brains More")*, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Arctiguana gave chase to [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)"), Rook, and [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") and went [Ultimate](/wiki/Ultimate_Arctiguana "Ultimate Arctiguana") once he caught up.
* In *[Collect This](/wiki/Collect_This "Collect This")*, Arctiguana fought [Simian](/wiki/Simian "Simian") and [Deefus Veeblepister](/wiki/Deefus_Veeblepister "Deefus Veeblepister") only to be defeated by [Unitaur](/wiki/Unitaur "Unitaur").


### Future (Original)


* Arctiguana first appeared in *[Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Episode) "Ben 10,000 (Episode)")*, where he froze [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Future) "Vilgax (Future)") in water.
* In *[Ken 10](/wiki/Ken_10 "Ken 10")*, Arctiguana froze [Mot Snikrep](/wiki/Mot_Snikrep "Mot Snikrep") before [Ken](/wiki/Ken_Tennyson "Ken Tennyson") as [Buzzshock](/wiki/Buzzshock_(Classic) "Buzzshock (Classic)") could defeat him.


### Future (Ultimate Alien)


* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, [Ultimate Ben](/wiki/Ultimate_Ben "Ultimate Ben") used Arctiguana's ice breath twice to defeat [Eon](/wiki/Eon "Eon")'s [servants](/wiki/Eon%27s_Servants "Eon's Servants").


### Comics


* In *[The Once and Future Ben](/wiki/The_Once_and_Future_Ben "The Once and Future Ben")*, Arctiguana was used by both 10-year old Ben and Ben 10,000 to freeze [Vilgax 3000](/wiki/Vilgax_3000 "Vilgax 3000") and send him into the past.

Etymology
---------


Arctiguana's name is a combination of "arctic", referring to his ice powers, and "iguana", referring to his reptile-like appearance.



Trivia
------


* [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)") originally named this alien "Absolute Zero". However, it was changed to "Arctiguana" on 10-year-old [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s suggestion, as he found the original name to be boring.[[2]](#cite_note-Ben_10.2C000-6)
* In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Arctiguana speaks with a [Jamaican](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jamaica "wikipedia:Jamaica") accent and a windy sound is heard whenever he speaks.
	+ Arctiguana's voice becomes slightly higher when used by a younger version of Ben.[[1]](#cite_note-BA-1)[[5]](#cite_note-Store_23-10)
* Arctiguana is the result of re-purposing and re-coloring one of [Perk Upchuck](/wiki/Upchuck "Upchuck")'s concept arts. His design originally had an olive green color scheme, glowing orange spikes on his back, and wore the Omnitrix symbol on his torso.[[TP 1]](#cite_note-Upchuck-3) [Tom Perkins](/wiki/Tom_Perkins "Tom Perkins") used the concept design as he did not want it to go to waste.[[TP 2]](#cite_note-14)
* Like [Buzzshock](/wiki/Buzzshock_(Classic) "Buzzshock (Classic)"), Murk Upchuck, [Snare-oh](/wiki/Snare-oh "Snare-oh"), and [Frankenstrike](/wiki/Frankenstrike "Frankenstrike"), Arctiguana appeared in **Omniverse**, but he did not appear in *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*.
* The way Arctiguana uses his ice breath to hover and fly is similar to how Godzilla uses his nuclear breath to accomplish the same feat.
* In the DS and 3DS versions of *[Ben 10: Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse_(Video_Game) "Ben 10: Omniverse (Video Game)")* and *[Ben 10: Omniverse 2](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse_2 "Ben 10: Omniverse 2")*, Arctiguana walks on two legs rather than four.
* 11-year-old Arctiguana was slated to appear in *[Hot Stretch](/wiki/Hot_Stretch "Hot Stretch")* but was excluded for time.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-15)[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-16) Despite that, the design for 11-year-old Arctiguana would be used for the *Ben 10: Omniverse* video game and recolored for [Freezelizard](/wiki/Freezelizard "Freezelizard").
	+ Although Ben unlocked Arctiguana around the same time as [Feedback](/wiki/Feedback "Feedback"),[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-unlock1-12) it was never said which one was unlocked first, as the writers would only directly decide on it based on the needs of a story which touches on such matters.[[MW 1]](#cite_note-unlock2-13)
