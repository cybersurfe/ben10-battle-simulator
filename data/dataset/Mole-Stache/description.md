Appearance
----------


Mole-Stache is a rodentoid alien with a green and black jumpsuit in which his ears stick out and also has a pair of white gloves. He has a big blonde mustache and beaver-like teeth, as well as a slight potbelly. He also wears a green and white belt in which the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol is located.



Personality
-----------


Mole-Stache speaks in a stereotypical gentleman manner and tends to use high vocabulary.

Mole-Stache's mustache is free-forming and about as maneuverable as any limb, able to stretch and form itself into a variety of shapes. While doing so, he can perform with it feats like wrapping enemies up, forming hands, catching drafts, and carrying himself around. It can also be used as a parachute or propellers.


Mole-Stache appears to have skill in hand-to-hand combat with the mustache, as he easily defeated [Fistrick](/wiki/Fistrick "Fistrick") with it.[[1]](#cite_note-Breakpoint-1)


Mole-Stache can fly by twirling his mustache around like a helicopter.[[1]](#cite_note-Breakpoint-1)[[2]](#cite_note-3)


Mole-Stache's mustache is strong enough to lift a truck[[1]](#cite_note-Breakpoint-1) and a tree.[[3]](#cite_note-4) On his own and despite his small size, however, Mole-Stache is strong enough to carry a [Revonnahgander](/wiki/Revonnahgander "Revonnahgander") with his bare hands while flying and without any trouble.[[1]](#cite_note-Breakpoint-1)


Mole-Stache can dig into hard surfaces, much like a real mole.


If Mole-Stache's mustache is cut or shaved off, it would quickly regenerate.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-5)



Weaknesses
----------


 [![](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/6/68/BP_%28472%29.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/218?cb=20150815142739)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/6/68/BP_%28472%29.png/revision/latest?cb=20150815142739)  Strength Limit

  
Aside from his malleable mustache and derived powers, Mole-Stache has no visible skills, hence why [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") regarded him as a useless alien upon transforming into him for the first time.[[4]](#cite_note-Outbreak-6)


Mole-Stache can have trouble staying upright while carrying something as heavy as a truck with his mustache.[[1]](#cite_note-Breakpoint-1)



History
-------


### 1773


* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, 16-year-old [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") as Mole-Stache defeated young [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") with [Skurd](/wiki/Skurd "Skurd")'s help.


### Omniverse


* Mole-Stache first appeared in *[Outbreak](/wiki/Outbreak "Outbreak")*, where he was accidentally unlocked by [Driba](/wiki/Driba "Driba") and [Blukic](/wiki/Blukic "Blukic") when they tried to fix the malfunctioning [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") before they switched him into [The Worst](/wiki/The_Worst "The Worst").
* In *[Store 23](/wiki/Store_23 "Store 23")*, Mole-Stache was one of several aliens [Ben Prime](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was showcasing for [Ben 23](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Dimension_23) "Ben Tennyson (Dimension 23)").
* In *[No Honor Among Bros](/wiki/No_Honor_Among_Bros "No Honor Among Bros")*, Mole-Stache defeated [Yetta](/wiki/Yetta "Yetta") off-screen.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-Imgur-7)
* In *[Breakpoint](/wiki/Breakpoint "Breakpoint")*, Mole-Stache flew himself and [Rook](/wiki/Rook_Blonko "Rook Blonko") to safety and defeated [Fistrick](/wiki/Fistrick "Fistrick").
* In *[It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 1](/wiki/It%27s_a_Mad,_Mad,_Mad_Ben_World:_Part_1 "It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 1")*, Mole-Stache flew with [Nighty Knight](/wiki/Nighty_Knight "Nighty Knight") to find [Mad Rook](/wiki/Mad_Rook "Mad Rook") and met [Mad Harangue](/wiki/Mad_Harangue "Mad Harangue").
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Mole-Stache appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

Etymology
---------


Mole-Stache's name is a portmanteau of the words "mole", referring his appearance, and "mustache", which alludes to his ability to fight with his malleable mustache.



Trivia
------


* The design for Mole-Stache came from [Jerry](/wiki/Jerry "Jerry"), drawn by Steven Choi and Chap Yaep.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-8)
* In his first appearance, Mole-Stache shared the same voice as Ben due to the Omnitrix malfunctioning. The out-of-universe reason for this was because the crew originally did not envision him as a usable alien for later episodes.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-9)
	+ Mole-Stache's voice was eventually changed to a [British](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom "wikipedia:United Kingdom") accent in *[Breakpoint](/wiki/Breakpoint "Breakpoint")* because the crew liked how he turned out.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-10) Mole-Stache speaks with this accent because the British are stereotyped as behaving gentlemanly.
* Mole-Stache is one of four aliens unlocked by [Driba](/wiki/Driba "Driba") and [Blukic](/wiki/Blukic "Blukic"); the other three being [Walkatrout](/wiki/Walkatrout "Walkatrout"), [Pesky Dust](/wiki/Pesky_Dust "Pesky Dust"), and [The Worst](/wiki/The_Worst "The Worst").[[4]](#cite_note-Outbreak-6)
* [Ben 23](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Dimension_23) "Ben Tennyson (Dimension 23)") does not have an alternate counterpart to Mole-Stache in his [Omnitrix](/wiki/Hero_Watch "Hero Watch").[[5]](#cite_note-11)
* Mole-Stache was initially planned to appear in *[No Honor Among Bros](/wiki/No_Honor_Among_Bros "No Honor Among Bros")*, where he would master his powers during a fight with [Yetta](/wiki/Yetta "Yetta"), but the scene had to be cut for time.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-Imgur-7)[[TJ 1]](#cite_note-12)
* [Albedo](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo")'s [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") does not contain Mole-Stache's DNA.[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-13)
* There were no plans to feature Mole-Stache in the [reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)") because the crew envisioned him as being only being exclusive to the *[Ben 10: Omni-Charged](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omni-Charged "Ben 10: Omni-Charged")* game based on the show.[[DR 1]](#cite_note-14)
	+ PHL Collective requested for Mole-Stache's inclusion in *Omni-Charged*,[[DR 2]](#cite_note-15) partially because he worked well with the VR mechanics for the game.[[AC 1]](#cite_note-16)
