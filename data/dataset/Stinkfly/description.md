### Ben as Stinkfly


Stinkfly has a bug/insect body and four insect-like flat legs with two arms, whose hands are human-style ones with three clawed fingers. He has four small eyes on long stalks connected to his large head, which can all be moved around independently from each other. His wings are light green and very fragile with lines on them, and his limbs are a brown-greenish color. His tail is an ultra sensitive receptor, making it like an antenna.[[pop-up 1]](#cite_note-2)[[2]](#cite_note-3)


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Stinkfly wore a white sleeveless jumpsuit with black lining which covered most of his body including his head and tail, his hands had a black coloration similar to fingerless gloves, and his eyes were yellowish-orange. He wore the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol on the top of his forehead.


In *[A Change of Face](/wiki/A_Change_of_Face "A Change of Face")*, Stinkfly wore a black hat.


In *[Don't Drink the Water](/wiki/Don%27t_Drink_the_Water "Don't Drink the Water")*, 4-year-old Stinkfly (dubbed Stinkyfly) has a smaller body and pupils in his eyes.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Stinkfly's eyes were recolored green and his hands were the same color as his limbs. 16-year-old Stinkfly wore the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest in the **Ultimate Alien** intro, though it was on his forehead in *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*.


In *[The Forge of Creation](/wiki/The_Forge_of_Creation "The Forge of Creation")*, 10-year-old Stinkfly looked the same as in the original series, except his eyes and Omnitrix symbol were recolored green and his hands were the same color as his limbs.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, 11-year-old Stinkfly looks mostly the same as in **Ultimate Alien** but with bigger wings, lighter skin, larger eyes and pupils, a bigger body and tail, and a slightly redesigned stinger. His clothes are white with black stripes and is spread only until his forehead.


16-year-old Stinkfly looks the same as his 11-year-old self, except that he is taller and the white portions of his clothing are recolored green. Much like his younger self, the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol is located on his forehead.



### Kevin as Stinkfly


[Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)")'s Stinkfly looked the exact same as Ben's did in the original series, but with a darker color scheme and without an Omnitrix symbol.

1/15
Stinkfly possesses two insectoid wings that allow quick and agile flying, and can change directions at exceptional paces.


Stinkfly has 360-degree vision, as he is able to move two eyes at the back of his head and keep the other two eyes at the front of his head.


Stinkfly can excrete high pressured streams of liquid from the pollen ducts located inside of his eyestalks and mouth. These liquids can be both a flammable toxin or an immobilizing adhesive, instinctively choosing what type of slime it can be.[[MW 1]](#cite_note-4)


Besides liquids, Stinkfly can release poisonous and methane gas.[[DR 1]](#cite_note-5)


Stinkfly's slime has properties akin to fire extinguishers, meaning that it is capable of putting out fires.[[3]](#cite_note-6)


Stinkfly is able to suck up his slime from his mouth to pull himself upwards.[[4]](#cite_note-The_Krakken-7)


Stinkfly is accompanied by a natural odor-like stink that comes from the oils he secretes to keep his exoskeleton joints moving.[[pop-up 2]](#cite_note-8)[[5]](#cite_note-9)


Stinkfly's black tail is extremely sharp with a stinger-like end that can tear and rend through steel almost effortlessly.[[6]](#cite_note-10)


Stinkfly's exoskeletal body is fairly strong, allowing him to survive unscathed while being smashed through buildings and being caught inside a large explosion.[[7]](#cite_note-Secrets-11)


Stinkfly possesses enhanced dexterity, being able to operate a cell phone with his three-fingered hands.[[8]](#cite_note-12)


Stinkfly has great strength in proportion to his body, being able to carry heavy loads while maintaining steady flight.[[9]](#cite_note-13) His carrying power comes to its limit when holding something very heavy, shown when he could keep flying while [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") was holding onto him.[[7]](#cite_note-Secrets-11)


Stinkfly's claws and sharp-pointed legs allow him to cling onto solid surfaces.


As a toddler, Stinkfly was able to emit a powerful herbicide via flatulation, which is capable of killing plant-like creatures.[[10]](#cite_note-14)

1/10
A more minor inconvenience is Stinkfly's odor, which can be a problem if he is trying to hide from anything or anyone that can smell.


Although his exoskeleton body is fairly strong, Stinkfly's wings are very fragile. Water is especially a problem for him, as it can render him incapable of flight if he gets too much on his wings.[[4]](#cite_note-The_Krakken-7)[[11]](#cite_note-15) Furthermore, he cannot swim very well due to the small surface area of his limbs.


Stinkfly seems to have trouble carrying objects or passengers for extended periods of time, as he struggled with carrying [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") and [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") while flying to the temple of Ek Chuaj.[[12]](#cite_note-16)


Stinkfly can be tied in place by his tail.[[13]](#cite_note-17)


Stinkfly is vulnerable against electricity, as shown when he was shocked trying to attack [Phil](/wiki/Phil_Billings_(Classic) "Phil Billings (Classic)") as [Terroranchula](/wiki/Terroranchula "Terroranchula").[[14]](#cite_note-OPaPP2-18)


Being a [Lepidopterran](/wiki/Lepidopterran_(Classic) "Lepidopterran (Classic)"), Stinkfly's goo can be neutralized by the fog of his natural predator, the [Buglizard](/wiki/Buglizard_(Species) "Buglizard (Species)").[[15]](#cite_note-19)[[14]](#cite_note-OPaPP2-18)



History
-------


### Ben 10


* Stinkfly first appeared in *[Washington B.C.](/wiki/Washington_B.C. "Washington B.C.")*, where he saved [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") from the [Mutant Cockatiel](/wiki/Mutant_Cockatiel "Mutant Cockatiel") and defeated [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)").
* In *[The Krakken](/wiki/The_Krakken "The Krakken")*, Stinkfly battled [Jonah Melville](/wiki/Jonah_Melville "Jonah Melville") and his crew until the former escaped with an unconscious [Captain Shaw](/wiki/Captain_Shaw "Captain Shaw").
* In *[Kevin 11](/wiki/Kevin_11_(Episode) "Kevin 11 (Episode)")*, Stinkfly escaped the police with [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)").
* In *[Tourist Trap](/wiki/Tourist_Trap "Tourist Trap")*, Stinkfly scared a kid in a car and saved a truck driver from a fire.
* In *[Secrets](/wiki/Secrets "Secrets")*, Stinkfly defeated two [Drones](/wiki/Drones "Drones"), but was defeated and reverted by [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)").
	+ Also, Stinkfly appeared as one of the many alien templates used for Vilgax's army in an imaginary sequence.
* In *[The Big Tick](/wiki/The_Big_Tick "The Big Tick")*, Stinkfly freed [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") and Gwen from some tentacles and fought the [Great One](/wiki/Great_One "Great One")'s [fol](/wiki/Leader_Alien "Leader Alien")[low](/wiki/Interpreter_Alien "Interpreter Alien")[ers](/wiki/Enforcer_Alien "Enforcer Alien").
* In *[Framed](/wiki/Framed "Framed")*, Kevin used Stinkfly four times to escape from police and from Ben.
* In *[Ultimate Weapon](/wiki/Ultimate_Weapon "Ultimate Weapon")*, Stinkfly flew Gwen and Max to the temple of Ek Chuaj.
* In *[Camp Fear](/wiki/Camp_Fear "Camp Fear")*, Stinkfly flew over [Camp Opinicon](/wiki/Camp_Opinicon "Camp Opinicon") while Gwen dropped athlete's foot powder all over the spores that were created by the [Mycelium](/wiki/Mycelium "Mycelium") in order to destroy them.
* In *[Tough Luck](/wiki/Tough_Luck "Tough Luck")*, Stinkfly stopped a helicopter from crashing while Lucky Girl saved a man who fell off of it.
* In *[They Lurk Below](/wiki/They_Lurk_Below "They Lurk Below")*, Stinkfly saved Gwen and [Eddie](/wiki/Eddie_GrandSmith "Eddie GrandSmith") from drowning and escaped some [Cyber Squids](/wiki/Cyber_Squids "Cyber Squids"). After he fell in the water and couldn't fly, they made their way through some vents and found some speedboats.
* In *[Back with a Vengeance](/wiki/Back_with_a_Vengeance "Back with a Vengeance")*, Stinkfly appeared five times. Firstly, Stinkfly flew a man back to his boat. Secondly, he spat slime into Gwen's pudding as a prank. Later, he escaped [Kevin 11](/wiki/Kevin_11 "Kevin 11") before turning into [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)"). Then, he was briefly seen with Max and Gwen before reverting. Lastly, in the [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)"), Stinkfly escaped from Vilgax before running into Gwen and was forced to surrender after Kevin grabbed her.
* In *[Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Episode) "Ben 10,000 (Episode)")*, Stinkfly tried to help [Future](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)") [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)") fight [Future Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Future) "Aloysius Animo (Future)"), but was restrained by the former.
* In *[A Change of Face](/wiki/A_Change_of_Face "A Change of Face")*, Stinkfly stopped a fire caused by [Charmcaster](/wiki/Charmcaster_(Classic) "Charmcaster (Classic)") and was caught in her [spell](/wiki/Spells "Spells") before being knocked out of it by Gwen.
* In *[Merry Christmas](/wiki/Merry_Christmas "Merry Christmas")*, Stinkfly acted as a reindeer to pull Santa's sleigh.
* In *[Super Alien Hero Buddy Adventures](/wiki/Super_Alien_Hero_Buddy_Adventures "Super Alien Hero Buddy Adventures")*, Stinkfly and Gwen spied on [Abel North](/wiki/Abel_North "Abel North").
* In *[The Return](/wiki/The_Return "The Return")*, Stinkfly caught some escaped prisoners with Gwen and Max's help.
* In ***[Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix](/wiki/Ben_10:_Secret_of_the_Omnitrix "Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix")***, Stinkfly tried to chase after Dr. Animo but was switched into [Wildmutt](/wiki/Wildmutt "Wildmutt") due to an Omnitrix glitch.
* In *[Don't Drink the Water](/wiki/Don%27t_Drink_the_Water "Don't Drink the Water")*, 4-year-old Stinkfly (Stinkyfly) defeated [Hex](/wiki/Hex_(Classic) "Hex (Classic)")'s mutated plants.
* In *[Ben 10 vs. the Negative 10: Part 1](/wiki/Ben_10_vs._the_Negative_10:_Part_1 "Ben 10 vs. the Negative 10: Part 1")*, Stinkfly caught some criminals.
* In ***[Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens](/wiki/Ben_10:_Destroy_All_Aliens "Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens")***, Stinkfly appeared thrice. Firstly, Stinkfly attacked Ben in a dream. Later, Stinkfly battled the [Evil Way Big](/wiki/Evil_Way_Big_(Destroy_All_Aliens) "Evil Way Big (Destroy All Aliens)") only to change back after a short time. Finally, Stinkfly was defeated by [Retaliator](/wiki/Azmuth%27s_Father "Azmuth's Father") before the Omnitrix switched him into [Heatblast](/wiki/Heatblast_(Classic) "Heatblast (Classic)").
* In *[The Forge of Creation](/wiki/The_Forge_of_Creation "The Forge of Creation")*, Stinkfly was defeated by [Ultimate Aggregor](/wiki/Ultimate_Aggregor "Ultimate Aggregor").

Trivia
------


* Stinkfly was the first alien Ben named onscreen.[[16]](#cite_note-20)
* Stinkfly's slime feels like a mix of rubber, cement, and warm oatmeal.[[CN 1]](#cite_note-21)
* In ***[Destroy All Aliens](/wiki/Ben_10:_Destroy_All_Aliens "Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens")***, the texture of Stinkfly's head was taken from a plant, while the texture of his wings was taken from that of an actual insect's.[[AC 1]](#cite_note-22)
* Stinkfly speaks in a gravelly voice throughout his appearances in the Classic Timeline, though his voice was high-pitched in *[Don't Drink the Water](/wiki/Don%27t_Drink_the_Water "Don't Drink the Water")*.
