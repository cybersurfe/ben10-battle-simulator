Appearance
----------


### Ben as Four Arms


Four Arms is a humanoid alien that is approximately twelve feet tall, has well-developed muscles, two pairs of four-fingered arms, and red skin. A black stripe goes from his chin to his lower lip, and he has four eyes: a main pair and a smaller pair below them, while he lacks ears or nose are found on his head. He has whisker-like spikes on each forearm.


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Four Arms wore a white T-shirt with a black line going down it, black pants, and fingerless gloves. He wore the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol on his upper left shoulder. His eyes were yellow and he had no hair.


While when under the effects of a cold in *[Side Effects](/wiki/Side_Effects "Side Effects")*, his skin turns from bright red to a dark shade of orange, and he develops pungent hives in his armpits.


In *[Game Over](/wiki/Game_Over "Game Over")*, while inside [Sumo Slammers Smackdown](/wiki/Sumo_Slammers_(Classic) "Sumo Slammers (Classic)"), Four Arms wore brown shoulder pads and knee pads, grey armor under his shoulder pads as well as black pants. He wore the Omnitrix symbol on a hat on top of his head.


In *[Don't Drink the Water](/wiki/Don%27t_Drink_the_Water "Don't Drink the Water")*, 4-year-old Four Arms was slimmer than his 10-year-old self, and had a bigger head and bigger eyes.


In [Destroy All Aliens](/wiki/Ben_10:_Destroy_All_Aliens "Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens"), Four Arms remains the same except he had pale red fingernails and his clothing has some type of fabric on the black stripe.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, 16-year-old Four Arms wore gold wrist and ankle bands, a pair of black underwear that has a gold waistband, as well as two golden sashes that went across his chest. His eyes were recolored green, he had a black ponytail, and he wore the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In *[The Forge of Creation](/wiki/The_Forge_of_Creation "The Forge of Creation")*, 10-year-old Four Arms looks exactly the same as in the original series except the Omnitrix symbol was recolored green.


In *[Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, Four Arms, in addition to his clothes in **Ultimate Alien**, wore pants, fingerless gloves, and had tattoo-like stripes on his body, making him look somewhat similar to his original series design.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, 11-year-old Four Arms looks the same as his 10-year-old self in **Ultimate Alien**, except his eyes are recolored green, his skin is a lighter tone, and he has a second set of pectorals for his other set of arms.


16-year-old Four Arms has three short spikes on the top of his head, a mustache-like design, the black stripe on the top of his head is replaced with small spikes and the same second set of pectorals as his 11-year-old self. He has no hair and wears a black tank top-like shirt with one large green stripe in the middle, black pants, a green and white belt where the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol is located, and black fingerless gloves with green cuffs at the wrists.



### Kevin as Four Arms


[Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)")'s Four Arms looked the same as Ben's did in the original series, but with paler skin and no Omnitrix symbol.



### Gwen as Four Arms


Four Arms had darker skin, hair and clothes based on [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Gwen_10) "Gwen Tennyson (Gwen 10)")'s, and lacked the vertical black stripe sported by Ben's version. Just like Ben, Gwen's Four Arms wore the Omnitrix symbol on her upper left shoulder.



### Ben 10,000 as Four Arms


[Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)")'s Four Arms had a better-developed forehead, more muscle mass, a different T-shirt design and a pair of jutting incisors. He wore the Omnitrix symbol on his chest.



### Alpha as Four Arms


Four Arms looked roughly similar to Kevin's [Tetramand mutation](/wiki/Tetramand_Kevin "Tetramand Kevin"), only he had four arms rather than six. He had four red eyes, black hair, and black shorts. He wore a Red Corrupted Ultimatrix on his chest, and there were red lines spread across his body.

1/13
Four Arms' most prominent ability is his enhanced strength, being able to lift several times his own weight and punch through various resilient materials with ease. Since Four Arms is one of Ben's strongest aliens in the original series, he relied on him the most when fighting large and powerful foes. Technically speaking, Four Arms is physically stronger than [Methanosians](/wiki/Methanosian "Methanosian") and [Vaxasaurians](/wiki/Vaxasaurian_(Classic) "Vaxasaurian (Classic)") at base size.[[DM 1]](#cite_note-3)


Four Arms' strength allows him to use anything heavy as a weapon, from big rocks he can throw several feet away, to beams and pillars that can be used as clubs. His great strength even extends to his jaws and bite, allowing him to bite metal and damage it.[[3]](#cite_note-4) He even develops two sharp fangs between his lower jaws as he gets older.


The older Four Arms grows, the stronger he becomes.[[4]](#cite_note-5) By age 16, Four Arms has become strong to the point where he was able to overpower [Looma Red Wind](/wiki/Looma_Red_Wind "Looma Red Wind"), the princess of [Khoros](/wiki/Khoros_(Classic) "Khoros (Classic)"), despite female Tetramands being generally stronger than their male counterparts.[[5]](#cite_note-6)[[6]](#cite_note-7)


Four Arms can create strong shockwaves by clapping all four of his hands together.[[7]](#cite_note-Game_Over-8)[[8]](#cite_note-MW-9)[[9]](#cite_note-HU-10) Ben learned this ability from [Tini](/wiki/Tini "Tini").[[pop-up 1]](#cite_note-11)[[7]](#cite_note-Game_Over-8)[[merch 1]](#cite_note-12)


Four Arms can alternatively create shockwaves by slamming his fists on the ground.[[8]](#cite_note-MW-9)[[10]](#cite_note-The_Alliance-13)


Four Arms can jump several times his own height, at least seven stories up, in a single bound.[[11]](#cite_note-14)[[10]](#cite_note-The_Alliance-13)[[1]](#cite_note-BaDGtMS-1)


Four Arms has enhanced reflexes, as he was able to catch and redirect missiles[[12]](#cite_note-15) in a single motion.[[13]](#cite_note-16) Four Arms is also capable of swimming.[[8]](#cite_note-MW-9)


Four Arms can climb up walls by pushing his fingers into them to make hand holds.[[14]](#cite_note-17)


Four Arms has a high level of enhanced durability due to his tough and thorny skin,[[pop-up 2]](#cite_note-18)[[7]](#cite_note-Game_Over-8) being able to casually take missiles from a [drone](/wiki/Drones "Drones")[[15]](#cite_note-19) and a slash to the chest from [Malware](/wiki/Malware "Malware") while barely feeling any pain.[[16]](#cite_note-20)


In correlation to his durable skin, Four Arms is highly resistant to fire, such as that generated by a [Pyronite](/wiki/Pyronite_(Classic) "Pyronite (Classic)").[[17]](#cite_note-21) His skin also makes him laser-proof.


When infected with a cold, Four Arms can sneeze out sprays of slimy snot from his mouth, and although disgusting, this can be useful against insects.[[18]](#cite_note-SE-22)

1/5
Four Arms' bulk can sometimes become a disadvantage, as all the extra muscle mass makes him relatively slower than other opponents and his immense size makes him an easy target for enemy attacks.


Four Arms' size can make moving through areas designed for small species, like the [Rust Buckets](/wiki/Rust_Bucket_(Classic) "Rust Bucket (Classic)") 1[[19]](#cite_note-23) and [2](/wiki/Rust_Bucket_2 "Rust Bucket 2"),[[20]](#cite_note-24) difficult.


Four Arms can feel pain from electricity, such as those generated by [Nosedeenians](/wiki/Nosedeenian "Nosedeenian")[[21]](#cite_note-25) and [Gimlinopithecus](/wiki/Gimlinopithecus "Gimlinopithecus").[[9]](#cite_note-HU-10)


Four Arms can get drowsy by [Porcupine](/wiki/Porcupine "Porcupine")'s quills.[[22]](#cite_note-26)


If under the effects of a cold, Four Arms' strength and stamina are severely reduced.[[18]](#cite_note-SE-22)


Four Arms can feel great discomfort while being attacked by a swarm of fire ants.[[18]](#cite_note-SE-22)



History
-------


### Ben 10


* Four Arms first appeared in *[Washington B.C.](/wiki/Washington_B.C. "Washington B.C.")*, where he defeated [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)")'s [mutant mammoth](/wiki/Living_Mammoth "Living Mammoth") and failed to save [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") from the [mutant cockatiel](/wiki/Mutant_Cockatiel "Mutant Cockatiel").
* In *[The Krakken](/wiki/The_Krakken "The Krakken")*, Four Arms pretended to be a sea monster to scare Gwen. He was then attacked by the [Krakken](/wiki/Krakken "Krakken") but escaped it.
* In *[Kevin 11](/wiki/Kevin_11_(Episode) "Kevin 11 (Episode)")*, Four Arms fought and defeated [Tetramand Kevin](/wiki/Tetramand_Kevin "Tetramand Kevin").
* In *[Tourist Trap](/wiki/Tourist_Trap "Tourist Trap")*, Four Arms played with a giant rubber band ball but accidentally caused some damage all around Sparksville with it.
* In *[The Alliance](/wiki/The_Alliance "The Alliance")*, Four Arms fought [Rojo](/wiki/Rojo "Rojo"), [her gang](/wiki/Rojo%27s_Gang "Rojo's Gang") and [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)")'s [Drones](/wiki/Drones "Drones"), but had to abandon the fight to take an injured [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") to the hospital.
* In *[Lucky Girl](/wiki/Lucky_Girl "Lucky Girl")*, Four Arms tried to save some people at a museum, freed Gwen from some branches, battled the stone statues that [Hex](/wiki/Hex_(Classic) "Hex (Classic)") had brought to life, and threw the latter into a coffin.
* In *[Side Effects](/wiki/Side_Effects "Side Effects")*, Four Arms, with a cold, pretended to be a giant bug to scare Gwen. He then saved [Councilwoman Liang](/wiki/Councilwoman_Liang "Councilwoman Liang") from some black widow spiders and accidentally destroyed [Clancy](/wiki/Clancy "Clancy")'s home while being attacked by ants.
* In *[Secrets](/wiki/Secrets "Secrets")*, Four Arms destroyed Vilgax's machine and was about to destroy a drone before the malfunctioning [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") switched him into [Ghostfreak](/wiki/Ghostfreak "Ghostfreak").
	+ Also, Four Arms appeared as one of the many alien templates used for Vilgax's army in an imaginary sequence.
* In *[The Big Tick](/wiki/The_Big_Tick "The Big Tick")*, Four Arms was defeated by the [Great One](/wiki/Great_One "Great One").
* In *[Framed](/wiki/Framed "Framed")*, [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") as Four Arms rampaged in a street. Later, Ben as Four Arms fought Kevin's [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)"), [Heatblast](/wiki/Heatblast_(Classic) "Heatblast (Classic)"), [Stinkfly](/wiki/Stinkfly_(Classic) "Stinkfly (Classic)"), himself and [Kevin 11](/wiki/Kevin_11 "Kevin 11").
* In *[Grudge Match](/wiki/Grudge_Match "Grudge Match")*, Four Arms briefly battled [Technorg](/wiki/Technorg "Technorg") before he and Kevin 11 were taken to [Slix Vigma](/wiki/Slix_Vigma "Slix Vigma") and tied together. The two of them then battled Technorg again.
* In *[The Galactic Enforcers](/wiki/The_Galactic_Enforcers_(Episode) "The Galactic Enforcers (Episode)")*, Four Arms fought [SixSix](/wiki/SixSix_(Classic) "SixSix (Classic)") and [Vulkanus](/wiki/Vulkanus "Vulkanus") and helped [Tini](/wiki/Tini "Tini") save a falling building.

Four Arms is a playable alien in the game.



### [Ben 10: Protector of Earth](/wiki/Ben_10:_Protector_of_Earth "Ben 10: Protector of Earth")


Four Arms is one of the first aliens available. He also has unlockable Dark Heroes and DNA Force skin. His special attack (which involves throwing a rock at his opponent) is the most powerful out of all the other playable characters in the game.



### [Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction](/wiki/Ben_10_Ultimate_Alien:_Cosmic_Destruction "Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction")


Four Arms is an exclusive character for the PS3 version of the game, similar to how [Rath](/wiki/Rath_(Classic) "Rath (Classic)") is an exclusive for the Xbox 360. Both have super strength as their main power. Four Arms can be unlocked by either completing the game or by entering the cheat code, "classic".


Since he is treated as an exclusive alien to a certain console, Four Arms is not required to be used at all in order to complete the game.



### [Ben 10: Galactic Racing](/wiki/Ben_10:_Galactic_Racing "Ben 10: Galactic Racing")


Four Arms is a playable racer in the game.



### [Ben 10: Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse_(Video_Game) "Ben 10: Omniverse (Video Game)")


Four Arms is a playable alien in the game (by both 11-year-old and 16-year-old Ben).

Four Arms is a playable alien in the game.



### [Punch Time Explosion XL](/wiki/Punch_Time_Explosion_XL "Punch Time Explosion XL")


Four Arms is the "Punch Time Explosion" attack and "Synergy Attacks" for [Young Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)").



### [Project Exonaut](/wiki/Project_Exonaut "Project Exonaut")


Four Arms is a playable Exosuit of the Atlas Brigade on Project Exonaut. His Weapon Mod increases the speed of the Ballista. Four Arms is available for purchase at level 30 for 2000 coins.

Trivia
------


* Four Arms was first envisioned as a superhero named [StrongGuy](/wiki/Ben_10_(Pre-Production)#StrongGuy "Ben 10 (Pre-Production)"). [Dave Johnson](/wiki/Dave_Johnson "Dave Johnson") designed Four Arms for the original series, which became the first design to get greenlit on the show.[[DJ 1]](#cite_note-greenlit-27)
	+ The design that was greenlit had few changes from Dave's original sketch,[[DJ 1]](#cite_note-greenlit-27) the most important one being that he had to make the secondary arms smaller than the primary arms so that they would not jut out.[[DJ 2]](#cite_note-Inspiration-28)
* Four Arms resembles [Machamp](http://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Machamp_(Pok%C3%A9mon) "bulbapedia:Machamp (Pokémon)") from the **[Pokémon](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon "wikipedia:Pokémon")** franchise, [Mehrunes Dagon](https://elderscrolls.fandom.com/wiki/Mehrunes_Dagon "w:c:elderscrolls:Mehrunes Dagon") from the **Elder Scrolls** franchise, and both [Goro](https://mortalkombat.fandom.com/wiki/Goro "w:c:mortalkombat:Goro") and [Kintaro](https://mortalkombat.fandom.com/wiki/Kintaro "w:c:mortalkombat:Kintaro") from the **[Mortal Kombat](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mortal_Kombat_(series) "wikipedia:Mortal Kombat (series)")** franchise, the latter of which is more noticeable able with his appearance in **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**.
	+ Despite that, Dave noted that he did not take inspiration from pre-existing characters when drawing Four Arms.[[DJ 2]](#cite_note-Inspiration-28)
* Four Arms is the first alien used by 11-year-old [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") in **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**.
* Four Arms needs twice as much deodorant as a [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)").[[AC 1]](#cite_note-GO-29)
* Four Arms was initially planned to be one of the playable aliens in the *[Game Creator](/wiki/Game_Creator "Game Creator")*, but was soon removed from the game entirely when **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")** aired.
* In the pre-production stage of **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Four Arms originally had a pair of jutting incisors, a different outfit equipped with shoulder pads and the Omnitrix symbol on his chest, as seen in [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt")'s roughs.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-30) Someone else made the final model based on them.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-31)
* In *[Goodbye and Good Riddance](/wiki/Goodbye_and_Good_Riddance "Goodbye and Good Riddance")*, a kid at Ben's school called Four Arms "Quad Hands" until [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") corrected him, similar to how [Jimmy](/wiki/Jimmy_Jones "Jimmy Jones") thought [Jetray](/wiki/Jetray_(Classic) "Jetray (Classic)")'s name was "Jeffrey" until he was corrected by Ben in *[Fame](/wiki/Fame "Fame")*.
* 11-year-old Four Arms is replaced with [Gutrot](/wiki/Gutrot "Gutrot") in the opening intro of **Omniverse**'s Arc 5. In the same intro, 11-year-old Four Arms is also replaced with 11-year-old [Blitzwolfer](/wiki/Blitzwolfer "Blitzwolfer"), specifically in the part where the former originally appeared alongside the 11-year-old versions of [Eye Guy](/wiki/Eye_Guy "Eye Guy"), [Grey Matter](/wiki/Grey_Matter_(Classic) "Grey Matter (Classic)"), and [Snare-oh](/wiki/Snare-oh "Snare-oh").
* Four Arms' transformation sequence was the first one to be designed.[[AC 1]](#cite_note-GO-29)
* [Richard McGonagle](/wiki/Richard_McGonagle "Richard McGonagle") considers Four Arms to be his toughest cartoon role, stating that he sounds like he had just gargled glass.[[AC 2]](#cite_note-YT-32)
	+ [Nolan North](/wiki/Nolan_North "Nolan North") auditioned to voice Four Arms in the original series, but lost the role to Richard.
