Appearance
----------


Terraspin is a green, [turtle](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turtle "wikipedia:Turtle")-like alien with large flipper-like arms, and stumpy legs. The tips of his arms also have three holes each, which can extend finger-like claws to use as fingers.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Terraspin had eight holes in his shell and a circular body shape.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Terraspin looks mostly the same, except he now has six holes in his shell, brown spots on his upper legs, toes, and shoulders, and a hexagonal and slightly larger body shape. The black stripes under his eyes are thicker, his legs are shorter, and his feet have each gained three toes.


Terraspin wears the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)")/[Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his chest.

Terraspin can create devastating wind blasts and tornados.[[4]](#cite_note-5) When doing so, he retracts his head into his shell, opens a hatch to see, his fins become triangular, and his legs combine into a similar shape. His shell stays static and suspended while his limbs rotate at high speeds.


The holes in Terraspin's shell eject powerful winds, as well as the reverse, creating strong vacuums. He can use his winds to fly, blowing air to hover over a surface and using his rotating limbs as propellers.


Terraspin's wind blasts are powerful enough to put out fires[[5]](#cite_note-6) and redirect objects.[[6]](#cite_note-7)[[DM 1]](#cite_note-8)


Terraspin possesses enhanced strength, as he was able to hold a giant robot with his bare hands while spinning,[[7]](#cite_note-RG-9) charge into a building by busting down a door,[[8]](#cite_note-RotS-10) punch a [mutant seagull](/wiki/Mutant_Seagull/UA "Mutant Seagull/UA"),[[9]](#cite_note-11) and carry a [Tetramand](/wiki/Tetramand_(Classic) "Tetramand (Classic)") on his back while keeping steady flight.[[10]](#cite_note-MHR-12)


Even though Terraspin has flippers rather than hands, he has enhanced dexterity as he can hold poles without fingers.[[11]](#cite_note-TUH-13)


Terraspin's arms become very sharp while spinning.[[10]](#cite_note-MHR-12)


Terraspin is immune to poisonous gas.[[12]](#cite_note-14) He is also selectively immune to both [spells](/wiki/Spells "Spells") and [mana](/wiki/Mana "Mana"), as demonstrated by [Galapagus](/wiki/Galapagus "Galapagus")[[13]](#cite_note-15) and [Ultimate Aggregor](/wiki/Ultimate_Aggregor "Ultimate Aggregor").[[14]](#cite_note-16)


Terraspin's shell possesses enhanced durability.[[3]](#cite_note-EfA-3)[[10]](#cite_note-MHR-12)


Terraspin can extend the black holes at the tips of his flippers into finger-like claws, as demonstrated by Galapagus.[[3]](#cite_note-EfA-3)


Terraspin is capable of using [magic](/wiki/Magic_(Classic) "Magic (Classic)"),[[DM 2]](#cite_note-17) as demonstrated by [Addwaitya](/wiki/Addwaitya "Addwaitya").[[15]](#cite_note-18)[[16]](#cite_note-19) However, Ben cannot stay transformed as Terraspin long enough to learn it without Master Control.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-20)

1/9
If he gets stuck to something and cannot spin his arms and legs, or if any the holes on his chest get blocked, Terraspin's wind powers will decrease.


If enemies jump onto Terraspin while he attempts to fly, he has to attempt to shake them off, making his flight unsteady.[[10]](#cite_note-MHR-12)


There are certain things that Terraspin's wind blasts are not strong enough to break, such as [Rook Ben](/wiki/Rook_Ben "Rook Ben")'s bi'nthak.[[17]](#cite_note-21)


Also while flying, Terraspin's arms become very sharp while spinning, making him essentially a spinning blade, which may cause unintentional damage.[[10]](#cite_note-MHR-12)


Unless he is flying, Terraspin's body is cumbersome and not very agile, as [Attea](/wiki/Attea "Attea") claims if he wanted to run, [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") picked the wrong species to turn into.[[18]](#cite_note-22) The position of his face also gives him a very big blind spot and he has to turn his entire body around to see behind him.[[19]](#cite_note-23)


If a small object is jammed in Terraspin's shell, it can cause him to stop in order to remove the obstruction. This also appears to cause him great discomfort, as he describes the ordeal to be "really not comfortable".[[11]](#cite_note-TUH-13)


Terraspin can be affected by magic if he is not using his magic immunity.[[20]](#cite_note-24)


Terraspin can easily slip on certain surfaces, such as water.[[8]](#cite_note-RotS-10)


If Terraspin spins his whole body while holding onto something at high speed, it would leave Ben very dizzy as a result when he reverts back to [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)").[[7]](#cite_note-RG-9)



History
-------


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Escape from Aggregor](/wiki/Escape_from_Aggregor "Escape from Aggregor")*, Terraspin's DNA was sampled and put in the [Codon Stream](/wiki/Codon_Stream "Codon Stream") when the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") scanned [Galapagus](/wiki/Galapagus "Galapagus")' DNA.﻿
* Terraspin first appeared in *[Reflected Glory](/wiki/Reflected_Glory "Reflected Glory")*, where he fought and destroyed a giant robot created by the [Forever Knights](/wiki/Forever_Knights "Forever Knights").
* In *[The Forge of Creation](/wiki/The_Forge_of_Creation "The Forge of Creation")*, Terraspin defeated some [Aggrebots](/wiki/Aggrebots "Aggrebots") on the [Rust Bucket 3](/wiki/Rust_Bucket_3 "Rust Bucket 3").
* In *[Absolute Power: Part 1](/wiki/Absolute_Power:_Part_1 "Absolute Power: Part 1")*, Terraspin blew [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") into a wall, but she used a [spell](/wiki/Spells "Spells") to slow him down, making him powerless and forcing him to switch into [NRG](/wiki/NRG "NRG").
* In *[Revenge of the Swarm](/wiki/Revenge_of_the_Swarm "Revenge of the Swarm")*, Terraspin, Gwen, and [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") broke into [Victor](/wiki/Victor_Validus "Victor Validus")'s old lab and met [Egor](/wiki/Egor "Egor").
* In *[Basic Training](/wiki/Basic_Training "Basic Training")*, Terraspin put out a fire in [Magister Hulka](/wiki/Magister_Hulka "Magister Hulka")'s barracks.
* In *[Simian Says](/wiki/Simian_Says "Simian Says")*, Terraspin briefly battled some [DNAlien](/wiki/DNAliens "DNAliens") [Arachnichimps](/wiki/Arachnichimp "Arachnichimp") until he reverted.
* In *[The Widening Gyre](/wiki/The_Widening_Gyre "The Widening Gyre")*, Terraspin fought some [mutated seagulls](/wiki/Mutant_Seagull_(Ultimate_Alien) "Mutant Seagull (Ultimate Alien)").
* In *[Inspector #13](/wiki/Inspector_13_(Episode) "Inspector 13 (Episode)")*, Terraspin blew all of [Inspector 13](/wiki/Inspector_13 "Inspector 13")'s bombs away to get [Julie](/wiki/Julie_Yamamoto "Julie Yamamoto") to safety.

* In *[Catfight](/wiki/Catfight "Catfight")*, Terraspin battled Princess Looma until Attea used the [Omnitrix Remote Control](/wiki/Omnitrix_Remote_Control "Omnitrix Remote Control") to change him into [Bullfrag](/wiki/Bullfrag "Bullfrag").
* In *[Universe vs. Tennyson](/wiki/Universe_vs._Tennyson "Universe vs. Tennyson")*, Terraspin defeated [Fistina](/wiki/Fistina "Fistina").
* In *[Rook Tales](/wiki/Rook_Tales "Rook Tales")*, Terraspin failed to take off Young One's tail.
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Terraspin appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.

Etymology
---------


Terraspin's name is a portmanteau of [terrapin](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terrapin "wikipedia:Terrapin"), which is a turtle or [tortoise](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tortoise "wikipedia:Tortoise"), as well as "spin," which alludes to his ability to do so.



Trivia
------


* Whenever Terraspin talks in **Omniverse**, a fan sound is played in the background.
* Terraspin and the other Andromeda aliens have their powers related to a certain element; for Terraspin, it is wind.
* Terraspin was the [Alien of the Month](/wiki/Alien_of_the_Month "Alien of the Month") in March 2011.
* Terraspin went through many name changes during development, but the early names were all shut down by the writer's legal team.[[PD 1]](#cite_note-link_1-25)
	+ Eventually, however, Terraspin was named by Kathleen David, the wife of writer [Peter David](/wiki/Peter_David "Peter David").[[PD 1]](#cite_note-link_1-25)
* Terraspin was designed by Steven Choi and Chap Yaep.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-26)
* Terraspin resembles, and looks roughly similar to, the Japanese monster [Gamera](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gamera "wikipedia:Gamera"); both of them are turtle-like in appearance and can draw into their shells and spin as either an attack or a form of flight.
* Terraspin did not appear in *[Where the Magic Happens](/wiki/Where_the_Magic_Happens "Where the Magic Happens")* because Ben simply did not know said transformation would have been particularly useful in the magical environment of [Legerdomain](/wiki/Legerdomain "Legerdomain") at the time.[[DM 3]](#cite_note-27)
* [Ben 23](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Dimension_23) "Ben Tennyson (Dimension 23)") has an alternate counterpart of Terraspin.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-28)
* When designing a version of [Shellhead](/wiki/Shellhead_(Tom_Perkins) "Shellhead (Tom Perkins)") in 2020, [Tom Perkins](/wiki/Tom_Perkins "Tom Perkins") felt the franchise did not need another turtle-like alien like Terraspin.[[TP 1]](#cite_note-29)
