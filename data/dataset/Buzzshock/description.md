Appearance
----------


### Ben as Buzzshock


Buzzshock's appearance is similar to an [Earth](/wiki/Earth_(Classic) "Earth (Classic)") dry cell. He is black with green eyes, a large lightning bolt-like stripe spanning from where his neck starts to where his legs start on his front and back sides, and a positive (+) symbol and negative (-) symbol on his back.


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Buzzshock's lightning bolt stripe was white, he had a hole on his head that could release electricity, and his [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol is located on his chest.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Buzzshock looked mostly the same as he did in the original series, except the hole on his head was replaced with a grey spot (making him better resemble a battery) and his [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol was recolored green.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Buzzshock has two bolts on each arm, the lightning bolt stripe is recolored green, and the top of his head is now shaped like a battery, with the top colored light green. His head is now more prominent, as his chin actually sticks out rather than being a part of a long, one-piece body, and his eyes are now almond-shaped instead of being circular. He wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on the top of his head.



### Ken as Buzzshock


[Ken](/wiki/Ken_Tennyson "Ken Tennyson")'s Buzzshock looked the same as [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)")'s Buzzshock in the original series.



### Bad Ben as Bad Buzzshock


[Bad Ben](/wiki/Bad_Ben "Bad Ben") as Buzzshock looks identical to [Ben Prime](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s Buzzshock in **Omniverse**, save for all the green parts being recolored mint green.

--------------------

* ![]()Electric Teleportation
* ![]()Sphoeroid Slime Conductivity
1/6
Buzzshock has the ability to manipulate green[[note 1]](#cite_note-4) electricity, and can pass through any electrical source using a form of enhanced speed.[[4]](#cite_note-TT-5)[[5]](#cite_note-6)


Buzzshock can control electricity and can inhabit electrical devices, controlling them from the inside, and in some cases powering them. However, he can also cause electrical technology to malfunction and fry when he passes through them. He can animate and possess electrical machines as a result.[[4]](#cite_note-TT-5)


Buzzshock has the capability to duplicate himself when he is severed, more specifically when he is cut in half or has enough power, as demonstrated by other Nosedeenians.[[4]](#cite_note-TT-5) However, he must first learn how and train himself in order to do it.[[MW 1]](#cite_note-7)


Unlike other [Nosedeenians](/wiki/Nosedeenian_(Classic) "Nosedeenian (Classic)"), Buzzshock's speech is understandable to other races.[[6]](#cite_note-MM-8)


Buzzshock has enhanced agility, as shown when he jumped over [Phil](/wiki/Phil_Billings_(Classic) "Phil Billings (Classic)") when he attacked him and landed on both feet.[[6]](#cite_note-MM-8)


Buzzshock can electrify liquids, such as a [Sphoeroid](/wiki/Sphoeroid "Sphoeroid")'s slime, to attack enemies.[[7]](#cite_note-9)


Buzzshock can use his powers to seamlessly fly, leaving an electric trail behind.[[3]](#cite_note-ATTWB-3)[[2]](#cite_note-TCoM-2)


Buzzshock is surprisingly strong for his size, being able to lift a [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)") at least by their underwear.[[4]](#cite_note-TT-5)


Buzzshock can emit a sonic scream capable of shattering glass, as demonstrated by other Nosedeenians.[[4]](#cite_note-TT-5)



Weaknesses
----------


* ![]()Small Size
1/1
Buzzshock's power can be taken away by fire, or when he is trapped in something made of a non-conductive material, as demonstrated with other Nosedeenians.[[4]](#cite_note-TT-5)


Buzzshock can be insulated with rubber-sheeted blankets, as demonstrated with other Nosedeenians.[[1]](#cite_note-AJftP-1)


If Buzzshock runs out of energy, he will fall asleep, as demonstrated with other Nosedeenians.[[1]](#cite_note-AJftP-1)


Buzzshock's energy can be absorbed by a species such as [Conductoids](/wiki/Conductoid "Conductoid"), as demonstrated with other Nosedeenians.[[1]](#cite_note-AJftP-1)


Buzzshock's small size can be a disadvantage against a bigger, stronger opponent like Phil.[[6]](#cite_note-MM-8)



History
-------


### Omniverse


* In *[Max's Monster](/wiki/Max%27s_Monster "Max's Monster")*, Buzzshock was unlocked by [Phil](/wiki/Phil_Billings_(Classic) "Phil Billings (Classic)") so he could feed off him. He then powered up a [Null Void Projector](/wiki/Null_Void_Projector "Null Void Projector") to defeat Phil and send him back to the [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)").
* In *[Cough It Up](/wiki/Cough_It_Up "Cough It Up")*, Buzzshock defeated [SixSix](/wiki/SixSix_(Classic) "SixSix (Classic)"), but was defeated by [SevenSeven](/wiki/SevenSeven "SevenSeven").
* In *[Rook Tales](/wiki/Rook_Tales "Rook Tales")*, Buzzshock failed to take off [Young One](/wiki/Rook_Ben "Rook Ben")'s tail.
* In *[The Color of Monkey](/wiki/The_Color_of_Monkey "The Color of Monkey")*, Buzzshock battled [Suemungousaur](/wiki/Suemungousaur "Suemungousaur").


### Future (Original)


* Buzzshock first appeared in *[Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Episode) "Ben 10,000 (Episode)")*, where he defeated [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic_Future) "Aloysius Animo (Classic Future)").
* In *[Ken 10](/wiki/Ken_10 "Ken 10")*, [Ken](/wiki/Ken_Tennyson "Ken Tennyson") as Buzzshock attempted to fight [Mot Snikrep](/wiki/Mot_Snikrep "Mot Snikrep") before the latter was frozen by [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)") as [Arctiguana](/wiki/Arctiguana "Arctiguana"). Later, Ken as Buzzshock briefly battled [Kevin 11,000](/wiki/Kevin_11,000 "Kevin 11,000") before the former reverted.


### Bad Ben's Timeline


* In *[And Then There Was Ben](/wiki/And_Then_There_Was_Ben "And Then There Was Ben")*, [Bad](/wiki/Bad_Ben "Bad Ben") Buzzshock chased an Omnitrixless [Ben Prime](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") only to be sent back to his own timeline by [No Watch Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(No_Watch_Timeline) "Ben Tennyson (No Watch Timeline)") as [Clockwork](/wiki/Clockwork "Clockwork").


### Comics


* In *[Black Knight's Flight](/wiki/Black_Knight%27s_Flight "Black Knight's Flight")*, Buzzshock weakened [the Black Knight](/wiki/Enoch "Enoch").

Trivia
------


* Buzzshock was designed by [Dave Johnson](/wiki/Dave_Johnson "Dave Johnson") in the original series.[[TP 1]](#cite_note-10) Buzzshock's **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")** design originally had a more rotund physique as well as a positive (+) or negative (-) symbol on either his chest or forehead.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-11)
* Despite appearing in the **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")** intro, Buzzshock was unavailable for Ben until *[Max's Monster](/wiki/Max%27s_Monster "Max's Monster")*.
	+ Before this, Buzzshock appeared in the non-canon **Ultimate Alien** comic *[Black Knight's Flight](/wiki/Black_Knight%27s_Flight "Black Knight's Flight")*.
	+ Buzzshock and [Rath](/wiki/Rath_(Classic) "Rath (Classic)") are the only aliens to have been unlocked by electromagnetic interference.
* In **Omniverse**, Buzzshock speaks with an extremely high-pitched voice, similar to one an ordinary [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)") being has after inhaling helium.
* Like [Frankenstrike](/wiki/Frankenstrike "Frankenstrike"), [Snare-oh](/wiki/Snare-oh "Snare-oh"), [Murk Upchuck](/wiki/Upchuck "Upchuck"), and [Arctiguana](/wiki/Arctiguana "Arctiguana"), Buzzshock appeared in **Omniverse**, but he did not appear in *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*.
* Buzzshock is one of five aliens featured in **Omniverse** who generate green electricity; the others being [Upgrade](/wiki/Upgrade_(Classic) "Upgrade (Classic)"), Frankenstrike, [Brainstorm](/wiki/Brainstorm "Brainstorm"), and [Nanomech](/wiki/Nanomech "Nanomech").
