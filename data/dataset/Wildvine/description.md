### Ben as Wildvine


Wildvine is a humanoid plant with one eye in the middle of his face, five vine-like legs, four long fingers on his hands, flytrap-shaped flaps with teeth covering his head, and pods on his back that he can use as explosives or smoke bombs.


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Wildvine had green skin, green teeth, five legs, the eye on his head was blue, and he had secondary eyes of the same color on each of his shoulders.[[TP 1]](#cite_note-2) He wore the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In *[Back with a Vengeance](/wiki/Back_with_a_Vengeance "Back with a Vengeance")*, Wildvine wore a yellow raincoat.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Wildvine looked mostly the same as before, except his skin was recolored brownish-green, his flytrap teeth were recolored black, and his primary eye, secondary eyes, and [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol were all recolored green.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Wildvine regains the green skin color from the original series, he now has only four legs, his teeth were recolored white, and his fingers are much shorter and thicker. The secondary eyes on his shoulders are replaced with patches of light green similar to those on his hands. He also now has a blue tongue, his teeth are more neat and his chin and flytrap teeth are bigger. 16-year-old Wildvine wears the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on a green and white belt.


In *[Breakpoint](/wiki/Breakpoint "Breakpoint")*, 16-year-old Wildvine wore two red and white wristbands on his wrists, a white sweatband on his forehead, a white gym towel around his neck, and a larger tan belt over his Omnitrix belt.


11-year-old [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") as Wildvine in **Omniverse** looks the same as his 16-year-old self, but wears the original Omnitrix symbol (which has been recolored green) on his chest to reflect his original appearance.



### Ken as Wildvine


[Ken](/wiki/Ken_Tennyson "Ken Tennyson")'s Wildvine looked the same as 10-year-old Wildvine in the original series.



### Albedo as Negative Wildvine


[Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Wildvine looks just like 16-year-old Wildvine in **Omniverse**, but is greenish-brown with a red eye and his flytrap teeth are black. Negative Wildvine wears a red and white belt with a red [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Recreated) "Ultimatrix (Recreated)") symbol on it.



### Gwen 10 as Wildvine


[Gwen 10](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Gweniverse) "Gwen Tennyson (Gweniverse)") as Wildvine looks similar to her male counterpart, but her body is shaped like a dress. Her fly trap is much larger and she has bulb-like hair with a ponytail, along with dark green lips. She has a skirt-like structure on her waist which covers the top of her leg tendrils. She lacks the spikes that Ben's Wildvine has on his arms, legs, and chest and she has one glowing pink eye. She wears the Omnitrix symbol, which is the same color as her eye, on her stomach.

Wildvine can grow and alter the size of his body like a real plant but also retract it again at will. He can also grow and extend his arms, legs and fingers into vine-like tentacles to grab and restrain others. He is also extremely flexible due to his plant-like body. His fingers can stick to walls, which allow him to swing around.


Wildvine's vines are strong enough to lift two weights while supporting himself. He can also increase the size of them.[[2]](#cite_note-BP-3)


Wildvine can merge with plant life, including [Earth](/wiki/Earth "Earth") trees. He can also change his color in order to camouflage better. He can make body parts, notably his head, appear on the plant he merges with.[[3]](#cite_note-4)


Wildvine can grow black fruit-like pods on his back that contain a whole host of substances (lethal and non-lethal), allowing him to use them as explosives or smoke bombs, as mentioned above. His pods can also grow vines.[[4]](#cite_note-RMP-5)


Wildvine can grow skewers on his legs to knock down enemies, as well as grow sharp thorns on his body.[[5]](#cite_note-ATTWB-6) He is also highly agile, as he can spin in the air to disperse his fruit bombs, and throw them into the air, basically firing on his enemies.


Wildvine possesses enhanced strength, as he was able to trap a [Loboan](/wiki/Loboan "Loboan") in his tendrils,[[6]](#cite_note-BW-7) stop a falling cargo lift from landing on [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") and [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") and hold it for a short time,[[7]](#cite_note-EE-8) and easily break rocks with his bare hands.[[4]](#cite_note-RMP-5)


Wildvine is able to regenerate his body if any part of it gets damaged, including his head.[[8]](#cite_note-Rad-9)[[4]](#cite_note-RMP-5)


Wildvine is capable of quickly tunneling underground.[[4]](#cite_note-RMP-5)


Wildvine is immune to the effects of gases, such as a flame retardant.[[7]](#cite_note-EE-8)


Wildvine may be immune to a [Vladat](/wiki/Vladat "Vladat")'s [Corrupturas](/wiki/Corruptura "Corruptura") if he can exert enough willpower.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-10)


Wildvine can tame [Gracklflints](/wiki/Gracklflint "Gracklflint") using his fruits, as demonstrated by [Pax](/wiki/Pax "Pax").[[8]](#cite_note-Rad-9)


Wildvine can close his flytrap, as seen during his fight with the [Werewolf](/wiki/Werewolf "Werewolf") when he dragged him underground.[[6]](#cite_note-BW-7) He can also trap people by closing his flytrap, as demonstrated by wild [Florauna](/wiki/Florauna_(Classic) "Florauna (Classic)").[[9]](#cite_note-11)


Although he as never trained himself to do so, Wildvine can grow vines, thorns, fruits or flowers in many different bright colors and variations, depending on the conditions of the environment. He also has a connection to his species' unique communication system, allowing him to speak with other Florauna.

Wildvine's vines can be tangled.[[4]](#cite_note-RMP-5) He can also be tangled up in his own vines.


Despite his enhanced strength, Wildvine can still have difficulty lifting certain objects with his bare hands, such as heavyweights.[[2]](#cite_note-BP-3)


Wildvine's vines can be used to throw him around.[[5]](#cite_note-ATTWB-6)


If Wildvine stretches himself too much, his arms will stay stretched and unable to shorten.[[7]](#cite_note-EE-8)


Being a plant-based alien, Wildvine is particularly sensitive to fire and cold. Unless he is given his energy source, he will quickly dry up, wilt, and perish.[[merch 1]](#cite_note-12)


Wildvine has a weakness against electricity, such as that generated by an [Amperi](/wiki/Amperi "Amperi"), as demonstrated with Pax.[[10]](#cite_note-13)[[8]](#cite_note-Rad-9)

### Prior to Ben 10


* [Myaxx](/wiki/Myaxx "Myaxx") added Wildvine's DNA to the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)"), unaware that the [Florauna](/wiki/Florauna_(Classic) "Florauna (Classic)") donor was possessed by [Zs'Skayr](/wiki/Zs%27Skayr "Zs'Skayr").[[pop-up 1]](#cite_note-14)[[11]](#cite_note-15)


### Ben 10


* Wildvine first appeared in *[Camp Fear](/wiki/Camp_Fear "Camp Fear")*, where he was accidentally unlocked by [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") and battled the [Mycelium](/wiki/Mycelium "Mycelium") until he reverted.
* In *[Tough Luck](/wiki/Tough_Luck "Tough Luck")*, Wildvine saved [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") and [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") from falling and defeated [Hex](/wiki/Hex_(Classic) "Hex (Classic)") and [Charmcaster](/wiki/Charmcaster_(Classic) "Charmcaster (Classic)").
* In *[Back with a Vengeance](/wiki/Back_with_a_Vengeance "Back with a Vengeance")*, Wildvine appeared thrice. Firstly, he was used by the [Master Control](/wiki/Master_Control "Master Control"). Later, Wildvine appeared in a photo with Max and Gwen. Finally, in the [Null Void](/wiki/Null_Void_(Classic) "Null Void (Classic)"), Wildvine defeated a trio of [Null Guardians](/wiki/Null_Guardians "Null Guardians") before switching into [Heatblast](/wiki/Heatblast_(Classic) "Heatblast (Classic)").
* In *[Midnight Madness](/wiki/Midnight_Madness "Midnight Madness")*, Wildvine defeated [Sublimino](/wiki/Sublimino "Sublimino").
* In *[Super Alien Hero Buddy Adventures](/wiki/Super_Alien_Hero_Buddy_Adventures "Super Alien Hero Buddy Adventures")*, Wildvine followed [Kane North](/wiki/Kane_North "Kane North") to his hideout, defeated an animatronic dragon, saved [Tim Dean](/wiki/Tim_Dean "Tim Dean"), and fought the former until he reverted.
* In *[Benwolf](/wiki/Benwolf_(Episode) "Benwolf (Episode)")*, Wildvine saved Max and [Wes](/wiki/Wes_Green "Wes Green") from falling to their deaths and battled the [Werewolf](/wiki/Werewolf "Werewolf") until he reverted.
* In *[Under Wraps](/wiki/Under_Wraps "Under Wraps")*, Wildvine fed some pigs with Gwen.
* In *[The Visitor](/wiki/The_Visitor "The Visitor")*, Wildvine spied on [Xylene](/wiki/Xylene "Xylene") and Max.


### Five Years Prior to Omniverse


* In *[Ben Again](/wiki/Ben_Again "Ben Again")*, Wildvine was used by 16-year-old Ben in 11-year-old Ben's body to battle [Eon](/wiki/Eon "Eon") alongside 11-year-old Ben as 16-year-old [Arctiguana](/wiki/Arctiguana "Arctiguana").
* In *[Evil's Encore](/wiki/Evil%27s_Encore "Evil's Encore")*, Wildvine was used to help get through the [Plumber Base](/wiki/Mt._Rushmore "Mt. Rushmore") that was being controlled by [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)").

### [Ben 10: Protector of Earth](/wiki/Ben_10:_Protector_of_Earth "Ben 10: Protector of Earth")


Wildvine is a playable character in the game.


Wildvine can cross gaps and swing through them. He can grow spikes on his legs, then slam them down on enemies, as well as spin in the air to scatter seed bombs, or throw seed bombs into the air, raining them down on his enemies. Wildvine can also stretch his arms.


Wildvine also has a DNA Force and Dark Heroes skin.

Trivia
------


* When Ben transformed into Wildvine in the original series, a sound could be heard that sounded like a raspy voice saying "vine".
* Wildvine's **Ultimate Alien** color scheme is similar to those of the [Florauna](/wiki/Florauna_(Classic) "Florauna (Classic)") tribe found on [Xenon](/wiki/Xenon "Xenon") from ***[Secret of the Omnitrix](/wiki/Ben_10:_Secret_of_the_Omnitrix "Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix")***.
* Wildvine's voice has changed over the course of his appearance in the classic timeline:
	+ In the original series, Wildvine spoke in an aged, gravelly voice.
	+ In **Omniverse**, his voice not only becomes similar to that of a hippie, but also a somewhat deeper version of [Feedback](/wiki/Feedback "Feedback")'s.
	+ In *[Ken 10](/wiki/Ken_10 "Ken 10")*, Ken's Wildvine sounds similar to his first voice, only slightly younger sounding.
* [Dave Johnson](/wiki/Dave_Johnson "Dave Johnson") designed Wildvine and came up with the idea that he could rip off the fruit from his back and throw it like grenades, which [Tom Perkins](/wiki/Tom_Perkins "Tom Perkins") later tweaked.[[DJ 1]](#cite_note-16) His earliest rendition was called [PlantGuy](/wiki/Ben_10_(Pre-Production)#PlantGuy "Ben 10 (Pre-Production)") and drawn by Steve E. Gordon.
* Chap Yaep designed Gwen 10's Wildvine.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-17)
* The bulb-like hair of Gwen 10's Wildvine is similar to the hair of [Princess Leia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Princess_Leia "wikipedia:Princess Leia") from the *[Star Wars](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star_Wars "wikipedia:Star Wars")* franchise.
* The Omnitrix symbol on Gwen 10's version of Wildvine is circular like her male counterparts, instead of having a cat-like shape like Gwen's other aliens.
* Wildvine and [Ditto](/wiki/Ditto "Ditto") are the only aliens to be manually (and accidentally) unlocked by Ben.
