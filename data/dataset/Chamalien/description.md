Appearance
----------


### Ben as Chamalien


Chamalien has a slim body that resembles a large purple salamander with darker purple camo-esque spots all over his body and a short tail. He wears a black helmet[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-5) with three triangular colored lenses, the left one being blue, the right red and the middle green, which hide his three eyes. The helmet also has spike-shaped fin on the back of his head. He has four-fingered hands and three toes on his frog-like feet. He also has a wide mouth with sharp teeth.


Chamalien wears the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.



### Albedo as Negative Chamalien


[Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Chamalien looks just like [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s Chamalien, except all of his eyes are red and he lacks an Ultimatrix symbol.

Chamalien's main ability is his camouflage, as he is capable of blending in with the color of his surroundings with such effectiveness that he appears invisible.


Chamalien is very agile, quick, flexible, and can climb walls and crawl on the ground with ease.[[2]](#cite_note-6) He also has sharp teeth and claws.


Chamalien's body is highly slippery and smooth, making him very difficult to grab.[[1]](#cite_note-PN775IM-3)


Chamalien has a long, retractable black barb that can come out of the tip of his tail; this feat was exclusively demonstrated by [Prisoner 775](/wiki/Prisoner_775 "Prisoner 775").[[1]](#cite_note-PN775IM-3)


Chamalien has enhanced strength, enough to push a car and a huge truck several yards away and defeat a [Vulpimancer](/wiki/Vulpimancer_(Classic) "Vulpimancer (Classic)") in battle. He is also durable enough to withstand being thrown against a car; both of these feats were also only demonstrated by Prisoner 775.[[1]](#cite_note-PN775IM-3)



Weaknesses
----------


 [![](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/7/72/NotLN_%28319%29.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/218?cb=20150802191802)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/7/72/NotLN_%28319%29.png/revision/latest?cb=20150802191802)   Trackable Footprints

  
Chamalien can easily be tracked by Vulpimancers using their enhanced senses.[[1]](#cite_note-PN775IM-3)[[3]](#cite_note-FatM-7) He can also be tracked by [Anodites](/wiki/Anodite "Anodite") through his [mana](/wiki/Mana "Mana").[[4]](#cite_note-8)


Although Chamalien appears to turn invisible when he camouflages himself, he actually just changes his color. Consequently, his shadow can still be seen and any place filled with too many bright lights can be a disadvantage, as demonstrated with Prisoner 775.[[1]](#cite_note-PN775IM-3)


Chamalien's camouflage can also be rendered useless if he is either carrying something or coated in another material such as powder[[3]](#cite_note-FatM-7) or oil,[[5]](#cite_note-9) as demonstrated with [Subdora](/wiki/Subdora "Subdora").


Chamalien can be controlled and hypnotized by [Vladats](/wiki/Vladat "Vladat") via their [Corrupturas](/wiki/Corruptura "Corruptura"), as also demonstrated with Subdora.[[6]](#cite_note-10)



History
-------


### Ultimate Alien


* Chamalien first appeared in *[Prisoner Number 775 is Missing](/wiki/Prisoner_Number_775_is_Missing "Prisoner Number 775 is Missing")*, where he was obtained by scanning [Prisoner 775](/wiki/Prisoner_775 "Prisoner 775")'s DNA and tried out by [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)").
* In *[The Flame Keepers' Circle](/wiki/The_Flame_Keepers%27_Circle "The Flame Keepers' Circle")*, Chamalien snuck into a building controlled by the [Flame Keepers' Circle](/wiki/Flame_Keepers%27_Circle "Flame Keepers' Circle").
* In *[Night of the Living Nightmare](/wiki/Night_of_the_Living_Nightmare "Night of the Living Nightmare")*, Chamalien appeared twice in a dream. Firstly, Ben as Chamalien ran away from [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") and [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") and hid in the woods until he reverted. Later, [Negative](/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") Chamalien ambushed Ben before switching into Negative [Big Chill](/wiki/Big_Chill "Big Chill").

Etymology
---------


Chamalien's name is portmanteau of "[chameleon](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chameleon "wikipedia:Chameleon")", an animal capable of changing its color, which alludes to his ability to camouflage himself, and "[alien](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Extraterrestrial_life "wikipedia:Extraterrestrial life")", a reference to Chamalien being an extraterrestrial life form, as well as a transformation.



Trivia
------


* Chamalien speaks with a light [German](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany "wikipedia:Germany") accent.
* When Ben first transformed into Chamalien, he named the form "Overkill" as a joke.[[1]](#cite_note-PN775IM-3) He eventually started using the alien's real name as of *[Night of the Living Nightmare](/wiki/Night_of_the_Living_Nightmare "Night of the Living Nightmare")*.
* In *[Universe vs. Tennyson](/wiki/Universe_vs._Tennyson "Universe vs. Tennyson")*, it was initially planned that Ben would use Chamalien to spy on [Chadzmuth](/wiki/Chadzmuth "Chadzmuth"),[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-11) but the whole scene was eventually dropped as the story was changed.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-12)
	+ As a result of this, Chamalien and [Fasttrack](/wiki/Fasttrack "Fasttrack") are the only normal aliens introduced in **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")** who do not appear in **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**.
	+ Had Chamalien appeared in **Omniverse**, [Dee Bradley Baker](/wiki/Dee_Bradley_Baker "Dee Bradley Baker") would have reprised his role.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-13)
* Chamalien was revealed in a picture released on the [**Ben 10** Facebook page](http://www.facebook.com/ben10) on April 17, 2011, as a reward for hitting 1,000,000 fans.[[FB 1]](#cite_note-page-4)
* Chamalien had concept art drawn by [Glen Murakami](/wiki/Glen_Murakami "Glen Murakami"). His final design was created by Glenn Wong, with color styling by Chris Hooten.[[GW 1]](#cite_note-14)
* Chamalien's appearance is similar to that of [Randall Boggs](https://disney.fandom.com/wiki/Randall_Boggs "w:c:disney:Randall Boggs") from the *[Monsters, Inc.](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Monsters,_Inc._(franchise) "wikipedia:Monsters, Inc. (franchise)")* franchise, and the [Cheshire Cat](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cheshire_Cat "wikipedia:Cheshire Cat") from *[Alice in Wonderland](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alice_in_Wonderland "wikipedia:Alice in Wonderland")* (referring to his smile), and his body shape also resembles Oscar from *[Oscar's Oasis](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oscar%27s_Oasis "wikipedia:Oscar's Oasis")*.
* Chamalien's three eyes are a reference to [the 1953 movie adaptation of](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_War_of_the_Worlds_(1953_film) "wikipedia:The War of the Worlds (1953 film)") *[The War of the Worlds](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_War_of_the_Worlds "wikipedia:The War of the Worlds")*. The Martians in the movie have three eyes that resemble Chamalien's. The colors red, blue, and green are the basic colors the human eye perceives.
* When asked what kinds of features Chamalien's helmet has, [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") compared the spectrum-wide visions to those found in helmets worn by [Yautja](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Predator_(fictional_species) "wikipedia:Predator (fictional species)").[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-15)
* There were no plans to feature Chamalien in the [reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)").[[DR 1]](#cite_note-16)
