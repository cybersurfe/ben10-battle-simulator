Appearance
----------


Blitzwolfer's appearance is based on a [werewolf](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Werewolf "wikipedia:Werewolf").


In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Blitzwolfer had grey fur, a long flowing mane, and a large bushy tail with three sharp claws on each hand and foot, and the inside of his mouth was green. He has spiked elbows and shoulders and wore a white jumpsuit with black stripes and the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") symbol on his lower stomach.


During the events of *[Benwolf](/wiki/Benwolf_(Episode) "Benwolf (Episode)")*, however, [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was slowly transforming into Blitzwolfer. During the first stage, Blitzwolfer mostly resembled Ben but with bushy sideburns, sharp claws on his feet and hands, sharp teeth, longer hair, and grey skin. The original Omnitrix symbol was worn on his left arm, much like when Ben is in [human](/wiki/Human_(Classic) "Human (Classic)") form.


During the second stage, Blitzwolfer's face started to appear more wolf-like. His nose became more noticeable with light grey fur surrounding his nose and mouth, and dark grey fur on areas of his face where the light grey wasn't. His eyes also went full green and his ears became pointed and pointing upward. He also gained height and muscle, and his arms and legs resembled his fully transformed form.


During the third and final stage, Blitzwolfer resembled his fully transformed form but had Ben's clothing. By this stage, the original Omnitrix symbol had vanished from his left arm.


In **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Blitzwolfer looked the same as before except his mane and tail were recolored black, he had lighter skin, and his mouth was colored white on the outside and red on the inside. He retained the same black and white jumpsuit from the original series and wore the [Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)") symbol on his chest.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, 16-year-old Blitzwolfer has a green suit, collar and green wristbands. He is more muscular than his original appearance. He now has four claws instead of three, as well as a green tongue and a white belt. His [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol is located on the collar above his fur.


11-year-old Blitzwolfer in **Omniverse** looks almost the same as his 16-year-old self, except his suit and collar are white with black stripes and his wristbands are black.

1/10
Blitzwolfer can emit a powerful green ultrasonic howl by opening his quadra-hinged muzzle that can either reflect incoming projectiles[[1]](#cite_note-AABiL-1) or propel himself upward like a rocket booster.[[2]](#cite_note-Benwolf-3) His sonic howls are powerful enough to damage [Petrosapiens](/wiki/Petrosapien_(Classic) "Petrosapien (Classic)"), which was demonstrated by [Kevin 11,000](/wiki/Kevin_11,000 "Kevin 11,000").[[3]](#cite_note-4) They also exceed the screaming capabilities of [Sonorosians](/wiki/Sonorosian "Sonorosian").[[DR 1]](#cite_note-5)


Blitzwolfer has enhanced senses of smelling and hearing, giving him great tracking and detection abilities.


Blitzwolfer possesses enhanced strength, as he was able to carry [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)") and [Kai](/wiki/Kai_Green "Kai Green"), and then [Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") and [Wes](/wiki/Wes_Green "Wes Green") without difficulty, as well as restrain the [Werewolf](/wiki/Werewolf "Werewolf").[[2]](#cite_note-Benwolf-3)


Blitzwolfer can jump exceptionally high, as he was able to leap up to the top of [Nyancy Chan](/wiki/Nyancy_Chan "Nyancy Chan")'s huge ball of pink yarn while chasing her.[[4]](#cite_note-Catfight-6)


Blitzwolfer has enhanced agility and reflexes, as seen when he fought the [Forever Ninja](/wiki/Forever_Ninja "Forever Ninja") on [Big Ben](/wiki/Big_Ben "Big Ben").[[1]](#cite_note-AABiL-1) He also possesses enhanced speed and stamina.


Much like an ordinary wolf, Blitzwolfer has sharp fangs and formidable claws, the latter of which he used to scratch the Jungle Guardian.[[5]](#cite_note-SoDS-7) He also has extraordinary night vision.[[2]](#cite_note-Benwolf-3)[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-8)


Similar to [Wildmutt](/wiki/Wildmutt "Wildmutt") and [Kickin Hawk](/wiki/Kickin_Hawk "Kickin Hawk"), the claws on Blitzwolfer's feet are prehensile.[[1]](#cite_note-AABiL-1)


Like all Anur System aliens, Blitzwolfer is immune to the effects of [Corrodium](/wiki/Corrodium "Corrodium"), as demonstrated by the Werewolf.[[6]](#cite_note-9)


In addition to his feet, Blitzwolfer's tail is also prehensile, and he can use it to suppress fire, as demonstrated by [Scout](/wiki/Scout "Scout").[[7]](#cite_note-10)



Weaknesses
----------


* ![]()Sensitive Hearing
1/1
Similar to most [Earth](/wiki/Earth_(Classic) "Earth (Classic)") canines, Blitzwolfer has sensitive hearing. Therefore, he cannot handle loud noises like that of giant bells[[1]](#cite_note-AABiL-1) or sonic howls of other [Loboans](/wiki/Loboan_(Classic) "Loboan (Classic)").[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-11)


Blitzwolfer cannot use his sonic howl if his muzzle is forced shut by something, such as [Thep Khufans](/wiki/Thep_Khufan "Thep Khufan")' bandages or a [Plumbers](/wiki/Plumbers_(Classic) "Plumbers (Classic)")' specialized mouth cover, as demonstrated by the Werewolf.[[8]](#cite_note-TR-12)


Blitzwolfer can be controlled and hypnotized by [Vladats](/wiki/Vladat "Vladat") via their [Corrupturas](/wiki/Corruptura "Corruptura"), as demonstrated with Scout, [Crüjo](/wiki/Cr%C3%BCjo "Crüjo"), and many other Loboan residents of [Anur Transyl](/wiki/Anur_Transyl_(Classic) "Anur Transyl (Classic)").[[9]](#cite_note-13)


Blitzwolfer can easily be scared by [Toepick](/wiki/Toepick "Toepick")'s [species](/wiki/Toepick%27s_Species "Toepick's Species").[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-14)



History
-------


### Ben 10


* Blitzwolfer first appeared in *[Benwolf](/wiki/Benwolf_(Episode) "Benwolf (Episode)")*, where he was added to the active playlist after the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") "sampled" the [Werewolf](/wiki/Werewolf "Werewolf")'s DNA.[[note 1]](#cite_note-17) However, [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") slowly transformed because the Werewolf's scratch left the Omnitrix stuck between Active and Capture Modes. Blitzwolfer pursued the Werewolf and eventually reverted after he saved [Grandpa Max](/wiki/Max_Tennyson_(Classic) "Max Tennyson (Classic)") and [Wes](/wiki/Wes_Green "Wes Green") from a lava flow.


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, Blitzwolfer was unlocked by [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Ultimate_Alien) "Ben 10,000 (Ultimate Alien)").


### Omniverse


* In *[An American Benwolf in London](/wiki/An_American_Benwolf_in_London "An American Benwolf in London")*, Blitzwolfer battled the [Forever Ninja](/wiki/Forever_Ninja "Forever Ninja") and saved [Kai](/wiki/Kai_Green "Kai Green") from falling off of [Big Ben](/wiki/Big_Ben "Big Ben").
* In *[Catfight](/wiki/Catfight "Catfight")*, Blitzwolfer chased after [Nyancy Chan](/wiki/Nyancy_Chan "Nyancy Chan") until a fleet of [Incursean](/wiki/Incursean "Incursean") and [Tetramand](/wiki/Tetramand_(Classic) "Tetramand (Classic)") warships appeared.
* In *[The Secret of Dos Santos](/wiki/The_Secret_of_Dos_Santos "The Secret of Dos Santos")*, Blitzwolfer defeated a [Jungle Guardian](/wiki/Jungle_Guardians "Jungle Guardians") with [Skurd](/wiki/Skurd "Skurd")'s help and accidentally found the Temple of the Sky in the process.
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Blitzwolfer appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.


### Comics


* In *[Pleasant Dreams](/wiki/Pleasant_Dreams "Pleasant Dreams")*, Blitzwolfer attacked Gwen while sleepwalking.
* In *[Special Defects](/wiki/Special_Defects "Special Defects")*, Blitzwolfer defeated Dirk and his vampire animatronic.
* In *[What Lies Ben-eath](/wiki/What_Lies_Ben-eath "What Lies Ben-eath")*, Blitzwolfer tried to defeat the Shadow Monster.

Etymology
---------


His placeholder name, Benwolf, is a combination of the words "Ben" and "wolf". It is an allusion to [Beowulf](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beowulf "wikipedia:Beowulf").


Blitzwolfer is a rearrangement of the CNN newscaster [Wolf Blitzer](/wiki/Wolf_Blitzer "Wolf Blitzer")'s name. Ben renamed the transformation "Blitzwolfer" after briefly seeing Wolf Blitzer on television.[[1]](#cite_note-AABiL-1)


"Blitzwolfer" is also a reference to both his werewolf-like form and his sonic howl's capability to blitz through its targets. It may also be a reference to the 1942 short [Blitz Wolf](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blitz_Wolf "wikipedia:Blitz Wolf") which features the Big Bad Wolf, a folkloric character with similar abilities as Blitzwolfer.



Trivia
------


* [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") originally called his [Loboan](/wiki/Loboan_(Classic) "Loboan (Classic)") form "Benwolf" because he thought the transformation was permanent.[[2]](#cite_note-Benwolf-3)
	+ Consequently, this makes Blitzwolfer one of the four aliens whose working name had Ben's name in it; the other three being [Snare-oh](/wiki/Snare-oh "Snare-oh"), [Frankenstrike](/wiki/Frankenstrike "Frankenstrike"), and [Nanomech](/wiki/Nanomech "Nanomech").
	+ Benwolf was eventually renamed "Blitzwolfer" to avoid embarrassment after [Kai](/wiki/Kai_Green "Kai Green") asked him if the alien was still called "Benwolf".[[1]](#cite_note-AABiL-1)
	+ [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") came up with the working name Blitzwolfer, and it ended up being used as no one else came up with a better name.[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-18)
* In the [original series](/wiki/Ben_10 "Ben 10"), Blitzwolfer's tongue retracted and stowed itself away when his muzzle split.[[TP 1]](#cite_note-19) This is changed in **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, as his tongue stays in place and is split in half.[[5]](#cite_note-SoDS-7)
* [Nyancy Chan](/wiki/Nyancy_Chan "Nyancy Chan") and her cats are afraid of Blitzwolfer.[[4]](#cite_note-Catfight-6)
* Kai is fond of Blitzwolfer and thinks he is cute in a pet-like way.[[1]](#cite_note-AABiL-1)[[5]](#cite_note-SoDS-7)
* Blitzwolfer's **Omniverse** appearance was designed by Steven Choi.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-20)
* From Arc 4 of **Omniverse** onwards, 16-year-old Blitzwolfer replaces [Humungousaur](/wiki/Humungousaur_(Classic) "Humungousaur (Classic)") in the opening intro of the show, particularly in the part where the latter used to appear alongside [Echo Echo](/wiki/Echo_Echo "Echo Echo"), [Bloxx](/wiki/Bloxx "Bloxx"), [Shocksquatch](/wiki/Shocksquatch "Shocksquatch"), [NRG](/wiki/NRG "NRG"), [Crashhopper](/wiki/Crashhopper "Crashhopper"), [Gravattack](/wiki/Gravattack "Gravattack"), and 16-year-old Ben at the end.
	+ Also, 11-year-old Blitzwolfer replaces [Rath](/wiki/Rath_(Classic) "Rath (Classic)") in the opening intro in **Omniverse**'s Arc 5. In the same intro, he also replaces 11-year-old [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)"), specifically in the part where the latter originally appeared alongside the 11-year-old versions of [Eye Guy](/wiki/Eye_Guy "Eye Guy"), [Grey Matter](/wiki/Grey_Matter_(Classic) "Grey Matter (Classic)"), and Snare-oh.
* In a former *[FusionFall](/wiki/FusionFall "FusionFall")* Halloween event, Dracula sold the outfits of Blitzwolfer along with Snare-oh and Frankenstrike.
* 11-year-old Blitzwolfer was not necessarily planned to appear in **Omniverse** flashbacks.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-21)
