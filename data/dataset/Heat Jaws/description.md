**Heat Jaws** is the combination of [Heatblast](/wiki/Heatblast_(Classic) "Heatblast (Classic)") and [Ripjaws](/wiki/Ripjaws_(Classic) "Ripjaws (Classic)")' DNA. He cannot be accessed unless the faceplate of the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)") is broken or by using the [Biomnitrix](/wiki/Biomnitrix "Biomnitrix").[[note 1]](#cite_note-fusions-1)

Appearance
----------


Heat Jaws has Ripjaws' torso, however his arms and legs are essentialy Heatblast's. Also, the fin that [Ripjaws](/wiki/Ripjaws_(Classic) "Ripjaws (Classic)") usually has on his back is replaced with a line of fire. 



Powers and Abilities
--------------------


* ![]()Pyrokinesis
* ![]()Underwater Heat Generation
* ![]()Pyrokinetic Touch
* ![]()Enhanced Durability
1/4
Being part-Pyronite, Heat Jaws possesses pyrokinesis similar to Heatblast. Surprisingly, his flames get stronger underwater. But since water normally extinguishes fire, he can shoot a high-pressure stream of scalding water without his flames going out.


Being part-Piscciss Volann, Heat Jaws' sharp teeth and jaws are as powerful as Ripjaws', being able to rip through steel. He can also swim underwater without extinguishing his flames, and only needs water whenever he is using his fire powers.


Compared to his component aliens, Heat Jaws' enhanced strength and durability are increased.



Weaknesses
----------


 [![](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/5/59/Mutant_%281587%29.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/190?cb=20210203073640)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/ben10/images/5/59/Mutant_%281587%29.png/revision/latest?cb=20210203073640)  Heat Exposure

  
Ben admits that Heat Jaws is "not the best combo", as fire and water-related aliens do not really mix together that well. Because half of his Piscciss Volann DNA needs water to live while the Pyronite DNA generates enormous amount of heat energy, which leads Heat Jaws suffering from extreme problems.


Similar to Ripjaws, Heat Jaws needs to stay out of heat or else he will dry out. But because if he constantly generates heat like Heatblast, then he will dry out quickly when using his fire powers.
