### Ben as Way Big


Way Big is a white colossal humanoid creature with a large fin on his head, which is black at the front and red at the bottom. He has blade-like growths on his shoulders and spikes on his waist. He also has red arms with two black lines and white fins on the elbows, wristband-like outgrowths on his wrists and yellow eyes on his head with two extra eyes protruding on his cheeks, one on each.[[TP 1]](#cite_note-4) He has red feet with two toes, a black sandal-like covering on the middle of his feet and three black lines on the side of his neck. The red parts of Way Big's body are made of an unknown cosmic material with metallic properties.[[DJW 1]](#cite_note-5) [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Original) "Ben 10,000 (Original)")'s Way Big is identical to this design.


In **[Alien Force](/wiki/Ben_10:_Alien_Force "Ben 10: Alien Force")** and **[Ultimate Alien](/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien "Ben 10: Ultimate Alien")**, Way Big's head fin was red on the front and black on the bottom, his arms were white with red fins coming out of them and his neck and wrists were black. Way Big's eyes are green and he did not bear the extra ones on his cheeks. He lacked the stripes on his arms as well as the blades on his shoulders and waist. He also had red boot-like feet and did not have the sandal-like coverings. Kevin as Way Big is identical to this design.


In ***[Destroy All Aliens](/wiki/Ben_10:_Destroy_All_Aliens "Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens")***, Way Big looked mostly the same as in **Alien Force** and **Ultimate Alien**, except the red parts were colored to match his portrayal in the original series.


In **[Omniverse](/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse "Ben 10: Omniverse")**, Way Big has two red lines on his neck, the blades on his shoulders and waist have returned and are red and he also has black fingerless gloves, similar to the ones [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)") wears in the same series. He is now more muscular, his head is less skull-shaped and has eyes on his cheeks again, which are more metallic. The fins on his arms are longer and he has regained the stripes on his arms and legs, and the wristband-like outgrowths on his wrists. Uniquely, both 16 and 11-year-old Way Big share the exact same design.


Way Big wears the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)")/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)")/[Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Omniverse) "Omnitrix (Omniverse)") symbol on his chest.



### Mad Ben as Mad Way Big


[Mad Ben](/wiki/Mad_Ben "Mad Ben")'s Way Big is near identical to Ben Prime's Way Big in **Omniverse**, except for orange replacing the eyes and the red parts. He also wears gray shoulder pads with orange spikes, spiked shoes, and orange wraps around his forearms and calves. His arm and head blades are also serrated.

1/8
Way Big is very strong (even for his size), being able to hurl targets into orbits from planets with an atmosphere as thick as [Earth](/wiki/Earth_(Classic) "Earth (Classic)")'s,[[MW 1]](#cite_note-6) as shown when he threw [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") (who was Way Big's size at the time) into orbit from [Primus](/wiki/Primus "Primus").[[3]](#cite_note-Primus-7) He can even lift objects larger and heavier than him, as he managed to lift the [Hyperspace Jump Gate](/wiki/Hyperspace_Jump_Gate "Hyperspace Jump Gate").[[4]](#cite_note-WotWP1-8) Technically speaking, he is powerful enough to destroy a small planet.[[JK 1]](#cite_note-9)


Way Big has great durability, being able to withstand attacks from enemies while barely even feeling them. He is also resistant to extreme temperatures.[[3]](#cite_note-Primus-7)


Way Big can jump very high.[[5]](#cite_note-SP2-10) He also has extremely quick reflexes, being able to react to [Milleous](/wiki/Milleous_(Classic) "Milleous (Classic)")' [Incursean Conquest Ray](/wiki/Incursean_Conquest_Ray "Incursean Conquest Ray").[[6]](#cite_note-TFoWP2-11)


Way Big is very skilled in hand-to-hand combat, as seen when he fought giant versions of Vilgax,[[3]](#cite_note-Primus-7) the [Trash Monster](/wiki/Trash_Monster "Trash Monster"),[[7]](#cite_note-TWG-12) [Trombipulor](/wiki/Trombipulor "Trombipulor"),[[8]](#cite_note-BaDGtMS-13) and [Malware](/wiki/Malware "Malware").[[5]](#cite_note-SP2-10)


Way Big can shoot a powerful cosmic ray[[DM 1]](#cite_note-14)[[note 1]](#cite_note-16) from the outer edge of his right hand by crossing his arms together.[[note 2]](#cite_note-17) His cosmic rays are powerful enough to overpower the Incursean Conquest Ray.[[6]](#cite_note-TFoWP2-11)[[DR 1]](#cite_note-18)


Due to the nature of his species' creation, Way Big can survive in the vacuum of space.


Way Big has enhanced speed, similar to [XLR8](/wiki/XLR8_(Classic) "XLR8 (Classic)") and [Fasttrack](/wiki/Fasttrack "Fasttrack") albeit with him leaving a red and white trail behind.[[7]](#cite_note-TWG-12)[[note 3]](#cite_note-21) Way Big also has a massive stride, so he can get to his destination quicker than other aliens.[[DJW 3]](#cite_note-22)

1/5
Way Big sometimes forgets how strong he is, as shown when he accidentally destroyed the statue of [Zavin](/wiki/Zavin "Zavin") by leaning on it. His size also gives him a slight mobility issue, since he has to be careful where he steps to avoid crushing objects and people around him, and easily gives his location away.[[10]](#cite_note-23)


Because of his size, Way Big cannot be used inside buildings or confined spaces.


Since Way Big's fin is a weak spot, a hit to that area can paralyze him.[[11]](#cite_note-24)[[12]](#cite_note-DAA-25)


Way Big is vulnerable to electricity, which can incapacitate him if it is powerful enough.[[12]](#cite_note-DAA-25)[[4]](#cite_note-WotWP1-8)


Certain beings that are about as small as [Nanomech](/wiki/Nanomech "Nanomech") can go inside Way Big's head and attack him.[[9]](#cite_note-IaMMMBWP2-15)


A [Kineceleran](/wiki/Kineceleran_(Classic) "Kineceleran (Classic)") can generate a tornado strong enough to lift Way Big off of his feet, as demonstrated with the [Way Bads](/wiki/Way_Bads "Way Bads").[[6]](#cite_note-TFoWP2-11)


Way Big can be harmed by [Argit](/wiki/Argit "Argit")'s quills, as also demonstrated with the Way Bads.[[6]](#cite_note-TFoWP2-11)


Way Big can be controlled by [Vladats](/wiki/Vladat "Vladat") via their [Corrupturas](/wiki/Corruptura "Corruptura").[[DJW 4]](#cite_note-26)


Way Big can be harmed by high-pitched noises, such as a [Sonorosian](/wiki/Sonorosian "Sonorosian")'s sonic screams, if they were to get close enough to his ears.[[DJW 5]](#cite_note-27) This weakness was first referenced when [Shocksquatch](/wiki/Shocksquatch "Shocksquatch") made an unsuccessful attempt at attacking a Way Bad using a megaphone made from [Echo Echo](/wiki/Echo_Echo "Echo Echo")'s DNA.[[13]](#cite_note-28)


Way Big can be subdued by the Hephestan Neuro Grip, but only if this move were to be performed by beings with hands much bigger than [Slapstrike](/wiki/Slapstrike "Slapstrike")'s.[[DJW 6]](#cite_note-29)[[DJW 7]](#cite_note-30)



History
-------


### Ben 10


* Way Big first appeared in ***[Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix](/wiki/Ben_10:_Secret_of_the_Omnitrix "Ben 10: Secret of the Omnitrix")***, where he was unlocked by [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)") and defeated [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)") and [his army](/wiki/Drones "Drones").
* In ***[Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens](/wiki/Ben_10:_Destroy_All_Aliens "Ben 10: Destroy All Aliens")***, Way Big appeared twice. Firstly, Way Big asked for [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)")'s homework in a dream. Then, Way Big battled [Evil Way Big](/wiki/Evil_Way_Big_(Destroy_All_Aliens) "Evil Way Big (Destroy All Aliens)").
* In *[Destroy All Aliens](/wiki/Destroy_All_Aliens_(Short) "Destroy All Aliens (Short)")*, Way Big appeared at the end of the short as a separate entity from Ben due to unknown reasons, where he picked up [Four Arms](/wiki/Four_Arms_(Classic) "Four Arms (Classic)") and [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)").


### Five Years Prior to Omniverse


* In *[Special Delivery](/wiki/Special_Delivery "Special Delivery")*, Way Big defeated [Dr. Animo](/wiki/Aloysius_Animo_(Classic) "Aloysius Animo (Classic)") and his [mosquito creature](/wiki/Mutant_Mosquito "Mutant Mosquito").
	+ The same scene reoccurred on a screen in *[Universe vs. Tennyson](/wiki/Universe_vs._Tennyson "Universe vs. Tennyson")*.
* In *[From Hedorium to Eternity](/wiki/From_Hedorium_to_Eternity "From Hedorium to Eternity")*, Way Big survived a massive fall and stopped [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)"), [Lucy](/wiki/Lucy_Mann "Lucy Mann"), and [Jonesy](/wiki/Jonesy "Jonesy") from falling.


### Alien Force


* In *[War of the Worlds: Part 1](/wiki/War_of_the_Worlds:_Part_1 "War of the Worlds: Part 1")*, Way Big was reunlocked by the [Master Control](/wiki/Master_Control "Master Control"), and failed to destroy a [Hyperspace Jump Gate](/wiki/Hyperspace_Jump_Gate "Hyperspace Jump Gate") to stop the [Highbreed](/wiki/Highbreed "Highbreed").
* In *[Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 2](/wiki/Vengeance_of_Vilgax:_Part_2 "Vengeance of Vilgax: Part 2")*, Way Big was one of the aliens who escaped from the [Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)"). He was lured back to [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") by [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") and [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)"), where the Omnitrix reabsorbed him.
* In *[Simple](/wiki/Simple "Simple")*, Way Big managed to stop the war between the red and blue armies by accidentally destroying the statue of [Zavin](/wiki/Zavin "Zavin") - resulting in them both teaming up against him.
* In *[Primus](/wiki/Primus_(Episode) "Primus (Episode)")*, Way Big defeated a giant [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)").


### Ultimate Alien


* In *[Video Games](/wiki/Video_Games "Video Games")*, Way Big crushed the [Stalker](/wiki/Stalker "Stalker") and [Will Harangue](/wiki/Will_Harangue "Will Harangue")'s car.
* In *[Absolute Power: Part 1](/wiki/Absolute_Power:_Part_1 "Absolute Power: Part 1")*, Way Big defeated [Gwen](/wiki/Gwen_Tennyson_(Classic) "Gwen Tennyson (Classic)").
* In *[Ben 10,000 Returns](/wiki/Ben_10,000_Returns "Ben 10,000 Returns")*, Way Big tried to destroy the [Hands of Armageddon](/wiki/Hands_of_Armageddon "Hands of Armageddon") with his cosmic ray and the help of [Ultimate Ben](/wiki/Ultimate_Ben "Ultimate Ben"), who used [Clockwork](/wiki/Clockwork "Clockwork")'s powers, but was destroyed by [Eon](/wiki/Eon "Eon")'s time ray. After Eon was defeated, however, Way Big was restored by [Ben 10,000](/wiki/Ben_10,000_(Ultimate_Alien) "Ben 10,000 (Ultimate Alien)").
* In *[The Perfect Girlfriend](/wiki/The_Perfect_Girlfriend "The Perfect Girlfriend")*, Way Big destroyed two [monster buildings](/wiki/Evil_Buildings "Evil Buildings") created by [Elena](/wiki/Elena_Validus "Elena Validus").
* In *[The Ultimate Sacrifice](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Sacrifice "The Ultimate Sacrifice")*, Way Big battled the [Sentient Ultimate forms](/wiki/Ultimate_Forms#Sentient_Forms "Ultimate Forms"), but eventually timed out.
* In *[The Widening Gyre](/wiki/The_Widening_Gyre "The Widening Gyre")*, Way Big fought the [Trash Monster](/wiki/Trash_Monster "Trash Monster") and threw it into the sun by creating a water vortex.
* In *[Ben 10/Generator Rex: Heroes United](/wiki/Ben_10-Generator_Rex:_Heroes_United "Ben 10-Generator Rex: Heroes United")*, Way Big appeared as one of the many aliens when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was explaining his story to [Rex](/wiki/Rex_Salazar "Rex Salazar").
* In *[Inspector #13](/wiki/Inspector_13_(Episode) "Inspector 13 (Episode)")*, Kevin as Way Big prepared to break into the [Techadon Factory](/wiki/Techadon_Factory "Techadon Factory") before being switched into [Nanomech](/wiki/Nanomech "Nanomech").
* In *[The Ultimate Enemy: Part 2](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Enemy:_Part_2 "The Ultimate Enemy: Part 2")*, Way Big went [Ultimate](/wiki/Ultimate_Way_Big "Ultimate Way Big") to battle [Dagon](/wiki/Dagon "Dagon").


### Omniverse


* In *[Blukic and Driba Go to Mr. Smoothy's](/wiki/Blukic_and_Driba_Go_to_Mr._Smoothy%27s "Blukic and Driba Go to Mr. Smoothy's")*, Way Big was defeated by a giant [Trombipulor](/wiki/Trombipulor "Trombipulor"), forcing him to switch into [Goop](/wiki/Goop_(Classic) "Goop (Classic)").
* In *[Showdown: Part 2](/wiki/Showdown:_Part_2 "Showdown: Part 2")*, Way Big battled [Malware](/wiki/Malware "Malware") before being absorbed by him
* In *[The Frogs of War: Part 1](/wiki/The_Frogs_of_War:_Part_1 "The Frogs of War: Part 1")*, Way Big saved some people from a sign that he accidentally knocked over before the Randomizer switched him into [NRG](/wiki/NRG "NRG").
* In *[The Frogs of War: Part 2](/wiki/The_Frogs_of_War:_Part_2 "The Frogs of War: Part 2")*, Way Big battled the [Way Bads](/wiki/Way_Bads "Way Bads") and destroyed [Emperor Milleous](/wiki/Milleous "Milleous")' ship.
* In *[Universe vs. Tennyson](/wiki/Universe_vs._Tennyson "Universe vs. Tennyson")*, Way Big defeated Trombipulor by stepping on him.
* In *[A New Dawn](/wiki/A_New_Dawn "A New Dawn")*, Way Big appeared for a short time when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") was cycling through his aliens.


### Future (Original)


* In *[Ken 10](/wiki/Ken_10 "Ken 10")*, Way Big punched [Kevin 11,000](/wiki/Kevin_11,000 "Kevin 11,000") into the ground out of anger after he hurt [Ken](/wiki/Ken_Tennyson "Ken Tennyson").


### Mad Ben's Timeline


* In *[It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 2](/wiki/It%27s_a_Mad,_Mad,_Mad_Ben_World:_Part_2 "It's a Mad, Mad, Mad Ben World: Part 2")*, [Mad](/wiki/Mad_Ben "Mad Ben") Way Big battled [Rath](/wiki/Rath_(Classic) "Rath (Classic)") and [Lightning Volt](/wiki/Lightning_Volt "Lightning Volt"), but was defeated by [Nanomech](/wiki/Nanomech "Nanomech").

### [Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction](/wiki/Ben_10_Ultimate_Alien:_Cosmic_Destruction "Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction")


Way Big is used by Ben during The Final Battle against the [Evil Way Big](/wiki/Evil_Way_Big_(Cosmic_Destruction) "Evil Way Big (Cosmic Destruction)") in [Tokyo, Japan](/wiki/Tokyo "Tokyo"). Way Big used the [Potis Altiare](/wiki/Potis_Altiare "Potis Altiare") to enhance the power of his cosmic rays to the level where it was powerful enough to launch the Evil Way Big to the [Moon](/wiki/Moon "Moon").

Trivia
------


* Way Big's original series design was created by [Tom Perkins](/wiki/Tom_Perkins "Tom Perkins"); the first design he made was approved as the crew was very busy when Way Big was conceived during development.[[TP 2]](#cite_note-31) Back then, Tom did not envision Way Big with cosmic powers because the script for ***Secret of the Omnitrix*** simply called for a giant alien.[[TP 3]](#cite_note-32) He also did not design his fin to be a weak point, and does not think it would make sense for such a prominent feature to be one.[[TP 4]](#cite_note-33)[[TP 5]](#cite_note-34)
	+ Way Big's **Alien Force**/**Ultimate Alien** design was created by Glenn Wong, with color styling by Chris Hooten.[[GW 1]](#cite_note-35)
	+ Way Big's **Omniverse** design was made by [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt") alongside either Steven Choi or Chap Yaep.[[DJW 8]](#cite_note-36) It would later serve as the basis for [Way Big](/wiki/Way_Big_(Reboot) "Way Big (Reboot)")'s appearance in the [reboot](/wiki/Ben_10_(Reboot) "Ben 10 (Reboot)").
* The parts of Way Big that have metallic properties can allow [Osmosians](/wiki/Osmosian "Osmosian") like [Kevin](/wiki/Kevin_Levin_(Classic) "Kevin Levin (Classic)") to absorb it.[[5]](#cite_note-SP2-10)
* Derrick viewed Way Big as Ben's sixth most powerful alien.[[DJW 9]](#cite_note-37) Prior to [Atomix](/wiki/Atomix "Atomix")'s introduction, [Matt Wayne](/wiki/Matt_Wayne "Matt Wayne") guessed that Way Big was a runner-up to [Alien X](/wiki/Alien_X_(Classic) "Alien X (Classic)") in terms of power.[[MW 3]](#cite_note-powerful-38)
* According to Derrick J. Wyatt, Way Big could be eaten by a [Gourmand](/wiki/Gourmand "Gourmand") if he were chopped up into bite-sized chunks.[[DJW 10]](#cite_note-39)
* Way Big's size is portrayed inconsistently in many of his appearances. His actual height is supposed to be nearly 100 feet (30.5 meters)[[CN 1]](#cite_note-40)[[merch 1]](#cite_note-41), at least 100 feet (30.5 meters)[[14]](#cite_note-42)[[12]](#cite_note-DAA-25) or 120 feet (36.576 meters).[[MW 4]](#cite_note-43)
* Way Big is the only alien in **Omniverse** whose 11-year-old appearance looks identical to their 16-year-old self.
	+ This excludes [Ball Weevil](/wiki/Ball_Weevil "Ball Weevil") or [Bloxx](/wiki/Bloxx "Bloxx"), as they were both used by 11-year-old Ben in the present day;[[15]](#cite_note-44) or [Snare-oh](/wiki/Snare-oh "Snare-oh"), who was incorrectly using his 16-year-old design when featured among 11-year-old Ben's aliens.
* Way Big's design and fighting style are based on the heroes of the **[Ultraman](https://ultra.fandom.com/wiki/Ultraman_(series) "w:c:ultra:Ultraman (series)")** series.[[TP 6]](#cite_note-45) For example, Way Big's cosmic ray attack is similar to Ultraman's signature technique, the [Specium Ray](https://ultra.fandom.com/wiki/Ultra_Beam "w:c:ultra:Ultra Beam").
	+ Both the transformation durations of the Omnitrix and Ultraman had their own [time limit settings](https://ultra.fandom.com/wiki/Color_Timer "w:c:ultra:Color Timer").
	+ In **Omniverse**, Way Big speaks with a [Japanese](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan "wikipedia:Japan") accent, further comparing him to Ultraman.
	+ However, what sets Way Big apart from Ultraman is that he is vulnerable to electricity, whereas Ultraman is actually immune to it.
* From Arc 4 of **Omniverse** onwards, Way Big is replaced with [Toepick](/wiki/Toepick "Toepick") in the opening intro of the show, specifically in the part where the former used to appear alongside [Shocksquatch](/wiki/Shocksquatch "Shocksquatch"), Bloxx, and [Crashhopper](/wiki/Crashhopper "Crashhopper").
* Way Big and [Diamondhead](/wiki/Diamondhead_(Classic) "Diamondhead (Classic)") are the only aliens who have ever defeated [Vilgax](/wiki/Vilgax_(Classic) "Vilgax (Classic)").
* Way Big is one of four aliens to make their debut in a movie or special; the others being [Eon](/wiki/Eon_(Transformation) "Eon (Transformation)"), [Nanomech](/wiki/Nanomech "Nanomech"), and Shocksquatch.
* Way Big is one of four aliens to have been unlocked by [Azmuth](/wiki/Azmuth_(Classic) "Azmuth (Classic)"); the others being Murk [Upchuck](/wiki/Upchuck "Upchuck"), [Bullfrag](/wiki/Bullfrag "Bullfrag"), [Atomix](/wiki/Atomix "Atomix").
* Way Big is one of only five aliens who appeared in each series of the Classic Timeline; the other four are Upchuck, [Cannonbolt](/wiki/Cannonbolt_(Classic) "Cannonbolt (Classic)"), Diamondhead, and [Ghostfreak](/wiki/Ghostfreak "Ghostfreak").
	+ So far, Diamondhead, Cannonbolt, and Way Big are the only aliens to have appeared in every series in the franchise, including the reboot.
	+ Additionally, Way Big is one of the only five aliens who escaped from the [original Omnitrix](/wiki/Omnitrix_(Original) "Omnitrix (Original)"); the others are [Spidermonkey](/wiki/Spidermonkey_(Classic) "Spidermonkey (Classic)"), [Goop](/wiki/Goop_(Classic) "Goop (Classic)"), Ghostfreak, and [Chromastone](/wiki/Chromastone "Chromastone").
	+ Furthermore, Way Big is one of four regular aliens to be temporarily destroyed and removed from the original Omnitrix/[Ultimatrix](/wiki/Ultimatrix_(Original) "Ultimatrix (Original)"), the others being Chromastone, [Feedback](/wiki/Feedback "Feedback"), and [Swampfire](/wiki/Swampfire "Swampfire").


Notes
-----


1. [↑](#cite_ref-16) Way Big's cosmic rays were originally colored pale blue in **Ultimate Alien** before being recolored green in **Omniverse**. Meanwhile, alternate versions of Way Big are shown firing cosmic rays in different colors. For example, [Mad Ben](/wiki/Mad_Ben "Mad Ben")'s Way Big fires orange cosmic rays.[[9]](#cite_note-IaMMMBWP2-15)
2. [↑](#cite_ref-17) In **Ultimate Alien**, Way Big's cosmic ray attack was executed by crossing his arms in an "L" shape, with his right forearm vertical and left forearm horizontal in front of it and the thumb edge of his hands facing his body. In **Omniverse**, this move is slightly revised, as Way Big now crosses his arms in an "X" shape.
3. [↑](#cite_ref-21) According to [Derrick J. Wyatt](/wiki/Derrick_J._Wyatt "Derrick J. Wyatt"), Way Big's enhanced speed was due to a glitch with the original Ultimatrix. This ability was removed from Way Big's DNA when [Ben](/wiki/Ben_Tennyson_(Classic) "Ben Tennyson (Classic)") acquired the new Omnitrix in *[The Ultimate Enemy: Part 2](/wiki/The_Ultimate_Enemy:_Part_2 "The Ultimate Enemy: Part 2")*.[[DJW 2]](#cite_note-19) However, it is unknown if this is canon as [Matt Wayne](/wiki/Matt_Wayne "Matt Wayne") seemed to be unaware of the existence of glitch powers.[[MW 2]](#cite_note-20)
